**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Sadly, the pear phone and the pear pad that they use on Nickelodeon shows such as *iCarly* and *Victorious* is not real. It is a prop, and a pretty blatant take on Apple products (get it? Apple, Pear, both fruits roughly the same size, etc) You can get a pear phone case and pear phone stickers to theme your iPhone to look like a pear phone. But in reality, there is no such thing as a pear phone. It\'s a TV prop.
