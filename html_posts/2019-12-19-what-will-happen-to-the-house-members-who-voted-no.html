**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What will happen to the House members who voted not to impeach President Trump?** The Republicans will be just fine. No Republicans thought that there was anything to this impeachment sham anyway, so they won't lose any votes back home that they weren't going to get anyway. They may have even picked up a few Independent voters, according to most polls. There are a few Democrats who voted against a few Articles. These were Democrats elected in 2018 in red districts which voted for Trump in 2016, and they were going to have a really hard time going back to their districts and explaining to all these Trump voters why he/she voted to impeach. So they made a political gamble and split their votes, voting for one article and voting against the other. That way, they can appear studious and thoughtful, as if they really analyzed the situation and voted their conscience. This may allow them the tiniest possibility that they'll be retained in office. But it's a political gamble either way.
