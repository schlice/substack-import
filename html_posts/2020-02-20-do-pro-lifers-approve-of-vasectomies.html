**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Do pro-lifers approve of vasectomies?** The pro-life movement has no specific views on vasectomies. Pro-lifers are concerned with humans who are killed after the sperm joins with the egg, not with what form of birth control anyone may choose to use. You're probably confused because you correlate "pro-life people" with "religious people." Not everyone who is pro-life is religious, nor does the pro-life position have as a requirement any specific religious teachings. The pro-life position, on its own, has nothing to say about vasectomies.
