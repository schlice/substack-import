**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The problem here is that you *planned* on baiting criminals and then *planned* on killing them, *planning* on citing self-defense as an excuse. All of this *planning* is what is commonly referred to as **premeditation*,*** and it is one of the things needed to support a murder charge. So what's likely in this scenario is that you would be arrested and charged with murder. Sometimes, police do this in what is known as a "sting" operation. So if you really want to do this, work with your local police department.
