--- title: '"If an armed burglar is confronted by an armed homeowner, are they both allowed to shoot each other? Who would get prosecuted?"' date: '2017-06-23' tags: - law-enforcement - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Short answer: yes. Since you're only discussing the actual shooting part, anyone in fear for their life has the right to defend themselves using whatever force is necessary, up to and including deadly force. Thankfully, you didn't ask us to tackle the legal questions surrounding who would end up going to jail assuming someone was still alive. Phew! But specific to the self-defense, everyone in America has the right to life, and you can use any force to counter that threat.
