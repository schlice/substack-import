--- title: '"Why are people so eager to surrender any of their rights to their government?"' date: '2018-05-03' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Why are people so eager to surrender any of their rights to their government?** Because whatever right they are eager to surrender (the only one that comes to mind is the Second Amendment) is not important *to them*, they see no usefulness in having it, and they believe that its continued presence is dangerous, and so they have no compunction seeing it dismantled or removed entirely.
