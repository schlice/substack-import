**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If hurricane Dorian does damage to Mar-a-Lago will Donald Trump pay for it or would the US taxpayers pay for the repairs?** The government would only pay for damages to government assets. For example, if USSS set up facilities at Mar-a-Lago, those would be paid for by the government. The government would not pay for any general damages to the facility.
