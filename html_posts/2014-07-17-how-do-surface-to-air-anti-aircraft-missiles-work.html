Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [The missiles have active guidance systems on board that guide the missile to the target. While there are several different kinds of anti-aircraft missiles, the two most common are **radar-guided** and **heat-seeking** missiles. The one assumed to have shot down the Malaysian Airlines aircraft would presumably have been a radar-guided missile.]{.rendered_qtext}
