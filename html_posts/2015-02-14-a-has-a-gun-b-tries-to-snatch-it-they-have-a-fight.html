--- title: "A has a gun. B tries to snatch it. They have a fight, and consequently shots are fired and B dies. How would this be dealt with legally?" date: "2015-02-14" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [The case will be to determine whether or not the shooting was accidental. If the finding is that the shooting was accidental, the charge would be involuntary manslaughter. If there was intent to shoot, the charge may be increased to murder.]{.rendered_qtext}
