**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Mukbang by itself, or by its nature alone, is not sinful. It's a show about eating. However, mukbang promotes unhealthy eating habits, and could potentially glamorize gluttony. A study found that people who frequently watch mukbang may be more susceptible to having poor eating habits and feel the impulse to eat food whenever it can be seen.[\[1\]](#kbbla){#cite-kbbla .citation_link} This is the danger that Christians face. Christians can watch mukbang, but they should be on guard, especially if they already have poor impulse control. It may lead them to the sin. \ Original question: Is Mukbang a sin in Christian law? If it is a sin, which one of the seven deadly sins describe it best?
