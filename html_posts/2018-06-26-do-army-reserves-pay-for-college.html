--- title: "Do army reserves pay for college?" date: "2018-06-26" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} In addition to the great GI Bill options you're hearing about here, if you are not yet in the military, you might be able to get into Army ROTC in your college (if they offer it) and there are scholarships available if you go that route. Once you complete college, you'll be commissioned as a 2LT in the Army Reserves. So it's the same thing, just in reverse (college first, then the Army Reserves).
