Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} This is exactly why we visit our neurologists regularly. We manage the disease and maintain a good quality of life. So what will you and your neurologist do? - Manage the disease. You'll keep tabs on the disease progress, and make sure the disease is progressing as slowly as possible. You'll do this by using diagnostic tools like the MRI and neurological exams to check the progress of the disease, and you'll control its progress by taking one of the approved MS therapy drugs. - Maintain a good quality of life. Learn what you can and can't do. Don't push yourself so hard that you hurt yourself, but exercise as you can. Be cautions of overheating, as heat is the enemy of MS. Remember: *progress, not perfection.* Give yourself some grace; MS is insidious and it tries to keep you from moving.
