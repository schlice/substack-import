**Content:** [In the United States, there is no blanket ban on mobile phones in schools and/or colleges. Mobile phones are generally allowed in the buildings, but in grade schools, they are either not allowed or required to be on mute or vibrate, in the classroom. This is up to the policy of the individual school or district. This is for several reasons:\ \ ]{.rendered_qtext} 1. They are distracting, and it is impolite to interrupt a lecture with your phone\'s noises 2. Children at that age are easily distracted, and the teacher would really like to teach the curriculum with the students\' full attentions, or as best as they can get it. \ College is similar, except that in most cases, phones must be on vibrate. Students in college are expected to be more mature, and whether or not they pay attention to the curriculum is their decision.
