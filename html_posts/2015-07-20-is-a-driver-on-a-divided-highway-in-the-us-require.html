--- title: "Is a driver on a divided highway in the US required to pull over for an emergency vehicle (police, fire, ambulance) that is going the opposite direction?" date: "2015-07-20" tags: ["law-enforcement"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [In Colorado, you are only required to move over if the emergency vehicle is on your side of the road on a divided highway.\ \ On a non-divided highway, all vehicles on both sides of the road must move.]{.rendered_qtext}
