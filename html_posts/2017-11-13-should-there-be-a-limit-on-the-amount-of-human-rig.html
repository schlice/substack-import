Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Why do there have to be some limits on individual rights?** I am free to exercise my rights. And so are you. But what happens if the free exercise of my rights interferes with your free exercise of your rights? Whose free exercise wins? Thus, some limits on individual rights.
