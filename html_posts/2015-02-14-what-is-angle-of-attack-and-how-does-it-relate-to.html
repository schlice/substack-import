**Content:** [In fluid dynamics, the angle of attack (AOA) is the angle between the direction of the oncoming air and the chord of an airfoil. Usually the chord is a line between the leading and trailing edge of the aircraft.\ \ Many modern aircraft do not have an AOA indicator, and in at least one aircraft accident that I know of, failure to fully understand the aircraft\'s AOA was a factor in a crash.]{.rendered_qtext}
