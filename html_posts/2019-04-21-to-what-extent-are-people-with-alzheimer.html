**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Alzheimer\'s is considered a disability as far as the Americans with Disabilities Act is concerned. Therefore, people with Alzheimer\'s are protected under employment law, and under housing law, against discrimination because of their disease, in just the same way that anyone else with a disability is protected.
