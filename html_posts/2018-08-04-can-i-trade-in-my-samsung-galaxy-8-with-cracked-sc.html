**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can I trade in my Samsung Galaxy 8 with cracked screen and get credit towards a new phone?** Depends on what you mean by "trade in." If you're literally talking about a trade-in program, some carriers offer this service. You can turn in a phone which you own outright, in any condition, and they will give you a certain amount of money for that phone, depending on the amount of damage. You can immediately apply that money towards the purchase of a new device, take it as bill credits, etc. If you don't own the phone (i.e. you're leasing it or making no-interest payments on its purchase), before you can trade it in, you have to fix it.
