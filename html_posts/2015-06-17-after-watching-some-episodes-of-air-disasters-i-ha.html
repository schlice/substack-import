Do mechanics get punished and if so, how bad it will be?"' date: '2015-06-17' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [In addition to the other answers is the theory of *corporate responsibility.* A mechanic acts as an agent of the corporation, so the ultimate liability is with the corporation.\ \ The mechanic who does shoddy work will be terminated with prejudice, which means it\'s a bad conduct termination and unless he lies about his past, he\'ll never get another job as an aircraft mechanic.\ \ The mechanic who *intentionally* causes a part to fail will be held individually criminally responsible. The mechanic who is not criminally negligent but just bad at his job will be fired, and the company will be held financially responsible for the accident.]{.rendered_qtext}
