**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Why did the Supreme Court delay the decision on the disclosure of Trump\'s finances?** This is an extremely important case, and it deserves to be handled with the utmost of care. Trump's presidency is unique because Trump is not a politician and his finances affects more than just himself. He has companies in his name, and his family's and others' finances are also directly related to his finances. Disclosure of those finances, for political purposes, can cost his companies many millions of dollars. Trump is also the first modern president who has not voluntarily released his financial information. This lack of candor has rankled his political opponents, who truly believe that he is only withholding his personal financial information because he has something to hide. The Court will be determining the scope of the separation-of-powers doctrine which is part of the design of the Constitution. It will determine the limits of Congressional oversight; in other words, how far can Congress go into investigating the financial history of a president before he became president. This decision affects not only Trump, but every other president to follow, so it is very important. This is why the court is moving slowly and deliberately.
