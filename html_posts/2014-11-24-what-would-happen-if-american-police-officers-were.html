**Content:** [I don\'t understand the logic.\ \ In the state of Colorado, \"open carry\" is legal. So an ordinary citizen can strap on a weapon with no training or licensing, but a highly-trained community-minded peace officer cannot?\ \ The only logical conclusion that I could find is that police would be essentially \"neutered,\" only able to counter the deadly force they face daily with non-lethal alternatives, such as batons, TASERs, and pepper spray.\ \ I would also see the use of other non-lethal tools increase, such as \"bean-bag\" guns or rubber bullets.\ \ Consequently, I think you\'d see police brutality increase, as officers would have to over-compensate for the fact that they do not carry lethal force while everyone they encounter can.]{.rendered_qtext}
