**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I assume you're talking about these. ::: {.ui_qtext_gif_noclick_wrapper background-url="images/qimg-ece426eecb75d7aa4de2d9fa078e99dd" gif-embedded="images/qimg-ece426eecb75d7aa4de2d9fa078e99dd" is-playing="true" interactive="true"} ![](images/qimg-ece426eecb75d7aa4de2d9fa078e99dd){.qtext_image style="max-width: 100%;"} ::: *(Image courtesy* [[*LaughingGIF.com*](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=6bb022&url=http://m.laughinggif.com/view/fwzzmqpttp/39.html){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="laughinggif.com"}]{.qlink_container}*)* Perhaps you can see just inside the upper segment on the leftmost number is a small window and something inside it. Yep, it's a radar emitter. The more sophisticated signs are "smart signs," and usually the portable ones the police put out are called "smart trailers." These generally contain a small computer which records the time of day and the average speed, to better utilize police personnel during patrol operations.
