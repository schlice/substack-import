**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Which NRA members on Quora would agree that 18-year-old Black Lives Matter activists with AR-15s would constitute a \"well-regulated militia\" deserving of Second Amendment protections?** Again, that whole "well-regulated militia" clause is confusing people. Please, please, please, take the time to read *DC v. Heller* **** **so you can understand who is allowed to own a gun and under what circumstances**. *Heller,* along with *MacDonald v. Chicago,* incorporated the 2nd Amendment to the states. It basically said that gun ownership is an individual right, not contingent on military service. If you are a citizen of the United States, you have that right. Period, end of story. You don't have to *do* anything to get the right. You don't have to be part of any group to get the right. Just as you don't have to *do* anything to get the right to free speech, you don't have to *do* anything to get the right to keep and bear arms.
