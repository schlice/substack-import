Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It is legal in any state in which you can buy cannabis. If you meet the requirements to buy cannabis, you can buy CBD oil. The requirements vary by state. Some states allow medical marijuana, which requires special permission from the state, and the requirements for each state differ. Some states have decriminalized possession of small quantities of marijuana. And two states (Washington and Colorado) have made recreational possession legal.
