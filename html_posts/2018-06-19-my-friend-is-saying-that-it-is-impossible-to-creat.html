**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **My friend is saying that it is impossible to create an AI that can mimic or exceed the human brain, how can I prove it otherwise?** The classic test to determine if an AI is as smart as a human is what's called the [[Turing test](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=753614&url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turing_test){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="wikipedia.org"}]{.qlink_container}. This is the gold standard for AI. If your computer AI can pass the Turing test, then this is how you prove that an AI can mimic or exceed the human brain.
