Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The problem with the argument is that the questioner assumes we need a replacement for the SR-71. While the MiG-31 could do something similar in flight characteristics to the SR-71 (albeit for a very short time and with great risk to the airframe and pilot), not even the SR-71 performs that mission anymore. It has been supplanted by *satellite* data. Over time, flying a satellite is much cheaper than flying a SR-71.
