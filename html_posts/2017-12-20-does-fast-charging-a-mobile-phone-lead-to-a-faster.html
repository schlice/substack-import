**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Does fast charging a mobile phone lead to a faster battery drain?** No, but there is evidence that constantly fast charging mobile phones leads to decreased overall battery life. Why? The fast charge circuit and the increased load on the battery generates more heat, and the battery components break down faster over time. However, we're talking something like an 11% reduction in battery life over two years (IIRC), and many people will swap out their phones for newer, shinier ones before that.
