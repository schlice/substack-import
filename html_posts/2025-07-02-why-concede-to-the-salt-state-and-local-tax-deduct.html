Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Because the concession wasn't made to Democrats. The concession was made to Republicans from blue states in order to gain *their* votes. Their constituents wanted the concessions. so a compromise was made: allow the SALT deductions *but phase them out over time*. So we get the Republican votes, and we still get the phased-in elimination of SALT deductions.
