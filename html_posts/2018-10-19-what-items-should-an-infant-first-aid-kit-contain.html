Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The only items I can think of off the top of my head that would be different than any other first aid kit would be an infant CPR mask and maybe a bulb syringe. Other than that, dressings and bandages, while smaller, should be the same as a full size kit.
