**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} *Fine*? Probably not. But the HOA normally *can* remove such an officer from the Board *if* the Bylaws and Articles of Incorporation require that Board members be residents of that community, not just homeowners. There may also be a process for that Board member removal spelled out in the same section of the Bylaws, but one way or another, if the rule about residency exists, the Board can remove a Board member who violates that rule.
