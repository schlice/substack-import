**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Where in Colorado do I report labor law violations such as not giving mandatory breaks and having safety violations in the workplace?** This one's easy. The bit about "mandatory breaks" is covered by the Colorado Department of Labor and Employment. They have [[an online form](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=1438e0&url=https://www.colorado.gov/pacific/cdle/complaint-forms){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="colorado.gov"}]{.qlink_container} to fill out to begin an investigation. > The Division of Labor Standards and Statistics may assist employees > performing work in Colorado with the recovery of earned compensation, > and labor law violations. If you wish to file a Wage and Hour Law > Complaint with the Division, you must fully complete and submit to the > Division a Wage and Hour Labor Law Complaint Form. The form includes > instructions on the complaint process and the authority of the > Division of Labor Standards and Statistics. As for safety violations, this could be more serious. If it's an occupational safety and health violation that could put the public at risk, I suggest you contact by phone the [[Colorado Department of Public Health and Environment](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=93a241&url=https://www.colorado.gov/pacific/cdphe/categories/concerns-and-emergencies){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="colorado.gov"}]{.qlink_container}. That website has contacts for all sorts of public health-related issues and how to report them. Good luck!
