**Content:** [A2A\ \ For me, a few reasons.\ \ My wife, who knows me better than I do, says that I have a \"servant\'s heart.\" Everything that I have done in my life has been some form of service. My military service was pretty obvious. After that, I went to work for a computer company\...in customer service. And now, I am working towards volunteering for the Sheriff\'s Office as a Reserve Deputy.\ \ But why law enforcement? It\'s in my blood. My grandpa and my dad were both cops. Once you have lived in a cop\'s family, it sorta \"grows\" on you. And I\'ve already been a reserve cop before: I was a READY Augmentee for Security Forces in the Air Force. I did really enjoy that work.\ \ So I know it\'s not for the money, since Reserve Deputies don\'t get paid. For me, I want to give back to my community in a tangible, meaningful (to me) way. I can fulfill my dream of continuing my family legacy in law enforcement and still provide the lifestyle my family has come to expect. This is what I chose.\ \ (BTW, this is a portion of the presentation I gave at Oral Boards when I was applying to the department).]{.rendered_qtext}
