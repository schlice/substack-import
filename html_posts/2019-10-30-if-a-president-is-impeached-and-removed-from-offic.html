**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If a president is impeached and removed from office, where would he serve his sentence if the cause is a misdemeanor or felony?** Impeachment is a political remedy, not a legal one. A president can be impeached without ever breaking a Federal law. If a president is impeached and removed from office because it is *alleged* that he violated a Federal law, he still has to be *indicted* by a Federal prosecutor, tried in Federal court, and convicted of an actual crime. Remember, you're still innocent until proven guilty in America.
