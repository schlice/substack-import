**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **I have a Republican friend who is telling me that the \$1,400 stimulus check is not coming and that it is only democratic propaganda. Is he right, or has my friend lost his marbles?** Your friend may have lost his marbles. I'm not sure about that. What I am sure about is that the latest stimulus has indeed been approved, and checks should start coming out this next week. The income limits for those to receive the maximum amount will remain the same as the last stimulus checks, and it is all based off or your adjusted gross income, or AGI. Individuals who earn up to \$75,000 in adjusted gross income, heads of household up to \$112,500, and married filing jointly with AGI up to \$150,000 will get the full \$1,400 per person. This time, however, the amount gets reduced much quicker. Payments will be capped at \$80,000 per individual, \$120,000 for heads of household and \$160,000 for married couples. People at those adjusted gross income levels and above will not get a check.
