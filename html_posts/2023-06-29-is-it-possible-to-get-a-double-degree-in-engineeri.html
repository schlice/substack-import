--- title: "Is it possible to get a double degree in engineering at the military academy?" date: "2023-06-29" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Probably not. You could double major in an engineering discipline and then in another STEM field, but you probably wouldn't have the time in your schedule to double-major in two engineering disciplines. One of my classmates in my graduating squadron double majored in Astronautical Engineering and Math. So I know that it is possible to do it, but I just don't think you have enough time in the schedule to cram two engineering disciplines in, with all of the other Academy commitments on your time.
