--- title: '"Is baptism, in fact, the only way to salvation?"' date: '2022-04-29' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Not at all. *Baptism is what saved people do.* - Baptism doesn't save you. You're baptized because you're already saved. Salvation comes from personal faith in what Christ has *already* done for you. Baptism is just the outward expression of that faith. - Baptism doesn't gain you extra favor with God. God already loves you completely. - Baptism is a step of obedience. Jesus made it clear that his followers demonstrate that fact through baptism by immersion. Obedience to his commands is one way in which we demonstrate our love for him. \ Original question: Is baptism, in fact, the only way to salvation?
