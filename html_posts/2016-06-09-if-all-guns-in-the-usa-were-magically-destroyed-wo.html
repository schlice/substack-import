**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Unless you can simultaneously destroy all guns from existence along with the knowledge of how to build one, destroying all guns in one location will only create a new market for illegal guns. If you outlaw guns, you'll create a tremendous black market for guns, and only the people willing to violate the law will have the guns which they will smuggle into the country illegally or manufacture illegally or 3-D print illegally.
