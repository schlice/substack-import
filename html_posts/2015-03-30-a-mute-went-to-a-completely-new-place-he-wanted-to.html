--- title: "A mute went to a completely new place. He wanted to drink some water in a house owned by a blind person. How will the mute ask the blind person for water?" date: "2015-03-30" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Braille computer display.\ \ The mute will type a message on the computer using the keyboard, which then displays on the braille reader below the keyboard. The blind person will read the \"display\" and get the water.\ \ It\'s the same technology that blind people use today to read computer screens.\ \ I do like [[Dusan Ondris](https://www.quora.com/profile/Dusan-Ondris)]{.qlink_container}\'s suggestion of text-to-voice. It should be simple enough on a mobile phone to use such an app, and it\'s likely that a mute person may have that app handy for day-to-day use.]{.rendered_qtext}
