Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} - Visit your local Force Support Squadron on base (formerly known as MWR --- morale, welfare, and recreation). They can help you find *all sorts* of things to do with your free time. - Get a part-time job on base. There are lots of facilities that employ part-time off-duty airmen to work. - You could, as you suggested, work on your degree, or work on an advanced degree.
