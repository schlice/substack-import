Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The charge in most jurisdictions is "brandishing a weapon," but if you didn't call the police right away, I doubt they will charge the individual now. You can always feel free to call the police department (non-emergency number, please) and ask to speak to an officer about the situation. You can also sue for assault, as brandishing a weapon is also an assault in tort. Check with a lawyer on this one.
