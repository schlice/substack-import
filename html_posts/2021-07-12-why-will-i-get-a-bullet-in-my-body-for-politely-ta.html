**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} We can really only answer this question when you can explain how you can "politely" **take away someone's property**. You can **buy** the property from them, but that may get a little expensive for you. You can **steal** the property from them, but the police might have something to say about that. Also, it's not very polite, and it might get you a bullet in your body if you break into their house to steal their property. You can **ask** them to simply give you their property. You can expect that they probably won't though. Unless you can tell me how you intend to "politely" dispossess someone of their legally-acquired property, there's really no way we can help you here. \ Original question: Why will I get a bullet in my body for politely taking away guns from people in America to stop mass shootings?
