**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **After buying a new phone who should I sign with for a text, talk, and data plan?** 1. Sign with a carrier whose network is supported by your new phone. 2. Sign with a carrier who offers the best coverage in the area in which you spend the most time 3. Sign with a carrier who offers the packages of data, talk, and text you like the best for the price you find the most agreeable.
