--- title: '"Can I call 911 for a suicidal friend who told me they are going to attempt suicide?"' date: '2021-11-18' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} If your friend told you when they plan on committing suicide, you can call 911 to report this attempt at self-harm. Understand what this will do: your friend will be "arrested" for their own protection, and they will be delivered to a hospital where they can be evaluated and can hopefully get help. How that all happens and what the process looks like will differ from state to state, but it will all take place looking something like that. Anyway, it doesn't hurt to call for help if you need it. If you believe your friend was genuine with their statement, you really should call for help. \ Original question: Can I call 911 for a suicidal friend who told me they are going to attempt suicide?
