**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **"The law is not what is written, the law is what is revealed to you when you follow your conscience." To what extent do you agree/disagree with this statement?** I fully and wholeheartedly disagree with this statement. The law *exactly is* what's written, it can't be any other way, and it can't because **when I follow my conscience, and when you follow your conscience, we may not arrive at the same place.** Following your conscience is great. Having a sound moral compass is a feature of a well-fashioned life. But not everyone has that feature in their lives, and *basing a system of law on following conscience means that there is no common baseline* for people to point to. In short, follow your conscience, but the law is the law, and it can only be what is written, so that it applies equally and similarly to all people.
