**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} So long as any resale of any product is allowed, the answer is yes. The typical type of private sale is called an "as-is" sale. Unless prior arrangements are made, this is the condition of sale that is assumed. This is because private sellers are not held to the same standards as retailers. For example, if you private sell a car, the seller is not required to ensure the car passes a state inspection before selling it. The seller is also not required to sanitize the inside of the car before selling it, considering what sorts of behaviors may go on in the back seat of said car.
