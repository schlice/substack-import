Answer










Content: [As a junior System Administrator in an IT department, you
perform all of the functions required by your department. Depending on
the department for which you work, you could be involved in standard
end-user system administrator duties:\
]

- Creation, modification, and deletion of user accounts, including
domain accounts, email accounts, SecurID accounts, and specific
internal server and system accounts
- Backups of user home directories
- Restoration of lost user files from backups
- Addition and removal of user workstations, imaging and re-imaging
workstations\
- Resetting user passwords\
- Handling user support requests

Organizations nowadays don\'t do hardware repair inside their
organizations. Hardware is sent out to a vendor for repair, or a vendor
comes on site to fix hardware. Unless you work for that vendor, you
won\'t be doing specific hardware repair. You may be doing hardware
upgrades on servers, but not usually on workstations.
