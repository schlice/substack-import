Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} > **Does Satan have any chance of entering Heaven?** No. Being angels, they have angelic minds, and thus when they chose to rebel, they did so with the full knowledge of the glory of God. In other words, it was a 100% free choice. > **Then why should those who serve Satan or claim to be of satanic > descent have any chance?** Because humans have *human minds*, not *angelic minds*. They do not have a full understanding or appreciation of the glory of God, and they have been corrupted by sin. It is for these reasons that humans have the *opportunity* to reject sin and accept God whereas the angels did not. > **What would be the consequence of cooperating with someone who > opposes God?** Hopefully you convince them to repent and believe. But everyone opposes God in some form or another, because we are all sinful and we have all missed the mark when it comes to God's sovereignty. What we have that the angels and demons do not: **hope**.
