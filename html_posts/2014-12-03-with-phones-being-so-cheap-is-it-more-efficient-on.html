**Content:** [Is it more efficient? Certainly. One phone that does nothing but GPS mapping and a second that just provides a Wi-Fi hotspot.\ \ But is it more convenient? Not really. You have to start your Wi-Fi hotspot instead of your mapping app, and you have to maintain two devices.\ \ As far as efficiency, it is. But it is definitely less convenient.]{.rendered_qtext}
