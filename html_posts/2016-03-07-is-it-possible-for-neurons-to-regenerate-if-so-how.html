--- title: '"Is it possible for neurons to regenerate? If so, how long does the process take?"' date: '2016-03-07' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Neurons can live for a hundred years, and while it is true that we can make new ones, it\'s also true that this process does not happen quickly or easily. That\'s why we are born with about 100 billion neurons, because we will need them for our entire lifetime. I don\'t know how long it takes a neuron to regenerate in an adult, but I do know that the process is a slow one.
