**Content:** [Why can\'t you yell \"fire\" in a crowded movie theater if there is no fire? Just because you have free speech doesn\'t mean you have an unfettered, unrestricted right to say anything you want at any time and for any conceivable reason.\ \ We tend to forget that every right we have in this country comes with an associated responsibility. You have the right to free speech, but you do not have the right to harass or intimidate with your speech. You have the responsibility to use your speech responsibly.]{.rendered_qtext}
