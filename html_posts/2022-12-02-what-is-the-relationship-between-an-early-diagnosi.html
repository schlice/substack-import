--- title: '"What is the relationship between an early diagnosis and prognosis for multiple sclerosis?"' date: '2022-12-02' tags: - health-autoimmune layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Some preliminary studies have shown a positive correlation between early treatment with MS and slower disease progression, and delayed onset of SPMS. These are long-term longitudinal studies, so they literally take decades to complete, but we're already seeing some of those results. This makes sense, since the MS medications slow the progress of the disease.
