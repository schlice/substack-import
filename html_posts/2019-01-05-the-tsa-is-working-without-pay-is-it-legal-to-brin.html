**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **The TSA is working without pay. Is it legal to bring cash to the airport and tip them? They are protecting our lives.** It's not illegal to give money to someone, unless you're paying for something illegal. However, it's not really a great idea to give money to a TSA agent. Besides potentially showing impropriety (i.e. they're letting your little pocket knife slip through because you paid them), because TSA is a federal agency, there are massive rules about federal agents receiving gifts. It's usually not allowed.
