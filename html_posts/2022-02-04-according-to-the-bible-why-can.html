**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The Catholics teach more about this than the Christians do, so let me give you the Catholic answer. The Catechism of the Catholic Church (CCC) teaches that angels are spiritual beings. They are not bound by time and space, as we are. We are bound by time and space. We have the ability to "re-think" our decisions, because we have been given *time* to do so. Angels, being spiritual beings and not being bound by space and time, do not have this luxury. When an angel rejects God, it is a definitive, complete rejection of his sovereignty.
