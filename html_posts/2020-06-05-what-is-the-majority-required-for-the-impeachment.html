Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How many votes are needed to impeach the president?** A simple majority in the House of Representatives is required to pass Articles of Impeachment against a sitting president. A supermajority of votes in the Senate is required to remove an impeached president from office.
