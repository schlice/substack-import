**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Quick US government lesson here: *Trump doesn't pass a tax bill.* Trump has spent a lot of time advocating for tax cuts, but it's not Trump's bill. It's the Republicans in Congress who passed (separate versions of) a tax bill. The bill still needs to go to committee and be resolved, and then they need to vote on it again before it goes to Trump for signature.
