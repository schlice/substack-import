**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Because decades of observation and scientific inquiry have proven and re-proven that **hating living and having no will to go through life is** ***abnormal.*** We recognize that it is not a normal human condition to hate life and lose the will to live. This is considered a mental defect, an abnormal functioning of the brain. It is a *medical condition*. **We do not treat medical conditions with euthanasia.** We treat medical conditions humanely, with counseling, therapy, and in some cases, medication, where it helps.
