--- title: "Could a glider be detected by radar?" date: "2015-09-15" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Depends on the type of glider. Aluminum skinned gliders which are towed or launched reflect enough radar signals to be seen on radar. Lighter gliders like hanggliders do not have enough flat metallic surfaces to reflect radar, and so will likely not be seen.]{.rendered_qtext}
