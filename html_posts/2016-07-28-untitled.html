**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The Boberg utilizes both the rotating barrel and the bullpup-style rearward magazine feed. The main spring on the Boberg is really small, such that you are surprised that it's actually the main spring. It has a very light slide action. Boberg claims that this gun is favored by those with limited upper body strength because it is just so easy to rack. I've noticed a common complaint among reviewers of the Boberg: if you don't fully rack the slide when reloading, the round can get jammed on the ramp and it's a pig to clear. It's a different sort of muscle memory mechanism than simply releasing the slide to reload and it will take practice, but the fact that it has a longer barrel in a smaller package is simply awesome. I personally love the design; this would make a fantastic back up gun or concealed pistol, since you're really not too worried about reloading your backup gun.
