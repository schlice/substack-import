**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} A2A Because that's silly. Just because you don't like a book doesn't mean you should clog your toilet over it. If you own a book you're not interested in reading, you should perhaps donate it. Maybe someone else might enjoy reading it more than you did. A better option would be to burn it. Just be aware of your municipality's outdoor burning rules, if you have any in place at the time. I wouldn't advise burning the book inside the house. It's just a book. The pages on which the words are printed aren't sacred, or imbued with any special magic. Feel free to dispose of it in any way you like. \ Original question: Why don't people flush the Bible down the toilet?
