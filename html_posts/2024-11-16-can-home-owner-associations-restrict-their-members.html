--- title: "Can home owner associations restrict their members from displaying Trump/Pence lawn signs on election day, even if other rules are suspended for that day?" date: "2024-11-16" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} In general, no they cannot. They can restrict the display of ***ALL*** signs, but they cannot restrict the display of specific signs which are not generally considered to be offensive. While some people take personal offense at a "Trump/Pence" sign, it is not generally considered to be offensive.
