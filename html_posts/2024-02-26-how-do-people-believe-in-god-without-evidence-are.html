Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Some people may believe without evidence, but most people believe in God with very good evidence. It's just not the evidence that you're thinking. It's not science, because you can't use science tools to find an immaterial God. We have to use other tools to provide the evidence of God's existence.
