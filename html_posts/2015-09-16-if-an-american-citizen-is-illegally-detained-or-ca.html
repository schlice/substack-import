--- title: "If an American citizen is illegally detained or captured in another country, how would the U.S. government or military respond?" date: "2015-09-16" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [For most ordinary citizens, they will not.\ \ You can make a request to the US Embassy in the country in which you are detained, but expect to stay in prison in a foreign country for a while.\ \ The Embassy can make a request to the US State Department to extradite you to the United States, but if we don\'t currently have an extradition treaty with the country in question, you may not have much luck.]{.rendered_qtext}
