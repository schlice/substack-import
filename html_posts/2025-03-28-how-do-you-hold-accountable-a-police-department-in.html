**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} First, you have to have *standing*. You either need to be the police department, or you need to be the towing company. You can't file a lawsuit to uphold the provisions of a contract on someone else's behalf. If the parties to the contract won't uphold the contract, you can't legally *make* them. If you *are* the police department or the towing company, great! Get a lawyer.
