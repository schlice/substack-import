--- title: '"Can people, like HRC, That are being investigated for crimes lawfully allowed to run for Office/POTUS at the same time?"' date: '2016-07-31' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Yes. There are no requirements to run for president. There are requirements to hold the office of the President, but not being under investigation is not one of them. Remember, in the US, you are innocent until proven guilty. And being *accused* of a crime is not the same as being charged with a crime or being indicted for a crime.
