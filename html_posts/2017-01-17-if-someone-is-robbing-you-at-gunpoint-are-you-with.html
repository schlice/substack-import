Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} In order to use lethal force against a person, you must be in fear for your life. I would speculate that someone who has a gun pointed at them probably fears for his life at that very moment. This means that the use of deadly force would be justified. It's for this reason that they have created this little gem: :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-7b04ff9ec206b479e8f8198fe5cd092b){.landscape .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-7b04ff9ec206b479e8f8198fe5cd092b" master_w="300" master_h="300"} ::: :::: The gun wallet. This is a Ruger LCP in a gun wallet. Inside your pocket, it "prints" like a wallet, not like a gun. That means the outline of the object in your pocket looks like a wallet. The bad guy has no reason to suspect that you're actually drawing a gun, not a wallet. For the person who gets drawn on and when that suspect demands money, drawing this instead might save your life. I'd be firing this and running for cover at the same time.
