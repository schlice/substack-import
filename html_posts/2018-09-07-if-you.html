**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If you\'ve been stopped by police and they request you open your trunk, do you have to?** Not without a warrant. If they do not have a warrant, they can either get one, or place you under arrest, at which point they can tow your car to the station and inventory its contents. But of course, both of those need *probable cause* (either to get a warrant to search or to arrest you). Without those, any request to open your trunk should be answered with, "Do you have a warrant?"
