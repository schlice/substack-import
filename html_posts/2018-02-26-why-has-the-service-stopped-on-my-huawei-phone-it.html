**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Why has the service stopped on my Huawei phone? It was ok from October until a week or so ago. Now I just get emergency only. What could the problem be?** One of two problems could be happening here: 1. Your SIM card is loose. Pop your SIM tray, try cleaning it out with compressed air, and re-inserting it. You may need to power-cycle the phone. 2. Your service has been disconnected. Check with your carrier and validate your contract status.
