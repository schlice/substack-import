--- title: "Do police officers keep records of the warnings for tickets they give out?" date: "2015-05-26" tags: ["law-enforcement"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Now that there are electronic records of stops, a warning can be recorded without a ticket being issued. And they are almost never kept against a specific vehicle (i.e. a subsequent stop revealing previous warnings). For most departments, records of warnings are not kept.]{.rendered_qtext}
