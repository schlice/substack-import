--- title: '"How can I upgrade my Samsung Galaxy Core Prime using PC?"' date: '2016-12-26' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Download [[Samsung Kies](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=0e538e&url=http://www.samsung.com/us/support/owners/app/kies){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="samsung.com"}]{.qlink_container}, which is Samsung\'s official management software for phones. It does much more than just upgrade phones. Make sure you choose the correct version. First, you'll want to find out what version of software your Core Prime is running. From the App Drawer, click Settings, then click About Device. Find the section labeled "Android Version." If it is 4.3 or higher, you can use Kies 3. If it is below 4.3, you should use Kies 2.6. Both are available for download from the Kies page I linked above. Good luck!
