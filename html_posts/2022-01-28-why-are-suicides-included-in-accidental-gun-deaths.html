**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Suicides are never included in ***accidentai gun death statistics*** unless someone is intentionally lying to you. Suicides are included in ***All Gun Death*** statistics, even though the gun wound was self-inflicted, when the *intent* of the statistic is to show incidents in which one person shoots another one. Suicides are added because 33,000 deaths a year looks like a much bigger number than 11,000 deaths a year (when you exclude suicide). \ Original question: Why are suicides included in accidental gun deaths when it is an intentional act and intentional suicides by cars aren\'t counted as an accidental car death?
