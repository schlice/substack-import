--- title: '"What is the purpose of asking for forgiveness if someone does not believe in God or the concept of forgiveness from a higher power?"' date: '2024-06-04' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} There is no purpose. In order to ask for forgiveness, you must ask *someone* for their forgiveness. If you wrong someone, you ask that person for forgiveness. If you do not believe that God exists, you have no one to whom you are accountable, so you have nothing for which you need to be forgiven. All is permissible (or at least all you can get away with). Of course, if God does exist, then living like He doesn't is probably not going to be a fruitful way to live, but that's not what you asked.
