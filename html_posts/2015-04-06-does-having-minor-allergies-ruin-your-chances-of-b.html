**Content:** [The qualifications state that you must \"have no history of hay fever, asthma, or allergies after age 12.\" If you had childhood allergies and you had medical treatment to correct them (i.e. allergy shots), this is generally waiverable. If you have history of allergies of any kind since age 12, this is not generally waiverable.\ \ If you have further questions about your specific case, you can [[chat](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=33f625&url=https://chat.airforce.com/chat/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="airforce.com"}]{.qlink_container} with an online recruiting adviser or you can [[use this form](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=5a6538&url=https://contact.airforce.com/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="airforce.com"}]{.qlink_container} to contact your local Air Force recruiter.]{.rendered_qtext}
