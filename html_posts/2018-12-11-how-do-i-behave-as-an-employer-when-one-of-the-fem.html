**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How do I behave as an employer, when one of the female employees is too attractive and many times she\'s been a love target for other male employees?** It seems like you're trying to make the assertion that your male employees are pigs and can't control themselves, and the poor defenseless woman who is simply too attractive for her own good needs to be shoehorned into a closet for her own safety. Perhaps you'll have to clarify a little bit of your question so we can answer it more thoroughly. - "How do I behave" --- What exactly does this mean? Are you asking what you do about this "untenable" situation? - "Too attractive" --- Are you saying that your employee is in violation of some dress or personal appearance standard at your workplace? If she is, then you deal with that issue. If not, then I don't see that there is anything you need to concern yourself with. - "Love target" --- This doesn't even make sense. Are you saying your male employees simply cannot control themselves? Are they acting inappropriately around her? If not, she can likely handle herself. Here's how you handle it: if your female employee has been harassed, you deal with the harassment complaint when it comes up. If she comes to you complaining of a hostile work environment, you fire the men responsible for creating a hostile work environment. It mostly seems like what you have to do is fire the children, and you hire **adults** next time.
