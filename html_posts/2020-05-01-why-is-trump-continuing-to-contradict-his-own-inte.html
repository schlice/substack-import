**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Why is Trump continuing to contradict his own intelligence community?** Intelligence is just one piece of evidence that constitutes the *body of evidence* any leader uses to make decisions. Intelligence is not infallible. Just ask George W. Bush. So it's not like he's ignoring known good facts. It's intelligence, and all you know is that he made a decision that does not align with the recommendation from the intelligence community. You don't know the *confidence level* of the intelligence he contradicted., Was it strong or weak? Was it verified by other intelligence agencies, or are our guys standing alone in their assessment? A good leader does not blindly follow the "experts." The leader has more to consider than the experts do.
