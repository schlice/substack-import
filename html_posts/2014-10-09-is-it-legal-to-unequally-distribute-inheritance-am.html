**Content:** [Your will is more likely to be contested if you don\'t state your reasons for why your inheritance is unequally distributed. Ensure that you are very clear that your inequality in distribution is not an oversight, the numbers were very clearly decided based on the value you placed on your personal relationship with each individual.\ \ I suggest that if you have an inheritance of some significance and you choose to be unequal, check with a lawyer about the proper wording to help prevent the contesting of your will, or at least help your wishes be well known once your will goes into effect.]{.rendered_qtext}
