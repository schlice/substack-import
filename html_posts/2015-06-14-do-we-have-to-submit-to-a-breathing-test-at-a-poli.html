**Content:** [You can refuse, but not without consequence.\ \ In some states, refusal is probable cause to detain you and force you to take a blood test, or to force you to drive to the station to get a test on an admissible breathalyzer. These cannot be refused.\ \ In some states, you can lose your license if you refuse.\ \ In some states, police have to get a warrant before they can take your blood, but they\'re fully prepared to do that too.]{.rendered_qtext}
