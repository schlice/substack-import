**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Nothing. An HOA with no CC&Rs is not a useful HOA as it can't really accomplish anything. It has no authority and no real vision or mission. In order to be a non-profit corporation, it needs Bylaws and Articles of Incorporation, but what is its purpose? If it has no CC&Rs, it has nothing to do. The CC&Rs are what gives the HOA its authority, its purpose, its ability to collect dues, and its ability to spend that money. In other words, the CC&R is the *raison d'Ãªtre* of the HOA.
