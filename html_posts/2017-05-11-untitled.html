**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} This is the first time I've read this as well. I was there in the room for the birth of all three of my boys. My oldest son was born 21 years ago, and they had a segregated, sterile delivery room, and then a "semi-private" LR room. My other two were all born in LDR rooms. I agree wholeheartedly with your sentiment. So I was in my scrubs when my son was born. I decided to let the doctor earn their paychecks ;-) And I cried like a baby throughout your story, with equal parts joy and sorrow. Excellent writing, thanks for sharing.
