It's also not wrong to want your death to be meaningful as well. It means that you have purpose, and even in death, your purpose will be served. For anyone who signs on the dotted line and says that they're willing to give their life for their country, having a clear sense of purpose is critical. It allows you to do your duty without hesitation. Just make sure you have those priorities in the right order. Do your utmost to come home safely. But know that your sacrifice will be honored if you don't make it.
