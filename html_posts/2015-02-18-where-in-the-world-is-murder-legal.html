**Content:** [State-sanctioned killing is called \"execution\" and is a punishment handed down through the legal system. Some countries still have executions; most have gotten rid of executions as a form of punishment.\ \ There are \"justifiable homicides\" which are common in many places throughout the world, but that\'s a decision that is made by a court. There may be killings that are \"allowed\" through religious or cultural rules in some parts of the world, but again, that\'s a decision reached through a religious or cultural inquiry.\ \ But as a broad brush statement, murder isn\'t really legal anywhere.]{.rendered_qtext}
