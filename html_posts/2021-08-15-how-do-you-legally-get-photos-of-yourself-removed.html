**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} If the website does not have your permission to post the photo over which you have copyright, you can send them a DMCA Takedown Notice. [[https://copyrightalliance.org/fa\...](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=404049&url=https://copyrightalliance.org/faqs/how-to-send-dmca-takedown-notice/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="copyrightalliance.org"}]{.qlink_container} Larger service provider sites even have DMCA forms online that you can simply fill out. Of course, if they still refuse to take it down, you will have to retain a copyright attorney and sue them for violating DMCA.
