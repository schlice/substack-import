**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} This is never the right question to ask. The right question to ask is: why should \ be *illegal?* If there is a compelling state interest for something to be illegal, then a law should be created. But if there is not, then there should not be. We don't *ask permission* from government to live our lives. And if someone is fortunate enough to have enough money to buy a whole company, why can't he? \ Original question: Why is it legal for 1 person to buy entire platforms like social media?
