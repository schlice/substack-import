--- title: "How can I download the HBO NOW app on a VIZIO smart TV?" date: "2017-07-11" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It [[does not appear](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=596ad3&url=https://www.vizio.com/via-chart){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="vizio.com"}]{.qlink_container} that Vizio supports HBO NOW in their Vizio Internet Apps Plus list. It appears that the only way to use HBO NOW on a Vizio TV is to purchase a [[compatible streaming device](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=ea72b3&url=http://help.hbonow.com/app/answers/detailHBO/a_id/11/~/which-devices-work-with-hbo-now){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="hbonow.com"}]{.qlink_container}. You can use a compatible game console (Xbox or PlayStation), or you can use a streaming TV device from the list, which includes Roku, Chromecast, Apple TV, and Amazon Fire TV or Fire TV Stick. You can also directly connect your compatible computer to your TV and view it that way as well. Chromecast is unique in that you actually stream the show from your computer or tablet to your Chromecast, but it does work. It also appears that some Samsung Smart TVs natively support the HBO NOW app.
