**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} ***If I am instructed to keep my hands up and get out of the car, how do I remove my seat belt without lowering my hands?*** **You don't.** You wait until instructed on what to do, or you tell the officer, "My seat belt is still on," and then wait for further instructions.
