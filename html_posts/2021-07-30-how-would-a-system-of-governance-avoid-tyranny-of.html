**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} (US Answer Only) A blockchain-verified vote does not overcome the problems which cause a direct voting system to be inefficient for the United States. Additionally, the verified vote using blockchain simply says that the vote was not tampered with in transit. It does nothing to address the fact that someone could still steal your password and impersonate you and send your vote. \ Original question: How would a system of governance avoid tyranny of the masses with a blockchain based direct voting system?
