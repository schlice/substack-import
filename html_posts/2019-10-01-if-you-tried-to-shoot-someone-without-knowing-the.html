**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If you tried to shoot someone without knowing the gun just had blanks, would you be charged with attempted murder?** Yes. You fit all the elements of the crime, except for the unlawful termination of a life. You even took an action in furtherance of the crime (you discharged a gun in someone's direction, even though it was only loaded with blanks, which you did not know). So for example, if I gave you a gun and told you it was loaded, and you fired it at someone, you could be charged with attempted murder. As far as your *mens rea*, you were in the appropriate state of mind to be a murderer, even though you failed to complete the *actus reus*.
