**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You said that this person who recognized you actually works at your psychiatrist's office, where you are a patient. If this is true, and that person told your co-workers that you are a patient at his of her office, they have most likely violated HIPAA privacy laws. I would speak to an attorney in your local area who specializes in HIPAA or medical practice.
