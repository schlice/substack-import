**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You *can*, but it's just not as easy to game on a Linux system as it is to game on a Windows system. Steam works, and any game that is Linux compatible in Steam will work on a Linux system.\* \* with effort. They don't always work right out of the box. They often take a little bit of effort, and sometimes quite a lot of effort, to work. Take a look at Linus Sebastian trying to master gaming on Linux. Admittedly, he's a bit of a Linux n00b, and he makes some really predictably bad Linux mistakes, but even he is able to get a system together and make it playable. It's a three-part series. Enjoy it.
