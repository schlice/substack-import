**Content:** [I think you can describe computers in the same way that they were described to me; using analogs for input, processing, and output. Compare a computer to a dishwasher. \"Input\" is dirty dishes. \"Processing\" is the cleaning cycle. \"Output\" is clean dishes.\ \ Once they have a grasp of I/O, you can start to talk about how we give computers directions.]{.rendered_qtext}
