Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Let's be more specific. Can someone call 911 if you overdosed? Yes, absolutely. In fact, we prefer you call. This gets both medical professionals to your location quickly to keep you alive until you can get to a hospital, as well as law enforcement personnel to investigate the consumption of illegal substances.
