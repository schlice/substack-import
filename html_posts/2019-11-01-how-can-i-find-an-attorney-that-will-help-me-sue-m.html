**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I've always found FindLaw to be a good resource. [[Wisconsin Lawyers - Wisconsin (WI) Attorney, Law Firm Directory](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=447b29&url=https://lawyers.findlaw.com/lawyer/state/wisconsin?DCMP%3DGoogle:ppc:kflportal:DIR_AttorneyStates-Wisconsin%26HBX_PK%3DWisconsin%2Battorney%2Blookup){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="findlaw.com"}]{.qlink_container} If you want a more official source, you can go to the State Bar of Wisconsin's website and search for an attorney there. I like the State Bar website because it allows you to either confidentially talk to a specialist or fill out an online form which will help direct you to the appropriate resource in your area.
