They're now claiming that taking a stance against the NRA is actually a "netural position." > *Delta's decision reflects the airline's neutral status in the current > national debate over gun control amid recent school shootings. Out of > respect for our customers and employees on both sides, Delta has taken > this action to refrain from entering this debate and focus on its > business. Delta continues to support the 2nd Amendment.* It takes quite a leap of logic to "take an action" in order to "refrain from entering the debate."
