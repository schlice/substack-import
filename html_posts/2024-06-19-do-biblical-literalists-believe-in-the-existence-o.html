**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Not just "biblical literalists." Scientists believe in the existence of Adam and Eve, too. They simply refuse to admit the correlation between our DNA ancestors and the Adam and Eve from the Bible. We know from investigation that "Y-chromosomal Adam" and "Mitochondrial Eve" existed. We even know they existed about the same time. Skeptics though do not believe that the two even knew each other. Nevertheless, we know that they both existed. If you believe that the Bible is indeed the inspired word of God, there is no reason to think that anything written in it is simply "didactic fiction."
