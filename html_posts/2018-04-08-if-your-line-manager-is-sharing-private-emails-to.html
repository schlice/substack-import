**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If your line manager is sharing private emails to the rest of her staff that she has had with a colleague, how do I tell her she is wrong? I'm still on probation and don't want to upset her for fear of losing my job.** You don't. You mind your own business, keep your head down, and keep your job. Just tell yourself, "Not my monkey, not my circus." Now, you might send an anonymous note to her supervisor and advise him/her of what's going on, because it might be a violation of company policy. Let her supervisor handle it. Beyond that, "not my monkey, not my circus."
