**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What are the requirements to have a Pennsylvanian driver license?** The requirements are the same as for any state's driver's license: - Be a resident of that state who is of legal age to drive a car - Pass a driving test (written and practical) - Pay a processing fee That's it. Very simple. Now clearly, there are more specifics, such as teen drivers requiring driver's ed, or exceptions for medical conditions, or many other specifics, but in general, the requirements are really simple and are the same requirements in every state. For more specifics about Pennsylvania's driver's licensing, check out the [[PennDOT Driver & Vehicle Services](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=6aab7b&url=https://www.dmv.pa.gov/Driver-Services/Driver-Licensing/Pages/Drivers-License.aspx){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="pa.gov"}]{.qlink_container} website.
