--- title: '"If someone is 50 percent disabled according to the VA, what benefits can they use?"' date: '2018-01-06' tags: - veteran-benefits layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Everybody\'s case is different, and everybody ends up with different compensation and benefits from the review of their particular case. However, at 50% disability you do have access to the VA Medical system to treat your particular disability. You should have received a benefit letter with your VA disability determination letter. This benefit letter should have described what benefits you are now entitled to being 50% disabled. If you don\'t have an explanation of benefits, call your VA Regional Customer Service Center.
