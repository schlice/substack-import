**Content:** [It is illegal to possess a firearm that fires more than one round per trigger pull without a Federal Firearms License.\ \ It\'s not illegal, in most places, to sell parts that allow you to create such a weapon.\ \ Some may argue that the devices actually are separate trigger pulls, so they keep with the letter of the law.\ \ Sure, you go ahead and hang on to that. It can be your defense in court.\ \ But what about the spirit, the intent of the law?]{.rendered_qtext}
