**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What warms your heart about your community?** For all of its faults (and there are many), *my community* *loves its public servants.* Our hearts collectively break when any officer is shot or killed, but it hurts that much more when it's one of our local officers or deputies. Here's the forinstance I'll give you: in early August, Officer Cem Duzel of the Colorado Springs Police Department [[was shot in the head](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=dfb9bd&url=https://gazette.com/news/colorado-springs-police-officer-wounded-in-shootout-is-stable-but/article_4b627cb0-981b-11e8-9486-e38a04dfbe55.html){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="gazette.com"} ]{.qlink_container}and lived. The outpouring of support for Officer Duzel and his family was [[immense](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=0aae25&url=https://www.krdo.com/news/springs-police-thankful-for-birthday-cards-to-cem-duzel/800784165){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="krdo.com"}]{.qlink_container}. For example, one of our residents who plays in a band [[held a benefit concert](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=f6ed96&url=https://gazette.com/news/benefit-concert-sunday-to-raise-money-for-injured-police-officer/article_a19be23e-c2a4-11e8-ab07-232f08f5c48c.html){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="gazette.com"}]{.qlink_container} to help pay for Officer Duzel's rehab costs, which will be fairly significant. Word of our city's generosity reached far and wide, [[drawing some celebrities to stop by](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=cd0878&url=https://koaa.com/news/covering-colorado/2018/08/24/the-hulk-visits-colorado-springs-police-officer-cem-duzel-in-the-hospital/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="koaa.com"}]{.qlink_container} and visit Officer Duzel. Warms my heart.
