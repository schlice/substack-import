--- title: '"Can a homeowners association limit rental properties?"' date: '2017-09-28' tags: - homeowners-assn layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} In general, HOAs have broad latitude to control the activities that take place within their area of control. Check you Association documents (your Covenants, and especially your PPR&R \[Policies, Procedures, Rules & Regulations\] Document) to determine what specifically your HOA requires when it comes to renting your property.
