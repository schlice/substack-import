**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} What [[Subhankar Das](https://www.quora.com/profile/Subhankar-Das-12)]{.qlink_container} is trying to say is that the reason Asus went with the more durable less scratch-resistant Gorilla Glass 4 on the Zenfone 3 is the same reason a company makes any business decision: **cost**. They were trying to control the costs on the Max and Laser, so they went with the cheaper glass (Gorilla Glass 3). It's more durable, but less scratch-resistant, which makes it cheaper.
