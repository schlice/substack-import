**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} For starters, like [[Luke Hayter](https://www.quora.com/profile/Luke-Hayter)]{.qlink_container} said, keep your nose clean. But just because you may have gotten yourself into some minor teenage trouble, don\'t think you\'re immediately disqualified. Everyone understands you\'re a teenager and you\'ll do stupid teenage stuff. ***Consistently*** doing the same stupid stuff would be a red flag. Doing something stupid *and learning from it* is normal. At your age, I\'d suggest you do the best you can in school, be involved in sports teams or pursue a workout schedule to maintain physical fitness, and be an example in your community and in your home life. Be a leader. If you go to college first, I\'d suggest a Criminal Justice degree or something along those lines.
