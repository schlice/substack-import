**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How do you get around the whole Posse Comitatus Act?** Petition your Representatives to repeal it. Otherwise, the military can't enforce civilian law. And I suspect that's the way you'd like it. Because the military isn't designed to be local law enforcement. I think you'd be much happier with your local chief of police or sheriff than you would be with the Army batallion commander. (EDIT) Another way to avoid Posse Comitatus is to follow the provisions of the Insurrection Act. If the Governor went to the President and claimed an insurrection was more than the fully-constituted National Guard could handle, the President is authorized to commit troops to the control of the Governor to quell the insurrection.
