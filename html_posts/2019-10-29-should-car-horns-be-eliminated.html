**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Should car horns be eliminated?** What safety device would you propose automobiles use in order to communicate in emergency situations? Horns are a safety device. If people in your area are using them improperly, the correct remedy is to contact the police, not to advocate for making automobiles less safe.
