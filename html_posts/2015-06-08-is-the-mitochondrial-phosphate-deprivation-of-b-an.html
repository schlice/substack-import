--- title: "Is the mitochondrial phosphate deprivation of B and T cells the cause of multiple sclerosis?" date: "2015-06-08" tags: ["health-autoimmune"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [We don\'t know what causes Multiple Sclerosis, so saying that mitochondrial phosphate deprivation of B and T cells is the cause of MS is incorrect.\ \ The answer is \"it could be.\"\ \ We know there is a genetic component, we know there is a hormonal component, we know that there is an infectious trigger that starts the disease process. We know that vitamin D has a prophylactic effect.\ \ But we do not know the cause.]{.rendered_qtext}
