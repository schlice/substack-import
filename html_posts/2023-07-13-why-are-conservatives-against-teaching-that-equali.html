--- title: "Why are conservatives against teaching that equality is good?" date: "2023-07-13" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **You need to be more specific.** **Equality** is good. We want equality. Everyone gets the same opportunity to succeed. To clarify, we call this *equality of opportunity.* **Equity** is bad. We'd like equity, but equity is not required. While everyone has the opportunity to succeed, not everyone will. We call this *equality of outcome*. That said, conservatives are all about the government using tax dollars to ensure *equality of opportunity.* They are completely against the government using tax dollars to ensure *equality of outcome.*
