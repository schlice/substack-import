**Content:** [V-MADS is an Active Denial non-lethal system which was developed when field commanders requested a non-lethal system to turn back advancing adversaries. It is a system that fires a 95 GHz frequency transmitter beam directly at advancing personnel. The waves penetrate normal clothing and approximately 1/64th of an inch into the outer layer of the skin and produces an extremely quick, extremely hot sensation. Two seconds in the beam raises skin temperature from normal to 130 degrees. It induces the individual\'s natural reaction to withdraw from pain. The pain will stop when the beam is switched off or the target steps out of the beam. You would need to be present in the beam for over 200 seconds before you were burned by the beam. The system has a range of approximately 700 yards.\ \ It can be countered by a variety of methods: thick clothes, trash can lids, even sprayed water that can absorb some of the beam before striking the troops.]{.rendered_qtext}
