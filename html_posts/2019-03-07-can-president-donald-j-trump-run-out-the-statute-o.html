**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can President Donald J. Trump run out the statute of limitations clock on any crimes committed in his first term by winning a second term?** Since a President can't be indicted while in office as President, the statute of limitations on any crime is normally tolled (paused) until he can actually be indicted.
