**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Why did Mitt Romney vote to convict President Trump in the impeachment trial?** It's a concept we call "virtue signaling." He knows it won't make a difference. He knows that the President will be acquitted. So it's a safe vote for him. He can vote however he likes and it won't change the outcome. So he votes in a way that "signals" to others what he wants to say. In this way, he's signaling that he's a RINO: Republican In Name Only. He's actually a Democrat. He's someone who believes that it's Congress' job to overrule the will of the American voter. He believes he knows better than you. He believes government should take care of all of your decisions for you. He's a Democrat. That's why he voted to convict.
