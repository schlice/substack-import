--- title: "Can an HOA send a ballot for special assessment before sharing the reserve fund study and yearly budget with its members?" date: "2024-05-18" tags: ["law", "homeowners-assn"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} We cannot answer this question. If there are restrictions in your Governing Documents (the CC&Rs, the Bylaws, or your Rules), then perhaps you have a complaint. And if there is nothing in your Governing Documents, and nothing in local or state law laying down any requirements, then it's likely that the Board has no obligation to do what you're asking. You might find it helpful before you vote on the special assessment to have them, but if it's not in your Governing Documents, you may not be able to force them to do so.
