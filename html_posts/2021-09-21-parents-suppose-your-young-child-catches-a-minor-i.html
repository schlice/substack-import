**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} (US answer) Depending on the level of injury or illness, we have multiple options for same-day visits. 1. Try your child's pediatrician. Many doctor's offices reserve some spots for same-day visits, although most will likely offer you a next-day appointment. 2. If that's not soon enough, we have *urgent care* facilities in nearly every moderately-sized city in the country. Urgent care fills the gap between something which can't wait for your doctor's next available appointment, but something which is minor enough to not require a trip to the emergency room. 3. If you cannot get in to see the doctor and you don't have access to a nearby urgent care (or it is after hours for urgent care), you can always be seen at the emergency room. They will stabilize the child and provide them with enough appropriate medication to last until you can get in to see your regular pediatrician.
