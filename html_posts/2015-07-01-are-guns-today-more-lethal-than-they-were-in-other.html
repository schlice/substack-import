Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Of course, it\'s fair to say that guns have advanced technologically over the years. Everything has.\ \ If anything, though, the gun is now far more accurate, and so is less likely to injure someone other than the intended target.\ \ The rallying cry of \"guns are technologically more advanced, let\'s get rid of them\" is disingenuous and logically sloppy. Many things are more technologically advanced than they were just a few years ago, yet there is no rallying cry to ban anything else that I am aware of. The gun simply performs its job better. That\'s not a reason to ban it. Guns are still the best tool to use to stop a human threat. Nothing else provides the level of certainty that the threat will be stopped like a gun.\ \ Now, if you want to argue that we shouldn\'t be allowed to use a gun to stop a threat, then argue that. But this argument holds no logical water.]{.rendered_qtext}
