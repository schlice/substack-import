--- title: "Can a homeowner association (HOA) legally require residents to provide the ID numbers on their cars\" date: "2024-10-03" tags: ["law", "homeowners-assn"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} As with all legal questions, the answer is "it depends," and in this case it depends on the rules in the HOA's Governing Documents. If the HOA needs to regulate who parks in the community's garage, the HOA can issue parking permits to cars which are permitted to use the garage. To issue parking permits, the car to which the permit is being issued can be recorded, including the "ID numbers" (in the US, we call them "license plates") assigned to the car.
