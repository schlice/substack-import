**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can a cop give you a ticket for parking in a parking meter spot but you are still in the car?** Can they? Of course they can. Will it hold up? Unlikely. "Parking" and "Standing" have two legally distinct definitions. If you're still in the car in the operator's position, your vehicle is technically "standing." Once you get out and leave the vehicle, it is now "parked." Of course, this is highly dependent on local laws, local definitions of parking and standing, and local laws regarding stopping, standing, or parking.
