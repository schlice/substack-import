**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can you still drive with multiple sclerosis?** If you're asking whether or not someone with MS is allowed to drive cars, the answer is yes. There is no law that revokes a driver's license once you are diagnosed with MS. If you're asking whether or not an MS patient can drive a car *safely*, that's between that patient and his or her doctor. If their impairment because of MS so impacts their reflexes, eyesight, or gross motor functions that they can no longer operate a car or do so safely, they should probably consider not driving. I would hope that a neurologist would have that kind of discussion with his or her patient.
