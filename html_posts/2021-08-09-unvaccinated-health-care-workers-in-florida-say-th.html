**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Of course they do. And unvaccinated health care workers should not be required to be vaccinated. **However, the hospital does not have to keep unvaccinated health care workers in direct contact with patients.** The hospital can reassign unvaccinated personnel to desk jobs in offices, away from patients. And if the hospital does not have a suitable position to relocate the unvaccinated, they may be forced to terminate them. Alternatively, the hospital can divide their ER and their ICU into locations for "vaccinated" and "unvaccinated" patients, if they have the capacity to do so. Then they can leave all the unvaccinated health care workers to deal only with vaccinated patients.
