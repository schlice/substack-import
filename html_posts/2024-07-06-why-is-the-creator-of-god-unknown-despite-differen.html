--- title: "Why is the creator of God unknown, despite different religions having their own interpretations of God?" date: "2024-07-06" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Whoever or whatever is referred to as "God," this being ***necessarily*** must be uncreated. Why? This being must be uncreated because if it is created, you can continually ask the question "who created it" *ad infinitum*. This is what we call the "infinite regress" and it is a problem when you have no **first cause**. The first cause is necessarily uncaused. The great Uncaused Cause. Ultimately, that is the being who created us, even if by proxy, by creating our creator.
