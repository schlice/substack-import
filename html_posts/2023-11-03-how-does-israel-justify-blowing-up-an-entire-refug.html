**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Well, when you put it ***that*** way... What Israel *actually* did was to destroy a Hamas terrorist stronghold in Jabaliya, northern Gaza, which happened to be located under an apartment building. As usual, Hamas hides behind civilians. If you want to know why refugee camps were targeted, you'll have to ask Hamas why they place military targets underneath civilian structures, in clear violation of the Geneva Convention and other international rules of war.
