**Content:** [There are many apps that do this, but the sending phone has to have a data connection to transmit its information. There arent\'t any tracking apps that I know of that currently use SMS to send the location data of the phone, so that might be something useful. It would be a true emergency phone locator when the phone doesn\'t have data coverage but does have a cellular signal (and presumably a GPS signal).\ \ Here\'s the basic idea: you pre-install the app on both the tracker and the tracked phones. The app has to have SMS privileges on the phone, so it may be a little tricky to do on iPhone without jailbreaking, but Android is absolutely possible. The trick is hooking the app into SMS so that the tracked phone can monitor SMS for a specially-coded message that says \"activate.\"\ \ The app on the tracked phone intercepts the SMS \"activate\" message and its tracking portion activates. It will then send an SMS to the tracker every minute with its GPS coordinates until the battery dies or the app receives a \"deactivate\" message over SMS.\ \ The app on the tracker phone will intercept the SMS and use the GPS coordinates to plot the location on a map.]{.rendered_qtext}
