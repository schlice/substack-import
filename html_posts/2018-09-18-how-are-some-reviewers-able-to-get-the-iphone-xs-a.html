**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How are some reviewers able to get the iPhone XS and XS Max before its released to the general public and do unboxing and review videos?** Some reviewers are directly contacted by manufacturers, and demo units are offered to them specifically to do unboxing and review videos. Who gets demo units is generally based on viewership and subscription numbers. It's a double-edged sword, however: pre-production units are still in beta, they're not full-fledged production units, so not all the features will be fully implemented in them For that reason, manufacturers often don't allow the unboxers to do full feature reviews. In some cases, they allow reviews of only certain features, not all of them.
