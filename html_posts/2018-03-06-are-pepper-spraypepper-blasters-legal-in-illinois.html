**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} As far as I'm aware, pepper spray is **legal** in Illinois for residents over 18. There are no current restrictions on strength, size, or canister style. It's even legal in Chicago, but city law prevents you from spraying it in an enclosed space with more than 20 people present.
