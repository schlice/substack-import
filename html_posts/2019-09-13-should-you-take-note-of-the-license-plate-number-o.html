Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Should you take note of the license plate number of a vehicle that speeds past you on a double line (no passing zone)?** You can. You can take that license plate to the police agency which covers that stretch of road and file a citizen's complaint. Yes, you will have to do it in person. But if the passing was egregious enough to you, then you can go and file.
