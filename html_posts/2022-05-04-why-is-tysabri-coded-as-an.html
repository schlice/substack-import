**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Tysabri can cause a hypersensitivity to antineoplastic (chemotherapy) drugs. Doctors will have to perform a "desensitization" procedure in order to "desensitize" the body after Tysabri treatment in order to safely administer chemotherapy.[\[1\]](#jzFPw){#cite-jzFPw .citation_link} \ Original question: Why is tysabri coded as an "Encounter for antineoplastic chemotherapy." Is it coded that because tysabri doesn't have code? Or is there another reason?
