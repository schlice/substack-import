**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **My county's stay at home order is till the end of May. I have a court date in another county that will open up earlier. Am I supposed to go to that court hearing?** Contact the clerk of the court at the courthouse where you are scheduled to appear. Stay in contact with them regarding the schedule. They will let you know whether or not your appearance is still on or will be postponed. Appearing in court, if it is not cancelled, is considered to be an essential activity.
