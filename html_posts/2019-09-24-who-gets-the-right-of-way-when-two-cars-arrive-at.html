**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Who gets the right of way when two cars arrive at a two-way stop from opposite directions but one car is make a left while the other is going straight?** The laws in nearly every state in the United States say that the vehicle going straight has the right of way over the vehicle turning, but remember these are all dictated by state law, and each state can be different.
