**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can President Trump pardon his impeachment(s)?** Impeachment is a political remedy and it is not related to whether or not someone has broken a law. Pardons are for **federal crimes**. You can't pardon an impeachment. Impeachments are for "high crimes and misdemeanors" but since impeachment is a political remedy, it doesn't necessarily have to be a violation of law which triggers an impeachment. Even speech which is perfectly legal, but unbecoming the office of the president (which is exactly what he did), is impeachable.
