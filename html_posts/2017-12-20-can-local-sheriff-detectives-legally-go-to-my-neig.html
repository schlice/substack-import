**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can local sheriff detectives legally go to my neighbors and ask them if they can use their property to surveil my property because they were told by my vindictive ex that I was committing crimes?** The short answer, as my esteemed colleagues have mentioned, is **yes** they can. If they are legally permitted (by the owner) to be in a place, and they can see your property from that place without violating any privacy laws, then they are absolutely allowed to be there and to watch you. As [[User-11418373085839729890](https://www.quora.com/profile/User-11418373085839729890)]{.qlink_container} suggested, if you're truly in this situation, you should hire an attorney and discuss your options with him/her.
