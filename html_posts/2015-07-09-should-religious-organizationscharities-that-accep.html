Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [No one should be receiving charity from the government.\ \ The government should cease all charitable operations of any kind, and return those monies to individuals.\ \ Individuals can then decide if they want to provide their money to charitable organizations.\ \ The government was never designed to be a charity factory, and redistribution of wealth in any form should be strongly discouraged in the government. It creates too many entanglements from which the government should be freed.]{.rendered_qtext}
