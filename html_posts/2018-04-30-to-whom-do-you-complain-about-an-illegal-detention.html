Answer

**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **To whom do you complain about an illegal detention by the police?** Get a lawyer. Explain the details of your case to your lawyer. Your lawyer will decide if there is enough evidence to warrant a charge of illegal detention, and if so, he will help you file the suit.
