**Content:** [Citizen\'s Arrest is an authority granted to a private person to detain an individual who they witnessed performing a crime. The citizen must have probable cause to effect the arrest.\ \ Almost all states have some form of citizen\'s arrest statute, and almost all states differ in how they implement the statute. Some allow you to perform arrests for any crime. Some only allow you to perform an arrest for a felony. Some require verbal announcement before force is used.\ \ It would have to be a really sketchy situation for me to jump in and make a citizen\'s arrest.\ \ My advice: **be a good witness.** Try to capture as many details about the situation as possible. Who was the person? What was he wearing? What did he do? When did he do it? Where did he go? Did he get into a car? Did he get on the bus? Did he run off somewhere?\ \ Most everyone has a cell phone with a camera these days. Maybe snap some pictures or record some video. That\'s way more useful than trying to effect a citizen\'s arrest.]{.rendered_qtext}
