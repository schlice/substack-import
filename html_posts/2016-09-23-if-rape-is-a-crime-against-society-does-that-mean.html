--- title: "If rape is a crime against society, does that mean rape victims have a civic duty to file a police report?" date: "2016-09-23" tags: ["law-enforcement"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Rape is a crime against society only insomuch as it "shocks the conscience" of society. Therefore, society is a victim insofar as society in general is harmed by rapes which occur in society. Society is not the actual victim. Rape victims have the ultimate choice whether or not they file a police report. I will never bemoan a rape victim if he or she chooses to not file a report for one reason or another. It's their choice.
