**Content:** [There is no Federal law; the laws against brandishing a weapon are implemented on a state level.\ \ Each state\'s laws are different, but in general, brandishing of a weapon means bringing the weapon to a ready state with the intent to threaten or intimidate.\ \ Specific laws regarding brandishing knives vary by state.]{.rendered_qtext}
