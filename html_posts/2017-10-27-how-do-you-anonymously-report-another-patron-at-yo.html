**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Reporting someone is not difficult --- just call 911. The problem is staying anonymous while you do it. Here are a few ways: - Find a pay phone and make a phone call. - See if there is a public use phone somewhere in the library. Make sure you keep yourself covered --- hats and glasses are useful. - [[Use a disposable phone number](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=15b282&url=http://blog.coverme.ws/en/201604-4-easy-ways-to-get-a-disposable-phone-number/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="coverme.ws"}]{.qlink_container} on your mobile phone.
