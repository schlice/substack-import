**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How do I unlock Google FRP when my phone is stuck with a Samsung keypad and not a Gboard?** Google's Factory Reset Protection does not require **Gboard** (the Google keyboard) in order to unlock it. It requires the last owner of the device to log in to their Google account in order to complete the factory reset. Additionally, the previous owner can go into their own Google profile on the web and delete the device from their recently used devices. This will also clear out the FRP if you are having difficulty doing this on-device.
