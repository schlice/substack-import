Trump gave comments to *Israel Hayom* [[in an exclusive interview](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=1ff710&url=https://www.israelhayom.com/2024/03/25/trump-to-israel-hayom-only-a-fool-would-have-not-acted-like-israel-on-oct-7/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="israelhayom.com"}]{.qlink_container} the outlet published yesterday. He first claimed that Biden's presidency contributed to the conflict: > He \[Biden\] can\'t talk. He\'s a very dumb person. He\'s a dumb > person. His foreign policy throughout 50 years has been horrible. If > you look at people that were in other administrations with him, they > saw him as a weak, ineffective president, they \[Hamas\] would have > never done that attack if I were there. Trump then noted that the disinformation campaign being waged by Hamas is actually hurting Israel's reputation, and he recommends that they conclude the conflict: > You have to finish up your war. To finish it up. You gotta get it > done. And, I am sure you will do that. And we gotta get to peace, we > can\'t have this going on. And I will say, Israel has to be very > careful, because you\'re losing a lot of the world, you\'re losing a > lot of support, you have to finish up, you have to get the job done. > And you have to get on to peace, to get on to a normal life for > Israel, and for everybody else.
