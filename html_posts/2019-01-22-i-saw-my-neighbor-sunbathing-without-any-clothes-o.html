**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **I saw my neighbor sunbathing without any clothes on in her backyard. I have young children in my house. Should I call the police for indecent exposure in public?** I see lots of commentary about what the police are "supposed" to do and how you should look away but I don't see any real *answers*. The answer is "it depends." First off, I'm assuming you're in the United States. Secondly, it depends on your state. Some states only classify indecent exposure as nudity with a sexual component (i.e. an aroused male, or a suggestively touching female). In other states, nudity in your own backyard is acceptable. In still others, nudity that is visible from anywhere (i.e. a second story into a neighbor's yard) is illegal. You'll have to check the laws in your state as to whether or not it is illegal.
