--- title: "You murder someone with no witnesses nearby, you left no evidence at the crime scene, there is no surveillance, the murder weapon and all clothing have been burned, and you have the perfect alibi. How do the police catch you?" date: "2017-04-13" tags: ["law-enforcement"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} How do you get caught? **time**. There is no statute of limitations on murder. So eventually, someone may put the pieces together and figure it out. It may take 50 years, and you may be old and gray when they do, but with no other physical evidence, the only other thing you have against you is time. Eventually, someone will figure it out. Or they won't. And you'll spend every day until you die looking over your shoulder, waiting for someone to figure it out, and come for you.
