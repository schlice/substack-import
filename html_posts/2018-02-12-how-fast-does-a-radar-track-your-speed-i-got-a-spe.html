**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The short answer: he had your speed before you saw him. He knows you slowed down and moved over, but in your case, he already had you. Acquisition speed of a typical radar unit is approximately 0.3 seconds. So by the time your brain registered the fact that the car was there, your speed was already up on the screen.
