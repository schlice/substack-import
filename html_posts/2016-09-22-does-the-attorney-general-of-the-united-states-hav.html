--- title: "Does the Attorney General of the United States have the power to disband a police department?" date: "2016-09-22" tags: ["law-enforcement"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The Attorney General is in charge of the US Justice Department. The police are a state agency and report to the Governor of that state. The US Attorney General does not have authority over local police departments. He cannot create them, so he can also not destroy them.
