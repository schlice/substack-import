**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You don\'t believe that the ***fully-constituted*** militias of all 50 states can quickly and easily overrun the US military? The US military would be insane to try to take on the fully-constituted force of all 48 contiguous states\' militias. Besides, in a nation of laws, the military force that is created and authorized only through the force of law will have a very difficult time following an order that is, by its very nature, antithetical to that law: attack US citizens. Most military members will not comply with such an unlawful order; as the Nazi war crimes trials showed, \"I was just following orders\" is not a defense. Military officers have the authority, and the responsibility, to ignore unlawful orders.
