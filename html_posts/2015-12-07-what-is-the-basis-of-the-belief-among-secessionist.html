--- title: '"What is the basis of the belief among secessionists and anti-Obama people that if they take up arms to either secede or to overthrow the U.S. Government, the U. S. Military will switch sides and join them?"' date: '2015-12-07' tags: - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} There is no such \"belief\". Unless you have some credible sources for your assertion, I\'m going to believe that this is a question which contains an assumption. I think you will find that if the government orders the military to attack civilians without just cause, in violation of the Posse Comitatus Act, you may find many military officers refusing those orders as illegal. However, if the military is ordered to assist a state government with quelling an insurrection in full compliance with the Insurrection Act, the military will comply. You\'re not going to see the military go against the orders of its chain of command unless you see the government ordering the military to act lawlessly, or in contrast with currently-established law.
