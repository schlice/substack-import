**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The funny part about this study is that it reveals that Trump and the Conservatives were right all along about how to keep deaths from COVID down, and the government didn't listen. The study posits that people died during COVID because they had **comorbidities**. If we would have had universal health care, the study suggests, these comorbidities would have been managed because people would have been able to afford to go to the doctor. And if those comorbidities were managed better, then those people (when they contracted COVID) wouldn't have died. It's an interesting line of logic. But it just goes to prove that the way to save lives during COVID was to protect those with comorbidities. **Which is precisely what the "marginalized" health experts (and Conservatives) were saying.** In other words, what this study should be saying is that **you should have listened to the other health experts** and you should have **targeted your protection towards people who had pre-existing health conditions**, and left the healthy to leave their homes and run their businesses.
