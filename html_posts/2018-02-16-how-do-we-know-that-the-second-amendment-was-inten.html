--- title: '"How do we know that the Second Amendment was intended to apply to repeating arms in spite of their rarity at the time that the Second Amendment was written?"' date: '2018-02-16' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The Second Amendment didn't specifically call out any type of arms at all. Repeating, wooden, flintlock, composite, etc. No characteristics were called out at all. How do we know that the Second Amendment was intended to apply to repeating arms? Because it doesn't say anything at all about any kind of "arms."
