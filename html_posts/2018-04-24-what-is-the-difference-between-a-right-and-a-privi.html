**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What is the difference between a right and a privilege if you believe that rights are only granted by the state and can be taken away?** Well, if you (incorrectly) believe that rights are granted by the state and can be taken away, then there is no difference between a right and a privilege, which is granted by the state and can be taken away. If, however, you believe the *actual* definition of a right, which contains a *moral* obligation or permission to do or say something, which is *not* granted by the state, then there is a big difference between a right and a privilege.
