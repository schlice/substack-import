**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Has anyone had a positive experience with Protandim in alleviating multiple sclerosis symptoms?** I had someone try to sell me some Protandim. I feel the same way about it as I do with all supplements: if you like the ingredients and you want to try it and it helps you, by all means, take it. But for the love of Pete, please do not stop your MS drugs, and please do not take Protandim *in lieu of* your MS medication. Please do not stop, start, or change your MS therapy without talking to your neurologist.
