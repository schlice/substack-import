Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [High speed car chases are going the way of the dodo.\ \ Many jurisdictions have banned them outright. Some jurisdictions are \"positive consent\" jurisdictions for pursuit. The on-duty sergeant must authorize the pursuit before it can continue, and it will only be authorized in extenuating circumstances, during low traffic periods, and for severe crimes. It will be rare all other times.\ \ The vehicle will not be tracked by helicopter, since the department does not routinely fly the helicopter and it does take time to get into the air and on scene. Generally, the officer simply takes the registration of the vehicle and follows up on it later.]{.rendered_qtext}
