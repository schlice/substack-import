**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Why do Starbucks baristas ask if you "want room" when I order "black coffee"? Are they not taught that "black coffee" literally means, "I don't need any room for cream"?** It could be as simple as the fact that you order black coffee because *you want to do your fixings yourself at the little fixing station* instead of letting them do your fixings. Additionally, when people don't ask for room and then take their black coffee to the fixing bar, they will *dump coffee into the trash to make room for their cream.* This makes a mess for employees to clean up, and if the bag happens to have a hole, it leaks out onto the floor. You can imagine what kind of mess that makes. Isn't it better that someone ask to clarify rather than assume and get it wrong?
