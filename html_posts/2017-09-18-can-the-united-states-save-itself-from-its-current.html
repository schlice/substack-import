**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I suppose you would have to believe that the United States is currently undergoing some form of "political death" in order for it to "save itself" from said death. I see no evidence of this "death" you speak of. Our political system is alive and (somewhat) healthy. Certainly, there are things that need to be changed. We need Congressional term limits. We need a Balanced Budget amendment. We need better border security. We need to allow DREAMers to stay in country without allowing them all the rights and privileges of citizens (i.e. voting), until they *become* citizens. Sheesh, it almost seems like the vast majority of problems in this country are Congress' fault, doesn't it? #thingsthatmakeyougohmmm
