**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} If the goal of rape was to forcefully produce a child against the mother's will, then I could see how you could conflate pro-life with pro-rape. **But rape isn't about making babies. Rape is about** ***power*.** It's about having complete power over your victim, including their sexual choices. Sure, in some cases, it's some incel who went nutz, but even in that case, it's still an exertion of control over another person. It's never about the baby. But here's the thing: **the baby is an innocent victim, too, just as much as the woman is**. Why do we take out our anger on the innocent baby? Did you know that a little over half of rape victims *actually keep their babies?* From the way this question is worded, it sounds virtually automatic that if you're a rape victim, you kill your baby, and anyone who is pro-life is therefore pro-rape. Not all rape victims kill their babies. As a pro-life person, let me tell you where abortion makes sense: **incest.** When a power dynamic in a family was abused and there is a father-daughter pregnancy, abortion makes scientific and physiological sense, considering that there is a better than 50% chance that the baby will be born with a debilitating birth defect. Now, that doesn't mean I think families should go hog-wild with their kids and then you should just feel free to abort the trash you create. There are still serious psychological impacts from parents having sex with their children. But when it happens (and that's actually sexual assault as well as incest), that baby shouldn't be forced to suffer just so that I can maintain moral consistency. That makes no logical sense.
