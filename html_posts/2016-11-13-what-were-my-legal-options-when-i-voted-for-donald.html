Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} None. Zip. Zilch. Zero. Nada. Once you cast your vote **and it is counted**, it\'s over. Your vote is permanent. Some localities allow you to change your vote up to the time the polls close, some do not. Regardless, once the polls close, the decision is final. The tribe has spoken, so to speak. Vote smarter next time.
