**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can the US Congress override Trump\'s National Emergency to fund the Border Wall?** The National Emergencies Act requires a joint resolution of Congress to terminate a national emergency declared by the President. The resolution can be vetoed by the President, and that veto can be overridden by a 2/3 majority in both houses.
