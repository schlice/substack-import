Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Do other soldiers stigmatize those who are court-martialed but not convicted?** I don't think so. I do think that if someone has gone though a court-martial and came out unscathed, it would remain as an item of concern in the back of my mind. I don't think it would change what I think of that person, but it might change *how* I think about that person. If the military says they can stay, they stay, and we accept them, period. That doesn't mean I have to go have a beer with him after work though.
