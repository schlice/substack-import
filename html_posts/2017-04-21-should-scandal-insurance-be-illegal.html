**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I don't think you have to worry yourself over making sure that scandal insurance can never be offered, because I can't think of a scenario in which it *could* be feasibly offered. The insurance agency would essentially have to be an Adjustment Bureau, essentially re-writing historical events such that what actually happened didn't happen. And as you likely know, once something gets posted to the Internet, it's *nearly impossible* to get rid of it. So what would the terms be? You can do anything, and we will make it go away? Sounds like science fiction to me. I don't think you should worry yourself over this.
