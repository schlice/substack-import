**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Maybe I don't understand what "the whole bureaucratic goose chase" is, but if your passport was 10 years old, it's expired by now. Adult passports are only valid for 10 years; youth passports are only valid for 5 years. The best you can do is apply for another one. At best, you could have used your old, expired passport as proof of citizenship and proof of identity but now you'll have to do that process all over again, and provide that documentation again.
