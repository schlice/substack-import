**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What is the highest amount of nicotine content legal in the US?** Since you're talking about "nicotine content," I can only assume you're talking about "vape juice," or the liquid used in e-cigarettes or in vape "mods." This liquid is usually referred to as "e-liquid." Commercial e-liquid is normally available in zero nicotine, 3mg, 6mg, 12mg, and 18mg, although the highest nicotine e-liquid may reach 36mg. These numbers are milligrams of nicotine per 1 mL of fluid, so it can also be expressed as a percentage. For example, 18mg/mL can also be expressed as 1.8%.
