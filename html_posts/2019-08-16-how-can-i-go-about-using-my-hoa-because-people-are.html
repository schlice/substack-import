**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How can I go about using my HOA, because people are building a strip mall behind my house and was not aware of informed on the decision? What can I do? This will invade my privacy and I will no longer feel safe.** I think you meant to ask how you can go about "suing" your HOA because of things going on behind your house. Your HOA is not responsible for telling you about ongoing building projects in areas outside of their control. So you can't really sue them for not doing something that they were not required to do in the first place. Secondly, if the area was zoned correctly, you should have known that the area behind your house was commercial property and that it could have had commercial buildings built on it. If it wasn't zoned for commercial, then the city would have had to re-zone it in order to approve the building there, and you would have been notified by the city of the zoning change. Finally, you are not locked into your house. You can always sell it and leave if you don't feel safe.
