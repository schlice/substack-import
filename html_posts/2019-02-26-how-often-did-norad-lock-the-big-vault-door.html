**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How often did NORAD lock the big vault door?** Not very often. The door only gets locked during an actual threat event against the United States. We simulate locking the door during every exercise, but under most normal circumstances, the doors remain open. We also will lock the doors during routine blast door maintenance. There's a large piece of Plexiglas on the back side of both of the doors, so you can see the locking mechanism at work, and you can see the bolts slide into place. It's a surreal feeling being inside the mountain when the doors actually do lock.
