Even in situations where my gun has inadvertently \"printed\" (i.e. shown through my clothing), I wasn\'t ever identified as a concealed carrier.\ \ And that\'s the point.\ \ Concealed carry, as has already been pointed out several times here, is exactly that: concealed. The only time you should ever know that I\'m carrying is when I tell you. Other than that, it\'s a secret. It\'s supposed to be.]{.rendered_qtext}
