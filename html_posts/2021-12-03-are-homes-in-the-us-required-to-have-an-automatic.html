--- title: '"Are homes in the US required to have an automatic lawn sprinkler?"' date: '2021-12-03' tags: - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} No, there is no Federal requirement for homes in the US to have automatic lawn sprinklers. While states have many and varied laws regarding the *use* of in-ground or above-ground automatic sprinkler systems (such as required additional equipment and usage specifications), there are no requirements to actually have them in any state law. Most homes (especially new construction) have them pre-installed by the builder, especially if the builder is building out the entire neighborhood (as in the case of a master planned community). \ Original question: Are homes in the US required to have an automatic lawn sprinkler?
