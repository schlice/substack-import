--- title: '"Is it legal to view Flash on an iPhone?"' date: '2017-01-17' tags: - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} There is no law that prevents Flash from running on an iPhone. That was a business decision made by Apple. You can jailbreak your iPhone (which has also been found to be legal, even though you may void your warranty) and you can then run Flash on it.
