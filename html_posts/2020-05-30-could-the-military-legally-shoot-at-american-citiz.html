**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Is it legal for military forces to be used on US citizens?** Under most circumstances, no. As others have mentioned, the Posse Comitatus Act forbids it. But there is one circumstance in which they can be engaged. If the fully constituted forces of a state National Guard can\'t quell an Insurrection, the governor can appeal to the President, and he can activate the military under the Insurrection Act.
