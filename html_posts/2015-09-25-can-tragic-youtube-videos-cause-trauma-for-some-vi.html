**Content:** [Certainly, any graphic video could cause persons to experience emotional trauma.\ \ However, these videos should not be *blocked;* they should be *flagged*.\ \ YouTube should put a disclaimer on the video which states that the following video is graphic and may contain scenes which might be disturbing to some viewers; viewer discretion is advised.\ \ There is way too much content for YouTube to review before it is released to the public, so it is up to us to assist. If you find a graphic video which is not flagged, then flag it.\ \ ]{.rendered_qtext} :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-4450415e77755efc8c2f176123a47f5c){.landscape .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-4450415e77755efc8c2f176123a47f5c" master_w="480" master_h="345"} ::: :::: \ Click the \"More\" button and click \"Report.\" The issue you\'ll choose is \"Violent or repulsive content\" and then click Submit.
