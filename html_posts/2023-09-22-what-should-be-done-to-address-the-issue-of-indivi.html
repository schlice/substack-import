**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Laws should be clarified. We should not be punishing the mother for getting an abortion from a licensed abortion provider. The appropriate target is the licensed medical professional who engages in unlawful medical practice. If abortion is illegal in that area and someone licensed to perform abortion performs one even thought the law says you may not, that is the person to whom we should be directing our efforts, not the mother. The laws should be made very clear that the mother is never the person who should face prosecution for terminating a pregnancy through an abortion.ยน \ ยน This assumes that the mother got an abortion from a licensed abortion provider. If she got a "back-alley abortion" or engaged in a "self-help abortion" where such activity is illegal, she could face charges herself.
