--- title: "If laws change, should the criminals that broke them be reimbursed?" date: "2014-08-25" tags: ["law"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [In all cases, people are judged based on the law that was in place at the time the crime was committed. Past versions of the law or potential future revisions of the law do not change the law that was in place at the time of the act, and the general rule is that the law in place at the time the crime was committed is the law that stands.]{.rendered_qtext}
