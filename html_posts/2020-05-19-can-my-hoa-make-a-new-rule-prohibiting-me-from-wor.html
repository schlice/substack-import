**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can my HOA make a new rule prohibiting me from working on my car inside my garage?** HOAs have broad latitude over the areas under their purview. In general, if their rule does not violate a Federal, state, or local law, they usually can do it. There may be certain circumstances why they would not be able to do it, such as a Covenants that prevents them from making specific types of rules (like rules about the interior of a home, including the garage). But other than that, the HOA can make such a rule, so long as it followed the rules about how to make a rule in its own Governing Documents.
