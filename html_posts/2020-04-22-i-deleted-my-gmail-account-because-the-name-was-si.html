**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **I deleted my Gmail account because the name was silly. Will anyone still be able to see the address?** No. Once the address is deleted, it no longer exists as an account or an address. Anyone trying to send email to that address will get a bounce message stating that the address in question does not exist. That said, any emails you sent using that account will still exist, and will still have come from \@[[gmail.com](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=fe8b21&url=http://gmail.com){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="gmail.com"}]{.qlink_container}, and that won't ever change.
