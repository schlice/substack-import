**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What\'s your opinion on the news that judge ruled in favour of CNN in initial proceeding and White House must restore Jim Acosta press pass immediately?** First of all, it's a temporary restraining order. There is no ruling. Second, it's an order on *5th Amendment* grounds, not 1st Amendment. The order states that Acosta was not granted his due process rights under the 5th Amendment when his hard pass was revoked, so he can *temporarily* have it back until the case is heard. Finally, I think that much is correct. If you have a 5th Amendment right to due process before the White House revokes your press pass, then he should have it back until he has due process to argue his case. Hopefully they just don't call on him during the briefings. I'm pretty sure there's no Constitutional amendment that requires you to be called on in a press briefing.
