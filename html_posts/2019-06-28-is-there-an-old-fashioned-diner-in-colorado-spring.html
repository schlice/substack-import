--- title: '"Is there an old-fashioned diner in Colorado Springs, CO?"' date: '2019-06-28' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} There are a few. But if you want that old aluminum rail car 50s style diner, check out Rosie\'s Diner on highway 105 in Monument. If you want the more Elvis rock and roll feel without the shiny aluminum rail car, try Gunther Toodys in the Springs. It\'s off of Woodmen and Powers.
