Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} No one has priority. First off, most agencies say that you can\'t just blow through an intersection at full speed; you must slow down to check for traffic which may not have noticed the big flashing lights or heard the siren. So they both should have stopped and worked it out like big boys and girls. That\'s what grown ups do.
