**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} First of all, **don't spend the rent money!** Once you work out the issue of who owns the property and who will be accepting your rent checks, they're going to want all that back rent (and they are owed it). Don't screw yourself over by spending the rent money and then realize you owe back rent. If you want to investigate this yourself, go to your county's tax assessor website. The real owner of every property is listed. Find the real owner of the property and figure out who will be accepting the rent. Keep all records of your transactions.
