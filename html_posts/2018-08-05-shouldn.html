**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Shouldn\'t there be a law that people who hold public office must send their children to public schools?** Why? Do you think that politicians send their kids to private school because they think they're *better* than everyone else? You wanna force them to send their kids to public schools to *knock them down a few pegs*? Or maybe you think that since politicians are voting to fund these schools, their kids should have to attend the schools they think are so wonderful. Force them to "eat their own dog food, fly their own planes," so to speak. However, I think politicians send their kids to private schools for the same reason that any American sends their kids to private schools: **they are objectively better than public schools**. And here's the kicker: this says more about the education system than it does about the politician.
