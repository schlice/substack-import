**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If landscapers kill, damage, or destroy plants on someone else\'s property (someone who did not hire them), can they be criminally charged?** Whether or not it is a criminal charge is dependent on state, but in all cases, such destruction must be willful (i.e. they intend to do so). Nearly every destruction of property law in nearly every state requires that the destruction be willful. If it's not willful, it likely can't be charged as destruction of property.
