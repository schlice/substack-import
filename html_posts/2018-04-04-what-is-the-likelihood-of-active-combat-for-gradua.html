**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What is the likelihood of active combat for graduates of USAFA?** It depends entirely on the AFSC into which the grad is placed. Not all AFSCs deploy regularly, and even for those that don't regularly deploy, there are groups within those AFSCs that are worldwide deployable and do regularly deploy. For example, I was a 33S (Logistics and Communications) on the comm side. There is comm at every base of every size worldwide, but we didn't deploy routinely. However, a group within the 33S career field (combat comm) *did* deploy routinely, but only if you were a part of combat comm. Pilots and cops, across the entire AFSC, are likely to be sent to active war zones pretty quickly.
