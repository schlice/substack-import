**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} What makes you think that Eve's sins weren't forgiven? Genesis 3:21 explains that God made clothes out of animal skins for Adam and Eve to wear. Most scholars believe that this was the first animal sacrifice, and a foreshadowing to the blood sacrifice of Jesus to forgive the sins of the world. In other words, *God covered Adam and Eve's sins with blood.* In **other** other words, this means that God included them in the salvation provided by Jesus. Therefore, it's a good bet that both Adam and Eve were saved and did go to heaven when they died.
