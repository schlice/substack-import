--- title: '"What is the difference between a deputy and a police officer?"' date: '2015-01-28' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [A \"deputy\" is a peace officer who works for a \"sheriff\'s office.\"\ \ A \"police officer\" is a peace officer who works for a \"police department.\"\ \ Usually, a \"trooper\" is a peace officer who works for a State Patrol.\ \ They are all state-certified peace officers, but work for different agencies.]{.rendered_qtext}
