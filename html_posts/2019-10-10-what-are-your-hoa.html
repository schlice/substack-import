**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What are your HOA's by-laws or declarations regarding owning a home business?** Most HOAs allow home-based businesses. However, the HOA would be concerned whether or not your HOA would increase traffic into and out of the neighborhood or required parking outside of what a single family residence would support. Any home-based business which caused traffic to increase into the neighborhood would not be permitted. But simply owning a home-based business is generally not prohibited.
