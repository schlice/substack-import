{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [If he\'s caught sleeping in the closet **during** work, I\'d say he either has a medical condition which prevents him from sleeping soundly at night, or he\'s doing something at night which is keeping him from sleeping. Perhaps he stays up late playing video games. Perhaps he\'s a serial rapist. We\'ll never know until we see him on the 6 o\'clock news. But his employer might have a problem with him sleeping on the job, and it might affect his potential for future employment at that establishment.\ \ If he\'s caught sleeping in the closet **before** or **after** work, there may be a situation that is preventing him from going home, or preventing him from sleeping at home. It might be something that the employer might have a problem with, or it might not. If no one is using the closet after or before work, there may not be enough harm done to pursue the matter.]{.rendered_qtext}
