**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **This is not a troll question. I read a lot of complaints and mockery about Donald Trump, yet the opposition is offering nothing credible in his place. Why is this?** When you cannot compete in the arena of ideas, your only attack left is an *ad hominem* (to the man) attack. Basically, they have nothing quantitatively better to offer, so they attack Trump as a person. Plus, since he literally is a caricature, it\'s really easy, so they don\'t have to debate him on the ideas. Anything he says, and they\'re just like, "But he\'s Trump!". He\'s not a politician. He has not spent his life concerned with his public image or what people will think or how his actions will play in the media. He doesn\'t care. Frankly, it\'s refreshing, to see what a non-politician can accomplish. And that *infuriates* the left. They hate that Trump can roll out of bed, make a decision, and be right most of the time. They hate that he doesn\'t have to have focus groups to decide what exactly he\'s going to say. so they *have* to destroy him. They have to prove that you have to be a politician in order to work in Washington. If Trump succeeds, it will show everyone that you don\'t have to be a lifelong politician in order to do well in government. They simply can\'t allow that, or it spells the end of their breed: the career politician.
