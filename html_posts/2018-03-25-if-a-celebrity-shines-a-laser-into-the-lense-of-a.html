Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The celebrity will be in exactly the same trouble as anyone else who destroys the property of others. The celebrity will be sued for destruction of private property, and will settle out of court by replacing the device, or more specifically, paying to replace the device.
