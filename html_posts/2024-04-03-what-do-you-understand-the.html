**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The answer is in Revelation, primarily in Revelation 13, and it is related to the "end times," the times when the Great Deceiver will unite all the nations of the world. He will appear to be a savior, but he will actually be Satan. He's sometimes referred to as "the Antichrist." He will also be assisted by the "false prophet," and it is this being who forces everyone to take the mark. It will include something visible to everyone, either on the right hand or on the forehead. Revelation tells us that he will make everyone take a mark in order to participate in financial transactions in society. In order to buy or sell in the new world, you will have to take the mark. It will only be given to those who worship the Antichrist. People have been worried that many modern-day items could be the mark. Some are worried about RFID chips in the hand, and are claiming them to be the mark. They are not. They may be part of what will ultimately become the mark, but simply having a microchip embedded in your hand is not, in itself, the mark. Remember that the mark is something visible on the hand or head, not just a card that you carry, and the mark is only given to those who worship the Antichrist, who is a single entity. Some people were worried about the COVID-19 vaccine, or "vaccine passports," being the mark; they are not. But the idea is headed in the same direction as the mark. However, the mark will be something visible, on the right hand or on the forehead, and it will be affirmatively given only to those who freely worship the Antichrist. 2 Thessalonians tells us that we will know who the Antichrist is when he is revealed. So we are certainly not there yet. Also, when he is revealed, it will be clear how the number 666 relates to him, so we can use that to dispel the idea that any of our current national leaders are the Antichrist.
