--- title: '"Can you opt out of an HOA after you have joined it?"' date: '2022-09-20' tags: - homeowners-assn layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Depends on the type of HOA. If it came with the house (i.e. it's tied to the deed), then it's a *mandatory* HOA. You can't leave it by any means, other than: - selling your house, or - dissolving the HOA (which is difficult, time-consuming, and not-free) If you joined it after you moved in, it's a *voluntary* HOA, and you can leave following whatever procedure the HOA has established for people to cancel their membership. \ Original question: Can you ever leave an HOA once you\'re in?
