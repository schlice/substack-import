Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What happens if someone gets served a summons to appear in court, but they don't go?** Normally, the judge finds you in contempt of court and issues a *bench warrant* for the person's arrest. This means the police can treat it just as they do any other arrest warrant. Depending on the reason the warrant was issued, they may not immediately serve the warrant. In such a case, it can sit and wait until the next time you have an interaction with police.
