Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [\"Regularly\" is a pretty big word. I don\'t think banks \"regularly\" pay billions in fines.\ \ Jail time is only indicated if criminal law is broken. Generally, there is no \"crime\" involved in bank misconduct. Banks are regulated by banking regulation, and can face fines by their constitutient organizations if they violate those regulations, but those regulation violations are not criminal violations of any Federal, state, or local law.]{.rendered_qtext}
