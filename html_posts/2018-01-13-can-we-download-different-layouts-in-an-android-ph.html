{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Android, unlike iOS, can have different \"launchers\" applied, which totally change the home screen. You can completely customize the home screen and icons, backgrounds, animations, widgets, colors, themes, and more! There are many to choose from, but some favorites are Nova Launcher and Action Launcher. They are highly customizable and a lot of fun. Search for \"launchers\" in the Google Play Store, or search for a few of the ones I mentioned.
