**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Do all states and jurisdictions comply with the national instant criminal background check system, (NICS) and if they don\'t should the federal government refuse FFL licenses to gun shops in all of the non compliant local and state jurisdictions?** Yes, all Federal Firearms License (FFL)-licensed dealers use NICS, as required by Federal law. It is true that some FFL dealers do not do the requisite background checks. The criminals know who these dealers are. Ideally, the BATFE would thoroughly monitor every FFL and would revoke their license if they violate the law. But BATFE doesn't have the resources to thoroughly monitor every FFL, and in the cases where they do find that a FFL has failed to follow the correct procedure, it is very rare that they actually revoke a license. Don't forget that the dealer himself has an appeals process he can follow, and he has a business and a livelihood to maintain as well. Nevertheless, BATFE could do more if they had more funding and more investigators.
