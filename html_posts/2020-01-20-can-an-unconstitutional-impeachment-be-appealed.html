**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can an unconstitutional impeachment be appealed?** Appealed to *whom*? In other words, what is the organization that is above the Congress which oversees impeachments? The Constitution says that the House has the *sole* authority to impeach, and the Senate has the *sole* authority to remove from office. This means that ***there is no one else***. Are you thinking maybe the Supreme Court? The court deals with *law*, and this is a matter of *politics*. The court does not handle matters of politics. Remember that impeachments are matters of politics. Are you listening to Trump's legal team who claims that the impeachment is "unconstitutional?" They're discussing the theory behind the phrase "high crimes and misdemeanors," and they are claiming that Trump's actions do not rise to that standard. They're calling the bending of the original meaning of "high crimes and misdemeanors" a "dangerous perversion of the Constitution." This may or may not be true, but the truth is that, regardless of what any legal scholar will tell you, high crimes and misdemeanors are anything that a majority of the House says they are, since there is no legal definition for what constitutes "high crimes and misdemeanors." The House has voted and said that Trump's actions *did* rise to the standard of high crimes and misdemeanors, and now it's up to the Senate to determine if the standard set by the House is enough to remove the President from office. So even if you could claim that Trump's actions didn't actually rise to the standard of "high crimes and misdemeanors" as the House said they did, what do you do? Your only option is to amend the Constitution and make it more clear what actually constitutes high crimes and misdemeanors for future presidents. So can an unconstitutional impeachment be appealed? No, because as long as the Constitutional process is followed, there is no such thing as an "unconstitutional" impeachment, and there's no one to whom you can appeal. The House followed its own rules and its Constitutional dictates and passed Articles of Impeachment. Period. End of story. That makes it Constitutional.
