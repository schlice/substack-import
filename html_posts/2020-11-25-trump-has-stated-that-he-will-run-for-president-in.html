**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Trump has stated that he will run for President in 2024. Do you think his cult will stand behind him for 4 years and then rise to the occasion again?** There is a small cohort of people who love Trump for Trump. They will support him in 2024, regardless of who else runs alongside him. Those people are the minority of Republicans. If the Republican Party puts up a strong candidate alongside him (and there are a few who have a shot --- Tom Cotton, Mike Pompeo, Nikki Haley, etc), he won't have a snowball's chance. Trump was a reaction to 8 years of Obama, and putting him up against someone who was as unlikable a candidate as Hillary Clinton ensured his victory. After experiencing the left's constant anti-Trump campaign for four years, and then putting him up against someone who is just likable enough, even if not energetic or enthusiastic (like Biden), and he loses. That should tell you something. With any luck, it tells the GOP something. But don't misinterpret. Simply because there is a cohort of "all-Trump-all-the-time" Republicans does not mean that all Republicans think this way. Far from it.
