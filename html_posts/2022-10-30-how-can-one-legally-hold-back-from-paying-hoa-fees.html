--- title: '"How can one legally hold back from paying HOA fees when maintenance and landscaping is being neglected and homeowners are doing those services? The management company responsible is not correcting the problem."' date: '2022-10-30' tags: - law - homeowners-assn layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You can't. What you *can* do is get a lawyer and find out how you can compel the management company to do their services correctly or lose their contract. But you can't hold back your HOA dues. The HOA will take your house from you if you do that. Don't mess with that. Get yourself a lawyer.
