Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [The police can subpoena whatever data they reasonably presume contains evidence required in an investigation. They need a *warrant* to do this, and the warrant is delivered to the company which then must turn over whatever it has.\ \ If it\'s like WhatsApp, and they don\'t retain your messages, there\'s nothing to turn over.]{.rendered_qtext}
