**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} No. There is no need to shelve your faith. Accepting the appointment does not require putting aside faith. It requires faith to accept the appointment in the first place. Trump\'s sins are between him and God, just as mine are, and just as Judge Coney Barret\'s are. We are warned in the Bible that judgement is the exclusive remedy of God. Not us. Treating someone differently because of their sins is exactly the opposite of Christian love. I wouldn\'t treat Trump any differently than any other flawed human. We are all sinners. We are all undeserving of the gift we have been given. Shelving your faith says "I know that God had a hand in this appointment, but I know better than God, and Trump is ***LITERALLY MORE EVIL THAN HITLER*** so I will turn down this appointment because of it." Do you see how crazy that sounds?
