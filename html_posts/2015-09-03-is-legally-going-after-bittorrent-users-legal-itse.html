Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [You don\'t have a reasonable expectation of privacy when you use BitTorrent. Your traffic is not encrypted. You are essentially using the public Internet. Whether or not someone has the right to monitor a particular network depends on the ownership of that portion of the network (i.e. your ISP can watch your usage of their system to ensure you are not violating their terms of service).\ \ If you are using TOR, that might be a different story. If you\'re using a VPN, it\'s a different story altogether. But just using a particular protocol doesn\'t give you a reasonable expectation of privacy.]{.rendered_qtext}
