--- title: '"How would the police handle a situation where someone had to knock out or otherwise physically restrain a friend from driving drunk?"' date: '2017-12-18' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} First of all, preventing someone from driving drunk should never escalate to the point of physical violence. The most physical you should ever get is taking their keys. The police should never be involved. Because if the police get involved, they will wait until the drunk person gets behind the wheel, and then arrest the drunk person for DUI. You don't actually have to be "driving" the vehicle before you can be arrested for DUI. But the bottom line is that this "friend" of yours will end up in jail if you get the police involved.
