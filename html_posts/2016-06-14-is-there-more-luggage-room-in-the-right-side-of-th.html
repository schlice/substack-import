**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Generally, no. The overhead bins of most aircraft are symmetrical. The only exception I can think of would be the Embraer ERJ-145 :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-ccdd7110f6c9c683dae3778999ac6e86){.portrait .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-ccdd7110f6c9c683dae3778999ac6e86" master_w="1125" master_h="1500"} ::: :::: This is a view from the front to the back, so the right side is on the left of this picture. Notice that the right side has two seats, while the left side only has one. There are only overhead bins on the right side, so in this case, there is more luggage room on the right side.
