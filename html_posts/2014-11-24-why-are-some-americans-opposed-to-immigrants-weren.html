**Content:** [I don\'t know why Americans would be opposed to immigrants. My grandparents were immigrants.\ \ I, however, as an American, am opposed to ***[ILLEGAL]{.underline}*** immigrants. Notice that I bolded, italicized, and underlined a missing word from the question.\ \ Millions of Americans have come to this country and been naturalized (i.e. made a citizen through a process overseen by the USCIS (US Customs and Immigration Service). It is a long process, sometimes taking years to complete. This is an issue. The administration needs to fix this. It is fixable. We can improve the immigration system.\ \ However, millions of immigrants have **[chosen]{.underline}** to ***violate the law*** and come into this country ***ILLEGALLY***, bypassing the immigration process and remaining in this country as \"undocumented aliens.\" Notice that there are also \"documented aliens,\" such as people who came into this country on a temporary visa and then simply decided to stay beyond the length of their visa. THESE are the immigrants I have a problem with.\ \ If your first act as a \"citizen\" of a new country is to ***VIOLATE THE LAWS OF THAT COUNTRY***, I have a problem with your continued residence in this country.]{.rendered_qtext}
