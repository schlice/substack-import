--- title: "Does my 4G mobile phone have a fixed IP address or does it change whenever I move to a different location or whenever I restart the phone?" date: "2015-03-08" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Phones use DHCP (Dynamic Host Control Protocol) to obtain new IP addresses from the carrier when the lease on the address expires. You don\'t get a new IP address every time the phone reboots, only when you restart the phone and the lease on the IP address has expired.]{.rendered_qtext}
