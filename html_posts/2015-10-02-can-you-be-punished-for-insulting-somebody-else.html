{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [If your speech incites a riot, you can be charged with that offense. If you use slanderous speech which causes harm to another person, you can be charged with that offense.\ \ But if you simply insult someone (\"Hey, you\'re ugly\"), that\'s not against the law in the United States, as it is protected by the First Amendment.]{.rendered_qtext}
