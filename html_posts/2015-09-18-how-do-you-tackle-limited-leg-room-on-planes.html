{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [There are several things you can do.\ \ 1. Sit somewhere where there is an open seat next to you so you can place your carry-on under that seat instead. Or sit in the last row and put your carry on under your own seat. This leaves the area at your feet open for you to stretch out.\ \ 2. If you\'re tall like me, you can bring your knees up and place them on the seatback in front of you, like kids do on school buses.\ \ 3. If no one is in the seat next to you, stretch your legs into the underseat area of the seat in front of you.\ \ 4. Or if all else fails, get up once or twice during the flight to stretch your legs.]{.rendered_qtext}
