**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can I sue cell phone makers like LG and Samsung for being the reason for my bad posture, which eventually lead to my cervical degeneration?** In the United States, you can sue anyone for anything. So to answer your question, **yes, you can** sue cell phone makers for your bad posture. But also in the United States, it is up to you to prove your case, as the court system works on an "innocent until proven guilty" basis. Therefore, it will be up to you to **prove** with a preponderance of evidence that the mobile phone manufacturers actually caused your bad posture. And ***that*** will be a tough row to hoe. In fact, there's a good chance that there is **correlation** between poor posture and mobile phone use, but there is no **causation**. This means that while some people slouch when using their mobile phones, it is not the phone that is **causing** you to slouch. In fact, the phone will work equally as well if you sit properly as it will if you slouch. Just from this reasoning alone, there is a fair chance that the phone is not actually causing you to slouch, but your improper posture while using a phone is the proximate cause of your cervical degeneration. Therefore, it is exceedingly unlikely (almost like the fate of the snowball in Hell) that you would prevail in such a lawsuit.
