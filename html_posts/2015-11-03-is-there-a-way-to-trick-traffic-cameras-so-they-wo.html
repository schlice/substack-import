**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} *Please Note: In almost all states, any device or treatment which obscures your license plate (and paint would be included) is* ***illegal*** *to have on your car while you\'re on the road. Check your state rules before using any of these products. Being caught with any of them could get you a ticket.* Yes, there are a number of solutions that allow your license plate to be obscured in such a way that when a camera tries to view your plate, it is obscured. One such tool is a variation of a Fresnel lens. When your plate is viewed straight on, it is visible. But from a high angle (such as where a camera would be located), the plate is obscured. :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-f07a31ae69a78c897d9edf48688a207c){.landscape .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-f07a31ae69a78c897d9edf48688a207c" master_w="437" master_h="226"} ::: :::: A second solution attacks the problem of red light cameras taking flash photos of your plate. Have you ever taken a picture of a street sign? Have you noticed how washed out the image of the sign is? Signs use a highly-reflective gloss paint (originally created by 3M) to reflect as much light back as possible. It\'s used in road stripe paint and signage paint all over the world. Now imagine spraying that clear-coat high-gloss reflective substance on your license plate, and what the impact would be to a camera which takes flash pictures: :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-ce9b6c705edfeee1b3fe846af897d78b){.landscape .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-ce9b6c705edfeee1b3fe846af897d78b" master_w="400" master_h="150"} ::: :::: You can tell that the contrast was turned up in the second photo, but still, you get the idea. There is a third system which is more technological, and is built into a license plate frame. It detects the flash from the camera and activates several bright infrared LEDs around the license plate to similarly \"blind\" the camera to the contents of your plate.
