**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Come 20 Jan, will democrats citing increased (esp. firearm) crime and chaos in large cities pass legislation to ban "assault rifles" and place restrictions on magazine capacity? Assumption: both houses and POTUS are democrat.** Yes. [[They have already stated](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=39e5c6&url=https://www.reuters.com/article/uk-factcheck-biden-orourke-guns/partly-false-claimvideo-shows-joe-bidensayingif-he-wins-hes-coming-forourguns-idUSKBN20S2ST){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="reuters.com"}]{.qlink_container} that this will happen. After accepting Beto O'Rourke's endorsement, Biden told Beto that if elected President, Beto would be the one to "take care of the gun problem, you're going to be the one who leads this effort." O'Rourke made his position well known as part of the 2019 Democratic Presidential Nomination debates, when he famously said, "Hell yes, we're going to take away your AR-15, your AK-47\[.\]" Biden already publicly supports a federal gun buyback program to reduce the number of weapons on the street. He also supports renewing an expired assault weapons ban imposed in the 1990's and universal background checks for gun sales.
