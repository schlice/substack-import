**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How did the United States Air Force feel about the depiction of the USAF on the show Stargate SG-1?** Overall, the production crew did a fantastic job of portraying the Air Force in a very positive light. While most of the details were sketchy and very much made up (I guarantee you there are no basements, let alone sub-basements, under NORAD), I think the crew did a great job. And deep down, I really would have loved to work for Don Davis. I think he would have been the best commander ever, and I worked for some stellar officers.
