**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Should police from defunded departments be entitled to career retraining funds and programs? To which fields of employment does your average officer have transferrable skills?** Of course they should, just as any person whose company goes out of business should. For what reason would you deny them access to such programs? Police officers have many transferrable skills. They could do direct transfers into private security (which is what will replace police if you do manage to get rid of them---police for the rich, none for anyone else). They could transfer into corporate security offices. They could do armed courier jobs. There\'s lots of possibilities.
