Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Owning a gun does not increase one\'s aggression any more than owning a fork increases one\'s hunger or owning a pencil increases one\'s proclivity to write a letter.\ \ The **person** uses the **tool** to commit an **act**. The **tool** does not force the **person** to commit an **act.** Nor does the **tool** make one more or less likely to commit an **act**.]{.rendered_qtext}
