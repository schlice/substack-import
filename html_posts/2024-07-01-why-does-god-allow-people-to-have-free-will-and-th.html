--- title: '"Why does God allow people to have free will and the ability to choose whether or not to love Him?"' date: '2024-07-01' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Love, by definition, must be freely given. It cannot be coerced.** C. S. Lewis wrote, "the Irresistible and the Indisputable are the two weapons which the very nature of \[God's\] scheme forbids Him to use. Merely to over-ride a human will (as His felt presence in any but the faintest and most mitigated degree would certainly do) would be for Him useless. He cannot ravish. He can only woo."
