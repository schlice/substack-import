**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Isn\'t the fact that the Secret Service doesn\'t allow guns to be brought to NRA conventions when the President is speaking a glaring hypocrisy, particularly when they always trot out the good guy with a gun line?** No. The only way the Secret Service disallowing guns at any one place would be hypocritical is if they arbitrarily allow concealed firearms at one location but disallow them in another. It doesn't matter if it's an NRA convention or a Catholic church. It is contained and sterilized. Period. So therefore, no hypocrisy.
