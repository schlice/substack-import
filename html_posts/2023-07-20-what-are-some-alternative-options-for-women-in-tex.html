--- title: "What are some alternative options for women in Texas who are denied abortions?" date: "2023-07-20" tags: ["law"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} If you're in Texas, you'll need to travel out of Texas to get an abortion unless you qualify for an exception. Exceptions are very limited and include: - To save the pregnant person\'s life - To prevent serious risk to the pregnant person\'s physical health. It is legal, however, to travel outside of Texas in order to get an abortion. You can visit the following site to find an abortion provider in a nearby state: [[https://www.abortionfinder.org/](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=bd7ae3&url=https://www.abortionfinder.org/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);"}]{.qlink_container} Another option, while less appealing to someone seeking an abortion, is carrying to term and using the Texas Safe Haven law (also known as the Baby Moses law).
