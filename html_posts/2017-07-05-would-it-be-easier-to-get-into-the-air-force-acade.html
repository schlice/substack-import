--- title: '"Would it be easier to get into the Air Force Academy if I enlist into the Air Force before applying?"' date: '2017-07-05' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} To be honest with you, I can't see how that would be "easier." Think about what you're saying. You enlist in the Air Force, attend Basic Training in Lackland, go to Tech School, serve three years as an enlisted member so that you can show that you have what it takes to be an Air Force officer so that you can be recommended by your chain of command so that you can attend the Prep School so that you can enter the Academy and take Basic Training all over again. That doesn't sound very "easy" to me.
