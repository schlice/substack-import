**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Do home security systems deter criminals better than firearms? Or are they different solutions to different problems?** Different solutions to different problems. Home security systems make your home less attractive to burglars. If a burglar is comparing two houses, and one has a security system, he'll choose to break into the house without the security system. It simply is one of several steps you can take to make your house less attractive to burglars than your neighbor's house. As we've said before, you don't have to make your house a fortress; you only have to make your house less attractive to attack than your neighbor's house. Firearms are not a deterrent to criminals, so they definitely are addressing a different problem. The problem that firearms addresses is the one that causes you to die as a victim inside your home from home invaders who seek to kill you. Firearms do not make your home less attractive to criminals who wish to break into a home (there's nothing on the outside of your home that advertises that you're armed). Firearms are your last line of defense when someone breaks into your home and intends to harm you.
