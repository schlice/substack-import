**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Would Trump be devastated if it becomes blatantly obvious to him that he is the most unpopular president ever?** I was unaware that the Office of the President was actually cover for a popularity contest. Hmm, I'm going to have to go away and rethink my life now. But before I go, the presidency is not a popularity contest. Popularity does not make someone effective at their job. As a commander, it was nice to be liked, but unnecessary. It's more important that my orders be followed than I be friends with everyone. You're never going to make everyone happy. It reminds me of this quote, attributed to the poet John Lydgate and later adapted by President Lincoln: "You can please some of the people all of the time, you can please all of the people some of the time, but you can't please all of the people all of the time".
