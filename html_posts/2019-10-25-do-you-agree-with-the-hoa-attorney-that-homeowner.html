**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Do you agree with the HOA attorney that homeowner associations are entitled to undermine constitutional rights?** HOAs are not government organizations. The Constitution (and the Bill of Rights) prevents government from infringing on your rights. Private organizations which you willingly choose to join can infringe on your Constitutional rights. For example, HOAs can control what signs you post outside your house and what they can and cannot say. They can prevent you from speaking freely on the street corner with a megaphone if it disturbs homeowners. They can prevent you from forming a church in your house, even though you have the freedom of religion. Those are just some of the ways that your rights can be curtailed, and I haven't even left the 1st Amendment.
