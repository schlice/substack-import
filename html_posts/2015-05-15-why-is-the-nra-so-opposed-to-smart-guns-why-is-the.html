Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [The gun is a very simple mechanical tool. It works in the harshest of conditions. It works when it is needed. So far, the \"smart guns\" out there are fragile, complex, can be bypassed, and don\'t always work (i.e. sometimes they let other-than-registered shooters fire the weapon, and sometimes they prevent the registered shooter from firing the weapon).\ \ If a gun is a tool to be used in a life-and-death situation, mistakes like these can cost you your life. Until the technology becomes as reliable as the underlying gun itself, it\'s not a viable technology.]{.rendered_qtext}
