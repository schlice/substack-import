Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Error 53 is caused when certain components in the iPhone have been replaced by third-party maintainers, such as the touchscreen or the fingerprint sensor. The only fix, previously, was to have your device serviced by Apple. However, due to mounting pressure related to forcing users to use Apple\'s repair services, Apple released an update to iOS 9.2.1 which will resolve the issue.
