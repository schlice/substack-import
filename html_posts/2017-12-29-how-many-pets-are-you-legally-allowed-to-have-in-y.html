**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How many pets are you legally allowed to have in your town, and do you have more pets than the town\'s legal limit?** Where I live, the city has limits on how many and what types of pets you can own, and our HOA has tighter restrictions on the same. In our HOA, I'm reasonably certain (off the top of my head) that you can have no more than four pets living with you on your property. I have never heard of this regulation enforced, and even if it was, it would only be because we had a Crazy Cat Lady who owned 20 cats and took in another 20 strays and her house smelled like a cat box from a block away. Not only does that produce a nuisance odor, but it is an environmental toxin, and hurts the neighborhood overall, so at that point the HOA would step in.
