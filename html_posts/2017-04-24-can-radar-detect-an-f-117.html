--- title: "Can radar detect an F-117?" date: "2017-04-24" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The radar cross section (RCS) of a target is defined as the effective area intercepting an amount of incident power which, when scattered isotropically, produces a level of reflected power at the receiving station which is equal to that from the target. - The RCS of an F-15 (which was not designed with any stealthy characteristics) is 25 square meters. - The RCS of an F-117 is 0.003 square meters. So above background noise, the F-117 is effectively invisible to radar.
