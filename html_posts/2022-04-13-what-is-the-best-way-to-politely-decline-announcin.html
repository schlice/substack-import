Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Just decline. When asked why, simply explain that you're not offended by anyone misgendering you, so participating in this "pronoun identification" exercise would be disrespectful to the people who *do* take offense at being misgendered. Declaring my pronouns when I don't require it, am not offended by it, and match the gender I appear to be, minimizes the experience for those who actually care. In other words, me, declaring my pronouns, would actually be offensive. \ Original question: What is the best way to politely decline announcing my correct gender pronoun to a group?
