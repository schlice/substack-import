**Content:** [See a doctor.\ \ Throat and ear pain when swallowing can be caused by any number of medical conditions. Most commonly, it is caused by sinus drainage irritating the throat and similar drainage from the sinus cavities through the eustachian tubes is also irritating.\ \ But there are a number of other conditions which can cause pain when swallowing. I highly suggest visiting your doctor this week. If your pain is accompanied by fever, chills, body aches, nausea or vomiting, get to your doctor today, or visit your local urgent care if your doctor\'s office doesn\'t have weekend hours.
