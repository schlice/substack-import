**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What rights do people have to keep their salary confidential or private?** What responsibility does anyone have to *disclose* their salary? As far as I know, with some exceptions for government employees, employees of private companies are under no obligation to disclose their salaries to anyone. So what right does a person have to keep his salary confidential? The same right to not do something that is not required by law. Just as I have a right to not cross the street if I don't want to, I have a right to not disclose my salary if I don't want to. It's true, sometimes when you apply for a new job, they want to know your old salary. The truth: *they don't have to have it!* They don't need your current salary to determine whether or not they are going to hire you.
