**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What legal recourse do I have if my HOA says that my Airbnb in my home where I live is considered commercial propery?** The only recourse you have is to hire a real estate attorney in your area. Make sure you hire a real estate attorney and that they specialize in HOA litigation in your area, as laws surrounding HOA authority vary wildly. While the legislature in most places gives broad authority to HOAs, the judiciary in most places applies the most narrow construction to HOA rulings that they can possibly get away with, so it's definitely not a futile act. As was already pointed out, it may actually end up being cheaper to sell your existing home and to buy a home in an area not so tightly controlled.
