--- title: '"What happens if a person is injured on an association property with posted private property signs?"' date: '2024-02-12' tags: - homeowners-assn layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The "Private Property" signs alert people that permission is required before entering. HOA members and their guests, under normal circumstances, have ongoing permission to access HOA property without additional permissions. If a person is injured on Association property, the Association maintains general liability insurance which would pay if the Association was found to be liable for any portion, or all of, the injury. The insurance adjustors handle the liability investigation.
