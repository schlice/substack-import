--- title: '"Do former US Presidents use their own personal planes?"' date: '2025-05-18' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Former US presidents, ever since Dwight D. Eisenhower, can fly on government aircraft after they leave office, including military VIP transport (like the Air Force C-21) as well as the same air transport fleet used by the current president, including the Boeing VC-25 (which is commonly known as Air Force One when the current President is on board). Additionally, US presidents can charter an airplane, the cost of which is covered by the government under the Former Presidents Act.
