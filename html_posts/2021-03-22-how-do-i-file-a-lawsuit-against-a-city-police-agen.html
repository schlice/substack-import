**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How do I file a lawsuit against a city police agency for unlawful practice, unlawful arrest, and unlawful search?** Hire a civil rights attorney in your area. Police can be sued for violating your civil rights. If you were charged with a crime, you can ask the court to exclude any evidence gathered during the unlawful arrest. You can also file a complaint with the police department. Your lawyer can help you do that, and having a lawyer help you makes the complaint process worlds easier.
