Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Your best bet is to do a factory reset. Your phone will wipe and will be re-installed from the recovery partition. Unless you've been doing some interesting phone hacking yourself, your recovery partition will have a clean version of Android in it. Once you wipe and reboot, your phone will reach out to the Google Play Store and re-install the apps you had previously. There may be some bits of data that don't get recovered, especially if they were not stored in a cloud-based account.
