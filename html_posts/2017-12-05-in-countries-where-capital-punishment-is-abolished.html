--- title: '"In countries where capital punishment is abolished, what is the highest sentence for first-degree, premeditated murder?"' date: '2017-12-05' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Without the death penalty, the highest punishment available is life imprisonment without the possibility of parole. Technically, this is sometimes expressed as multiple, concurrent life sentences, but functionally it means the same thing: the convict will spend the remainder of his natural life in prison, and will die there.
