Content: [To expound on the Anonymous answer (which is correct),
\"ditching\" at too high of an angle will cause the aircraft to
\"smack\" the water and break apart, and ditching at too low of an angle
will cause the aircraft to nose-dive into the water.\
\
The SOP is to try to land somewhere dry. Ditching is a very dangerous
and only to be used as a \"last-ditch\" (no pun intended) effort to get
down safely.]
