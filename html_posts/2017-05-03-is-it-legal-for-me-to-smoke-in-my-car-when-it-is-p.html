--- title: "Is it legal for me to smoke in my car when it is parked on school property?" date: "2017-05-03" tags: ["law"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} While the car is indeed your property, when you *voluntarily* drove the car onto school property, you agreed to respect school rules, which includes not smoking on school property. You (and your car) agree to be bound by those rules, and you tacitly agreed to that when you drove your car onto school property. If you instead leave you car in the parking lot and walk off of school property (perhaps onto the public sidewalk outside of school property), you can have your smoke.
