**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Someone is coming into my house with a key when I\'m away. Calling the police doesn\'t help; neither does speaking to the owner of the building nor using video equipment. What can I do to end this madness?** It's easier to beg for forgiveness than to ask for permission. Your solution is simple. Hire a locksmith and change the locks. Do not ask for permission, just do it. Then give a copy of the key to the landlord. In most cases, landlords don't care if you change the locks, as long as they have a copy of the key. End the madness. Change the locks.
