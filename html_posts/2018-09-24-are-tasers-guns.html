**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Are tasers guns?** The answer it it depends on what state you're in. In the vast majority of states, Tasers are considered to be personal protection devices, and are not classified as firearms. Some states do classify them in the same categories as firearms and control their use similarly, but most states do not. Be sure to check the state laws where you live before you purchase a Taser. [[This quick overview](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=188a95&url=https://buy.taser.com/pages/state-requirements){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="taser.com"}]{.qlink_container} from Axon, the company that makes the Taser, should be a good starting point. Technically speaking, Tasers use compressed gas to propel their darts, so they are not classified as "firearms" which use gunpowder and primer.
