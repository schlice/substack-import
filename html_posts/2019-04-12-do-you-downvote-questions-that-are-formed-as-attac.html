Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Do you downvote questions that are formed as attacks on conservatives, Republicans, or other parts of the center-right coalition and why?** No. Not at all. Why? ***Because that's not what the Quora voting system is for.*** Votes are cast for *high-quality content.* Votes will move a question up or down in other users' feeds, or make it visible to a larger segment of the Quora population. Similarly, upvoting answers is reserved for *high-quality content.* Answers which are both correct and engaging get upvoted, *even if I disagree with what the user says.* There are many popular Quorans with whom I fundamentally politically disagree. I still consistently upvote their content, because it is high-quality content. *So how, then, do we deal with questions which attack any group (not just Conservatives)?* **The answer is to** ***Report*** **that content.** You can do that through the three dot menu underneath the question. Click it, and choose *Report.* :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-4c822357b6f8e4caa6b95b328b13774f){.landscape .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-4c822357b6f8e4caa6b95b328b13774f" master_w="772" master_h="377"} ::: :::: If a question attacks a person or group, I immediately report that question as Harassment. *But Bill*, I can hear you saying right now, *when you Report a question, it automatically downvotes that question! So why are you saying we shouldn't downvote the question?* The answer is **if your downvote is in relation to a Report, then that's Quora using their algorithm to show the answer to fewer people because of the alleged harassment, not because of your opinion on the tone of the question.** The report is the important part; the downvote is secondary to the report. The downvote alone only fixes the symptom of the problem, not the problem itself. **To that end, using downvotes alone does nothing to alert Quora to harassing behavior, and the misuse of their platform.** Please, don't downvote questions like this. Please Report them, and the system will downvote the question automatically.
