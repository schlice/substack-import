**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Should you call 911 if you see somebody canvassing your neighborhood with hate-speech filled pamphlets?** If there is a crime in progress, or a crime is imminent, you should call the police. But distributing leaflets is not a crime, unless the person is clearly trespassing. Distributing leaflets, or "flyposting," is not generally considered to be a crime. The contents of the leaflets does not generally modify the legality of the activity. The only exception is religion, which is even more protected than general information leaflets. I suggest you check with your local city to see if you have an ordinance against flyposting, or if you live in an HOA-controlled neighborhood, see if your HOA allows flyposting. If it does, you might discuss changing that with your HOA.
