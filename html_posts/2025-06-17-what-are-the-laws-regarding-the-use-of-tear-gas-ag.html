**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} This question is way too broad to be able to answer with any level of usefulness. Are there violent agitators or paid Democrat full-time protesters mixed in with the peaceful protesters causing trouble? If you are at a protest and you are peaceful, you have no intention of causing harm, and violence breaks out, I suggest you get the heck out of there. Police will use crowd control to quell the violence, and you could inadvertently be caught up in that. There is no law which says that if there are peaceful protesters in a crowd which becomes violent that crowd control measures cannot be deployed. In other words, **don't associate with violent agitators**. If they are there, you should not be there.
