**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What can you do if your HOA is corrupt and incompetent?** "Corrupt" means they are violating laws. Get together with your neighbors, retain a real estate attorney, and file a lawsuit. "Incompetent" means they don't know how to manage money, or they don't know how to properly discharge their duties. While not illegal, it's not a great situation to find yourself in. In this scenario, you can either - move, or - run for the Board and fix the problems yourself. I did the latter. I served six years on our Board, three as President. I'd like to say I fixed many of the problems. Some remain unfixed, but at least it was the choice of all the homeowners to leave it that way. Then I moved.
