**Content:** [I think you are asking about the \"lost memory\" part of your question. While [[Matthew Bohrer](https://www.quora.com/profile/Matthew-Bohrer)]{.qlink_container} already told you about being charged while in your coma, once you come out, you can still be charged for the murder, even though you remember none of it.\ \ Basically, the law considers what your state was at the time you committed the crime. Your lack of memory may be taken into consideration at sentencing, but whether or not you remember your crime doesn\'t alleviate you of the requirement to stand for your crime.]{.rendered_qtext}
