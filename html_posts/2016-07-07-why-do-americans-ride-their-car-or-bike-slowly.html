**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} In the United States, we have very few places which allow speeds over 120 kph (roughly 75 mph). Most places don't allow speeds that fast. Watch any episodes of *Top Gear* where the presenters shot clips in the USA, and you'll see them lamenting about how they can't go over 55 mph on some roads (roughly 88 kph).
