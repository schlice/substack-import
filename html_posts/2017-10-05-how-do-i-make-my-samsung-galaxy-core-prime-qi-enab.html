Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} In order to Qi-enable a device which is not natively Qi-enabled, you must add an external Qi adapter, usually part of a phone case. The case and the Qi adapter will plug in to your USB port on the bottom of the phone. You can then set your phone onto a Qi charging pad and it will work.
