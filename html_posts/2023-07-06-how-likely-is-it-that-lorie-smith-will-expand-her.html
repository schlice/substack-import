Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} About as likely as me winning the PowerBall three times in a row. That doesn't mean that she won't do business with gays or lesbians. She already has. She's made great websites for gay or lesbian clients. When she starts offering *wedding* websites, however, she will not be offering websites which celebrate same-sex weddings. The Supreme Court said that the First Amendment prohibits her state (Colorado) from forcing her to create expressive designs speaking messages with which she disagrees. **TL;DR: It's not about the** ***people*, it's about the** ***message*.**
