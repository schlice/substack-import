It was just a few years ago that Bill Clinton was having sex in the Oval Office, and then lied about the whole thing. The House passed impeachment charges, but the Senate didn't think enough of those charges to remove him from office. So the message we heard from Washington was, "We know what you did, we know you lied about it, but you can still be President." Fast-forward to today. We already know what kind of a man Trump is. He's a pussy grabber. Access Hollywood made sure we knew that, loud and clear. Here's the thing though: we knew about all of this before the election, and we (collectively) elected Trump anyway. So will this be a beneficial scandal to promote? I doubt it: - Most of America (in fact, nearly everyone who voted for Trump), will probably just shrug. "We already knew he was a pig, so having an affair is like, 'meh, I could see him do that.'" - Others will be relieved that it was a consensual relationship and not a sexual assault scandal as is being highlighted by the #metoo movement.
