**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Do hospitals run warrant checks on pregnant patients? Misdemeanor warrants felony warrants?** No. Hospitals are not the police. Hospitals have no compelling interest in determining whether or not a patient has any outstanding warrants. Just as my dentist doesn't care whether or not I have any outstanding warrants, neither does my doctor or the hospital.
