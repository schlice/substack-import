Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It does not appear that HBO Go is supported on Vizio Smart TVs. Note that HBO Go (the service included with a traditional HBO subscription through your cable or satellite provider) and HBO NOW (the standalone service you can purchase at any time) are both supported on the same list of devices. Clearly, there is either an incompatibility between Vizio's system of apps and the HBO apps, or there is a contractual issue between HBO and Vizio that is preventing the app from being fielded on Vizio TVs. This normally has something to do with supportability and how much access Vizio demands to HBO's code, and vice versa. For example, HBO may demand more control over their own software (i.e. for support or upgrade) than Vizio is prepared or even able to offer. If they can't reach an agreement on those points, the app doesn't get fielded. Note that I do not know the actual reason why the HBO family of apps is not on Vizio Smart TVs; I am merely explaining why these apps sometimes don't make it on. I don't know if Vizio and HBO are working to get their apps natively supported within Vizio's app framework. This is all just general information. It looks like you will have to use a compatible streaming device to use HBO Go or HBO NOW, such as an Apple TV, Chromecast, Amazon Fire TV or Fire TV Stick, or a compatible gaming console (PlayStation or Xbox).
