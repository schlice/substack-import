The problem is that booby traps are *indiscriminate*. They will hurt a thief just as badly as they will hurt an innocent child who was playing nearby. A booby trap cannot determine *intent*. What if I had to move that cooler to get to someone who was injured? I wasn't trying to steal your cooler, but now you've injured me, and now you'll be responsible for any medical costs I incur due to your trap. Additionally, you can't injure someone to protect *property*. You can use force to defend against force, but this is not that situation. A better option would have been to use something that is non-injurious.
