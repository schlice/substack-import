**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It was already tried, and was already ruled unconstitutional. In 1993, the late Senator Daniel Patrick Moynihan from New York proposed a 10,000% tax on ammunition. The court ruled that ammunition is an essential part of a firearm, and that taxing it this way violates the Second Amendment by disproportionately affecting the poor, therefore denying them their rights under the Second Amendment.
