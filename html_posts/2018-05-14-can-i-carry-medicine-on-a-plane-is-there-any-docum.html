**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can I carry medicine on a plane? Is there any documentation required? Can I carry a bottle that is already open or I need to carry a sealed bottle of medication?** Yes you can. Prescription medication must be legal (i.e. no medical cannabis) and must be prescribed to you. Please keep it in the original bottle with the prescription label on it. Some specialty medication (like pre-filled syringes) comes with a prescription card. In any case, keep it all together. Please try to keep over-the-counter medications you wish to carry on board in its original packaging if at all possible (i.e. in its original bottle or box). Medications in baggies could be questionable.
