**Content:** [Wi-Fi allows you to upload your pictures to a cloud picture hosting provider real-time, effectively giving you unlimited storage capacity while you\'re connected to a Wi-Fi access point.\ \ GPS allows you to \"geotag\" your pictures, which will place the latitude and longitude of your current position in the metadata of the picture. Your cell phone already does this. It allows for intelligent photo software to organize your photos based on the location the photo was taken.]{.rendered_qtext}
