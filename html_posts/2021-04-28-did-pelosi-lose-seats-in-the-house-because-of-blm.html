**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} No, "Pelosi" didn't lose seats. Democrats are losing seats in the House because their states are shrinking in population as people move out of those states into others. Democrats are losing those seats because those states declining in population are traditionally blue states. People are fleeing high tax, heavy regulation states and fleeing towards lower tax, lighter regulation states. In other words, they are voting with their feet. The number of House Representatives is partly based on the population of the state, determined every 10 years in the census, as required by the Constitution. The number of representatives goes up or down depending on the population change. \ Original question: **Did Pelosi lose seats in the House because of BLM advocacy for police defunding?**
