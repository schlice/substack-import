I would also venture to say that you and I have looked over the totality of the information and have come to different conclusions. This is because humans have free will and can make decisions. The basis of the decisions we make will determine the outcome of that decision-making process. If you choose to only use the scientific (and the current limits of scientific knowledge) you will only reach one logical conclusion: God does not exist. I choose to not limit my knowledge to the extent of human scientific endeavor, as there is still much that scientists (admittedly) do not know. I choose to include God\'s inspired word to guide my thoughts and actions in the way He wants me to be. I determine that through my own introspection following the reading and understanding of His word.]{.rendered_qtext}
