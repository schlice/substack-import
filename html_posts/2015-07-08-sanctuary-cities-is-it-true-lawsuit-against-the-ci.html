Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Sanctuary cities notwithstanding, I can see lawsuits against the city for harm caused by specific people failing for that reason; lawsuits against police departments alleging specific harms caused by police action or inaction have failed for similar reasons.\ \ The case law has shown that police have a general duty to protect citizens, but the police do not have a specific duty to protect any individual unless special arrangement has been made beforehand, or the police have you in a custodial situation in which you cannot protect yourself.]{.rendered_qtext}
