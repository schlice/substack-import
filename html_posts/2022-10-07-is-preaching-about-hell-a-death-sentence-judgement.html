**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} In church circles, sermons about hell are known as "attendance reduction sermons" as the Sunday following such a sermon traditionally finds the church with a lower attendance --- people just don't want to hear about hell. But it's definitely a Biblical subject, and it's the proverbial elephant in the room. It needs to be discussed. We need to know what awaits us if we don't repent.
