**Content:** [No. Terror events are a political designation, not an enforcement designation.\ \ Certain terror events, like the Fort Hood shootings, were incorrectly classified as \"workplace violence,\" or in other words, a disgruntled employee going crazy, to avoid the appearance of more domestic terror events.\ \ School shootings are most generally caused by persons who are mentally unstable or who have some mental disease or defect. Terrorists are generally of sound mind.]{.rendered_qtext}
