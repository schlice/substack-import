**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} *NOTE: This is a US framed question, so it is a US-framed answer.* I missed where they promised that no one would die from COVID, and that any deaths can only be attributed to mismanagement at the federal level. We know that severe COVID causes severe respiratory distress. We also know that people with pre-existing respiratory illness, which is the fourth (chronic lower respiratory illness) and eighth (P&I - pneumonia and influenza) largest killers of people worldwide, are hit very hard by severe COVID. It's no surprise that a virus which causes severe respiratory distress would hit hard against people who already have respiratory issues of some kind. We also had few effective treatments against the virus and its actions. We now have a vaccine which has been distributed widely in this country, we have a moderate understanding about how the virus transmits, and we have some very effective treatment options thanks to the Herculean efforts of doctors and nurses on the ground in emergency departments across the country. **No politician is responsible for anyone's death.** Unless a politician is **literally** standing between you and the vaccine, or standing between you and a treatment, they cannot be responsible for those deaths. Yes, political mismanagement is a travesty, but it is the state which controls vaccine distribution, not the federal government. If you don't have access to a vaccine, you must first look to your state to find out why not.
