**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Not in the United States. Here, we have freedom of choice. We are allowed to choose medical treatment which, between us and our doctors in private, legally-protected conversations, are best for us. We are not mandated to accept medical procedures which we do not want, regardless of future disease which may result from that decision. Should we say that all vapers who suffer lung cancer which we only learn of 50 years from now only be given palliative care? If it\'s good enough for a vaccine, it\'s good enough for all life choices which result in bodily harm in the future.
