Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [First of all, you can\'t \"make\" someone fall in love with you, Godly or otherwise. It will happen when it happens and not a second sooner.\ \ In order to find the person you\'re looking for, make sure you\'re looking in the place you want to find the person you\'re looking for.\ \ You may be pretty active in the church, but are you active in the place where single men are expecting to find single women? Are you active in the singles groups in your church? I attend a Christian church, and we have singles groups that do monthly social functions.\ \ What about your friends? I assume they are churchgoers as well; do they go to other social functions with similarly-churched people? Have you tried attending any of those functions?\ \ And finally, to add a more Christian bent to my answer, have you asked God about this? God will bring the right person into your life when He is ready to do so. Telling God that you want Him to bring you someone *right now* is the height of arrogance. Who are we to place demands on God? When God is ready, He will place the right man in your life. Because the timetable is not up to us, is it?]{.rendered_qtext}
