**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Do the people at NORAD really have the time to track a non-existent Santa?** Well, yes and no. Really, we just set the Santa Monitoring System (SMS) to automatic. It keeps a lock on the sleigh and posts the update to the NORAD Tracks Santa website, which feeds all of our other systems. It's pretty simple and doesn't task the systems much. But should we? It's outside of our core mission of Missile Warning. We do anyway, out of the goodness of our hearts.
