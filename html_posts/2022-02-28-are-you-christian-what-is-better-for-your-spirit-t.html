**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I can't answer who between those two makes a better human. There have been wonderful humans who didn't believe in God at all. So simply believing in God or following his commands does not automatically make you a better human. But what is better for your *spirit* is to do **both**, not either/or. You must believe in God, love God, and repent of your sins, and live by his teachings. \ Original question: Are you Christian? What is better for your spirit, to live practicing the Christ\'s doctrine or believing in God, in the Virgin, etc.? Who is a better human: s/he who practices the Christ\'s teachings or s/he believer in God who do not practice them?
