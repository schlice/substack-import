**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} *(US Answer Only)* Depending on where you live, it may not be. This is something which varies by state. States define for themselves what is considered to be indecent exposure, and while some states define that as any exposure of genitalia to anyone who has not affirmatively consented to seeing such things (i.e. New Jersey), other states define it as only "sexually-explicit" or "sexually-suggestive" exposure (i.e. Colorado).
