--- title: '"If I became a police officer, would this make me immune to obeying traffic laws?"' date: '2017-09-25' tags: - law-enforcement - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Not only does this **not** make you immune to obeying traffic laws, but in our department, every contact with local law enforcement, both on and off duty, must be documented and delivered to the chain of command. Too many of those (even those where you did not get a ticket) will force an internal review of your training and perhaps a closer look at whether or not you should remain employed at that agency.
