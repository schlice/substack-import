**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Is lying about military service or veteran status...i.e. \"Stolen Valor\" illegal?** No. You may tell anyone you like that you're a military veteran, even if you're not. You can even wear a military uniform if you like. All protected by the 1st Amendment. Once you try to wear your uniform into Starbuck's and get 10% off of your latte because you're "military," you've committed fraud. In short, so long as you do not financially benefit from your lie, you're allowed to lie about being military.
