**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If I tell someone to commit an illegal act but they do not do it, am I guilty of anything?** In general, no. Conspiracy to commit a crime requires more than one person to agree to commit a crime together. It then requires one of the people involved to take "a substantial step" towards the execution of that crime. One person telling another person to commit a crime does not amount to conspiracy, especially since no one took any substantial steps towards actually committing the crime.
