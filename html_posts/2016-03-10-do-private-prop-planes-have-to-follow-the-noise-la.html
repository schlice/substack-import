--- title: '"Do private prop planes have to follow the noise law by not flying at night?"' date: '2016-03-10' tags: - law-enforcement - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} No, aircraft noise, first of all, is controlled by the FAA, not the local police. Aircraft must fly at a minimum above 500 feet AGL, so in general, noise shouldn\'t be a terrible concern. But if it is, try to get the tail number of the aircraft and contact your local FAA office. They can check to ensure that the aircraft meets noise regulations.
