**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I have never heard of antidepressants killing neurons. SSRIs, or "Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitors" work by preventing serotonin reabsorption into presynaptic cells, presumably making more serotonin available for postsynaptic receptors to bind to. Killing the cells which need to bind to serotonin would be contraindicated to help depression.
