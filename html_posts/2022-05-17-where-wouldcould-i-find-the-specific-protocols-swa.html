**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} There is no central repository for that information. In some cases, states set those requirements for their law enforcement agencies, and in others, there is no specific state guidance and it is left to the agency to determine. So to answer your question, your best bet is to make the request to the law enforcement agency in question. You may be able to make a Freedom of Information Act request for their explosives handling protocols. The department may be able to avoid your request under one of the exemptions to FOIA, namely Exemption 2: Information related solely to the internal personnel rules and practices of an agency. But you can at least try. \ Original question: Where would/could I find the specific protocols SWAT teams use when handling explosives? For example, safety equipment used, distance before detonation, ensuring civilians are out of range.
