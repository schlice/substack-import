--- title: '"My dad threatens to kick me out of his house at 22 in the UK. What legal actions can I take?"' date: '2015-07-10' tags: - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [At 22, you are a legal adult in the UK.\ \ Your dad is under no obligation to permit you to continue to live at his house once you have reached the age of majority, which is 18 in the UK.\ \ It\'s time to either move out, or sign a lease agreement with your dad whereby you pay him rent to continue to live there. At that point, you have some legal rights, but without a lease agreement, you have none. You live there at the pleasure of the homeowner, who can evict you at any time.]{.rendered_qtext}
