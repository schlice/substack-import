Any time you ask yourself a "Can God..." question, the answer is ***yes, God can***. So can Jesus fulfill a prophecy through someone today using the Holy Spirit? Yes, God can. But whether or not He *will* is dependent on Him and the prophecy. Most of the remaining prophecies will be fulfilled at the end of the age.
