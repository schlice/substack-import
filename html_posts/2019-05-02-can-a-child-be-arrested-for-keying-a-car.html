**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can a child be arrested for keying a car?** While destruction of property is, in most places, a misdemeanor (which means you can indeed go to jail for it, for up to a year), most adult laws do not directly apply to children. Children are adjudicated (when the are) by the **juvenile justice system**. The system is goaled towards rehabiliting youthful offenders. Parents and children can face a variety of consequences, such as mandatory counseling, probation, community service, youth court, and in the most severe of cases, youth incarceration.
