**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The laws are different in every state. In Colorado, if you're on a two-lane road and you're approaching a vehicle, you have to turn your high beams off within 500 feet of the approaching auto. If you're following someone, you have to turn off your high beams when you reach 200 feet behind that person.
