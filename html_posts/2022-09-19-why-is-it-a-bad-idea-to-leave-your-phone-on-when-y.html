Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It's not. At worst, it may cause your phone to charge slower because it is trying to use energy while it is also charging the battery. But the difference is so slight as to be mostly negligible. And if it's overnight, what difference does it make? Otherwise, there is no harm to the phone by leaving it on while it charges, overnight or otherwise.
