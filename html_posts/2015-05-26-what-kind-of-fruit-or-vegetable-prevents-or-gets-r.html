--- title: '"What kind of fruit (or vegetable) prevents or gets rid of diabetes?"' date: '2015-05-26' tags: - veteran-benefits layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [There is no fruit or vegetable that prevents or gets rid of diabetes. People with diabetes benefit from the same healthy diet as everyone else.\ \ Fruits and vegetables are healthy for you, but they also contain carbohydrates. Their carb load (and incidental sugar load) have to be included in meal planning.]{.rendered_qtext}
