**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **In an emergency, when an ambulance is necessary, are the victims billed by the emergency service?** In the United States, ambulance service is considered a *medical* function, not a state governmental function. Therefore, ambulance services are billed to the patient's medical insurance.
