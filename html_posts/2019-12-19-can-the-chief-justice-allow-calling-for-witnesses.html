**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can the Chief Justice allow calling for witnesses by the Democrats in the Senate trial if President Trump is impeached by the House?** Procedurally, the Chief Justice is not part of that decision. That decision will be made by the Senate, as the Constitution permits the Senate to determine how it will conduct the trial. This means that the majority will determine the rules, much like the majority in the House determined the rules for the impeachment investigation. So practically, it's up to McConnell and the Republicans whether or not witnesses will be called, how many, and for how long the debate will continue before any votes are cast.
