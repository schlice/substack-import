**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Our HOA has a rule that says that informational/celebratory/seasonal signs can be up for 7 days without approval, longer if you seek approval. We also have a rule that says that you can't have signage in your windows facing the street without approval. There is nothing unconstitutional about an HOA enacting rules in accordance with their Governing Documents which they can use to control how the neighborhood looks. You voluntarily entered that agreement when you bought a house in an HOA-controlled neighborhood. And that's where you are being confused. You voluntarily allowed the HOA to control how your house looks from the outside when you bought a house in a mandatory HOA neighborhood. We have a house right now that has a MAGA-leaning sign in their window, and we're fighting against that one, too.
