**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} A2A You know the old and overused adage, **guns don't kill people, people kill people**? There is some truth to that. As most other answerers have pointed out, many others have killed many more people without guns. On 9/11, fewer than 20 people killed over 2990 people and injured more than 6000 more. Not a single gun was used. Getting control of guns won't stop people from killing people. Getting rid of guns won't stop people from getting them. Instead of talking about **gun control,** let's start talking about **people control.**
