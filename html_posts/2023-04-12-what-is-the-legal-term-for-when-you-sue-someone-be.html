--- title: '"What is the legal term for when you sue someone because their product was defective and caused an accident/injury to yourself or others?"' date: '2023-04-12' tags: - law layout: post ---

**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The legal term is a **product liability claim.** A consumer good that is deemed defective gives rise to a claim for damages when it results in harm or illness.
