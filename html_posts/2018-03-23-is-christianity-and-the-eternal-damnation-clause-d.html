**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Is Christianity and the eternal damnation clause designed more with agnostics in mind in order to keep them hedging their bets with how they should think and behave?** The "eternal damnation" clause is for everyone, not just agnostics. It's for everyone. Because it applies to everyone. John 3:16: > *For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that > whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life.* Notice it says "whoever believes in him." That literally applies to everyone. And then it says that whoever believes in Him "shall not perish." This means that some will perish; clearly, the ones who do not believe in Him. Eternal damnation. It's isn't just for agnostics anymore.
