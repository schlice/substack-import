**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If I live in the US can I be prosecuted for answers describing illegal activities?** You cannot be prosecuted for describing how to commit a crime (it's monumentally stupid, but it's not illegal). You *can* be prosecuted for admitting that you gained your knowledge of how to commit a crime *by actually committing a crime*. It doesn't matter where or when you admit to a crime, be it an online forum or in a bar. If you admit to committing a crime, you can be investigated and prosecuted based on your own un-coerced admission.
