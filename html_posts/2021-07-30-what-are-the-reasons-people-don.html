**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} There are a variety of reasons someone would choose to not be vaccinated. There are religious objections to vaccines. There are health reasons which might make vaccinations dangerous to the person taking it. And of course, there are those people who just don't trust the government. In addition, this vaccine is not approved by the FDA. It has not gone through rigorous testing, and we don't know if there are any long-term effects from taking it. Ultimately, objections to vaccination are no different than any other vaccine ever. However, my understanding of **freedom** includes a belief that society should allow adults to make consequential decisions about their lives, which includes the decision to forgo a vaccine which might preserve their own life. This decision should have no impact on your life. If you're vaccinated, don't worry, you won't get COVID, despite the fear mongering of the legacy media and the public health establishment. Breakthrough infections are exceedingly rare for the vast majority of us, and if they do happen, they would be mild anyway.
