Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} If you're asking whether or not the US government will ever change gun laws in such a way that guns will effectively be unavailable to the public, the answer is no. The reason is because ***they can't***. The Second Amendment prevents them from banning guns. In order to ban guns, they must first repeal the Second Amendment.
