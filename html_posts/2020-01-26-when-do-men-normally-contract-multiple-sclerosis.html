**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} [[Spruce Cox](https://www.quora.com/profile/Spruce-Cox)]{.qlink_container} is correct, between 20 and 50, although while uncommon, much younger (pediatric MS) and much older people (late-onset MS) can still develop the disease. It is much less likely before age 20 (12%) and above age 50 (7%).
