**Content:** [First, you have to expound a little.\ \ What kind of door are you looking to control? Car door? House door?\ \ Second, there aren\'t many controllers that open and close a door. Some more modern cars have power doors and power liftgates that will open and close on their own, but generally you\'ll only find that on the liftgate, or the side sliders on a minivan. Driver/passenger doors are manual.\ \ So first, you need to identify what you\'re trying to open/close/lock/unlock.\ Second, those functions need to have a powered method by which they can peform those functions.\ Finally, you can wire in your Arduino into the control lines for those devices.\ \ I suggest Raspberry Pi. [[Here\'s a thread](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=a45ac1&url=https://www.raspberrypi.org/forums/viewtopic.php?t%3D96035%26p%3D669557){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="raspberrypi.org"}]{.qlink_container} to get you started.]{.rendered_qtext}
