**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How do I get my stolen vehicle back if it is not registered under my name? The car was stolen with the bill of sale and pink slip. Everything was in the car.** Hire an attorney. But [[Dennis](https://www.quora.com/profile/Dennis-945)]{.qlink_container} is correct, you have a tough row to hoe here. But you can make it work if you can contact the previous owner and convince them to testify that they sold the car to you. Receipts or bank statements showing the transaction taking place and the money changing hands between you and the previous owner would probably be enough to establish that you actually did purchase the car when you said you did, and that the current bill of sale and title were obtained fraudulently. This is not impossible, but it is not easy. Good luck. And for anyone else reading this, fair warning: **never store the title to the car in the car itself.** Anyone with that title "owns" the car, as they can pretty quickly generate a fake bill of sale showing you "sold" the vehicle to them. They can then register the vehicle and all is well.
