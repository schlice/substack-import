**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Is there any data on people who are admitted to US service academies but never graduate?** Each individual service academy keeps that information themselves, and it is sorted by class year. You would have to approach each service academy separately to retrieve this information. As far as I know, there is no global repository for this data from all service academies. The number of people who are admitted and leave before graduation is sorted based on reason for leaving. Some leave because they become medically disqualified for military service while they are there. Some withdraw by choice. Some are ejected due to some policy or rule violation. Some academically fail out. You'll note, however, that those who do not meet athletic standards can still graduate and receive their Bachelor's degree, but cannot be commissioned as officers. They are required to repay their service commitment in the enlisted ranks. I had at least one classmate who fell into this category.
