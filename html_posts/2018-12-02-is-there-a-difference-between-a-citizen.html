**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Is there a difference between a citizen\'s arrest and detaining someone for the police?** Yes, there is a big difference. One is a citizens arrest, and the other is unlawful detention. **Can you detain a most wanted fugitive and hold them for the police?** **No.** Citizens arrest is a very specific function which varies by state. Almost universally though, you can only effect it when someone perpetrates a crime in your presence. Detaining anyone without special provision to do so in the law is a crime.
