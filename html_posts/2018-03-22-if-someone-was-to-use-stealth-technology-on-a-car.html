**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If someone was to use stealth technology on a car, would they be able to outsmart the radar speed check?** Mythbusters tested this. They found that radar absorbent material (RAM) paint, which is very dense, would make the car far too heavy to be practical, and is ridiculously expensive. [[They tested a lot of other ways to beat radar](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=f4f5eb&url=https://mythresults.com/episode18){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="mythresults.com"}]{.qlink_container} as well. They also didn't work.
