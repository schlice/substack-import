--- title: '"Why do people think that having armed guards for school is crazy?"' date: '2017-02-25' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Because people believe that somehow, having the mere presence of guns in school will harm their children. Not that school security guards would ever harm children with those guns, or use them inappropriately. Simply the mere presence of the gun in the school imposes some level of psychological harm on their children. They believe the school is sacrosanct. They believe that no violence should ever occur in a school, and the answer (to them) to school violence is not armed guards. Not that they've come up with a better answer, mind you. Just that armed guards *can't be* the best way to handle school violence. It's true that many people and places are protected by armed guards. Many more people self-protect, more people than you would probably guess. But somehow, the school is different, because, well, it's a *school*. There just shouldn't be guns in a school.
