**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can one set term for the board members of the HOA in the United States?** The Governing Documents of the HOA set the terms for the Board. Usually, Board members are elected for a specified term and can then be re-elected. It is the Bylaws which specifies how the business of the HOA will be run.
