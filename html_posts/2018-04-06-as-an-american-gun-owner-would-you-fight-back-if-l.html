**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **As an American gun owner, would you fight back if law enforcement came to confiscate your firearms?** Like, try to kill them? Who exactly do you think I am? I certainly would not "fight back" in that way. I would allow law enforcement to search my house from top to bottom, assuming they had followed all the requisite laws in order to do so (assuming the 4th Amendment was still on the books, I'd want to see a search warrant). I'd show them my two empty gun safes and explain the tragic boating accident that caused all of my guns to fall to the bottom of the reservoir. I'd show them the gas receipt from driving to and from the lake. Tragic, sad tale.
