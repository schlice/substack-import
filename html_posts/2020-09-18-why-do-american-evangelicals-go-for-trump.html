**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Why do American evangelicals go for Trump?** Oh, that's easy, which is why I can speak for Evangelicals even though I am not one. We live under a president who has more aggressively defended religious freedom than any US president in modern history. And we want to keep that. President Trump's record on defending religious freedom in America is impressive, and it is growing. There is no sign that it is a shrinking fad. We can expect a White House occupied by Joe Biden and Kamala Harris to not just drop the Trump administration\'s defense of religious freedom, but reverse course to start working aggressively against it. History is our teacher. Joe Biden worked in an Obama White House which did just this. His administration *aggressively* argued to the Supreme Court that religious institutions should not have the freedom to determine who teaches their faith. Thankfully, they were rejected by the court, in a stunning 9--0 vote. But that didn't stop the administration from attempting to tear down the First Amendment freedom of expressive association, the doctrine of church autonomy, and the ministerial exception. The latter was decided in the case of *Our Lady of Guadalupe School v. Morrissey-Berru* just a few months prior. In that case, the court held that the "ministerial exception" protects the right of religious institutions to choose who teaches and passes down religious doctrine when the teachers perform religious functions at the school. When the Obama administration argued against that doctrine again in [[*Hosanna Tabor Evangelical Lutheran Church & School v.
