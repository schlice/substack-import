**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The most common use of the ICMP protocol is in the "ping" utility. I wrote it in little letters, but it is actually an acronym. It stands for "Packetized Inter-Networking Groper." it is a network testing utility, verifying low level physical connectivity not reliant on upper level software.
