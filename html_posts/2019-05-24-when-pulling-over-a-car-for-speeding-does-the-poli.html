**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **When pulling over a car for speeding, does the police car automatically take a picture of the license plate of the car? A police officer said that his car did this after pulling me over. Was he lying?** First, police cars have dash cams that record everything out the front. So yes, in a way, they always do. Some police cars are equipped with [[Automated License Plate Recognition](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=cf92ed&url=https://www.eff.org/pages/automated-license-plate-readers-alpr){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="eff.org"}]{.qlink_container} (ALPR) systems, although this is less frequent.
