**Content:** [If you are not experiencing any issues, your lesions are called \"silent\" and the majority of lesions you experience will be this way.\ \ The report doesn\'t suggest a good or bad prognosis. But if your lesions with \"significant resolution\" appear with contrast dye, then the lesions are active. This may be a sign that your medication is not working for you, depending on how long you have been taking it.\ \ But that determination (whether or not you are making good prognosis) should be made between you and your neurologist.]{.rendered_qtext}
