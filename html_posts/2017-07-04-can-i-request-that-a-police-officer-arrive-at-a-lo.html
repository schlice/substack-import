--- title: "Can I request that a police officer arrive at a location at a specific time?" date: "2017-07-04" tags: ["law-enforcement"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You can call and ask. They will *try* to accommodate your request, but the needs of higher-priority calls will always take precedence. If you need to have police at a specific location at a specific time for a specific period of time, you can hire them. Please try to do this as far in advance as you can, so that they can schedule resources.
