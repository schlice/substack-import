**Content:** [If I may be so bold as to suggest some reading, I recently read a book called *Daring Greatly* by [[Brené Brown](https://www.quora.com/topic/Bren%C3%A9-Brown)]{.qlink_container}. The sub-title is \"How the courage to be vulnerable transforms the way we live, love, parent, and lead.\" Brené is a \"shame researcher\" and has a great TEDx talk that went viral on YouTube.\ \ In essence, she talks about how our personal shame holds us back, and having courage by allowing ourselves to be vulnerable is a more authentic way to live your life. I highly recommend her book to you. If you want the Cliff Notes version, look her up on YouTube.]{.rendered_qtext}
