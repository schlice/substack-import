**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You are allowed to ask. The officer is not required to tell you. He is only required to tell the judge. If it is safe and if he feels like taking the time to explain the law to you, he can do so, but it is entirely up to him. He is under no legal obligation to explain it to you.
