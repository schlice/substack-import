{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What happens if a president resigns? Does the VP immediately become president?** Yes, the 25th Amendment says: > *In case of the removal of the President from office or of his death* > ***or resignation****, the Vice President shall become President.* Therefore, as soon as the President resigns, the Vice President becomes Acting President. Once the Acting President is sworn in, he becomes President.
