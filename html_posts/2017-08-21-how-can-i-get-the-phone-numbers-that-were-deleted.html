Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} If you are the account holder, your mobile phone carrier has the log of received/sent calls. If you have online account access, chances are you can simply log in to your carrier's portal and find the call log. But even if they don't display it on the screen, a quick call or chat with Customer Service can usually net you the call logs you're looking for sent to your email.
