**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If Trump dies and VP Pence becomes President, will Nancy Pelosi become VP?** Excellent question. The answer is no. If Trump dies and Pence becomes President, he gets to ***appoint*** his Vice President. The two will serve out the remainder of Trump's term. The Presidential Succession Act only applies to the Office of the President.
