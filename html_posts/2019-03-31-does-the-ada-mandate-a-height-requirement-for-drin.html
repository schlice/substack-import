**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Does the ADA mandate a height requirement for drinking fountains so they\'re more accessible to people who use canes?** Generally, no. ADA generally requires two heights: standing height, and wheelchair height with room to roll up to it. There are generally no specific requirements for people with canes.
