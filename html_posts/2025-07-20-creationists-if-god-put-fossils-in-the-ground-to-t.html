**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Why would you think that God put fossils in the ground? Young Earth Creationists generally believe that the fossil record was primarily formed by the global catastrophic Flood of Noah, rather than God creating fossils directly in the ground as part of a long geological process. They interpret the sudden appearance of fossils and their distribution as evidence for a rapid, catastrophic burial event consistent with Noah\'s Flood, which they believe occurred thousands of years ago.
