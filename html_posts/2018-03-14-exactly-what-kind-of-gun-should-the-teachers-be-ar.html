--- title: '"Exactly what kind of gun should the teachers be armed with?"' date: '2018-03-14' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Exactly what kind of gun should the teachers be armed with?** Teachers should be armed with exactly the kind of gun they feel comfortable carrying and concealing, and exactly the gun they have trained with. Teachers (or anyone, for that matter) should only conceal a handgun that they feel comfortable firing, drawing from concealment, and placing shots reliably at a pre-determined distance. This is almost the wrong question to ask, because it presumes that "we" are "arming teachers." We are not so much arming teachers as we are "not preventing teachers from arming themselves." This means that the onus is on the teacher to meet all the requirements placed on them to get concealed carry permits; the onus is not on us to provide them with arms, or even dictate what is appropriate.
