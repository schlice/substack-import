**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What is the full extent of the president\'s authority when executive emergency powers are in place?** It depends. [[A prior Senate investigation](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=9092ad&url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Senate_Report_93-549){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="wikipedia.org"}]{.qlink_container} had found 470 provisions of federal law that a President might invoke via a declaration of emergency. Each one of those provisions of law could activate one of 136 distinct statutory emergency powers which were delegated to the President. They don't all become available at one time. You declare a provision of Federal law that you are invoking, which, along with it, comes a distinct statutory emergency power.
