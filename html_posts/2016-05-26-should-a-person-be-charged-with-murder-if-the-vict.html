--- title: '"Should a person be charged with murder if the victim is resuscitated?"' date: '2016-05-26' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You cannot be charged with murder if the victim is alive. It's as simple as that. Murder is the unlawful taking of a life, and depending on the severity, with forethought and malice. If there is no taking of a life, there is no murder.
