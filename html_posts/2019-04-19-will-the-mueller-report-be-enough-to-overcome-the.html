**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Will the Mueller Report be enough to overcome the elephant in the room AKA \"The good of the country\" and finally flush Trump out of office?** Most certainly not. Even if the House Democrats look at Mueller's report and determine that there is enough evidence in the "emanations and penumbras" (because it's not in the written text) that Trump attempted to obstruct justice, and they decide to impeach him over it, the Senate will never vote to remove him from office. Based on how Democrats in Congress are handling Mueller's findings, that looks like it will be in 2024.
