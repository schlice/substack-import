**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What can I do if I was pulled over and got a citation for something I didn\'t do? I was driving normally when suddenly a cop pulled me over and told me I didn\'t stop at a stop sign. However, there was no stop sign at all.** Don't pay the ticket. Instead, go to court and argue that there was no stop sign. Your ticket should have a court date, so go argue your case to the judge. Show photos of the road proving there was no sign. Make the state prove you ran a sign and make them show you where it was and that it wasn't obscured.
