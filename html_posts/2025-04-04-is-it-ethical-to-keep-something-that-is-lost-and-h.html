--- title: "Is it ethical to keep something that is lost and has not been claimed?" date: "2025-04-04" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} So long as you have exhausted all reasonable efforts to get the item back to its owner, then it's not unethical to keep it. Only you can know if you exhausted all *reasonable* efforts to get the item back to its owner. Therefore, it is only you who can determine if keeping the item is ethical.
