**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What are NORAD\'s procedures for planes with a 7600 code or radio failure?** NORAD defers to the Regional ATC Centers for actual ATC matters. NORAD is only engaged when an aircraft without a valid flight plan crosses the ADIZ, or when an aircraft veers off of its assigned flight plan and is unresponsive to ATC communications.
