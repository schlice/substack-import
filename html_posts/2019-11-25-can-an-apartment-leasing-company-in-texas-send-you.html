Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can an apartment leasing company in Texas send you a text message to advise you of an eviction?** Usually, while the text message may be a convenience, it is not considered legal notification of eviction. I'm unaware of any jurisdiction where text message is the only form of notification required to process an eviction. The eviction notice will be attached to the door of the unit, such that you cannot enter the unit without seeing the notice. It will be attached in such a way that you will have to remove it to enter the unit.
