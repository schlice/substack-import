**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What are the consequences to a U.S. president that commits perjury under oath during an impeachment trial?** If he actually lies under oath *during his impeachment trial*, the case for impeachment gets that much stronger. I would expect that the president would be removed from office. That's the consequence.
