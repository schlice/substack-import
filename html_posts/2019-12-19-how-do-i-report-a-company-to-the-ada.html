**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How do I report a company to the ADA?** Who is the ADA? The American Dental Association? The American Diabetes Association? Are you perhaps referring to the Americans with Disabilities Act, which is a law, not a government organization? If you want to take action against a company for violating the Americans with Disabilities Act, speak to an employment attorney in your area who specializes in the ADA.
