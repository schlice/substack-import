--- title: '"If you accidentally call 911 (emergency services) should you stay on the line to tell the operator it was accidental or should you hang up immediately?"' date: '2019-06-16' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If you accidentally call 911 will the police definitely come to your house even if you call and tell them its an accident?** No, but the key to whether or not they will come out to your house is whether or not you hang up on them without telling them about your accident. Short answer: if you accidentally call 911, ***stay on the phone!*** Tell the 911 operator that you accidentally dialed. Answer all of their questions, and *wait for them to tell you it's okay to hang up.* If you do this, the police will not be dispatched. If you accidentally call 911 and then hang up, there's a chance that something could be wrong where you are, and they may very well send a unit out to confirm you're okay.
