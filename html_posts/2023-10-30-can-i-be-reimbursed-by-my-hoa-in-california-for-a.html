**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Certainly! But I think you may be unclear on the method by which you obtain your "reimbursement." Yeah, that's right, lawsuit. Retain an attorney and "request" that they refund the money you sunk into removing the cover that they approved, and when they don't pay (they won't), file a lawsuit. In other words, there's no statutory way to be "reimbursed." If you were monetarily damaged by another's incompetence, the remedy is a lawsuit.
