**Content:** [(Answer is for the US only)\ \ Control of national airspace is a right which is held by the Federal government, as it is their responsibility to manage Federal airspace. The FAA controls all airspace within the borders of the US. This right cannot be delegated to the states or to the individuals.]{.rendered_qtext}
