**Content:** [Look closely at what the product claims to do:\ ]{.rendered_qtext} - BB cream instantly covers spots, blemishes, dark circles and dullness to give you a fabulous make-up finish. - It is enriched with Multi-Vitamins that work from within\* to give you treatment like fairness#. - Its SPF 15, PA++ protects your fairness in the sun, and its matte texture leaves your face feeling non-oily, fresh and bright through the day. Then check the fine print:\ \ \ > Make-up finish/look via coverage. Results not equal to face make-up > categories like the eye, lip, blush etc.\ > \ > \*within epidermin.\ > \ > #Refers to treatment in the cosmetic domain.\ > \ > Results not equal to clinical/invasive skin treatment. Use daily for > best results.\ \ There is nothing there that says you will get fair. There is nothing there that says you will retain fairness. It says you will get \"treatment-like fairness\" and they are talking about cosmetic treatment. So translated, that means it\'s makeup, and not much else.\ \ Their products never claim to make your skin lighter.\ \ So it\'s not likely they can be sued for false advertising, since their products say exactly what they will do and what they won\'t (results not equal to clinical/invasive skin treatment).
