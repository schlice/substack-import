**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If someone made a bait package that, by design, exploded and killed a package thief, how would such a case play out in court? Would the package designer be guilty of murder?** Yes. Booby traps are illegal for just this reason. First of all, it is not the bait package designer's place to determine that a thief deserves the death penalty when a court would never prescribe such a remedy. Secondly, booby traps are *indiscriminate*. They can harm good guys in just the same way that they can harm bad guys. What if the thief's kid happened upon the stolen package and triggered it? The only way to make a real bait package is to ***over-engineer*** it.
