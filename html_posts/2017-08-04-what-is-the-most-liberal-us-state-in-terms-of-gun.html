**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} > *What is the most liberal US state in terms of gun laws (people are > most free to own a gun)?* To answer your specific question, no state can restrict your ability to ***own*** a gun. I may not be able to legally *carry* that gun, or even have it in my *home*, but no one can prevent me from *buying* a gun, so long as I meet the requirements for doing so. I may not be allowed to carry it out of the store, but I can still buy it. Now, which state is the most free when it comes to *possessing* a gun that I legally purchased? **Arizona**. They are a Constitutional Carry state (also called "permitless carry"), meaning that if you can own it, you can carry it, anywhere, open or concealed. They have the best laws when it comes to transfer of guns, they allow anything that the Federal government doesn't ban, and they have a very strong gun culture.
