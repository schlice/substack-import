**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Now that a federal judge has sided with CNN and ordered the White House to reinstate Jim Acosta\'s press pass, how will President Trump retaliate?** First of all, it's a temporary restraining order. The case hasn't been heard yet. Second, it's a restraining order on ***5th Amendment*** grounds, not 1st Amendment grounds. Basically, the judge said that Acosta has a 5th Amendment right to to due process when his hard pass is to be revoked, and the White House didn't give it to him, so for now, he can have his hard pass back. Finally, how will Trump respond? Hopefully it's simply by not calling on Acosta in the press room until after his actual hearing.
