**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I don't understand this "supposedly know nothing" statement you've made. Are you saying that before Jesus came, no one had any knowledge of God? Because the Bible doesn't agree. > For when Gentiles who do not have the Law do instinctively the things > of the Law, these, not having the Law, are a law to themselves, in > that they show the work of the Law written in their hearts, their > conscience bearing witness and their thoughts alternately accusing or > else defending them --- Romans 2:14--15 NASB1995 This passage talks about the fact that some people, even though they have never heard the Gospel, still live as if they have. This is backed up by studies which show that young children know right from wrong without ever having been taught it. We have an innate sense of fairness and justice and morality. That's not by accident; God wrote it on our hearts.
