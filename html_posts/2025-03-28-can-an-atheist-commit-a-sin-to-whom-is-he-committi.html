Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Sin is, literally, "missing the mark." But if you don't pattern your life after trying to hit God's marks, then you cannot, definitionally, sin. To be more clear, the atheist can commit acts that the theist would consider sinful. The atheist cannot commit acts that the atheist would consider sinful.
