**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Some mothers do hold funerals for their stillborn children, or children they lose in pregnancy due to miscarriage or other causes. They remember them either on the day they were supposed to be born, or on the day they were stillborn. One of my friends lost a child due to miscarriage and she says she now has an "angel baby" and she remembers her angel baby all the time. It's a very painful thing to have to go through. The census, on the other hand, is a count of living people, not a count of all people who have ever existed.
