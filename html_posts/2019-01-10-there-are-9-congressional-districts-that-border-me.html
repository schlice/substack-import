{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **There are 9 congressional districts that border Mexico. None of the congresspeople in these districts support the wall. Why do the politicians in DC ignore the wishes of the people on the ground?** Because border security is a **national** issue, not a state one. States are barred from contributing to national defense. So the will of the people on the ground is not relevant when it comes to border security.
