**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Why is it illegal that President Donald Trump\'s campaign has spent nearly \$100,000 of donor money to pay legal bills to the firm representing Jared Kushner?** It's not. The article sure does make it seem like it is though, doesn't it? But nowhere in the article can you find what law may have been broken. There's a reason for that. Because there is none. Kushner is named as a defendant in a lawsuit brought by the DNC regarding his work on the campaign. The campaign is covering his legal expenses in responding to that lawsuit. Simple.
