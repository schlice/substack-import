**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Um, go to the store? The morning after pill (or Plan-B) prevents the fertilized egg from implanting in the womb, so an abortion technically never happens. Our grocery store sells them. You can pick them up right at the Customer Service counter, and this is in Florida, where abortion is illegal after 15 weeks. Even in states which have completely banned all abortions, you should still be able to buy Plan-B pills, because it's technically not an abortion. \ Original question: How can you get morning after pills now that Roe v.
