**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Why do religious people (of all faiths), lie about what their scripture says, when the text is outdated (i.e. when a follower of an Abrahamic faith, Islam or Christianity for example) says that a murder, or rape is always forbidden?** I get where you're coming from. But what you have to understand is that there are different Greek words for "killing" and "murder." In Christianity and in the Christian Bible, rape is not discussed as a crime. Adultery is considered a crime, as well as murder. So while killing is sometimes allowed (and sometimes prescribed), murder is not.
