**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What do you need to have legal rights over your own painting or instrumental?** (US answer only) Technically, you need *nothing*. Your original works are protected from the moment you create them and record them in a place where others can perceive them. However, in order for that protection to be *recognized*, you must file for a copyright. A fully-filed copyright is needed to initiate legal actions against those who might try to duplicate your original works. Take a look at the [[U.S. Copyright Office](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=49661f&url=https://www.copyright.gov/index.html){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="copyright.gov"}]{.qlink_container} to find out how to register a copyright.
