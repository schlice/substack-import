**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It is well-known that several of the recent high school and college shooters idolized and tried to emulate the Columbine shooters Dylan Klebold and Eric Harris. Their memoirs, Facebook posts and other social media indicated their admiration for the pair\'s bravery and their desire to go out just as big. Indeed, limiting \"air time\" for the shooters, large-scale demonization of actual shooters, and reduction of other mass media coverage of shootings, could go a long way to showing these shooters that their \"15 minutes of fame\" will actually be closer to \"15 seconds.\"
