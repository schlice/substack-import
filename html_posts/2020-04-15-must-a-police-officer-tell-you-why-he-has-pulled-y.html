Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Are police required to tell you why they pull you over?** In most states, there is no legal requirement for the police to tell you why they stopped you. Their legal requirement is to tell a **judge** why they stopped you. They tell you out of courtesy. Legally, they are only required to ensure you are a licensed driver and that your registration is valid and that your insurance is up to date (requirements for those things vary by state).
