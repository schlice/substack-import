--- title: "What are possible treatments and cures for MS illness?" date: "2017-07-11" tags: ["health-autoimmune"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} There are treatments available, but they do not treat the actual disease itself. The treatments available today lessen the severity of relapses, reduce the duration of relapses, and increase the amount of time between relapses. The treatments also slow the progress of the disease. All of the approved MS medications do these things, with varying degrees of success for each person, as well as various routes of administration (SC injection, IM injection, infusion, or oral pill). The treatments still do not treat the actual **cause** of the disease, because it is still unknown.
