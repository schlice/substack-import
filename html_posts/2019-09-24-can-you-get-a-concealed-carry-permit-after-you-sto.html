**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can you get a concealed carry permit after you stop working in the marijuana industry in Colorado?** The answer, as with all legal answers, is "it depends." The marijuana industry is controlled at the state and local level, while concealed carry permits are issued by a county sheriff in Colorado. In most cases, persons working in the marijuana industry are not barred from concealed carry. However, some county concealed carry applications ask if you "unlawfully use or are addicted to a controlled substance that\'s illegal at the federal level." If you answer honestly and say that you also use as well as being employed in the industry, it is possible that a sheriff *may* deny your application, since marijuana is still illegal at the federal level. So depending on how you answer the application questionnaire, you *may* be denied a permit if you use marijuana along with working in the industry. But of course, this is always at the discretion of the county sheriff in the county in which you are applying.
