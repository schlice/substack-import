Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I think the 18-wheeler is infinitely more difficult than "flying" a Cessna. I think "landing" a Cessna is more difficult than driving an 18-wheeler. So in order of difficulty, based on how much instruction one would need to accomplish each task: - Flying a Cessna straight and level - Performing a turn or a climb or descent in a Cessna. - Driving an 18-wheeler, meaning starting, stopping, and making it move, including a gear change or two. - Landing a Cessna.
