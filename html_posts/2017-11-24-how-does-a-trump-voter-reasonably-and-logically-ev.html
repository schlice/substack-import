Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How does a Trump voter reasonably and logically evidence that they made the right choice?** How does *any* voter reasonably and logically evidence that they made the right choice? - Voter has criteria he uses when making a choice - Voter makes a choice - Voter watches to see if criteria were met. So whether or not a Trump voter knows they made the "right" choice is whether or not the criteria they had in place when they made that choice are being fulfilled. Note that Trump is almost one year into his term; he still has three more years to accomplish the things that might be present on any one voter's punch card.
