Answer










Content: [She would be tried for murder of the two innocents. She
would likely be found not guilty by reason of duress, where a person
performs an action as a result of violence or threat of harm or injury.
Duress is defined as any unlawful threat or coercion which causes a
person to perform an act which they would normally not perform.\
\
The case of the murder of the two innocents should have no appreciable
effect on her previous charge.]
