--- title: "Can I block a website on my phone and still get it on my computer?" date: "2018-08-01" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Depends on how you block. If you\'re using the same browser platform on both devices, and your blocking plug-in is the same, there is a fair chance that you can block on one platform and see the block on the other. However, it\'s not always the case. In fact, usually browsers are different between mobile and desktop, so it\'s less likely that you will see the same blocks on mobile that you see on desktop.
