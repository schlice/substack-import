--- title: '"If you name someone else as the driver for a red light camera, does that person get a ticket?"' date: '2016-08-11' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Short answer: no. Red light tickets are issued to the registered owner of the vehicle. That owner is responsible for the actions of drivers he allows to operate his vehicle. The owner is required to pay the ticket. The owner can then attempt to extract payment from the driver if he chooses, but red light camera violations are paid by the registered owner of the vehicle.
