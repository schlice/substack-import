As you postulated, shouldn't those be reversed? Why is it that blacks are killed nearly twice as often by police as whites? In order to answer that question, we need to answer this question: With only 13% of the population, why do blacks commit over half of all of the violent crimes in this country? It appears to me, then, that **blacks are shot more frequently because they are more frequently involved in criminal activity.**
