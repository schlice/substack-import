**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Where do non-Christians get the basis for determining what is evil and monstrous? What moral basis is the non-Christian using to determine that God is somehow evil? As to murder, murder is the unjustified killing of a person. Except every time God killed someone (or ordered His people to do it), He already judged them. So is it murder when we kill a Death Row convict? No, because the killing is justified. Same with God.
