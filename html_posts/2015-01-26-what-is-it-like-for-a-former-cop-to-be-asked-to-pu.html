**Content:** [I was in a situation similar to [[I.L.Mitchell](https://www.quora.com/profile/I-L-Mitchell)]{.qlink_container}\'s story: I was testing a radar detector with my dad (who was still active, so I\'m not actually answering the question, sorry about that) on a stretch of road that should have been, by all rights, completely deserted at that time of year. It was November, and we were on Ocean Parkway, just off of Jones and Tobay Beach. Those are the barrier beaches on the south side of Long Island. The beaches were completely deserted on that day, since it was cold and misty. We were testing a radar detector as well as a new car I just got. Yes, it was a sports car. Needless to say, we were exceeding the speed limit (by a lot). We discovered that the car can go really fast and you\'d hardly feel it. We also discovered (the hard way) that the radar detector (which my dad bought second-hand) didn\'t work at all. The officer brought his handheld RADAR gun out of the car and blasted it directly at the detector, and it didn\'t wink at all. Completely worthless.\ \ The speed was way scarier than being pulled over.]{.rendered_qtext}
