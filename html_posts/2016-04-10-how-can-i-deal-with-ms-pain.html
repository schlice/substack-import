**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} [[Michael Soso](https://www.quora.com/profile/Michael-Soso)]{.qlink_container} is right on the money. Depending on the type of pain you are experiencing, there are a variety of medications that are viable to manage neurological pain. [[This page](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=122213&url=http://www.nationalmssociety.org/Symptoms-Diagnosis/MS-Symptoms/Pain){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="nationalmssociety.org"}]{.qlink_container} over at the National MS Society discusses the various types of pain that people with MS may experience and the types of medications that have been found to be useful treating that pain.
