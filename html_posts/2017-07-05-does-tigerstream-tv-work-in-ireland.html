Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} TigerStream is nothing more than an Android device running [[Kodi](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=bd903c&url=https://kodi.tv/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="kodi.tv"}]{.qlink_container}. If Kodi works in Ireland, then TigerStream will work in Ireland. Kodi is just a platform for plugging in streaming software. Some streams will be available in Ireland, some will not. There are lots of streams available, so there's bound to be at least one that is available in Ireland.
