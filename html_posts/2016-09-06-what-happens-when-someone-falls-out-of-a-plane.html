Answer

**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It's not the fall that kills you. It's the rapid deceleration at the conclusion of the fall that tends to be problematic. It's a fairly safe bet that you're going to die. Although it is possible to fall from that height and live, it's highly unlikely.
