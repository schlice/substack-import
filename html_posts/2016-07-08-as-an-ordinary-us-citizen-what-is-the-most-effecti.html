--- title: "As an ordinary U.S. citizen, what is the most effective thing I can do to contribute to gun control and gun safety in this country?" date: "2016-07-08" tags: ["law"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Education. This is the most important thing you can do. If you don't own a gun, go down to your local gun range and take an "introduction to firearms" course. Almost every gun range I know offers one. It's inexpensive and gives you a comprehensive introduction to what firearms are, what they do, and what they feel like when you fire them. In this way, you too can separate the facts from the hype when it comes to what you're told. If you do own a gun, store it safely. There are numerous gun safes which, using technology, allow you almost instantaneous access to your gun while keeping it out of the hands of bad guys or curious visitors to your home. Once you're educated, teach others about gun safety. Teach them that law-abiding gun owners are not the enemy. Law-abiding gun owners are the safest group in this country, as they tend to also follow other laws just as carefully.
