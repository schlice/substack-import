Android Wear allows for video to be shown on a watch. Apps like Glide on WatchOS or the native Messaging app on Android Wear allow you to record voice messages and send them as text messages. But since our watches don't yet have front facing cameras on them, having a two-way video call through the watch isn't quite a thing yet. If enough of the market wants to use their watch as a video camera for a FaceTime call, Apple will start including small front-facing cameras in their watches. I'm just not sure there's a huge demand for it yet.
