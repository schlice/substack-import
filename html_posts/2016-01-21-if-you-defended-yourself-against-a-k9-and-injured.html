--- title: '"If you defended yourself against a K9 and injured (or killed) the dog, what would be the legal repercussions?"' date: '2016-01-21' tags: - law-enforcement - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} In almost every jurisdiction that I am aware, police K9s are considered sworn peace officers, despite the fact that they could not raise their right hand or salute when their badge was pinned on. If you injure a police K9, you would be charged with aggravated assault against a peace officer, and if you killed the K9, you would also be charged with destruction of government property (while states may consider them peace officers, they are still property, not people).
