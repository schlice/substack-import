**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Is there any acupuncture kind of treatment for multiple sclerosis?** Multiple sclerosis has many symptoms, and they are different for each person. While acupuncture is not useful to treat MS itself (the demyelinating disease), acupuncture can be useful to treat some of its symptoms. When used in conjunction with traditional therapies, complementary and alternative medicine can be useful to improve quality of life and to alleviate symptoms.
