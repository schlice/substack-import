--- title: "How are Schwann cells affected in multiple sclerosis patients? How is this condition treated?" date: "2016-06-02" tags: ["health-autoimmune"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Schwann cells produce myelin in the Peripheral Nervous System (**PNS**). Oligodendrocytes produce myelin in the Central Nervous System (**CNS**)**.** MS affects the CNS, not the PNS. MS affects myelin and oligodendrocytes, not Schwann cells.
