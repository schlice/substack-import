Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [The website operator will likely have to claim immunity under Section 230 of the Communications Decency Act.\ \ The website is responsible for the content it authors. But under Section 230, website operators have \"safe harbor\" protections for third-party activity in most cases. Unless the website is creating or \"developing\" the illegal transaction, it\'s likely that it will be protected.]{.rendered_qtext}
