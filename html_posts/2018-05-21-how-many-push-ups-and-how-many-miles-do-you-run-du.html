--- title: '"How many push-ups, and how many miles do you run during the 4 years at a military service academy? For a school project."' date: '2018-05-21' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It would be nearly impossible to calculate. For starters, many push ups take place at odd times and odd locations. Basically, any time you are standing around waiting for something else to happen, you will find yourself in the front leaning rest position. Since those times are many and varied (you also become fully qualified in "hurry up and wait,") calculating the number of push ups done would be next to impossible. Running is worse, since some people actually *enjoy* running. They actually do it on their own! In truth, I used to be one of these truly insane people. I hesitate to even hazard a guess, mostly because I think it may scare me.
