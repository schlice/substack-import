**Content:** [It\'s unlikely that an ID card gets faked, but it\'s far more likely that a badge or shield gets faked.\ \ You can always ask for ID, even of officers in uniform. I have never heard of an agency that does not require their officers to carry their ID cards along with their badges. It\'s part of an officer\'s \"credentials,\" or \"creds\" for short.]{.rendered_qtext}
