**Content:** [Larry\'s answer is superb. Rather than mess with the beauty of it, I\'d like to re-phrase the whole \"fires of Hell\" thing. I believe that a fair definition of Heaven is \"living immersed in the full grace, love, and presence of God\" and Hell is \"living in the absolute void and absence of God and His love.\"\ \ Heaven would be like having all of your senses immediately multiplied tenfold and getting seven or eight new senses, and Hell would be like living in an empty apartment, having all of your senses immediately dulled, and losing your hearing too. No sunlight, just a single lightbulb in the ceiling. No windows, no tech, no nothing. Forever alone. Can you live that way? Certainly you can, but it\'s no way to live. You will find that living in the absence of God\'s love is torture. You may as well be on fire.]{.rendered_qtext}
