**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What law states that the death penalty is feasible in certain states of America?** State law is written by states, and only states determine whether or not they will institute a death penalty. There is no federal law that allows states to pass certain laws. There are some federal bans on some items which have been incorporated to the states. There is no federal ban on states passing laws instituting death penalties for crimes in those states.
