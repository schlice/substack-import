**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It's actually shorter to list the states with complete abortion bans led by Democrats: Louisiana and Kentucky. Twelve other states have enacted complete abortion bans: - Idaho - North Dakota - South Dakota - Oklahoma - Texas - Missouri - Arkansas - Mississippi - Alabama - Tennessee - Indiana - West Virginia Twenty one states in total either ban abortion or restrict the procedure earlier in pregnancy than the standard set by *Roe.* The additional seven states which have set limits earlier than *Roe* did includes two states controlled by Democrats: Arizona and North Carolina (and North Carolina is unique because they have a Republican-controlled Legislature and a Democrat governor, and some of this legislation was passed over the governor's veto by a legislative supermajority).
