**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can I sue a company for using a picture of my child without my knowledge?** The answer, as with all legal answers, is "it depends." In this case, it mostly depends on where they got the picture, and what they're using it for. If they simply took a picture of your child out in public, then in most cases, the picture is able to be used in nearly any way they want. They can even post it online. What they ***cannot*** do is use the picture for commercial gain. If the picture is on their website where they used it to advertise a product or sell something or make money for profit, this is illegal. \ *This answer is not legal advice, nor is it intended to be used as a substitute for legal advice.
