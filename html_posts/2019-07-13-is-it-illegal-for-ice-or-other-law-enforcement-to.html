**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Is it illegal for ICE or other law enforcement to go inside a religious building and arrest someone?** No, there is no special protection for anyone inside of a church. If the church is open to the public, it is open to law enforcement. There is literally nothing special about a church that would prevent law enforcement (including ICE) from doing their jobs there.
