--- title: '"Could war be made illegal?"' date: '2016-07-27' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} A2A Yes, war can be made illegal. But that would be illogical. We would be removing the ability of our own country to fight back if another country chose to invade us. We would be tying our own hands and signaling to the world that we no longer intended to remain a sovereign nation, as any nation could invade us and it would be illegal for us to retaliate. Therefore, wars can only be legitimized in the United States through act of Congress. While the President has first-strike authorization, he must get approval from Congress to continue. It is up to Congress to approve the war and to fund it.
