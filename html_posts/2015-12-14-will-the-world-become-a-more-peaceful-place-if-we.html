--- title: '"Will the world become a more peaceful place if we took all the guns away? What problems would that solve? What problems would that create?"' date: '2015-12-14' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The only way that the world would become more peaceful if we could (somehow) remove all the guns from it instantly would be if the guns were the cause of the violence. Remove the gun, remove the violence. However (and gun control advocates often seem to miss this point), **guns do not cause violence**. Guns are a tool, one of many which could be used to commit violence. Guns, by themselves, do not cause violence any more than pencils cause spelling errors or forks cause obesity. You need a consciousness behind the gun to cause violence. Therefore, using that understanding, removing the gun will not cause peace, because the gun does not cause violence. Removing the gun will remove a tool that can be used for good or for ill. The gun will be replaced with something else, most likely a sword, a knife, a baton, pepper spray, Tasers, bow and arrows, and spears.
