--- title: "Does Dell update or exchange their old laptops with new ones?" date: "2017-02-21" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Dell has a buyback program for enterprise equipment. They do not have a buyback program for commercial laptops. Dell will help you trade in your laptop and will give you a Dell gift card for doing so, but they will not buy back or exchange old laptops. If you want to get more significant money for your laptop, I suggest selling it yourself on eBay or some other marketplace. If you're more interested in simply recycling the old laptop and getting a little money for your trouble, [[Dell will help you with that](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=38988b&url=http://www.dell.com/learn/us/en/19/campaigns/trade-in-program){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="dell.com"}]{.qlink_container}. In fact, you can trade in almost any electronics in the same place and earn Dell gift cards.
