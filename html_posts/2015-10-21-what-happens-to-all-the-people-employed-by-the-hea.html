Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Health insurance, as an industry, will become a thing of the past. People employed with the health insurance industry will have to find more meaningful employment in some other industry. Perhaps they could remain in insurance, but move to other companies like Allstate that provide other types of insurance like home or auto.\ \ Part of the reason that this has not yet happened in the US is because health insurance is big business, and health insurance lobbies in Congress are very powerful. They understand that single payer will kill their industry.]{.rendered_qtext}
