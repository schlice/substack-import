**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} They are not illegal to own in the United States. However, your state might not register it as a motor vehicle allowed to drive on the road. There are a few states which will register them as a car, but most states will only register them as a motorcycle. You will have to follow all of the rules for registering and insuring a motorcycle, and having a motorcycle license to drive one.
