**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What do you think of the judges ruling on 11/16, allowing Jim Acosta to have his White House pass back, saying it was wrong for the White House to revoke it?** While I'm certain that's what you wanted the ruling to say, that's not exactly what happened. First of all, it's a temporary restraining order. That means that the case has not yet been heard, so there are no "rulings" yet. Second, it was on ***5th Amendment*** grounds, not 1st Amendment. The judge basically agreed with CNN that Acosta was not afforded due process in having his hard pass revoked, and that this was a violation of Acosta's 5th Amendment right to due process. It says nothing about whether or not Acosta has a 1st Amendment right to a hard pass. Finally, he may have his hard pass back, but Sanders is under no legal obligation to call on him. I hope she simply bypasses him and calls on other, more mature CNN reporters until his case can be heard.
