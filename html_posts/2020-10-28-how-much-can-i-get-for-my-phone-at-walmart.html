**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How much can I get for my phone at Walmart?** If the Walmart or Sam's Club location participates in [[CExchange](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=463cbf&url=https://walmart.cexchange.com/online/home/index.rails){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="cexchange.com"}]{.qlink_container}, you can get anywhere from \$1 to over \$500 for a working phone, right there in the store. Of course, the exact amount depends on the model you are trading in and its condition. Just head to your local Walmart or Sam's Club and ask an associate in electronics whether or not they are a participating store. Alternatively, using the link above, you can run the same process even if your local Walmart doesn't directly participate. You answer some questions on the website, ship them your device, and after they evaluate it, you will receive an instant Walmart eGift card which you can put towards anything in store or at [[walmart.com](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=60e068&url=http://walmart.com){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="walmart.com"}]{.qlink_container}.
