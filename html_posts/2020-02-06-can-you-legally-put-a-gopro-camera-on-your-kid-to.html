**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can you legally put a GoPro camera on your kid to keep track of them at school and during the day?** *Inside* of the school, you will likely run into the same issues with putting a GoPro on your kid as you would with anyone taking a GoPro into many areas in the school, such as locker rooms and bathrooms. Additionally, the school has the right to control students' use of electronics, as teachers legally act *in loco parentis* in most cases. The school can simply claim that the camera interrupts the basic operation of the school, and they can ban it or turn it off. If the child refuses, they can send him home. *Outside* of school, your kid can do whatever he likes when he's out in public, including recording anything he sees. Clearly, he is bound by any other recording device restriction (such as the aforementioned bathrooms and locker rooms).
