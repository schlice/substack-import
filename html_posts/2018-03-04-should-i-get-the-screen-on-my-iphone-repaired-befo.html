--- title: '"Should I get the screen on my iPhone repaired before trading it in?"' date: '2018-03-04' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Depends on how you get the screen repaired and what "trade-in" you're talking about. If you are talking about simply trading in your old device for a credit towards a new one, the calculation is simple math: how much does it cost to repair the screen vs. how much extra you'll get for a device with an unbroken vs broken screen. If you are talking about a program like T-Mobile's JUMP program, where you trade in your old phone for a new one, you **must** have a phone with an unbroken screen before you can turn it in. And you must have your screen repaired by insurance, not the Fix-y guy in the mall. Check with your carrier to validate this part.
