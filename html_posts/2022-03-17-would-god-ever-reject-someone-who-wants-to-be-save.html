Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} No. God will never turn away those with a *willing* heart. Even people that you wouldn't expect can be saved. Just look at the Bible. Even the criminal who was crucified beside Jesus was saved. He saved prostitutes and thieves. And the one thing they had in common was a repentant heart and a willing spirit. They had *faith*, and their faith in Christ was what saved them. \ Original question: Would God ever reject someone who wants to be saved?
