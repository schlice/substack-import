Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Assuming that you\'re in some sort of combat scenario.\ \ 1. Grab it and throw it back.\ \ 2. If you can\'t do that, grab your bucket (helmet), and dive on top of it, placing your bucket on top of the grenade, and your body on top of the bucket. Your feet and lower legs will probably get some shrapnel, but better that than everyone else in your squad dying.\ \ Remember what Spock said: \"The needs of the many outweigh the needs of the one.\"]{.rendered_qtext}
