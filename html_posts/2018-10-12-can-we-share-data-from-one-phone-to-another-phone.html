**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can we share data from one phone to another phone with a** ***Li*-*Fi*** **system?** Today? No. No phones currently support the Li-Fi standard. In the future, I can see phones supporting Li-Fi, but I still think we're maybe 5--10 years out before a phone starts to embed the technology. The infrastructure needs to support it first, and then you'll see handsets picking it up. I could see it used in specialized equipment first, and then rolled out to phones later on. It's a promising technology though.
