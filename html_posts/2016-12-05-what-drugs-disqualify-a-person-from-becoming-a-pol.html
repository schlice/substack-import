--- title: "What drugs disqualify a person from becoming a police officer?" date: "2016-12-05" tags: ["law-enforcement"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The short answer: recent ones. If anything comes up on your drug panel when you get your inprocessing physical, you're gone. The longer answer: every department has its own limitations. Also, every department understands that people are human, and that college-age kids experiment. Heck, President Clinton smoked pot (but he didn't inhale...yeah right). If you tried basic rec drugs as a kid, but you did what the vast majority of normal people do and never touch the stuff again, you should be fine. If you were using anything illegal last week, you're pretty much not going to get in.
