**Content:** [For people with Relapsing/Remitting MS (RRMS), every day brings a new wonder when you open your eyes. \"Will my eyes work this morning?\" \"Will my legs support me when I step onto the floor?\" \"Will I have trouble with my morning constitutional?\" \"Will I have trouble swallowing my breakfast this morning?\" \"Am I going to be too tired to go out with the family tonight?\" \"Am I going to lose focus at my job today?\" Or, more worryingly, \"Are any of my previous symptoms going to come back today?\" MS brings a range of \"unknowns\" to your day, each and every day.\ \ The trick to living well with MS is knowing how to quiet your demons and live for the day you have rather than worrying about the days you may not have.\ \ I can tell you that I haven\'t quite mastered living well with MS. It\'s a process. I\'ve gotten much, much better at it, but I know I\'m not \"there\" yet.]{.rendered_qtext}
