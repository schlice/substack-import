**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Maybe. You'll have to read your Association's Governing Documents to find out. It's different for every Association, and different in every state. Also note that beyond your HOA or Condo Board, you may have state requirements for installing security cameras (such as signage indicating that security cameras are in use). So be sure to check your state laws as well as your HOA/Condo Board Governing Documents. \ Original question: Do you need permission from your homeowners\' association or condo board to install a security camera?
