**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can you demand proof of a search warrant if the police want to enter your house?** If the police knock on your door and say that they have a search warrant, they will normally hand you a copy of that warrant while they enter. If the police knock on your door and say that they have a search warrant and they refuse to show it to you, make note of the time and date that this occurred. Video proof is helpful (take out your phone and record them or take snapshots). Your lawyer will need this information. You can demand to see the warrant all you like, but it's unlikely you'll be able to physically prevent them from entering. They have battering rams and superior force, and if they want in, they're coming in. Fight with them in court, not on your doorstep.
