Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Are police officers trained to use their taser gun before they fire their real gun at a suspect?** Most certainly not. When you drive a nail, are you trained to try to drive the nail with your hand first, before you use a hammer? Of course not, because the hammer is the right tool for the job. The Taser is appropriate for some situations. The firearm is appropriate for some other situations. Situations that require a Taser do not always escalate to situations which require a firearm. Similarly, not all situations which require a firearm must begin with a Taser. The firearm is deadly force. If a situation calls for the use of deadly force, the firearm is used. If a situation does not call for the use of deadly force, the firearm is not used. The Taser may be used. Another less lethal device could also be used, such as pepper spray. It all depends on the situation.
