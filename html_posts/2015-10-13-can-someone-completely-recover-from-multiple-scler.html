**Content:** [There is no cure for multiple sclerosis. So the short answer is no, you cannot recover from multiple sclerosis.\ \ Some people have achieved remission to the point where it\'s nearly impossible to tell that they have MS in the first place. Some people have a form of MS called \"benign MS\" which exhibits one symptom and then goes into remission, for as many as 10 years or more.\ \ However, no one has ever \"completely recovered\" from MS.]{.rendered_qtext}
