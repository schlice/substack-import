**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Is it legal for a civilian to buy a battering ram in the U.S.?** Yes, there are no laws (as far as I know) against either buying a battering ram or building one. You may violate laws by *using* your battering ram, but simply owning one is not illegal.
