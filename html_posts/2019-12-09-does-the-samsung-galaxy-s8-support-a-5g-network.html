Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Does the Samsung Galaxy S8 support a 5G network?** No. You will have to purchase a 5G-capable phone, which contains the correct network adapters to communicate with a 5G network, in order to operate on a 5G network. This isn't something that can be "updated" via a "software fix." This requires a completely different radio, and a different modem. You will have to purchase a phone which can operate on a 5G network in order to utilize 5G technology.
