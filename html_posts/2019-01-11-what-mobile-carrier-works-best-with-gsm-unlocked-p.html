{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can you take an unlocked phone to any carrier?** No. You can take an unlocked phone to any carrier *who supports that phone's operating bands.* If you want to ensure that your unlocked phone will work on your new carrier, your best bet is to find out [[what wireless technology your unlocked phone is using](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=cc0ae2&url=https://www.gsmarena.com/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="gsmarena.com"}]{.qlink_container}, find out [[what carriers support that frequency](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=1c8915&url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_United_States_wireless_communications_service_providers%23Technologies_used){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="wikipedia.org"}]{.qlink_container}, and then ask those carriers if you can use your unlocked device on their network.
