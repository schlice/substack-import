Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can a homeowners association forbid the construction of a wheelchair ramp to the front entrance of a house?** No, with caveats. The HOA cannot *forbid* you from building a wheelchair accessible ramp to your house. That would be in violation of the Fair Housing Act. However, they can dictate how the ramp looks, what materials it is made out of, and possibly if there is more than one suitable location for the ramp, which location you can use.
