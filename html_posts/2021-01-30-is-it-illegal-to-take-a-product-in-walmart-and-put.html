**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Is it illegal to take a product in Walmart and put it in the store somewhere hidden so you could come back the week or however long it sits until you make the purchase?** No, it is not illegal. There is no law on the books relating to relocation of products in Walmart. It's not illegal to take product from one area of the store and move it to another. Not having been a Walmart employee, I'm not sure if it violates store policy. Store personnel normally do "recovery" when they find an item in one area of the store that doesn't belong there. It's more work for store employees, but in general, as far as I know, it doesn't violate store policy.
