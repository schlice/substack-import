**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Fortunately, the law is based on the \"reasonable man\" standard. The \"reasonable man\" would have to believe that the killing of a doll kills a person. The reasonable man does not believe that sticking a pin in a doll kills a person, regardless of what the \"suspect\" believes.
