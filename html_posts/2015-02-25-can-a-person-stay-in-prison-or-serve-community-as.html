**Content:** [You can\'t be sentenced until you\'ve been tried. You can\'t be tried until you\'ve been arrested. You can\'t be arrested until you make overt actions towards committing a crime.\ \ So no, you can\'t pre-pay for your crime under the US justice system. Your time in jail is for a specific reason, not as a hobby.\ \ Jails are too overcrowded to accommodate hobbyists.]{.rendered_qtext}
