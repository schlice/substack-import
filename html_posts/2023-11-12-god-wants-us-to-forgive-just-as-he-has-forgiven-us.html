**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Close. At Jesus' death, we were *redeemed*. We had already been forgiven. God forgives unconditionally. He forgives and He forgets. Jeremiah 31:34 says, "I will forgive their wickedness and will remember their sins no more.". This verse is echoed in Hebrews 8:12 because Jesus fulfills this promise. The problem with people is that they withhold forgiveness. God never withholds forgiveness. God forgives and forgets. Jesus death and resurrection redeems us, but we were already forgiven. If we would forgive half as fast as God forgives us, the world would be a much better place.
