**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Where does the notion that a cop can go a whole career without drawing a gun except on the range come from?** Your question is flawed. There is no notion that a cop can go a whole career without drawing a gun except on the range. There *is*, in fact, a notion that a cop can go a whole career without ***firing*** a gun except on the range. And it's more than just a notion. It's provable fact, supported by statistics. Cops routinely draw their firearms during the normal course of a career. In some cities, it may be almost daily, maybe even multiple times in a single shift. But it is fewer than 1% of police officers who will actually fire their weapons outside of the range.
