**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Is it legal for a mother to shower with her 14 year old son?** Not sure why I'm seeing an A2A on a question with over 100 answers, but since I was asked, I'll answer. I'll provide the simple answer, since it seems that much of the morality of such a scenario has already been discussed. I will not discuss the morality of such behavior. The specific question asked was "is it legal." In most states, a mother and her son showering together (i.e. using the shower at the same time) is not illegal. There are specific behaviors while in the shower which *could* be illegal (if they stray into sexual territory), but the simple act of a mother and son sharing a shower at the same time is, by itself, generally not illegal. Whether or not this behavior *should* be illegal is a matter for much debate, as you can see by the wealth of varied opinions in this thread. But it's not, at its most simple activity, illegal.
