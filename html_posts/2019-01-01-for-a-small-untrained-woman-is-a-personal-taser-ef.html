**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **For a small, untrained woman, is a personal TASER effective for warding off an attack?** No. A Taser is much less effective than, say, pepper spray. For most assailants, pepper spray is highly effective and does not cause lasting damage. Tasers must be deployed precisely in order to be effective, and some use cases make Tasers ineffective. Additionally, pepper spray is far less regulated than Tasers, so depending on where you live, you may not even be legally permitted to carry a Taser without a concealed weapons permit, assuming you're even allowed to possess one at all.
