**Content:** [It isn\'t \"necessary\" in the fact that the plane will crash if the wheels aren\'t stopped. But leaving the wheels spinning creates excess friction on the axle, especially when the wheel is rotated 90 degrees sideways. So braking the wheels reduces the wear-and-tear on the axles and hubs of the wheels. It also creates unnecessary noise and vibration inside the aircraft when the wheels continue to spin.]{.rendered_qtext}
