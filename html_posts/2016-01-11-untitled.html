**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} In the United States, firearms are designated as \"lethal weapons.\" Since pepper spray and Tasers are \"less-than-lethal,\" they are not covered under federal firearms regulations. That said, states can choose to regulate them as they wish, and some do not allow citizens to carry Tasers unless they are registered in the same way that firearms are.
