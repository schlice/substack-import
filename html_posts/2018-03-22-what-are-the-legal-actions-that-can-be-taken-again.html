**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Legally, you may have violated Facebook's Terms of Service by creating a fake account. If you did (and off the top of my head, I'm not sure), you can be punished by being banned from using Facebook. If the girl was underage, you can be arrested and charged with receiving child pornography, but (you say) you didn't download or keep a copy of the images, so there may only be the Facebook "paper trail" to show that you did receive the images. If they can tie the fake account to you, you can be thus charged. If the girl was not underage, the photos may have simply violated Facebook's Terms of Service again when she provided them to you on their platform. They may prohibit sending or receiving adult content (again, off the top of my head, I'm not sure). She may face a Facebook ban herself.
