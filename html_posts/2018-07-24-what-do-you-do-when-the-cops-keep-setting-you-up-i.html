**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What do you do when the cops keep setting you up intentionally to be arrested using illegal surveillance to strategically set up elaborate false arrests?** Wow, where do you live? The police in your town literally have *nothing better to do* then to set up elaborate false arrest scenarios using "illegal surveillance" to specifically target *you*? Either your local cops are really really bored (which is a good thing) or you did something to really really piss them off such that they'd waste lots of time and risk cases getting thrown out of court by using "illegal surveillance" just to give you a hard time. Regardless of why the police are doing this, what you do is **get a lawyer.** If the police really are targeting you, using "illegal surveillance" to harass you, your lawyer will help you file suit against the department alleging harassment. If, on the other hand, their surveillance is actually legal (or if it's wiretaps, supported by a court order), you're going to need that lawyer to defend yourself, because some pretty hefty charges are soon to come your way.
