**Content:** [Please remember that police officers are just doing their jobs as well. They want the stop to go smoothly. Perhaps you didn\'t know you were speeding. If you honestly don\'t know, you can say, \"Honestly, I don\'t know why you stopped me.\"\ \ Remember that your best bet is to be honest and polite. The VAST majority of police officers are good, hard-working people that are trying to keep the public safe.]{.rendered_qtext}
