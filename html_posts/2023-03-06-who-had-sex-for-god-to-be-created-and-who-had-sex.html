Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} God was not created. God is ***eternal***, which means He has no beginning, and no end. He just *is*. He is the eternal First Cause. It is necessary that He be eternal, because if He is not (i.e. He was created), then your question would be logical, as something or someone would have needed to create Him.
