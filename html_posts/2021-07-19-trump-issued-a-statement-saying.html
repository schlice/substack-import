**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Let's clarify terms. The people I'll call "vaxxers" are people who believe that the government should force everyone who is able to get the vaccine whether they want to or not, because it's best for everyone. The people I'll call "vaxx-choice" believe that their immune systems will handle the virus just like their immune systems handle everything else that is thrown its way. They'll possibly get COVID-19, and they'll also possibly (although it's a slim chance) end up in the hospital. There is also the possibility that they will die. They accept this possibility. This group also includes people who have already contracted COVID-19 and have antibodies against the virus naturally, and don't believe they should need to get the vaccine. By this point in time, people should all have heard that COVID-19 exists. They should know that it causes upper respiratory issues. It can hospitalize you, and it can kill you. They also are fully aware that a vaccine exists, but it has not been approved by the FDA. The FDA has granted it an "Emergency Use Authorization" **which is not a full approval.** The vaccine is only temporarily approved to use with patient's consent, knowing that it has only been anecdotally tested. Full-scale testing is ongoing, and the people being tested are the people who have received the vaccine. Yes, for every one of us who got the vaccine, we are in the test group. The vaccine is being tested on us. Don't believe me? You mean you didn't get information about the [[CDC's v-safe program](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=ac9d33&url=https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/vaccines/safety/vsafe.html){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="cdc.gov"}]{.qlink_container} when you got the vaccine? They're watching you for symptoms and to see if you develop COVID-19. Additionally, recently there have been spikes in disease activity. There is a Delta variant out there as well. What we've learned is that [[nearly all of the new COVID deaths are among the unvaccinated](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=82cbab&url=https://apnews.com/article/coronavirus-pandemic-health-941fcf43d9731c76c16e7354f5d5e187){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="apnews.com"}]{.qlink_container}. What we don't know is what percentage of those infections are in people who have already had COVID-19 and got better. In other words, we don't know if any of these people had it and got it again. From that article: > An Associated Press analysis of available government data from May > shows that "breakthrough" infections in fully vaccinated people > accounted for fewer than 1,200 of more than 107,000 COVID-19 > hospitalizations. That's about 1.1%. > > And only about 150 of the more than 18,000 COVID-19 deaths in May were > in fully vaccinated people. That translates to about 0.8%, or five > deaths per day on average. None of the previous was opinion. It is all fact. Regardless, these facts tell us two things. - If you don't want to get COVID-19 and you haven't had it yet, get the vaccine. - If you have the vaccine, the chances are literally slim to none that you'll get sick with it. There are some anecdotal reports of some businesses not offering sick time to account for the side effects of the vaccine, which can take a few days from which to recover, and this is causing some hesitancy amongst some to get the vaccine, but the vast majority who haven't gotten the vaccine are "vaxx-choicers" using my definitions from the above. They also fully know that if you're vaccinated, your risk of getting the virus is virtually zero. **They don't care.** They also believe that the government hasn't really acted in the most trustworthy way throughout the course of the pandemic. Whether or not you choose to risk infection is entirely up to you. It's not up to anyone else. The "vaxxers" believe that it's up to **you** to keep **them** safe. That's illogical. That's not how we do healthcare in this country. We don't keep you safe by making sure that no one around you can get sick. We keep you safe by making sure that you can't get sick.
