**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It\'s actually quite simple to shoot a gun. Young or old, it\'s a simple mechanical tool. I\'m not quite sure what you mean by \"shooting safely.\" Are you asking how do you shoot and only hit your intended target, or how do you shoot without injuring yourself? In order to shoot and only hit your intended target, you need practice. In order to shoot without injuring yourself, follow the true definition of gun control: \"using both hands to hold your gun.\" Remember, it\'s a controlled explosion going off in your hands and is always a little startling, no matter how many times you do it.
