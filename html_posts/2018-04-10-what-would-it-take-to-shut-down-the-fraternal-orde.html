**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What would it take to shut down the Fraternal Order of Police?** While I have no idea why you'd want to do such a thing, the answer is the same for any club or organization, not just the FOP. *You need to have a majority of the Board of Directors and a majority of the stakeholders agree that the organization is no longer meeting its goals and objectives, and agreeing that the best course of action is to disband the organization.* There is no outside influence you can exert that will shut down an organization as old and as large as the FOP. None. They will only shut down when they choose to shut down.
