--- title: '"Does Jesus Christ have a brother or sister?"' date: '2023-06-13' tags: - theology layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The Bible says clearly that Jesus had half-brothers and half-sisters. Jesus had four brothers: James, Joseph, Simon, and Judas ([[Matthew 13:55](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=4fe4c1&url=https://biblia.com/bible/esv/Matt%252013.55){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="biblia.com"}]{.qlink_container}). [[Matthew 12:46](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=a2adad&url=https://biblia.com/bible/esv/Matt%252012.46){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="biblia.com"}]{.qlink_container}, [[Luke 8:19](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=fff2f3&url=https://biblia.com/bible/esv/Luke%25208.19){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="biblia.com"}]{.qlink_container}, and [[Mark 3:31](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=5b4fe8&url=https://biblia.com/bible/esv/Mark%25203.31){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="biblia.com"}]{.qlink_container} say that Jesus' mother and brothers came to see Him. The Bible also shows us that Jesus had sisters, but they are not named ([[Matthew 13:56](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=ae2027&url=https://biblia.com/bible/esv/Matt%252013.56){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="biblia.com"}]{.qlink_container}). There are other parts of the Bible in which Jesus' siblings are addressed: - In [[John 7:1-10](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=49ac48&url=https://biblia.com/bible/esv/John%25207.1-10){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="biblia.com"}]{.qlink_container}, His brothers go on to the festival while Jesus stays behind. - In [[Acts 1:14](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=e6e454&url=https://biblia.com/bible/esv/Acts%25201.14){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="biblia.com"}]{.qlink_container}, His brothers and mother are described as praying with the disciples. - [[Galatians 1:19](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=2b8255&url=https://biblia.com/bible/esv/Gal%25201.19){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="biblia.com"}]{.qlink_container} says that James was Jesus' brother. The most natural conclusion of these passages is to interpret that Jesus had actual blood half-siblings.
