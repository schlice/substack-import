--- title: '"Does the move over law apply to all vehicles with flashing lights? Are landscape vehicles included?"' date: '2017-10-04' tags: - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} In the US, the answer is "it depends." It depends on local laws, but in most states, emergency vehicles have red and/or blue lights. Yellow lights are "caution" or "slow-moving" vehicles. They may also suddenly stop without warning. They won't be overtaking drivers, and do not have the right-of-way. Usually, you will overtake them.
