**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **At what point will the Republican leaders in the House and Senate finally say this country is more important than their donors and the Koch brothers and stop Trump?** I think you're going to have to be a little bit more clear about what exactly you think is going on in Washington. - How are Representatives and Senators voting against the will of the voters and in line with "their donors and the Koch brothers?" - What is Trump doing that "needs stopping" that Congress is failing to do? Representatives and Senators should always be voting in their constituents' best interests, or they may not get re-elected. I would have though that would have been clear by this point, but I guess not: if you don't vote in accordance with the will of the electorate, you don't get re-elected. More to the point: if your representative or Senator is not voting in the way you like, don't re-elect them. I'm still curious what you think Trump is doing that needs stopping, or what Trump can do without the support of Congress that similarly needs stopping. Unemployment is at its lowest rate in over 5 decades. Black and Hispanic unemployment is at its lowest rate, ***ever*.** Tax cuts have created jobs and have put more money back in your pocket. Stifling government regulations have been rolled back at an amazing rate, getting government off of your back and allowing you to be more free. The military is getting funding again so that they can continue to fight both the Global War on Terror as well as other military campaigns simultaneously. We are stopping terrorist from making their way onto our shores to execute their terror plots. We are trying to enforce the border, despite Democrats' best efforts to stop us. So I ask again: what exactly is Trump doing that "needs to be stopped?"
