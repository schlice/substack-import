**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Is it at all kind to force a woman to bring a baby to term when we ***know*** (not suspect) that the baby will have a short, painful life, and will most definitely die? There are a few arguments to be made, including misdiagnosis, and the possibility of using the infant organs as donors. But the crux of this argument should be that such a diagnosis, regardless of abortion laws, should leave the mother the choice about whether to carry the baby to term or abort. But a *means test as a basis for determining abortion?* Patently ridiculous.
