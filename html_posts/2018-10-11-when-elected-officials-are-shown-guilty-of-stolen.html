**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **When elected officials are shown guilty of Stolen Valor, how should they be held accountable?** The correct accountability method is to not re-elect that official. You can also *recall* that official from state office (if your state has provisions for such a recall election), or for US government officials, you can urge the body to look into impeachment.
