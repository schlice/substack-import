**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **As a white female police officer, what do I do if I find a stranger in my house? Do I open fire since I fear for my life?** The first part of the sentence ("As a white female police officer") is completely irrelevant. Let's just replace that with, "As a citizen of the United States," because that's the only qualification that matters. Now as for the rest, it depends on which state you're in. For most states with a strong "Castle Doctrine," the answer is you confront the individual with the threat of deadly force. If they don't comply, you use whatever force you must to stay safe.
