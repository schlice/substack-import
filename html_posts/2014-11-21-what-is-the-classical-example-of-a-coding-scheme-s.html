**Content:** [It is called parity. Take a look at how RAID uses a [[parity drive](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=d1a714&url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Parity_drive){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="wikipedia.org"}]{.qlink_container} to understand how parity works.\ \ There is a practical limit, however, to how many m parts can remain and still rebuild the whole file. Lose too many parts and the group will become unrecoverable.]{.rendered_qtext}
