Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} While election of new officers takes place at the beginning of the Annual Meeting, the new Board is not empaneled until the next regular Board meeting. Therefore, the currently empaneled Board fulfills their duties at the Annual Meeting.
