Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} When we enter the academy, we are given a paper copy of the Colorado Revised Statutes. Attached to the inside of the back cover is a CD that allows you to read all the statutes on a PC. So I went to Lexis-Nexis and bought an electronic copy of the Colorado Revised Statutes in PDF format, which sits on my iPad. Very rarely do I open the book any more.
