**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} While they may legitimately loan you \$5000, it likely comes with ridiculously high interest rates, or collateral requirements, or difficult repayment requirements. These practices are what we like to call *predatory lending*. One thing you can do is to do your homework on the lender.
