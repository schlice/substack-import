**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Do the police have the legal powers to remotely access my phone?** Not inherently, but they can get it. It\'s called a **warrant.** They have to present evidence to a judge and get that judge to sign the warrant, and then they can retrieve whatever information is listed on the warrant.
