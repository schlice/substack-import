**Content:** [Tobacco is not part of the war on drugs because it is not illegal. The question is: should it be?\ \ If we determine what should be illegal based on how **damaging** it is to your body, alcohol and tobacco should be highly illegal, along with most illegal drugs. Marijuana shouldn\'t really be on the list, as it is more helpful than hurtful.\ \ If we determine what should be illegal based on how **addictive** it is, the marijuana should not even be on the list, but many other drugs, including tobacco and alcohol, should. Nicotine is more addictive than cocaine.\ \ If we determine what should be illegal based on whether or not it has any **medicinal benefit**, then marijuana, cocaine, and opium should be off the list. Nicotine should definitely be on this list.\ \ But in the United States, we don\'t determine what is illegal by any of those factors. It\'s done by politicians, political agendas, and big business, not common sense.]{.rendered_qtext}
