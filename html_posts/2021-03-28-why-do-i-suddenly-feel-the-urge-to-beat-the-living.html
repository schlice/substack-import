**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Why do I suddenly feel the urge to beat the living crap out of someone?** Because you're a human being. Feeling emotions is normal. Feeling anger is normal. Wanting to take out your aggression on someone is also normal. What ***isn't*** normal is having this feeling ***all of the time*** or ***most of the time***. If you feel this occasionally, and you're able to control yourself, congratulations, you're a human. If you feel this all the time, seek help. :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-3544e3fe008937f31a840d4deb626633){.portrait .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-3544e3fe008937f31a840d4deb626633" master_w="455" master_h="494"} ::: ::::
