**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How much money can an HOA charge a homeowner per month for pets exceeding a weight limit?** In general, fines for violating HOA R&Rs need to be "reasonable," but they can charge you that reasonable amount up to daily, if they choose. So instead of charging you per month, they can charge you per day for violating certain rules.
