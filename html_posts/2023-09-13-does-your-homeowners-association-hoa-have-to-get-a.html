**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} In general, answers to HOA questions are going to be "check your CC&Rs" and in this case, it's no different. Check your CC&Rs to find the exact correct answer for your HOA. ***In general***, in most HOAs, the Board of Directors does not need additional approval to raise dues, implement fees, or impose special assessments, with some exceptions. Some construction projects may need homeowner approval before they are started, but some repair-related assessments do not need prior approval. The Board is normally specifically empowered to maintain the budget and collect money as needed.
