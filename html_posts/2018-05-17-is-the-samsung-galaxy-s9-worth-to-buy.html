Is it worth it to buy that phone?** I will defer to [[Lewis Hilsenteger](https://www.quora.com/topic/Lewis-Hilsenteger-1)]{.qlink_container}'s opinion on that topic. Lew hosts the popular YouTube channel, "[[Unbox Therapy](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=1f2b69&url=https://www.youtube.com/user/unboxtherapy){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="youtube.com"}]{.qlink_container}," where he discusses technology trends and does device reviews. But as a test, he started carrying and using the S9 (actually, the S9+, but the comparison is similar). He then did a video comparing the S9+ to the Pixel 2 XL. He went into the video without a preconception about which device he was going to choose. His observations are quite interesting. I'll let you watch for yourself.
