**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Why does it seem as if the GOP is getting revenge for the United States twice voting for Barack Obama? Have they ever been this bloodthirsty before?** You answered your own question. It *seems* that way. It's your interpretation of what you're seeing. I'm going to link you over to another answer to a previous Quora question: [[*What do you think happened to America to bring about a President like Trump?*](https://www.quora.com/What-do-you-think-happened-to-America-to-bring-about-a-President-like-Trump/answer/Anthony-Zarrella)]{.qlink_container} This answer, I think, perfectly encapsulates what Republicans were *feeling* after eight years of an Obama administration. Many of the policies you see being implemented now are policies that you would never see during an Obama administration. So while they are not implementing these policies *in order to be vindictive,* the appearance is that these policies are exactly opposite of how we were doing things, but that's because the policies we are implementing now are in direct contradiction to the last eight years of US policy, both foreign and domestic. And they are not *bloodthirsty*; they are *determined* to implement the policies they believe will be helpful to this country, and to its people. This is what they were elected to do, and they're doing it.
