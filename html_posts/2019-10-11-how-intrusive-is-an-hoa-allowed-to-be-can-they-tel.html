**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How intrusive is an HOA allowed to be? Can they tell me how to landscape my backyard?** HOAs can be as intrusive as their Covenants and Rules & Regulations allow them to be. **In general**, HOAs deal with the outside of your house. Things like how your yard looks, what products you can use to landscape, what your landscaping looks like, what colors and products you can use on the outside of your house, etc. It is very infrequently that an HOA controls the inside of your house. The only time I have seen an HOA be that intrusive is when it deals with garages. I have seen an HOA state that you cannot use garages as storage units and that cars must be stored in garages before they get parked on driveways (it was a pretty restrictive HOA). So can they tell you how to landscape your backyard? Absolutely.
