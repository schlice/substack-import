--- title: "How do I stop my iPhone from telling me that I need to back up my iCloud?" date: "2017-08-22" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Turn off iCloud Backup. Go to Settings \> *\* \> iCloud \> iCloud Backup. Uncheck iCloud Backup. I recommend you then back up your iPhone using iTunes instead. 1. Connect your iPhone to the computer running iTunes. Make sure you have already disabled iCloud Backup. 2. Right-click (or Ctrl-Click) on your device 3. Select *Back up*.
