**Content:** [What qualifies a person to be President of the United States is exactly what the Constitution says qualifies a person to be President of the United States.\ \ Article 2 Section 1 Clause 5 states the qualifications for the office of President and Vice President:\ ]{.rendered_qtext} - Natural born citizen - At least 35 years old - Has inhabited the United States for at least 14 years. \ I\'m not sure what the delusion is that would make someone believe that he meets all of these qualifications. When you find out what delusion that is, be sure to let us know.\ \ People try to say that candidates somehow are not \"qualified\" to be President, when the only qualifications for office are the ones listed in the Constitution.\ \ Being a good leader is really all that\'s needed to be a good President. You don\'t have to know everything, but you do need to know enough to have a good staff, listen to them when they advise you, and overrule them when they go off track. The leader sets the vision, the staff sets the policy.
