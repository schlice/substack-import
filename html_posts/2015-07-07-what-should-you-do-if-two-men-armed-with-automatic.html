**Content:** [Depends on where you live.\ \ If you live in a state with an active and well-tested *Castle Doctrine*, then by all means, defend yourself and your family by all means necessary, using all force available to you, up to and including deadly force.\ \ If you do not live in a strong Castle Doctrine state, I still suggest you defend yourself and your family by all means necessary, using all force available to you, up to and including deadly force. After you stop them, however, you may be facing some tough legal challenges to your own defense of your own family in your own home. But your family will be safe.]{.rendered_qtext}
