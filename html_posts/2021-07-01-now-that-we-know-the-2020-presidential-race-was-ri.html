**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Even if the Supreme Court rules that the 2020 Presidential election was 100% rigged, we won't have a 2021 election. The election took place. 50 states and the District of Columbia all had separate elections, and they all certified their results. Congress accepted the certified results of the election, and power transferred hands peacefully, as it has done for over 200 years. Since the election was held Constitutionally, the results are permanent. If we find concrete evidence that a state's election results were so badly rigged **that it would have changed the outcome of the election,** then some very pointed looks at that state will start to take place. How were they able to *certify* a state's results with such widespread election tampering? People will be fired. Election processes will be changed. The specific avenues which permitted election tampering will be modified so that they can no longer cause such tampering. Some very unusual things happened in the 2020 Presidential election, things that hopefully, are being modified by the states to prevent from occurring in the future. \ Original question: Now that we know the 2020 Presidential race was rigged, will we have a 2021 election?
