**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Marijuana is still a Schedule I drug, which still means that at a Federal level, it is completely illegal. There are no exceptions. However, some states have *decriminalized* its use, either for medical or recreational use, which simply means the *state* will not pursue charges against you. How your employer treats its use is entirely up to them. Whether or not they recognize your **state** medical exemption is, again, up to them.
