**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It seems to be. One of its best features: the batteries are rechargeable. Additionally, the Pebblebee Clip (the one that mimics the small puck-sized AirTag) has its own built-in carry loop, unlike Apple's version which requires a third party accessory. Prices are on par with AirTags, and they work seamlessly with Apple's Find My network, so they're a solid competitor to AirTags. :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-73ddce86a452b64d924f97417cace51d){.landscape .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-73ddce86a452b64d924f97417cace51d" master_w="718" master_h="492"} ::: :::: Both the single Clip and Card sell for \$29.99, and you can get a 4-pack of Clips for \$99.99.
