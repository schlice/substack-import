--- title: '"If there was a giant Kinder Egg that had a gun as the prize, would it be illegal in the US because it was a Kinder Egg or legal because it was a gun?"' date: '2017-06-17' tags: - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It wouldn't happen because Kinder Eggs are marketed to children, and handguns are illegal to be sold to children for a reason, so it would shock the conscience, so it would never be approved. But simply in terms of legality, **if** you could legally import the Kinder Egg (or maybe do the assembly of the eggs in the US), and **if** you had a FFL dealer selling the eggs **and** performing background checks on everyone who buys the eggs, it *might* be legal. But the mere fact of Kinder Egg (i.e. marketing to kids) blows the whole thing out of the water, even if you could make it legal.
