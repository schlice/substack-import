Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You're going to have to try to get an exemption from the Condo Board. You're going to have to make a good case that you should be the exception to the rule (which was likely in place when you bought your condo, so you should have known that you wouldn't be able to rent it out).
