**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What makes Christianity different from other religions, what makes it so believable?** Christianity is the only major world religion in which its believers are saved by *grace alone.* In this respect, there are only two major world religions: Christianity, and "everyone else," as all other major world religions require some form of action (i.e. "human achievement") in order to attain their salvation. Even some subsets of religions which call themselves Christian or retain some Christian theology or belief still require human achievement in order to achieve Heaven or Nirvana or Valhalla or Paradise or whatever they believe is the final end-state of mankind. Certain denominations or cults, such as Jehovah's Witnesses, Seventh-Day Adventists, Church of Christ, Roman Catholics, Russian and Eastern Orthodox, and others still require something that needs to be accomplished or is necessary for you to do in order to achieve salvation. Christianity is the only religion which states that there is absolutely nothing you can do to earn your way into Heaven (i.e. "divine accomplishment"); your salvation has already been bought and paid for. **What makes it so believable?** It's actually quite believable. What's fascinating is how unbelievable all other world religions are in this respect. When Mormons come to my house, I'll often ask them questions about what gets them to heaven, and we'll usually have a discussion about just how much they've done to merit that reward. I'll ask where, on a scale of 0 to 100, they think they fall, in terms of just how good they've been. No one has yet told me 100, as everyone acknowledges some failings. Why wouldn't they? It's only human. Then I ask them what they believe God expects, based on what they have read in the Scriptures, and each says 100. It's true, God expects perfection. Jesus even expands on this, making the Ten Commandments even more stringent. "You have heard that it was said, 'You shall not commit adultery.' But I tell you that anyone who looks at a woman lustfully has already committed adultery with her in his heart." (Matthew 5:27--28 NIV) Of course, based on that standard, who among us is *not* guilty of at least some sin? No one. We are all sinful, and we have all fallen short of God's standards. Therefore, it is impossible for anyone to do enough to get themselves into Heaven. It is *only* possible with God. Only God can save you through his grace.
