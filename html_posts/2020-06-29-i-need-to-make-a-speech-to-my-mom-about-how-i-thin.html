**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **I need to make a speech to my mom about how I think that God does not exist. Can you help me by providing evidence to support this claim?** No, because you cannot prove the non-existence of something. For example, I can tell you that purple eagles exist, and you can tell me that they don\'t. For me to prove they do, I just have to find some evidence that points to the fact that they do or did exist at some time. For you to prove that they don\'t, or never did, you literally have to be able to visit all places at all times to show that there were no purple eagles there. Clearly, it is impossible to prove that something never existed or does not exist. Your best bet is to take another angle. Show that he doesn\'t exist by trying to argue that the description of God doesn\'t match reality. For example, if God is infinitely merciful, then why does he allow suffering? If God is all-powerful, why did he have to send his son to Earth as a man? Why doesn\'t he simply reveal himself to the world now, instead of having us suffer through all these false religions and atheists if they are all wrong? (By the way, there are excellent answers to all of these questions already; I\'m just giving you the best options you can to try to make your case)
