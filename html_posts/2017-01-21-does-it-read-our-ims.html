Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} If you are asking whether or not the company is actually sitting there reading your IMs, then likely not. But are they *monitoring* your IMs? Probably. If they are smart, they monitor ***all*** traffic in and out of the network. They have automated systems that look for *keywords* that might indicate company sensitive information is leaving the network. You using a company network to IM implies your consent to such monitoring.
