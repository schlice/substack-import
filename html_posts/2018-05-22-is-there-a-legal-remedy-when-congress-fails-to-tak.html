Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Is there a legal remedy when congress fails to take action against improper presidential actions?** Absolutely. It is perfectly legal to not vote for the offending politicians in the next election. This is the "legal remedy" built into our system of government. When the elected representatives of the people fail to adequately represent the people, the people retain the right to not vote for them again, and they peacefully leave office. They don't get to stay in power forever. They only get to stay in power as long as the people continue to vote for them.
