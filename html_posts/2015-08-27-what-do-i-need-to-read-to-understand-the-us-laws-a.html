**Content:** [Have you ever seen a law library? Guess what those books are? Half of them are Federal and State laws, and the other half are documented court cases which comprise the bulk of *case law*.\ \ There are volumes upon volumes of laws to read, so many in fact, that it would be far too challenging to read them all.\ \ My advice: learn the generalities about US law. Take a civics class. Learn how the government works, learn how the Constitution works. Learn the differences between Federal and state laws.\ \ Then, when you have specific questions about laws, you can look those up individually.]{.rendered_qtext}
