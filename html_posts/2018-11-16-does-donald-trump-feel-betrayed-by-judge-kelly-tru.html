**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Does Donald Trump feel betrayed by Judge Kelly, Trump\'s appointee, who just ruled that Jim Acosta\'s White House press pass be reinstated?** **He probably does, but he shouldn't.** If Trump did his job, he should have appointed judges who follow the law and the Constitution. Apparently, there is at least a question of whether Acosta's 5th Amendment due process rights were violated, and a temporary restraining order was issued to, at least temporarily, preserve that right. This is a decision which, while I find curious how the courts intend to override White House security protocol with a Constitutional amendment, I can get behind. Again, I don't have to like it or agree with it, but I do have to at least support it temporarily, as it is only a temporary restraining order.
