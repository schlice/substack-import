**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How can I purchase a car with suspended license but have driving privelages \[sic\]?** You don't need a license to buy a car. It certainly helps (it's hard to do a test drive from the passenger seat), but it's not mandatory. You do have to have insurance for the car, or be able to get insurance, but a driver's license is not required to buy a car.
