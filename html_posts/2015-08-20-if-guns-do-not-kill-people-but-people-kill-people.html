Governments sometimes believe that they do (or at least be able to defend themselves against others who believe they do), which is why they have tactical nukes.\ \ Since individuals aren\'t killing mass quantities of people, thermonuclear devices for individuals do not make logical sense.]{.rendered_qtext}
