**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If someone says that guns are bad and nobody should own one, what kind of fallacy would that be?** It's not a fallacy in logic. It's simply an opinion. If you were to say instead, "Guns are bad; everyone knows that, so therefore no one should own them," that's what we call an **appeal to omniscience**, because you are claiming that "everyone" knows something, but in order to claim that, you have to know what "everyone" knows.
