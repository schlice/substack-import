**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} My vet, who saw this dog from the day she was 6 months old until the day we transitioned her on to the next life, cried with us. She loved my dog as much as I did. She pushed the injection in, and had to leave the room because of her tears. There is no shame in shedding a tear for ones who touched our lives as intimately as our pets do. It shows you're human and you actually care. That's a good thing. Vets are totally understanding. Modern vet facilities even have a special room set aside for when we have to send our pets on, and it has its own dedicated exit so you don't have to traipse back through the lobby blubbering like a baby. This is a very new design trend, and it hasn't totally caught on yet, so don't be surprised if your vet doesn't offer this.
