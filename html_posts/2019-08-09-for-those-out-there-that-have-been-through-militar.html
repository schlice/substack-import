**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **For those out there that have been through military basic training, are you or anyone you know immune to CS gas or pepper spray?** Military training involves CS tear gas. Only specialized units use pepper spray (i.e. military law enforcement, and not in every situation). Everyone trains on CS tear gas in the military, because it has immediate, debilitating effects, but it rapidly clears from the body with virtually no incidence of long-term effects. I went through it as a trainee, and I administered it as a trainer. I have never seen someone be able to remove their gas mask and breathe normally. What I have seen is a difference in the amount of time it takes to affect someone. Most people are immediately impacted by the gas. Besides the choking, you literally get fluid from every pore in your face: eyes water, nose runs, saliva, and for most, it's instantaneous. Just this massive explosion of snot and tears. Some can actually take a few breaths before starting to choke and tear up. But everyone is affected by the gas.
