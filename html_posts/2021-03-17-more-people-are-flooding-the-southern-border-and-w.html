How is this Biden\'s fault?** Biden campaigned on loosening immigration controls. He also indicated that he would halt construction of the border wall, would end Trump's policy of "wait in Mexico" for asylum, and would halt all deportations. But trying to placate his base while ignoring the need for more enforcement, without a solid plan for the (predictable) increase in migrant activity at the border has only made this problem worse. More migrants are now attempting riskier desert crossings, placing more people at risk. Surges in immigration tend to be coupled with hope. Biden gave hope of a kinder, gentler immigration process, without addressing what to do when the surge actually took place. **TL;DR: The current crisis at the border is 100% caused by Biden's campaigning promises and subsequent policy initiatives.**
