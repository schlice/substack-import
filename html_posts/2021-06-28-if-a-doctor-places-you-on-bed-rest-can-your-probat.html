Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} If you don't even call your PO and tell him what the doctor said, then maybe he'll try to revoke you for failing to report. But if you at least work with him and tell him that your doctor ordered bed rest, revoking you for not reporting would be something you could appeal to the Parole Board. So long as you can show that you tried to work with the PO and he's being stupid about it, you should be fine. \ Original question: If a doctor places you on bed rest, can your probation officer revoke you for not reporting?
