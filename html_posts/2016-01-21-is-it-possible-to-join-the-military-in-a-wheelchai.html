{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Unfortunately, you must be able to walk in order to join the military. It is considered a [[disqualifying medical condition.](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=e6f666&url=http://www.military.com/join-armed-forces/disqualifiers-medical-conditions.html){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="military.com"}]{.qlink_container} You also have to have a certain level of mobility; you can see all of those requirements at the link I posted.
