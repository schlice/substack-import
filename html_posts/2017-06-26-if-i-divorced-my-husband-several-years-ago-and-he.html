--- title: '"If I divorced my husband several years ago and he just passed away a couple of months ago, would my marital status legally be divorced or widowed?"' date: '2017-06-26' tags: - policy-analysis layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You are only widowed if your husband dies while you are married. If your divorce was finalized and accepted by the court two years ago, then you are divorced. You have been divorced for two years. The death of your former spouse does not change your marital status.
