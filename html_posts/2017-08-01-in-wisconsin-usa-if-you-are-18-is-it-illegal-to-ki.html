--- title: "In Wisconsin, USA, if you are 18, is it illegal to kiss someone who is 16?" date: "2017-08-01" tags: ["law"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Dating is not illegal. Holding hands is not illegal. Kissing is not illegal. **Sexual intercourse** with someone under the age of 18 **is** **illegal** in Wisconsin. Also, Wisconsin does not have a "close in age" law, so if an 18 year old has sex with a 16 year old, the 18 year old can be charged with statutory rape. Also in Wisconsin, if both participants are underage, they can **both** be charged with statutory rape, although this would be the exception rather than the rule. *(Source:* [[*Wisconsin Age of Consent & Statutory Rape Laws*](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=ffcdd7&url=https://www.ageofconsent.net/states/wisconsin){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="ageofconsent.net"}]{.qlink_container}*)*
