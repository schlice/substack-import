Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can an HOA require a homeowner to decorate for the holidays?** Unless that Association also requires homeowners to be part of the group for the holiday required, no. For example, if your association only allows Jews to buy houses in your area, then the HOA could also require homeowners to decorate for Hanukkah. However, since housing cannot be conditioned on religion, this would be illegal, so therefore, an HOA can similarly not force a homeowner to decorate for the holidays.
