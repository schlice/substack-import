Answer










Content: [Happens in every war. We know it happens. It takes a long
time for enough soldiers to exhibit enough symptoms to be able to start
seeing patterns in their symptoms and attributing them to certain
environmental exposures. It\'s not an overnight deal.\
\
That said, we still take care of our troops and we treat the symptoms
they present with, regardless of whether or not we have identified
certain exposures as causing longer-term health
issues.]
