--- title: '"What is the process for an HOA to determine if a homeowner has violated rules? Who is responsible for making this decision?"' date: '2024-10-02' tags: - law - homeowners-assn layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} As with all legal questions, the answer is "it depends." The specifics will always be found in the individual HOA's Governing Documents. In some smaller HOAs, the Board maintains this responsibility. In others, the management company will routinely patrol and note violations. In still others, the HOA appoints a committee to handle violations.
