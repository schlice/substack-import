**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} > *Yesterday is history.*\ > *Tomorrow is a mystery.*\ > *Today is a gift. That\'s why we call it the present.* \ Live for today. Don\'t live in your past with regret. There is nothing you can do about your past. What\'s done is done. Take stock of what you have today.\ \ I would trade your condition for mine in a hot second. TRUST ME when I tell you that there are others who are far worse off than you are (regardless of the cause of their conditions), and they still make the best of their lives. I might just be one of them.\ \ My decision to serve my country left me with multiple sclerosis. Wanna trade? Yeah, didn\'t think so.\ \ However, there are people who rely on me, people who look to me for their guidance, people who I brought into this world and now I have the responsibility to care for and to lead and to teach. Because my legs don\'t want to work some days doesn\'t mean that I\'m less of a person because of it. If you lose an arm, or a leg, are you less of a person? Are your body parts what makes you a person, or is it something else?\ \ You are still you, even with your condition. Be the best you that you can be.
