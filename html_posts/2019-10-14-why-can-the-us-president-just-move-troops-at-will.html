**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Why can the U.S. President just move troops at will?** Why can the CEO of a corporation relocate a factory from one city to another? Because he's the CEO. Why can the President move troops at will? Because he's the Commander-in-Chief of US Armed Forces. When he says "Move these troops there," his generals say "Yes, Sir" and they move the troops. Understand he doesn't make these decisions unilaterally or without counsel. He does this after discussing the issues with his generals and the component commanders as are seated on the Joint Chiefs of Staff.
