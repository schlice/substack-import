**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} There are some very limited reports of people with cancer in remission receiving a vaccine and then seeing increased cancer activity. However, there is not enough evidence to indicate that the vaccine causes relapses. Remember: correlation does not equal causation. The vaccine may indeed cause enlarged axillary lymph nodes, which can be mistaken for a cancer flare. And of course, the warning about taking the vaccine while immunocompromised still exists. However, there doesn't appear to be a causal link between taking the vaccine and a relapse in a cancer in remission. \ Original question: Did cancer that had been in remission for some people suddenly come back after getting the covid shot?
