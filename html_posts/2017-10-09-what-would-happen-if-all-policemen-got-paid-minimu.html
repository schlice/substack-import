**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Minimum wage is reserved for *entry-level* jobs. The whole point of minimum wage is to pay it towards a job that you are trying *not* to incentivize people to make their careers. The idea behind minimum wage is to give young kids some opportunity to work and gain a little experience with things like punctuality, dealing with customers, and gaining a little time in a job before moving on to your career. Police are career professionals. Police work is definitely not an entry-level job, and is definitely not the kind of job where you want entry-level people. Pay an entry-level wage, and you'll get entry-level employees.
