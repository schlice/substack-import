--- title: '"Does having a baby give you the right to a disabled parking spot?"' date: '2016-01-11' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [No. The only persons who can park in a handicapped spot are persons who have been issued a handicapped hang tag or license plate by the DMV. Those are issued when a doctor has verified an issue which limits mobility. It may not be a visible issue.\ \ Generally, having a baby does not impair one\'s mobility to such a degree that they would qualify for a handicap tag, but that call is made by the doctor. The mother must have a medical condition caused by childbirth that so limits mobility that they qualify for handicap parking.]{.rendered_qtext}
