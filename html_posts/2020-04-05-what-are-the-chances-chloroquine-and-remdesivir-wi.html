**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What are the chances chloroquine and Remdesivir will cure COVID 19?** Zero. These are most definitely not "cures" for COVID-19. A "cure" would be something that completely kills the virus, or completely and with 100% efficacy, immunizes us against it. Chloroquine and some other drugs being tested (azithromycin, remdesivir, etc) can "...alter the pH of the compartment in which some viruses are brought into the cell. This can interfere with the process of depositing the virus\' genome inside the cell and thus block the virus\' ability to reproduce."[\[1\]](#rIvSK){#cite-rIvSK .citation_link} At best, these drugs are antivirals in side-effect only. At best, these drugs make it harder for the virus to replicate in the body, making it less harmful and reducing the severity and duration of an infection, but crucially, **they don't prevent you from becoming infected or experiencing any symptoms**. A "functional cure" would be something akin to a vaccine. These drugs are most certainly not vaccines.
