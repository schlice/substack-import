**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} > ***As there is a clear correlation between gun owners and deaths > related to guns in America.*** Your question is based on inaccurate information. There is no clear correlation between gun ownership and gun-related death. In fact, exactly the opposite exists: there is an inverse relationship between gun ownership and gun deaths. It is an inverse relationship simply because there is no correlation between **lawful** gun ownership and **crime**. None. Whatsoever. There is no causal link between the number of firearms laws, the number of firearms owned, the number of licensed concealed carriers, or any other number related to lawful gun ownership, and crime. Why? Because **lawful gun owners don't commit crimes with their guns.** If you have some evidence (not fake news stories) to prove your point, please present it. > ***What do you (Americans) think of abolishing the 2nd amendment?*** There is no political will to do so. There is no political will to do so because Americans aren't about to dismantle our rights, rights that tens of millions of Americans enjoy without committing any crimes whatsoever, because of a few wing nuts going all nutty.
