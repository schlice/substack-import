Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If a national emergency lets the President take funds from other government programs for something, can he take funds from investigations into him?** The National Emergencies Act allows the President to activate any one of about 130 specific provisions of Federal law by declaring a national emergency. Stopping investigations is not one of those provisions.
