{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [We don\'t call them \"APBs\" (All-Points Bulletin) any more. These days, we release a **BOLO** (Be On the LookOut). BOLOs are distributed verbally during shift change, and also distributed electronically when the technology allows it. Officers are then required to \"be on the lookout\" for the suspect person, vehicle, stolen item, etc. Nearly every organization has them, and you may even receive BOLOs from neighboring agencies if their search parameters spread wide enough.\ \ BOLOs are general orders to look out for specific things. When the search area becomes more concentrated, and dedicated resources are tasked to a search, your BOLO turns into a **manhunt.**]{.rendered_qtext}
