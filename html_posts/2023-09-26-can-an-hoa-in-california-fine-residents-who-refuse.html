--- title: "Can an HOA in California fine residents who refuse to install an EV charger due to lack of space (small lot size/short driveway)?" date: "2023-09-26" tags: ["law", "homeowners-assn"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} If the HOA has within its Governing Documents the requirement to have an EV charger on your home, then they can issue fines for failing to follow that provision. But if they arbitrarily decreed that all homes must have an EV charger installed, and there is nothing in their Governing Documents that permits them to make such a decree, they may not have the authority to do so.
