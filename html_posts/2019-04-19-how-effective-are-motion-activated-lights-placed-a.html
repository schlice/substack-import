**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How effective are motion activated lights placed around my home in an effort to deter burglars?** As a part of your "defense in depth" strategy, using motion-activated lights are very effective, especially to light up corners and secluded areas around your house which are easy for burglars to hide in and take their time entering your home.
