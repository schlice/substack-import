--- title: '"How many states are enforcing bans on abortion at all stages of pregnancy, and how does that number compare to bans in states after about six weeks of pregnancy?"' date: '2024-04-12' tags: - policy-analysis layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} As of April 8, 2024, there are 15 states which completely ban abortion at all stages, with very limited exceptions. As of April 8, 2024, Georgia and Florida are the only states which ban abortion after only six weeks of pregnancy. Other limited abortion states include North Carolina, Utah, Nebraska, and Arizona, but they are all somewhere between six and fifteen weeks. [[https://states.guttmacher.org/po\...](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=60e836&url=https://states.guttmacher.org/policies/georgia/abortion-policies){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="guttmacher.org"}]{.qlink_container} Florida is unique because their state supreme court upheld the constitutionality of a 15-week ban, but the state passed a 6-week ban which would only go into effect if and when the state supreme court found that the 15-week ban was constitutional.
