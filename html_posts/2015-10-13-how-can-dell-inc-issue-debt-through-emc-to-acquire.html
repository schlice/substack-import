**Content:** [*NOTE: I am an employee, but I have no more inside information than what you\'ve seen in the press releases.*\ \ \ \ Not sure where you got your information, but Dell is not \"issuing debt through EMC.\" Dell, along with their equity capital partners, are essentially acquiring new debt to finance this deal. Dell will spend the next few years following the completion of this transaction (which is expected to be 2Q16) paying down that debt, but their expected revenue from the combined operation more than permits them to pay down that debt. The eight or so banks that have already signed agreements with Dell to finance the deal seem to think so, anyway.]{.rendered_qtext}
