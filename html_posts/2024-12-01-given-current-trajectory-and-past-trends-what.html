**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} So let's look at "current trajectory." Since this question was posted in the "Christians and Christianity" Space, it's fair to look at Christianity. Therefore, "current trajectory" is *increasing* worldwide. In 2023, the Christian population was estimated to be around 2.604 billion, a 44.5 million increase from mid-2022. By 2024, the Christian population is expected to grow to 2.63 billion. And by 2050, the Christian population is projected to reach 3 billion or more. So given current trajectory, the concept of organized religion will never disappear.
