**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How can you find the fire rate of the gun within bursts like the XR-2 from COD BO3 instead of the overall fire rate?** Burst fire won't cycle any faster than the cyclic rate of the gun, so the overall cyclic rate should be able to tell you the burst rate as well.
