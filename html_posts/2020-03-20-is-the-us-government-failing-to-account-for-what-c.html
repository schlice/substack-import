**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Is the U.S. government failing to account for what could be thousands of additional infections because of ongoing problems with testing?** Clearly, we will miss some infection by only testing those with symptoms. Remember, **people can be contagious with no visible outward symptoms of disease**. However, while this does impact our accounting, it has no effect on the spread of the virus. Only isolation and social distancing can prevent the spread of the virus. Testing does not prevent or cure disease.
