**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Several things in your comment differ from the question: 1. High-speed chase. In a chase, the officers do not expect the vehicle to stop, so officers are not generally looking for that. They expect the motorcycle to gain some distance (using the aforementioned acceleration) and for the driver to bail. They're not expecting him to stop, and they're certainly not expecting him to stop hard. 2. Better braking. A motorcycle can definitely stop in a much shorter distance than a car.
