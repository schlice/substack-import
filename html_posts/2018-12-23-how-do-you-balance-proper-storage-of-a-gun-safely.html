**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How do you balance proper storage of a gun safely with having it ready for immediate use in case of an emergency?** Store it in a semi ready condition, and get a quality safe with some method of rapid unlock. I store my firearms in the safe with fully loaded magazines but empty chambers. My gun safes have rapid biometric entry (but keyed backups). I can nearly instantly get in to my safes.
