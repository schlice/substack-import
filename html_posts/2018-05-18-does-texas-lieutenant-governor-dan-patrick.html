**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Does Texas Lieutenant Governor Dan Patrick\'s idea about reducing the number of entrances and exits in schools to prevent school shootings have merit?** He may have misspoken slightly, but he's absolutely right. We need to limit the number of entrances into schools, and we need to limit the use of all of the other doors. All of them. Make one teacher entrance controlled by key fob or entry card. Other than that, all other entrances are secured. How many entrances and exits are there to the county courthouse (which he also mentioned)? There's one entrance you can use. The rest are there, but you can't use them. Of course, there are other exits, but have you ever heard of a **fire door**? :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-6a54f78024f9d8985655582b3db024fa){.landscape .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-6a54f78024f9d8985655582b3db024fa" master_w="2592" master_h="1750"} ::: :::: Turn all other doors except for the main entrance and the secured teacher entrance into **alarmed** fire doors. Post security guards at the two doors. This is really not difficult, people.
