**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Since the Vatican is an independent country with its own laws, and its also filled with priests, is raping little boys legal there?** No. The Holy See adopted most of its laws directly from Italian law. It also includes several United Nations Resolutions and some other additions and edits. It was most recently updated in 2013, when they clarified the laws on crimes against children, in response to some of the latest issues they were facing.
