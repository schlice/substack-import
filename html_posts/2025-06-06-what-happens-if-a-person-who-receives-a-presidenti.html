--- title: "What happens if a person who receives a presidential pardon owes restitution to their victims?" date: "2025-06-06" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} If the restitution was not paid before the pardon was issued, or if the restitution was paid and the Treasury Department had not yet disbursed it, the person gets to keep it, or gets it back. If the restitution was already paid and the Treasury Department already disbursed it, then the person won't get it back.
