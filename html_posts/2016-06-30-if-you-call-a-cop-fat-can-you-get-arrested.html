--- title: '"If you call a cop fat, can you get arrested?"' date: '2016-06-30' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You can't get arrested in the US for calling a cop fat. What you *will get* is a lot of attention from the police that you probably didn't want in the first place. And they may find something that they *can* write you a ticket on, which will piss you off more than the cop's weight. So unless you like irritating yourself, I'd keep the comments to a minimum around the cops.
