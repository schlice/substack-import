**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How can I hide my device information on a public WiFi?** I'm going to assume by "device information" you mean your hardware specs and your MAC address. In most standard Android and iOS builds, you can't change your hardware identifiers, and you can't spoof your MAC address. Under normal circumstances, you must "jailbreak" or "root" your device and install special software that allows you to "tweak" these values so that the network sees them differently. As for hiding your actual data traffic, use a VPN. VPN software is built in to most devices these days, and Samsung actually has a VPN service that it offers for a monthly subscription. It automatically engages when you are on a public unsecured Wi-Fi.
