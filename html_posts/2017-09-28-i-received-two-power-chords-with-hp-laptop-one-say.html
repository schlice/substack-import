--- title: '"I received two power chords with HP laptop. One says 7A, 125V and other is 2A, 250V. Which one should I use?"' date: '2017-09-28' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It depends on what country you live in. If you live in America (both North and South), and your country runs off of 110V power, use the 7A cord. If you live in Europe where you have 220V power, use the 2A cord.
