**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} What would happen is that "may issue" laws in any state in which they exist would immediately become unconstitutional, and would effectively become unenforceable. The state would be required to replace the "may issue" laws with laws which comply with the Constitution, laws which complied with the Supreme Court's ruling. These would, in essence, be "shall issue" laws.
