**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} "Non-lethal" implies that the round never causes death. Even beanbag rounds can kill if you shoot them at close enough range at vital enough areas. The terminology "non-lethal" is being replaced by "less than lethal" in most circumstances, since it more accurately describes the function of the round.
