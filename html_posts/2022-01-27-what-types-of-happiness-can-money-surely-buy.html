Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Money can buy happiness, but only up to a certain point.** Numerous studies have found that there is a dollar-for-dollar increase in happiness (or reduction in negative emotions) up until about \$70,000. Then, as dollars increase, the reduction in negative emotions decreases, up to a zero reduction at around \$200,000. :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-387a0c32b1406e3c34f3a4f8184d25b7){.landscape .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-387a0c32b1406e3c34f3a4f8184d25b7" master_w="2208" master_h="1931"} ::: :::: \ Original question: What types of happiness can money surely buy?
