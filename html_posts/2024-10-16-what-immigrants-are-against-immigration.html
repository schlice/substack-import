**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Your question is not precise enough. Precision in language is critically important. And it's why you are confused. Immigrants are indeed against immigration. ***But more precisely***, ***legal*** immigrants are against ***illegal*** immigration. "Illegal immigration" is a misnomer anyway. You can only be an "immigrant" if you follow the laws of this country in order to get here. "Illegal immigrants" is a term made up by politicians to confuse you into thinking that certain political parties are "anti-immigrant." But they're not. They are only against criminals. Anyone who breaks the law is, by definition, a criminal.
