**Content:** [It is neither of the items you have cited. Specifically, it is ***their*** interpretation of their religion that leads them to do the things that they do. Most Muslim scholars will tell you that this is not an \"exact\" interpretation of their religion. As with most religions, the texts must be taken in context and as a whole. Cherry-picking certain passages and using them to model your behavior is a poor way to handle historical texts which must be read in context and with the whole of the document in mind.\ \ Poor interpretation of religious texts has led to some very bad consequences in the past. The Spanish Inquisition and the Crusades are examples from the Roman Catholic church, so in my opinion, no religion is immune from such invalid thought.]{.rendered_qtext}
