**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} So you asked if this was "okay." Okay, in this context, has a few meanings. If you mean "okay" as in "is it legal," the answer is yes in the US anyway. Your mother is still your legal guardian and makes decisions on your behalf until the day you turn 18. If you mean "okay" as in "is it good parenting to ignore your wishes" then the answer is probably not.
