**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How do you think Trump will try to get out of this Manafort and Cohen situation?** There's nothing to "get out of." At worst, if you squint hard and look sideways at it, Trump may have violated campaign finance laws by paying off Stormy Daniels to keep her quiet. However, that's unlikely because there are substantial non-political reasons why he would have wanted to pay off Stormy Daniels, which makes the payments permissible. Nevertheless, the worst possible outcome for Trump is that his campaign is found to have violated campaign finance laws, and he pays a fine. Just like Obama's campaign. They paid a fine. Life went on. I hate to bring up the elephant in the room, but someone has to: *literally none of this has anything whatsoever to do with Russian collusion in the 2016 election.* Sure, a couple of guys may have broken Federal law. Manafort didn't pay his taxes correctly. Cohen also had tax issues. Cohen paid hush money to a porn star. But none of this has anything to do with Russia, or collusion, or using Russia to rig an election. None of it. So again, there's nothing to "get out of." This situation will resolve itself. Two guys go to jail for their own crimes. Life goes on.
