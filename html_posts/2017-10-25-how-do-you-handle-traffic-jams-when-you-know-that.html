--- title: '"How do you handle traffic jams when you know that there is nothing that you can do about it?"' date: '2017-10-25' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} If they are expected, try to leave a little earlier or a little later, so you can avoid them. If they are unexpected, then there is nothing that you can really do about them. Try to find an activity that you can do while you\'re sitting in traffic that you enjoy. Maybe you can read a book, or listen to a book on tape. Maybe you\'d like to write a book. Maybe you like Angry Birds. I\'m sure that there\'s something that you can find to fill the time.
