**Content:** [To expound on [[Suzanne Walsh](https://www.quora.com/profile/Suzanne-Walsh-5)]{.qlink_container}\'s answer\ \ Does it cause real harm? Depends on what you call \"real harm.\" Does it perpetuate an agenda instead of pure, unadulterated fact? Absolutely.\ \ Is that \"harmful?\" Only insofar as people will listen to exactly what they want to hear, and not the pure, unadulterated truth. It \"harms\" people because it doesn\'t allow for individuals to think for themselves. It provides the facts, and also provides an opinion about those facts.]{.rendered_qtext}
