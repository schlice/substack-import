Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Fornication is not illegal anywhere in the United States. There are laws surrounding certain categories of fornication, and those laws pertain to the age of the participants. In most places in the United States, the ***age of consent*** is 18. Having sex when one participant is below the age of consent is a crime called **statutory rape**. Several states have conditions on their statutory rape laws called **close-in-age laws**. These laws state that if the two participants are "close in age" to each other, the statutory rape law does not apply. For example, a 19 year old having sex with a 17 year old. The idea behind these laws is that it is more abhorrent for a 40 year old to have sex with a 17 year old than it is for a 19 year old to have sex with a 17 year old. This is not true in every state, and not all states have close-in-age laws, so check your local laws if anyone might be under 18.
