Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I had a good bout of Optic Neuritis which never really fully healed. I still have trouble with fluorescent lighting, and running causes my eyesight to go all wonky and I see double vision. I hate running now. I also have weakness on the entire right side of my body. I frequently trip over nothing because I haven't raised my foot up enough. I also frequently drop things from my right hand because my grip isn't as strong as I think it is. Going up stairs is a fun challenge. If I'm not actively concentrating on walking, I'll usually trip. I've been fortunate to be on medication from the very first symptom I ever had, so I've been able to at least slow down the progression of my disease. Beyond the symptoms I've described, I seem to have managed to keep my MS from getting worse. For now.
