--- title: '"Can state flags fly to the same height to the flag of United States?"' date: '2015-09-14' tags: - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [It depends on the configuration of flags. According to US Flag Code (which is part of Federal law), in a group of flagpoles, the US flag has to be farthest right (from the perspective of the building that they adorn, but they can all be the same height).\ \ ]{.rendered_qtext} :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-e88e4356bd4ad24c8844d5f42f1fb618){.landscape .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-e88e4356bd4ad24c8844d5f42f1fb618" master_w="181" master_h="154"} ::: :::: \ If the flags are on the same flagpole, the US flag has to be on top.\ \ :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-6af0ba3b573aa70ba999f9b316943574){.landscape .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-6af0ba3b573aa70ba999f9b316943574" master_w="259" master_h="194"} ::: :::: \ \ If you have a group of flagpoles and the center pole is higher than the others, the US flag must fly on the highest pole, and the other flags can be distributed around it.\ \ :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-db435f8d49b95d0e5849669c81494888){.landscape .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-db435f8d49b95d0e5849669c81494888" master_w="118" master_h="114"} ::: ::::
