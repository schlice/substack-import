**Content:** [This isn\'t a question we can yet answer. It\'s like asking \"is it true that white lab coats causes cancer in laboratory animals?\" It\'s not a question we can answer because it simply hasn\'t been discovered yet.\ \ We don\'t yet know the cause of Multiple Sclerosis, althogh there are some good ideas. We know that women get MS at a rate of two to three times that of men, so we know that there is some hormonology involved. To what extent, and how, that works, is still being investigated.\ \ There are other factors as well: ethnicity, geography of childhood, genetics, and that all-important and as-of-yet undiscovered environmental trigger (likely a virus) that all play into whether or not someone will develop MS.]{.rendered_qtext}
