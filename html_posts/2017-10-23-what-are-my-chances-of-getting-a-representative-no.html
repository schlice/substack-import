--- title: '"What are my chances of getting a representative nomination for USAFA in Wisconsin?"' date: '2017-10-23' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It depends on how many people apply in your year and how many available openings your representative has at that institution. Each Representative can have no more than five cadets in any service academy at any one time, so your chances depend on how many slots that Representative has currently open, how many nominations he/she plans on giving, and how many people apply. As far as whether or not any one representative will be more or less difficult from which to secure a nomination, no one can really say. They will all use some combination of application, test, essay writing, and board interview in order to grant a nomination, so at least that much will be similar.
