**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The twelve tribes of Israel are descendants of Jacob, son of Isaac, and grandson of Abraham (who was renamed Israel, who the 12 tribes are named after). The patriarchs of the 12 tribes of Israel are the children of Jacob. But there are a lot of other descendants than just the direct lineage between Noah and Jacob and the 12 Tribes. :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-b0593a10086ce70befcd572be4dcbea2){.landscape .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-b0593a10086ce70befcd572be4dcbea2" master_w="1607" master_h="1088"} ::: :::: *(Image courtesy Drnhawkins,* [[*CC BY-SA 3.0*](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=dce62c&url=https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="creativecommons.org"}]{.qlink_container}*, via* [[*Wikimedia Commons*](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=4e3eea&url=https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Genealogy_noah.jpg){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="wikimedia.org"}]{.qlink_container}*)* As you can see, there are many other descendants outside of the direct lineage from Noah to Abraham.
