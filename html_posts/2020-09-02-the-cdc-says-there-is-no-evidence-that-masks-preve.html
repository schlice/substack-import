**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **The CDC says there is no evidence that masks prevent catching coronavirus, but why does the CDC wear them?** There is no definitive study that says that cloth face coverings "prevent catching coronavirus." However, several anecdotal studies cited by the CDC absolutely show that wearing cloth face coverings reduce the spread of the virus. One study came from the Journal of the American Medical Association (JAMA) and shows that adherence to universal masking policies reduced SARS-CoV-2 transmission within a Boston hospital system. [\[1\]](#CsSBW){#cite-CsSBW .citation_link}The other one came from CDC's Morbidity and Mortality Weekly Report (MMWR), and it shows that wearing a mask prevented the spread of infection from two hair stylists to their customers in Missouri. [\[2\]](#NKyor){#cite-NKyor .citation_link} Therefore, CDC issues **recommendations**, since it has no authority to order citizens of any state to do anything. The CDC absolutely recommends wearing face coverings while out in public.[\[3\]](#vBgyD){#cite-vBgyD .citation_link} Footnotes [\[1\]](#cite-CsSBW){.citation_index} [[Time for Universal Masking and Prevention of Transmission of SARS-CoV-2](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=dde64d&url=https://jamanetwork.com/journals/jama/fullarticle/10.1001/jama.2020.13107){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="jamanetwork.com"}]{.qlink_container} [\[2\]](#cite-NKyor){.citation_index} [[Absence of Apparent Transmission of SARS-CoV-2 from Two Stylists\...](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=50be06&url=https://www.cdc.gov/mmwr/volumes/69/wr/mm6928e2.htm?s_cid%3Dmm6928e2_w){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="cdc.gov"}]{.qlink_container} [\[3\]](#cite-vBgyD){.citation_index} [[Coronavirus Disease 2019](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=241264&url=https://www.cdc.gov/media/releases/2020/p0714-americans-to-wear-masks.html){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="cdc.gov"}]{.qlink_container}
