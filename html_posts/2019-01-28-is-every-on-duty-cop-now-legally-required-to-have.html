**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Is every on duty cop now legally required to have a body camera?** In the context of this question, I'll assuming you're referring to the nearly one million police officers in the more than 18,000 law enforcement agencies across the country. And in that context, I can emphatically say **no**, not every cop is legally required to have a body camera. Maybe every cop in a single city, or maybe even every cop in a single state, *may* be required to have a body camera, but that would require levels of funding that not every department or every state has, and it is highly dependent on the state or the city. In fact, there are scant few *laws* that require body cameras anywhere in the country. Use of body cameras is generally regulated by the department, at the direction of their governing body. It is not generally mandated by law. Mostly this is due to the aforementioned funding issues surrounding use of the cameras.
