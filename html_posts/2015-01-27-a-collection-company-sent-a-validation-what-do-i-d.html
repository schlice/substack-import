Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [It looks like they gave you what you were asking for: an accounting of the debt they believe you owe. From here depends on a few factors:\ ]{.rendered_qtext} 1. Did you ever have an account with them? If you never had an account with them, that is the basis for your reply.\ 2. Assuming you had an account with them, did you actually accrue the charges listed on their photocopy? If you didn\'t actually accrue the charges, then you are contesting their accounting of your service.\ 3. Assuming you did accrue the charges they showed you, did you pay the charges? If you did, you should be easily able to show them dates and amounts of your remissions. I\'d probably show them whatever proof I had and then tell them to cease and desist with further attempts to collect from you unless they choose to pursue the matter in court.\ \ If they continue to attempt to collect after you have provided your proof, you will have to fight them in court, and add harassment to the list of charges. At this point, a lawyer\'s assistance will be needed.
