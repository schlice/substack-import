--- title: '"What is the difference between low intensity conflict and law enforcement operations?"' date: '2015-09-12' tags: - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [The entity performing the operations, the reason for the operation, the entities involved, and the theater of operations.\ \ **Low-Intensity Conflict**\ [Entity performing operations:]{.underline} National military\ [Reason for operations:]{.underline} Defense of the nation and/or national interests.\ [Entities involved:]{.underline} Militaries of more than one nation\ [Theater of operations:]{.underline} Almost always occurs outside the borders of the nation.\ \ **Law Enforcement operations**\ [Entity performing operations:]{.underline} State law enforcement agencies\ [Reason for operations]{.underline}: Peacekeeping and/or law enforcement\ [Entities involved:]{.underline} Law enforcement agencies and civilians within that nation.\ [Theater of operations]{.underline}: Within the borders of a nation.]{.rendered_qtext}
**Content language:** English
