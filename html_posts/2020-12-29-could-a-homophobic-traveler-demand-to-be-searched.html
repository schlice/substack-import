**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Could a homophobic traveler demand to be searched by a woman instead of a male officer at airport or border checks?** Yep. They can demand whatever they want. However, especially at border checks, there is no requirement to acquiesce to any of those demands. The choice would be: 1. Allow this man to search you 2. Do not cross the border. The traveler would have to determine which was more important: his homophobia, or crossing the border. The choice would be up to him. See? Life is all about choices.
