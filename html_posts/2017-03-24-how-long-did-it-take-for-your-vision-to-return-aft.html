--- title: '"How long did it take for your vision to return after you were diagnosed for optic neuritis?"' date: '2017-03-24' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} My vision recovered to the point where the ON was no longer a significant factor after about 4 months. It generally takes anywhere from 3 to 6 months for ON to resolve itself, but it can be shorter, or even significantly longer. Depending on the cause of the ON, symptoms can remain for a significant period following the onset of the condition.
