**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Are Zyrtec or Claritin safe to take daily all year round? Are there issues with long term use?** There are no documented, well-known issues with long-term use *so far*. These second-generation antihistamines have only been around for roughly ten years, so there isn't much longer-term studies that have been done than that amount of time. But from what we have so far, there isn't anything which has been documented which shows any known long-term issue with their use. There are, of course, anecdotal issues that occur. But there is nothing documented to show repeatable, known long-term issues.
