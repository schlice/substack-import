Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} If you don\'t like being told what to do, ever, it\'s a nuisance. If you believe that you should be able to do whatever the hell you like with your own property and neighbors be damned, then you\'ll hate HOAs. If you like assurance that your neighbor won\'t make his house a pit and kill your property values, it\'s a necessity. If you like lower trash prices than you could ever negotiate on your own, it\'s very nearly a necessity.
