**Content:** [It really is a very personal decision, and it can really only be answered by the individual traveler.\ \ To me, the answer to that question depends on the length of the journey. And it\'s a very personal decision. Personally, I\'m okay with the amenities (food, seats, etc) in a 747 flying overseas sitting in business class. Some people can\'t sleep in the business class seats (which just recline a lot, they don\'t recline horizontally) and need a fold-flat seat like you get in first class. You do get better food in first class than you do in business class, but whether or not it is worth it is really up to the person sitting in that seat. To me, the food in business class is good. It\'s the difference between a high end sedan and a luxury sedan.\ \ In business class, I have the same amenities (access to the same movie system) that I have in first class, I have power outlets (both 110V and USB power), I have WiFi, so to me a business class seat is perfect. But again, it really is up to the person in the seat.\ \ If it\'s a special occasion and you\'re really pulling out all the stops, I\'d go first class. But if not, I\'d save the money.]{.rendered_qtext}
