**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What is the best (legal) way for Trump\'s administration to stop the caravan from crossing Mexico into the United States?** They can go so far as to close the border to all non-citizens. The President has the sole authority to close the border at his discretion, to any individual or group, for any reason he deems appropriate for national security. All legal, part of Federal law. I have no doubt that the group will not be illegally crossing in to the US.
