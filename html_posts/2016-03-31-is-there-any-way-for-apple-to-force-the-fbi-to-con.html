Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Short answer: no. Longer answer: A respondent cannot force a plaintiff to continue to press charges. If a plaintiff chooses to drop the charges, the judge dismisses the case. The respondent does not have the option of saying \"No, I don\'t want the case against me dismissed.\"
