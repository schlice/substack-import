--- title: '"In the military, do they actually make you scrub the floors with a toothbrush?"' date: '2017-08-10' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Never cleaned floors with a toothbrush. But my training NCO was not happy with the 5 o'clock shadow I had developed one day, was convinced that I didn't know how to shave (or that I was just lazy and didn't do it) and told me to report to his door with a razor. Which I did. He then told me to shave. In front of his door, at attention. Yes, *dry shave*. After he was satisfied that I had shaved enough, I returned to my room. I hadn't looked in the mirror, but my roommate was worried for my safety when he saw all the blood on my neck.
