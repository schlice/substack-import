--- title: '"If on a bridge would police officers prefer you pull over right away, even though this will cause a large traffic jam, or continue until past the bridge then pull over?"' date: '2015-02-23' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Most departments use the \"safest stop location\" rule when directing their officers on how to make a traffic stop. Officers will direct you to the safest location in which to stop your vehicle, either by waiting until you have passed the bridge, or directing you via the overhead PA system in the patrol car where to stop your vehicle.\ \ Regardless, if the officer initiates the stop on the bridge, by all means, stop on the bridge. If he wants you to continue past the bridge, he will indicate this using the PA system.]{.rendered_qtext}
