**Content:** [I \"rode along\" on an extradition from Colorado to New York.\ \ How was it? Very anti-climactic.\ \ Picked him up at the El Paso County CJC, he was handcuffed. Took him to the airport, walked him around the security checkpoint, got on to the plane (first), sat in the back of the plane, and once the door was closed, his handcuffs were removed (it was FAA policy at the time, don\'t know if it still is). When we landed, the handcuffs went back on, and we left the plane (last), and he was picked up by uniformed officers at the end of the jetway. Easy peesey.\ \ There are probably other transports that Federal Marshalls could tell you about, since for the most part, prisoner transportation is handled by the U.S. Marshall service.]{.rendered_qtext}
