**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Take a look at Quora. Can we answer legal questions here? The answer is yes, because the answers we give are general information only. No answer here is intended to create an attorney-client relationship, nor is it a solicitation to create one. We also try not to answer specific legal questions, because of the very same problem. We only address specific questions when their answers have general applicability. The same goes for medical questions. All of our medical answers are for general informational purposes only, not intended to create a diagnosis or create a doctor-patient relationship.
