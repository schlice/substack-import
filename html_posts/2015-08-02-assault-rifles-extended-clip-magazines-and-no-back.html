**Content:** [Assault rifles is a nebulous term. If you mean the traditional definition of assault rifles, which means automatic weapons, those are most definitely illegal.\ \ Extended magazines are illegal in most states.\ \ All gun sales from gun shows or gun Stores require background checks. Only private party sales don\'t require background checks in most states.\ \ So you\'re mostly wrong.]{.rendered_qtext}
