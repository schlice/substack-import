**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How do I check if someone has had charges against him or if he is a pedophile?** This is two separate questions. It's not necessarily easy to see if any particular person has ever had criminal charges filed against them. It's much easier to see if someone has been ***convicted*** of a crime. Once you've been convicted in court, it becomes public record. You can simply call the court which services the case, and you can find out. There are many public databases which also contain conviction records. Go ahead and search Google, I dare you. But records of other things, such as dismissed/acquitted charges or pending charges are not universal from state to state. You may not be able to reliably find that information. On the other hand, finding out if someone is a convicted pedophile is much easier. Anyone convicted as a sex offender must register on the [[sex offender registry](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=055c09&url=https://www.nsopw.gov/?AspxAutoDetectCookieSupport%3D1){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="nsopw.gov"}]{.qlink_container} and can very easily be searched.
