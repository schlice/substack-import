**Content:** [You asked if the police have the legal right to deny a person their civil rights. Since detention is a denial of your right to freedom, if a police officer detains you, you have just had your civil rights denied.\ \ So yes, legally, a police officer can deny anyone their civil rights.\ \ If you feel your civil rights have *unjustly* or *unfairly* been denied, you can hire a lawyer and sue the department for unlawful arrest.]{.rendered_qtext}
