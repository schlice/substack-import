**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I assume you mean \"outside the range,\" because every police officer fires his or her gun on a regular basis in the gun range. Outside the range, the vast majority of police officers go their entire careers never firing their weapons outside the range. I will have to get you statistics, but it is a very, very small number.
