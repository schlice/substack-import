Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} According to the TSA website, **yes.** Handcuffs are allowed in both checked and carry-on bags. But as with all things (according to the website), "the final decision rests with the TSA officer on whether an item is allowed through the checkpoint."
