Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [It depends on your definition of \"held accountable.\"\ \ If \"held accountable\" means \"retribution,\" you may not be satisfied. The court may not rule against the criminal, in which case you won\'t get your \"pound of flesh.\"\ \ If \"held accountable\" means \"judged fairly by the court,\" then the adjudication the criminal receives should be enough to see that the person is \"held accountable.\"]{.rendered_qtext}
