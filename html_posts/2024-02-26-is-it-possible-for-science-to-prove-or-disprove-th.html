--- title: '"Is it possible for science to prove or disprove the existence of God? If so, what scientific methods could be used to do so?"' date: '2024-02-26' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} No, it is not possible for science to either prove or disprove the existence of God. Since God exists outside of the universe He created, He must necessarily be immaterial (since He is outside of the material universe) and eternal (He is outside of time). So you can't use physical tests to find a non-physical thing. It's like using a metal detector to find a plastic cup, or using your multimeter to find your pulse. Science is the wrong tool to do it. This doesn't mean you can't prove God exists ***at all***, it just means you can't use science to do it.
