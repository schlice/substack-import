**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I'd do what I was told to avoid being *summarily shot*. One of the perks of martial law: no habeas corpus. Some of the things that will probably happen if martial law is enacted in the United States: - The suspension of the Constitution, probably starting with the first and second amendments. - Confiscation of firearms; it has happened and it will happen again. - Suspension of habeas corpus: imprisonment without due process and without a trail. - Travel restrictions, including road closures and possibly even quarantine zones. - Mandatory curfews and mandatory identification. - Automatic search and seizure without warrant.[\[1\]](#fiPrW){#cite-fiPrW .citation_link} Footnotes [\[1\]](#cite-fiPrW){.citation_index} [[Martial Law in the United States: How Likely is it, and What will happen under Martial law?](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=e6b6bb&url=http://offgridsurvival.com/martiallaw-unitedstates/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="offgridsurvival.com"}]{.qlink_container}
