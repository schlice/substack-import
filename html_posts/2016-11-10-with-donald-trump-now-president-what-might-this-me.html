**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} This pretty much sums up what it means: :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-aa7297473b0f8408dcfe314fd61f9932){.portrait .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-aa7297473b0f8408dcfe314fd61f9932" master_w="557" master_h="820"} ::: :::: Specifically, it means several things: - We don't have to worry about calls for repeal of the PLCAA (Protection of Lawful Commerce in Arms Act), which protects gun manufacturers from liability and lawsuits simply when properly-functioning guns are used illegally - We don't have to worry (quite so much) about calls for "common-sense gun regulations" which, as we've learned under an Obama administration, are really calls to reduce access to guns for law-abiding citizens to try and control lawlessness, and for silly regulations that govern cosmetic changes and limitations on standard-sized magazines. And the big one: - We don't have to worry that our government will support hate groups like Black Lives Matter and their defense and encouragement of open murder of police officers. We can be certain that if anything can be done to crack down on those types of ideas, it will be done. Don't get me wrong, if they have legitimate complaints, we'd be happy to hear them, but so far they have spewed nothing but lies, hate, and vitriol. We don't need that. Some other pro-2A legislation we may see: - Congress may propose "nationwide concealed carry" or "national reciprocity" such that any state would recognize the concealed carry permits issued in any other state, assuming a minimum level of training and background checking is performed by every state (which also, by extension, standardizes currently disparate training and background check requirements across the states). - Congress may seek to standardize punishments for using a gun in the commission of a crime, making those charges mandatory and not subject to plea bargaining. What it will also mean: - Declining gun sales. When there is no threat that "guns will be taken away" or severely limited, sales of new guns will go down. Fear is a great motivator. When there is no fear, there is little motivation.
