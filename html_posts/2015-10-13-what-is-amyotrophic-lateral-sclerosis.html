**Content:** [This is the scientific name for the disease otherwise known as \"Lou Gehrig\'s Disease\" after the famous baseball player. This is a degenerative brain disease that destroys neurons. ALS destroys \"motor neurons\" which are the ones that control voluntary movement within the body, eventually causing patients to lose the ability to walk, eat, move, and breathe.\ \ \ Find out more at [[http://www.alsa.org](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=21430e&url=http://www.alsa.org){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);"}]{.qlink_container}.]{.rendered_qtext}
