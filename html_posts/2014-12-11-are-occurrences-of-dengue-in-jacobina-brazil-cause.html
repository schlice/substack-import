Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [As you correctly assume, there is no causal link.\ \ - Cases of dengue are rising.\ - Genetically modified mosquitoes which reduce dengue-carrying mosquito populations have been released.\ \ THEREFORE,\ - Cases of dengue are rising because genetically modified mosquitoes have been released.\ \ Students of logic know that this is a logical fallacy. There is no causal link between the first two statements. Unless there is a causal link between the mosquitoes and the increase in the incidence of dengue, then it is illogical to assume that the genetically modified mosquitoes are causing the increase in dengue.]{.rendered_qtext}
