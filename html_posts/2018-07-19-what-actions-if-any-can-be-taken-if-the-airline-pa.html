**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What actions, if any, can be taken if the airline passenger sitting directly next to you or your child is viewing pornography in flight?** 1. Try talking to the passenger first. Explain that his screen is visible. Request that he either change his choice of content or reorient himself so that his screen is no longer visible (even accidentally) to your child. 2. If that doesn't work, involve the flight crew. They can either request that the passenger modify his viewing, or they can re-seat you (if availability exists to do so). One way or another, you can easily resolve the situation.
