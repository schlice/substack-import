Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [A specific education is not necessary, but is preferred.\ \ Each department does its own hiring and training in each location, although all training for all peace officers within a state is reviewed and certified by a state-level agency. Most of them call it a POST board (Peace Officer Standardization and Training), to ensure that their officers are trained to an equal minimum standard to enforce the laws within that state.\ \ The acceptance process, under Federal law, cannot discriminate by age, race, color or sex. In most jurisdictions, you have to be a US citizen, you must have graduated high school or hold a GED, and you generally need to have an excellent driving record.]{.rendered_qtext}
