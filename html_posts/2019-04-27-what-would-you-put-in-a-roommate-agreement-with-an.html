**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What would you put in a roommate agreement with an illegal sublet? (Yes it's not ideal, but there are extenuating circumstances on both sides).** Subletting your apartment when your lease agreement does not permit subletting, or violating the city's rules regardless of what the lease says, leaves **you** in trouble with the landlord, not your tenant, so your roommate agreement is useless because you'll both be on the street. Best advice: try to work with your landlord to either allow the sublet, or add the roommate to the lease.
