**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Maybe you're willing to roll the dice with some unapproved treatment, but not me. This is my health we're talking about. Besides, the largest majority of patients have some form of insurance which assists with drug costs. Manufacturers are more concerned with keeping you on their drug and getting money from insurance than making you pay out of pocket, so they all have payment assistance programs which will cost you little to nothing out of pocket, so long as you stay on the drug and you have insurance of some form (even Medicare qualifies).
