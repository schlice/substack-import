**Content:** [In logic, this is correct.\ \ A \--\> B\ B \--\> C\ \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\ therefore, A \--\> C\ \ This is **valid logic**. For example:\ \ Joe has gray hair.\ Gray hair looks old.\ Therefore, Joe looks old.\ \ The inverse is **fallacious logic:**\ \ A \--\> C\ B \--\> C\ \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\ therefore A \--\> B\ \ Example:\ \ Joe has gray hair.\ My dog has gray hair.\ Therefore, Joe is a dog.\ \ An example of fallacious logic on Quora:\ \ [[Since most pro-lifers tend to be pro-gun supporters, why don\'t they use the same logic that, \"Guns don\'t kill people. People kill people.\" to also \"Abortions don\'t kill people. People kill people.\"?](https://www.quora.com/Why-do-people-who-consider-themselves-pro-life-not-support-gun-control/answer/Bill-Stein)]{.qlink_container}]{.rendered_qtext}
