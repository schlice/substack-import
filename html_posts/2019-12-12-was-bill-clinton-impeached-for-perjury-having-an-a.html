**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Was Bill Clinton impeached for perjury, having an affair with Monica Lewinsky, or both?** Clinton was actually impeached for several crimes, such as suborning perjury and lying to Congress. He repeatedly lied to investigators who were investigating, among other things, the Whitewater scandal, the Travelgate scandal, and the alleged misuse of FBI files. The probe extended into Monica Lewinsky when she was connected with Vernon Turner, a subject of the Whitewater investigation. Lewinsky was preparing to perjure herself and advised Linda Tripp to do the same. Clinton was deposed on January 17, 1998, and he lied about having sexual relations with Lewinsky. Clinton was impeached with two Articles. The first was for lying to the grand jury about his affair with Lewinsky and his attempts to tamper with witnesses. The second was for obstructing justice in the Paula Jones lawsuit by suborning and encouraging perjury, and various other offenses related to Lewinsky.
