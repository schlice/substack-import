--- title: '"If god never causes bad things to happen then what about what he did to Adam and Eve after they ate the forbidden fruit?"' date: '2023-07-15' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} So to you, punishing bad behavior is "causing bad things to happen?" I think the "bad thing that happened" was Adam and Eve disobeying God. God expelling them from the Garden of Eden, to live and die in the new sin-filled world they brought about, is a natural consequence of what they brought on themselves through their own actions. God simply cannot abide sin, so when Adam and Eve sinned, God expelled them from the Garden. This is an EFFECT, not a CAUSE. God didn't cause that to happen. It's the effect of their bad actions.
