**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} In an HOA, where the common area is owned by the HOA, it is the HOA which maintains the common area. They use HOA dues to pay for this maintenance. Homeowners *should not* be maintaining this area. Unless the homeowner is being paid as a contractor for the association, homeowners should not maintain common areas. There could be problems with liability should the homeowner be injured and need to make a claim against the common area insurance policy maintained by the HOA on the common areas.
