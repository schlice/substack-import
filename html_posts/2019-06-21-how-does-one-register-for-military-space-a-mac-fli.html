{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How does one register for military Space-A /Mac flight?** Visit the [[AMC Space Available Travel Page](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=237ff7&url=https://www.amc.af.mil/Home/AMC-Travel-Site/AMC-Space-Available-Travel-Page/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="af.mil"}]{.qlink_container} and fill out an online Form 140. You can take a paper copy with you down to your nearest passenger terminal. Here's [[a great article](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=ea781d&url=https://www.militaryonesource.mil/recreation-travel-shopping/travel/travel-planning/plan-your-trip-with-space-a-travel){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="militaryonesource.mil"}]{.qlink_container} that tells you all about Space A travel. Don't forget that, at least for now, military members can also take advantage of [[TSA Pre-Check](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=3b51f6&url=https://www.tsa.gov/travel/special-procedures/military-travelers){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="tsa.gov"}]{.qlink_container} if you don't want to, or can't, travel Space-A.
