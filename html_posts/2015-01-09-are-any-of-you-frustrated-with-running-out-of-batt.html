**Content:** [As an Android user, I can change my batteries. I bought two batteries and an external battery charger from Amazon for about US\$20. If I\'m going somewhere with spotty coverage (where my phone will be using lots of battery to search for signal) or where I know I\'ll have the screen on for extended time periods, I\'ll grab the extra battery and drop it in my pocket. If it dies, I swap it out. Simple.\ \ Don\'t invest in wireless charging until it becomes more integrated into the actual phone itself. Otherwise, I think the systems are just to kludge-y right now.]{.rendered_qtext}
