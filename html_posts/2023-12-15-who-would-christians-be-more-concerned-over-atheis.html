--- title: '"Who would Christians be more concerned over, atheists or believers who dislike the traditional Christian God?"' date: '2023-12-15' tags: - theology layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I think Christians are concerned about both. **But believers who simply misunderstand God have a better chance of being saved than atheists.** Some people are simply not destined to be saved. I know it sounds harsh, but it's nevertheless true. And if that is true, then die-hard atheists are beyond salvation through their own choices. But Christians who misunderstand God have a chance to improve, learn, and be redeemed. I'd want to invest more time in the misunderstanding Christian than in the die-hard atheist.
