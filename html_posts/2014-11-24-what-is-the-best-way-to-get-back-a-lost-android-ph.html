--- title: '"What is the best way to get back a lost Android phone if the internet was switched off during the theft?"' date: '2014-11-24' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [You\'re not likely to get it back.\ \ However, your only real chance is to notify your carrier of the theft. They will flag the IMEI (the phone\'s serial number) in their system, and anyone trying to re-activate that phone with a new SIM will be denied, and the phone will be retained until the police arrive.\ \ The carrier will give you a printout showing that the phone has been flagged stolen and will contain the same IMEI. Take that report to your local police station and file a stolen property report. If the phone is activated, the thief can be arrested if he is found in possession of the phone with the matching IMEI.]{.rendered_qtext}
