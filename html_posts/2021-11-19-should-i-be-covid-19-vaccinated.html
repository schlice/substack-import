**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You should always talk to your doctor before you make decisions about vaccines, and you should consider your current health situation and your current medications, and any other risk factors you may face. Don't ask the Internet if taking a vaccine is a good idea. *In general* (not specifically for you), the vaccine is beneficial for most healthy people. In general, the vaccine reduces your chance of contracting the virus, and if you do, you have a greater chance of only experiencing mild or moderate symptoms. You *greatly* reduce your risk of being hospitalized with the virus if you get the vaccine.
