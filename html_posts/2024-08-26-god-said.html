**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Not everything in the Bible is *prescriptive.* Sometimes, some things in the Bible are *descriptive*. God telling Adam and Eve to "be fruitful and multiply" was a command given to *them,* not to *you*. It was prescriptive for *them*, but it is descriptive for *you*. God would like you to have children, but you are under no *command* to have children. Because if that were true, then sterile men and women would automatically go to hell. Anyone for any reason who did not have children would go to hell, and that condition does not describe those who are destined for eternal torment.
