There are even services that allow payments via Bitcoin to be paid out at Mexican ATMs, and the recipient is paid in pesos. \[1\]\ \ I only find the plan dubious because while Trump claims that \$22 billion is the amount of remittances back to Mexico from illegal immigrants, that figure is incorrect. \$22 billion is *all* remittances from the US to Mexico, not just from illegal workers.\ \ And it is *pittance* when compared to the reported \$64 billion that Mexico\'s drug cartels make in the drug trade to the US alone.\[2\]\ \ If the wall stops that money, there\'s no way Mexico would contribute anything to help cut off their own money.
