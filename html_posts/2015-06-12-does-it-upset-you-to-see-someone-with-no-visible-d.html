--- title: '"Does it upset you to see someone with no visible disabilities take a service dog into a store or other public building?"' date: '2015-06-12' tags: - veteran-benefits - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Understand that not all disabilities are visible.\ \ Also understand that the law doesn\'t require that a service dog wear a vest, or that a person with a service animal have a visible disability.\ \ Business owners are allowed to ask only two questions of someone with an animal in a business:\ \ ]{.rendered_qtext} - Is that a service animal specially trained to treat a recognized disability under the Americans with Disabilities Act? - What is this animal trained to do? \ That\'s it. Beyond that, if someone has a service animal with them, they cannot be restricted from any public area in which people are allowed to be.
