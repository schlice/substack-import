Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [To what are you going to compare the services offered by the police?\ \ In order to perform cost-benefit analysis, you have to put the cost of a police force, and the benefits you receive from it, against other forms of law enforcement. Currently, police are the only law enforcement agents authorized to perform police activities.\ \ Since the police is the only game in town, you cannot put the police up against any other alternatives, so CBA is not warranted.]{.rendered_qtext}
