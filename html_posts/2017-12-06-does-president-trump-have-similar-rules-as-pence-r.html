**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} President Trump has not said whether or not he has started following the Pence/Graham rule of never being alone with a woman who is not his wife, or if he's going to attend a function in which alcohol is served, ensuring that his wife is there. I'm pretty sure Trump is rarely alone as President, and if he is, I'm certain that there is at least one other person who is aware of it. [[The rule](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=732641&url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Billy_Graham_rule){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="wikipedia.org"}]{.qlink_container} was actually first practiced by Billy Graham, the Evangelical preacher. In a series of meetings in 1948 in Modesto, California, Graham and a group of other Evangelical leaders vowed to \"avoid any situation that would have even the appearance of compromise or suspicion.\" > The accountability agreement, which has become known as the > \"[[Modesto > Manifesto](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=e49626&url=https://www.christianhistoryinstitute.org/magazine/article/the-modesto-manifesto/){.external_link > rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" > onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" > qt-tooltip="christianhistoryinstitute.org"}]{.qlink_container},\" > covered not only their interactions with women, but also commitments > to integrity with respect to finances, their interactions with local > churches, and publicity.[\[1\]](#sJyZC){#cite-sJyZC .citation_link} Pence, as a public figure, follows the rule to protect himself. It honestly has nothing to do with protecting women. In fact, critics of the rule say that it marginalizes women, depriving them of opportunities to network and share their ideas. However, Pence knows, in the era of Al Franken and John Conyers, that an accusation can ruin your career. To avoid the possibility of such a career-ending accusation, he follows this rule. It's too bad that society has changed so much that ***the mere accusation*** of sexual misconduct can cost you your entire life's work. However, it has. In light of this, smart men will do what they must to protect themselves.
