--- title: '"What are the legal implications of naming two of your children the same first name without providing different middle names or last names?"' date: '2014-04-07' tags: - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [While there are many practical problems with doing so, legally there is no problem with naming your two children with exactly the same name. In the US, persons are identified using ***personally identifying information*** which includes name, Social Security Number, address, and birthday. Even if they are twins, they will have unique Social Security Numbers, so they will still be legally identifiable even if their names are identical.]{.rendered_qtext}
