**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Why would an incompetent president be reelected?** There are several reasons why an incompetent president would be re-elected. 1. He may be incompetent, but still wildly popular. 2. Or maybe, "incompetence" is subjective. In other words, what one person finds "incompetent," other people find perfectly acceptable. Remember, political competence is not something you can objectively measure. It is highly subjective, and much confirmation bias exists in determining it. In other words, if you already thought of the president favorably, then you are far more likely to think of his actions as "competent," and vice-versa.
