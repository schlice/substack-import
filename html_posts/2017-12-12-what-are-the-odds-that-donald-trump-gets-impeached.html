**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What are the odds that Donald Trump gets impeached for collusion with Russia in the near future?** Somewhere between slim and none. The whole Russia collusion distraction isn't enough for Republicans to impeach. You'd have to show something that actually rises to the level of "high crimes and misdemeanors" while in office before Republicans would even think about impeachment. If Congress wants to do something useful, it can define a crime and write a law. It can make collusion a crime. Until then, there isn't enough to sway Republicans to vote for impeachment. And even if they DO impeach him, do you think they will be able to get 67 Senators to vote to convict? I don't know. We'll have to see what the party lines look like if an impeachment actually does go forward to the Senate.
