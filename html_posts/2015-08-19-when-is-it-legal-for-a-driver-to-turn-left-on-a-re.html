**Content:** [If you are facing a red arrow, you cannot turn left at all.\ \ Most states (38 of them, in fact) allow you to turn left onto a one-way street from a red light, assuming you come to a full stop (same rules as when turning right on red) AND you are currently on a one-way street.\ \ Five states allow you to turn left onto a one-way street, even if you are on a two-way street.\ \ The remainder (with a couple of local exceptions) don\'t allow any left turns on red at all.\ \ See [[the Wikipedia page](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=b18674&url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Left_on_red%23Left_turn_on_red){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="wikipedia.org"}]{.qlink_container} for more details.]{.rendered_qtext}
