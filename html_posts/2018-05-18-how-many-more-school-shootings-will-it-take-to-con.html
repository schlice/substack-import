Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How many more school shootings? How many more children are murdered before our legislators cease worrying about being reelected and enact sensible gun laws?** There already *are* sensible gun laws. Heck, there are *ridiculous* gun laws. There is already an *insane* amount of gun laws. So answer this, questioner: ***What new gun law would stop a shooter?*** This shooter probably broke about 10 different laws just bringing a gun onto school property. Clearly, the laws we have preventing guns on schools didn't stop someone from doing it. Wanna know why? **Laws don't prevent people from doing bad things. Laws provide a framework for us to prosecute people who violate them**. If you want to ***prevent*** bad behavior, *laws are not the answer.* Laws help you to prosecute people who violate them. Laws don't stop people from doing bad things.
