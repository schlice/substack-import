**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How can I find a licensed firearm instructor willing to teach a total beginner?** There are many places you can go, but probably the easiest for you will be to visit your nearest gun range. They will not only probably offer classes themselves (including ones for people just starting into firearms from the ground up), but if you don't want to take their classes, they will have a list of NRA-certified instructors who you can contact directly to inquire about lessons.
