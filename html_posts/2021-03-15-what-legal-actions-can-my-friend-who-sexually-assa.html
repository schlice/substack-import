**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What legal actions can my friend who sexually assaulted me take if I expose him online?** If someone sexually assaulted you, he's not your friend. Instead of "exposing him online" and risking the defamation lawsuits, go to the police. Sexual assault is a crime. Then, after the police charge him, feel free to dish on social media. Just make sure everything you say is 100% true and verifiable. But remember: everything you say can be used against you in court, even if you haven't been Mirandized.
