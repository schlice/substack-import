He then calmly explained that he didn\'t really see any issues with my conduct, and even offered to burn a DVD with the footage on it for her to take, so she could use it in her own legal action if she chose to do so, which she rather impolitely declined before storming out of the interview room.\ \ I would actually love to see more video being used in law enforcement.
