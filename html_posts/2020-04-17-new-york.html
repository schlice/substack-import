**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **New York\'s governor just signed an executive order requiring everyone to wear a face covering. Why did this come so late?** *What leads you to believe that this is late?* The state was already under a mandatory stay-at-home order, so mandatory face coverings were largely unnecessary, as most non-essential businesses were closed anyway. The governor is now trying to set the conditions under which he can start advising people to go back to work and re-open the state. He has decided that everyone wearing face coverings in public is a start. It's such a good start, that he's doing it now for everyone. If we can't slow the spread with social distancing only, we'll help slow the spread by mandating face coverings.
