**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Is it true Gen. Kristin Goodwin was removed as Commandant of Cadets at the USAFA for supporting SRS for trans cadets or just because she was openly gay herself?** Neither. While we don\'t know the exact nature of the charges against her which led to her removal as Commandant, it is not a violation of UCMJ to have an opinion. It\'s not even a violation of UCMJ to publicly announce your opinion. It may be a policy violation to speak on behalf of the Air Force when you are not authorized to do so, but that still doesn\'t rise to the level of a crime. Since it\'s not illegal to be openly gay, not is it illegal to have an opinion about SRS, neither qualify as a reason to relieve a decorated officer from her job.
