**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How do you feel when people who have handicap tags for a family member use the designated parking when the handicapped individual isn\'t in the car?** It doesn't upset me that people violate the law. We have so many laws in this country, I'm not completely certain that I don't violate laws that I'm completely unaware of. It upsets me when people do this *deliberately*, knowing what the law is and intentionally violating it. It takes up a spot for someone who probably truly needs it, and it's just another example of the selfish "it's-all-about-me" attitude people tend to have these days. If you are aware of this, you should report it.
