--- title: '"Say Osama Bin Laden was still alive and somehow in America. If a US citizen shot him dead on the streets, would that person go to prison?"' date: '2016-01-18' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Most likely. US citizens are not authorized to assassinate anyone. Neither is any police department in the US. If a US citizen were to shoot him dead on the streets, that citizen would be arrested and charged with Murder 1. Once at trial, the possibility of extenuating circumstances (i.e. it was Osama bin Laden) and plea bargains could reduce the time served to a minimum.
