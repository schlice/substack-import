**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What does it mean when they say a California judge yesterday dismissed the Stormy Daniels defamation suit against Michael Cohen and Donald Trump \"with prejudice\"?** A dismissal with prejudice means that the court is dismissing the case based on its merits. This means that a court has looked at the merits of the case and has made a decision based on the merits. Dismissal with prejudice is a *final judgement*. This means that the case cannot be brought again. The case is permanently dismissed; the plaintiff cannot bring this action again.
