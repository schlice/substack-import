**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} So a "savior" is a person who saves you from torture? Okay, let's say this savior saves you from torture. You live a nice, comfortable life. *But what happens when you die?* Jesus saves us from **eternal** torment, not **earthly** torment. Jesus saves and redeems your *soul*, not your *body.* He even tells us that we **will** have trouble in this life. > I have said these things to you, that in me you may have peace. In the > world you will have tribulation. But take heart; I have overcome the > world. --- John 16:33 ESV There will be tough times in this world. *But Jesus overcame this world.* > So if the Son sets you free, you will be free indeed. \-- John 8:36 > ESV Jesus doesn't promise you an easy life. Jesus promises you a ***glorious eternity.***
