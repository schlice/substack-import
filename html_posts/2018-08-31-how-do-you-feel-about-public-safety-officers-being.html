--- title: "How do you feel about public safety officers being offered and accepting free food, drinks, and products at stores? Do you think it" date: "2018-08-31" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It\'s not an ethical issue. Business owners can give away whatever they choose, to whomever they choose. However, it presents an image of favoritism, and if officers know they get free stuff, they will frequent that place. That\'s not a bad thing for the business owner, but it makes for the appearance of uneven, unfair policing, favoring the businesses who can afford to give out free stuff. So in order to avoid even the appearance of impropriety, our agency and others like it prohibit deputies from receiving anything for free while on duty.
