**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Many sins are about the condition of the heart rather than the actual action. So in this case, I'd ask why you are hacking a computer. Security researchers and penetration testers routinely attempt to hack computers in order to discover their security flaws and either get them fixed, or advise their clients how to fix them. Malicious hackers attempt to hack computers in order to steal personal information or infect the computer for future nefarious use. Which one are you? Both fall under the heading of computer hacking, but the condition of the hacker's heart, and his intentions, are very different in both cases. In the case of the malicious hacker, what he is doing would rightly be considered stealing and would thus be sinful. \ Original question: Is it a sin to hack a computer?
