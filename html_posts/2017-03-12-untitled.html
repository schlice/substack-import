**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You are fortunate. I continually find issues related to how my parents raised me, that affect me today. Physically, emotionally, spiritually, I'm continually dredging things up that can be related back to my childhood and the environment in which I was raised. I have lower GI issues, and I am learning that many of the negative emotions which are tied to those issues can be traced back to my feelings about my childhood. Let alone the criminal activity. That one should be the big disqualifier right there. Yeah, I know, the dichotomy. But it's really not. The same qualities which make one a good cop also make one a good criminal. All you need is a lack of a suitable moral compass, and away you go. I try to be a better parent than my parents were, but it appears I'm also hampered as a parent by the habits which I was taught as a child. It's a constant struggle.
