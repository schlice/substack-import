**Content:** [You\'re already screwed because you made a verbal contract with no defined repayment terms. Thankfully you did tell him that you were actually going to set a date at some time in the future, so with enough advance notice, I think you can set a date without too much damage to your friendship.\ \ I don\'t think you\'re ready for court though. First, write a letter indicating that you are adding the repayment terms to your verbal contract. Send it certified, give him plenty of time before you require repayment, but set a start date.\ \ Then, if he still fails to repay you, you can look at court options.]{.rendered_qtext}
