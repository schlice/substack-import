--- title: '"Does alcohol affect you faster if you have multiple sclerosis?"' date: '2016-08-17' tags: - health-autoimmune layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Short answer: no. MS does not affect absorption of alcohol into the body. Some of the approved MS therapies may interact with alcohol, but the disease itself doesn\'t affect how the body deals with intoxication.
