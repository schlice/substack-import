**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} First of all, don't worry, there is no harm in leaving it up. There's also no harm in you looking at it. Where you can start to run into issues is if you *worship* *the image of Jesus as he is depicted*. This is because any image of God or Jesus necessarily *constrains* them to that image, and they are so much more powerful and awesome than can ever be captured in an image. Images might be useful to guide our prayer, but they are very easy to abuse as they end up becoming the *focus* of the prayer instead of a *conduit* for prayer. Don't pray to the picture. Pray to the Christ.
