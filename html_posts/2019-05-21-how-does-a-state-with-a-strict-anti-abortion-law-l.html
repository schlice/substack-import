**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How does a State, with a strict anti-abortion law, like Missouri, handle the following situation: a rape victim becomes pregnant. Is the rapist liable for child support, as the father?** Yes, because child support isn't about protecting the mother or punishing the father (or the other way round, as the situation may be). Child support is about supporting the child. Since you made the child, and it can be proven that you are the biological father, you are financially responsible for that child until it turns 18. If the mother chooses to keep it (and many rape victims do), the father is responsible for child support.
