**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How do you add an appendix in the Bill of Rights?** The only "appendix" you can add to the Constitution (the first ten amendments of which are called the "Bill of RIghts") is another Amendment. See the Constitution for the rules on how to pass an Amendment.
