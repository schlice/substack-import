{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Are most traffic tickets just a way for police departments to drum up revenue for new cruisers?** No. In most states, ticket revenue goes to the *state*, not directly into the department issuing the ticket. The police enforce state law, so the state collects the revenue, not the department. Funding for vehicles comes from a completely separate funding source from state government, not from traffic tickets.
