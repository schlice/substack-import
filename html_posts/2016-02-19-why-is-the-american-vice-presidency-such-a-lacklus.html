**Content:** [Because the Constitution.\ \ Constitutionally, the Vice President has two jobs: the president of the Senate, and sitting around waiting for the President to die.\ \ Presidents, therefore, give the VP lots of jobs to do. They delegate responsibilities to the VP to ease the burden of the Presidency.\ \ He may be the second string QB, but he still has to be a qualified QB.]{.rendered_qtext}
