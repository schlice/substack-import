**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **By what authority is Donald Trump choosing Jerusalem as Israel\'s capital?** Trump is not *choosing* Jerusalem. Congress *recognized* Israel's sovereign right to name their own capital city in 1995, when they passed the Jerusalem Embassy Act. Trump is merely fulfilling **the law** which, incidentally, **three presidents failed to fulfill:** Clinton, Bush 43 and Obama. If Congress has heartburn with this, they should repeal their own law. I doubt this will happen though.
