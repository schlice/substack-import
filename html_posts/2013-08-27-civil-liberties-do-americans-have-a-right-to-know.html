--- title: "Civil Liberties: Do Americans have a right to know what the laws are that they can be imprisoned for disobeying?" date: "2013-08-27" tags: ["law"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [There are two parts to this answer: Yes, Americans have a right to know the laws. In a nation of laws, the citizen has not only the right to know, but the responsibility to educate themselves on the laws they are required to follow.\ \ However, a lack of knowledge of the law is not an excuse for violating the law. The same penalties are applied to someone who violates the law whether they knew the content and meaning of the law or not.]{.rendered_qtext}
