--- title: '"Why did the ultrasound say no pregnancy after an abortion while the pregnancy test shows positive?"' date: '2021-09-10' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Pregnancy tests test for the presence of pregnancy hormones in the blood or urine. It may take some time before those pregnancy hormones return to pre-pregnancy levels in the blood after the fetus has been removed. So it is still possible that you can still pop positive on a pregnancy test soon after an abortion. In fact, It can take anywhere **from 16 days up to 2 months** for hCG levels in the body to fall below the levels detected by a pregnancy test. \ Original question: Why did the ultrasound say no pregnancy after an abortion while the pregnancy test shows positive?
