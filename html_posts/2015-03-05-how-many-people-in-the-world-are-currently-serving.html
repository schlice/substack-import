**Content:** [The United States has the largest prison population in the world. According to the [[Bureau of Justice Statistics](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=d01618&url=http://bjs.gov/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="bjs.gov"}]{.qlink_container} (BJS), in 2011, 2,266,800 adults were incarcerated in all US prisons which is about 0.94% of adults in the U.S. That figure is up to around 2.3 million today, which is 1% of the adult population in the US.\ \ Worldwide, it is estimated that approximately 10.1 million people are in jail.]{.rendered_qtext}
