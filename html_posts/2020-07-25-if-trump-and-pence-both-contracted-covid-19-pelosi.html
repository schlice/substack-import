Would she be able to nominate someone to the Supreme Court?** If Trump and Pence both contracted COVID-19, they'd both have to die from it before Pelosi would become President. In the few months she'd have until the election, RBG would have to retire, Pelosi would have to nominate a successor, and then the Senate would have to give its advice and consent. I'd bet dollars to donuts that the Senate would not provide advice and/or consent, or if they did, they would surely vote no. McConnell would pull the same trick he did with Merrick Garland, and the nominee wouldn't even make it to the floor for a vote. He'd say that Pelosi wasn't an elected president, so he'd wait until the next elected president in November nominates someone.
