**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **In states with legal recreational cannabis, does the law enforcement have a way of knowing if your weed is legal or from the black market?** *(Answer is specific to Colorado; other states may have different rules)* No, and in short, they don't care. Only sellers are regulated, the product itself is not regulated. Since only the marijuana retailers are regulated, not the actual product itself, law enforcement's only concern with users is their compliance with consumption laws (i.e. you have to be old enough to use it, you can only use it in your house, you still can't drive under the influence, etc). Once you have it, they don't care where it came from. Think about it. You can grow it yourself. How are they ever going to know where you got it from?
