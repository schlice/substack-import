**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **I see a man with a gun walking down my street and there\'s a kid playing outside. What should I do?** Depends on the law in your locality. We're presuming you live in the US. If your state does not permit open carry of firearms, call the police and tell them someone is open carrying a firearm. Let the police handle it. The cost of disallowing open carry needs to be passed back to the government, and if they find its enforcement to be too expensive, they will either change it or increase funding to the police. If your state does permit open carry of firearms, then you have not provided enough information to determine whether or not you should be worried. Is the firearm being carried incorrectly? If it is a handgun, is it in a holster? If it is a rifle, is it on a sling? Is it being carried correctly? Is the person being threatening with it? You have a lot of questions to answer before you can determine what to do.
