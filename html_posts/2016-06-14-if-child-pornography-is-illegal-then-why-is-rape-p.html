**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Morally, is it wrong to write a book which details how to assassinate the president? Is it wrong to write a book which details how to make a pipe bomb? And is it wrong to film a movie which details a rape? Morally, it's dependent on your particular set of morals. For some people, that may be completely acceptable. For others, it is abhorrent. Legally, while you can film a sexual activity and market it as a rape but have statements showing the actors are all over 18 and consent to filming the scene, you cannot film sex with a child and claim that the sex is consensual. Sex with a child is illegal (it is real rape, not pretend-movie rape), so recording sex with a child is, in and of itself, a crime.
