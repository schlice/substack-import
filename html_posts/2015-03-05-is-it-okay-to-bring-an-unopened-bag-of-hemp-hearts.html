Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [No, unless the seeds have been sterilized. Unsterilized hemp seeds are a Schedule 1 controlled substance and cannot enter the U.S. without the appropriate DEA paperwork.\ \ For any other hemp products, if the hemp still contains THC and can cause THC to enter the body, it cannot enter the U.S.]{.rendered_qtext}
