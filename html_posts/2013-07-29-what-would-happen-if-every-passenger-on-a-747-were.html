Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [As long as people are not moving forwards and backwards too much changing the center of gravity on the plane, everyone standing up and dancing shouldn\'t have much impact at all. Assuming that the plane is at altitude and cruising with autopilot engaged, changes in all three axes of rotation should be dampened and corrected automatically. You would have to have everyone move forwards or backwards dramatically in order to affect pitch, and you would have to have everyone flop to one side of the plane or the other in order to potentially impact roll, even though that\'s much less likely. It would be nearly impossible for passengers or cargo to affect yaw.\ \ \ Hypothetically speaking, of course.]{.rendered_qtext}
