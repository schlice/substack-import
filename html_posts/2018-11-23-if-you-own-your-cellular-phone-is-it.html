**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It can be. If you own it outright, you can request that your carrier provide you with unlock codes so that you can use your device on any carrier which supports your device. They should do this without cost. Most carriers have automated systems which check your device to see if you owe anything on it, and then provide codes and procedures to remove carrier locks. You can usually find that on their support website.
