--- title: "I want to help make America a gun-free society. What steps do I take? Are there any specific organizations or movements I can join?" date: "2016-05-01" tags: ["policy-analysis"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You can\'t do anything by yourself. While laudable, you simply don\'t have enough inertia. And if events like Columbine or Sandy Hook or Aurora couldn\'t generate enough momentum, neither can you personally. You should look into partnering with organizations like [[the Brady Center](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=c0015e&url=http://www.bradycampaign.org/bradycenter){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="bradycampaign.org"}]{.qlink_container}, [[Moms Demand Action](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=58b4fa&url=http://momsdemandaction.org/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="momsdemandaction.org"}]{.qlink_container}, and the [[Violence Policy Center](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=5f280c&url=http://www.vpc.org/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="vpc.org"}]{.qlink_container}. These organizations are all working towards a gun-free America, albeit in different ways. You should definitely look at voting Democrat. Hillary Clinton has a very strong anti-gun policy. She has made plain that she wants to roll back the *Protection of Lawful Commerce in Arms Act* so that victims of gun violence can sue the manufacturer of that gun. She would like to destroy the gun manufacturing industry in the United States by suing them out of existence. Bernie Sanders doesn\'t have quite as radical a view against PLCAA, but as a Democrat, he is most definitely anti-gun by definition. You might try taking different tacks as well, like successfully suing Ford when a drunk driver kills a family, or suing Ginsu when a knife is used to commit a murder. You might be able to generate some anti-gun momentum that way. It\'s definitely an uphill climb, but \"the journey of a thousand miles begins with just one step.\" By the way, I\'m pro-gun. I do what I want.
