I suspect Israel will offer the people in Gaza the same deal they did at the end of the Six Day War: *if you accept Israel\'s right to exist, if you accept Israel as the valid authority in this region, you can live in peace, as full citizens of Israel.* It\'s a good deal. For the Arabs in the region, they would be the only Arabs in Europe living in an actual democracy. And just as after the Six Day War, some will take that deal But some will not. And in this case, they will be asked to leave. I use asked loosely. They won\'t actually be asked to leave. They will be escorted out of the county. Israel can no longer afford to allow them to remain when all they desire to do is destroy Israel. They have no desire for peace. They have no desire for compromise. Just think about the chants that they have been shouting at the pro Palestinian rallies. "From the river to the sea, Palestine shall be free.\" Where\'s the river? It\'s the Jordan River, the eastern border of Israel. Where is the sea? It\'s the Mediterranean Sea, the western border of Israel. They want nothing more than the complete destruction of Israel. Israelis can no longer afford such people on their shores.
