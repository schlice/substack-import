**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **The tragic shooting at Sandy Hook school took place in March of 2012. I saw a claim that between then & March of 2018, 1,166.6 kids had been killed by "gun violence" per year: How could anyone claim that this figure is possible?** Simple. Include a very broad range of definitions in the term "gun violence." Namely, anyone who has died through the use of a gun. This includes anyone who committed suicide, and anyone under the age of 18 involved in gang violence (not innocent victims, but gang members under the age of 18). This will get you numbers similar to this.
