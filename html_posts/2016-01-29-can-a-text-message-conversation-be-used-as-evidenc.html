--- title: "Can a text message conversation be used as evidence in a court of law?" date: "2016-01-29" tags: ["policy-analysis", "law"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Yes, SMS messages can be used as evidence, if they are material to the case at hand. Generally, during discovery, SMS message conversations are retrieved by subpoena directly from the carrier, not from any individual handset. This preserves the integrity of the evidence and makes it admissible as evidence in court.
