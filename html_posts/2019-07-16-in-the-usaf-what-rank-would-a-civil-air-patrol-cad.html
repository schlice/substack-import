**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **In the USAF, what rank would a Civil Air Patrol cadet be able to enlist at? Does earning the Spatz award get you higher rank when you enlist?** Both questions answered at once: Any Civil Air Patrol cadet who earns the Mitchell Award can enlist at the rank of E-3. There's no further special advancement for achieving the higher Spaatz Award. Also, you should know that ROTC and USAFA both look very favorably on Civil Air Patrol experience. Approximately 10% of each incoming USAFA class has CAP experience.
