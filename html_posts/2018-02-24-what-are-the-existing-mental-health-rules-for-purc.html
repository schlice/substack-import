**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What are the existing mental health rules for purchasing a firearm in the US? i.e. you are disqualified if x, y, or z.** You need to have either been involuntarily committed to a mental institution, or ruled by a judge to be a "mental defective." After deinstitutionalization, the bar was set pretty high for judges to actually involuntarily commit someone to a mental institution or place that "mental defective" label on someone.
