**Content:** [Fears are normal. Everyone has them. Don\'t worry about your fear. You don\'t need to decrease your fear; *you need to increase your courage.*\ \ Mark Twain said, \"Courage is resistance to fear, mastery of fear, not absence of fear.\" Fear helps to heighten your senses, make you sharper. It increases adrenaline, which also enhances your senses and muscle responses. So fear by itself is not a bad thing.\ \ Being paralyzed by your fear is an issue. If you simply cannot get past your fear, there may be an underlying issue at play. If you can\'t move past your fear, you should seek the advice of a licensed psychiatrist or psychologist.\ \ Physiologically, unless you have some sort of disease (such as brittle bone disease), the spine is not that easy to break. The spine is surrounded by vertebrae, which can help to block impacts and prevent the severing of the spinal cord. The human body is very resilient in that way. It has existed for thousands of years in this shape, and has proven to be very reliable. Spines don\'t routinely break for no reason.]{.rendered_qtext}
