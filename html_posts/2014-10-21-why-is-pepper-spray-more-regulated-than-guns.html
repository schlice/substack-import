**Content:** [Are you kidding???\ \ There are hardly any regulations on pepper spray. No background check, no license, no training, nothing. It\'s no more complicated than buying a bottle of water.\ \ I think there is at least one more regulation than that when buying a gun.\ \ One or two.]{.rendered_qtext}
