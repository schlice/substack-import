Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Yes, commercial arbitration is legal. Most times, arbitration is an agreed-upon restriction that both parties knowingly enter into when signing a contract. Enjoining both parties from lawsuit and instead forcing them into binding arbitration is a feature of many commercial contracts. Relief can usually be obtained quicker and for less money.
