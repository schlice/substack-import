**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} What evidence would you accept? In order to determine what evidence you will accept, I will ask you your question, but will change one thing. Here you go: > ***Is Alexander the Great just a fictional myth complied from others > and the surroundings? Provide evidence!*** So how would *you* answer that question? What evidence would you provide that *you* would be satisfied with? I'm certain you will be able to produce satisfactory evidence proving Alexander the Great actually existed. Now ask yourself: ***Why is that same evidence insufficient for Jesus but it is sufficient for historical figures like Tiberius Caesar, Julius Caesar, and Alexander the Great?*** I would also submit to you that *the amount of evidence which proves the existence of Jesus of Nazareth is* ***greater*** *than the amount of evidence we have proving the existence of Tiberius Caesar and Alexander the Great.*
