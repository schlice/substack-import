--- title: "Can the police search a business if they have received a phone call indicating that the business carries stolen goods?" date: "2015-05-23" tags: ["law-enforcement"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [They can use the call (known as a \"tip\") to open an investigation. If the investigation reveals enough information to get a warrant, a judge will issue a warrant allowing the police to search for something specific. The police can then execute that warrant, looking for the items detailed in the warrant.]{.rendered_qtext}
