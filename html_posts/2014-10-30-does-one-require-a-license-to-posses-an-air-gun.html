However, to appease those who don\'t like toy guns, these toys are not normally stocked on shelves near toys. They are usually stocked on shelves near sporting goods and outdoor gear.\ \ Toys such as these are required to have bright orange barrels so as to distinguish them from real guns.]{.rendered_qtext}
