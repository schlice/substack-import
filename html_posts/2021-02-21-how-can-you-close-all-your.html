**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} By "opened tabs," it sounds like you\'re talking about the Samsung internet browser. If that\'s the case, down in the bar at the bottom of your screen will be an open tabs indicator. When you click that, you will see a listing of all of your open tabs. If you click the triple dot menu in the upper right hand corner of the tab management page, the first option will be "close all tabs\". If, by open tabs, you mean all of your open apps in your phone, the way to close those is to click the apps view button in your bottom bar. :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-1bcc1466439292b2361dc2efcfd3cc93){.landscape .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-1bcc1466439292b2361dc2efcfd3cc93" master_w="1045" master_h="216"} ::: :::: On modern versions of OneUI, just swipe up from the bottom of the screen. Once you click that, you will see all of your open apps, and a big button underneath that says "close all."
