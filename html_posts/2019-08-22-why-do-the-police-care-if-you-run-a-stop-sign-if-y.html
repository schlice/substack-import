**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Why do the police care if you run a stop sign if you've looked and nobody is coming?** Because unfortunately, the law is not built with "do X unless you can do Y instead." The law says "Do X." Period. The law requires you to come to a full and complete stop at the line. A complete stop is indicated when your tires stop moving.
