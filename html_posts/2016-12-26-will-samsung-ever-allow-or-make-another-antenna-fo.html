Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The Note 4 already runs on the T-Mobile network. If the phone runs on the AT&T network (which it does), it runs on the T-Mobile network. They both use GSM networks, and any phone of the Note's build quality will use multiple GSM frequencies, and both carriers use multiple GSM frequencies. You could unlock an AT&T Note 4 and use it on the T-Mobile network if you have one. HOWEVER You can't use a Verizon Note 4 on the T-Mobile network, as Verizon's phones are CDMA and T-Mobile's phones are GSM. The tech is totally different. Not only do GSM phones have a different wireless radio, CDMA phones are not SIM card capable. GSM phones use SIM cards, so it's more than just changing a radio.
