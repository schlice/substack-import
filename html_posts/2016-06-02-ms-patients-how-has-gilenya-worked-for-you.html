Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I've been on Gilenya for 15 months. I have had no new lesions, and no active lesions on any scan I've had during that time. I haven't experienced any of the episodes of muscle weakness that caused me to switch off of Copaxone after 7 years. I have had my fair share of side effects, but they are side effects of being on an immunosuppressant therapy. I used to get frequent sinus infections as a kid. I was mostly past that as an adult. Within five days of starting Gilenya, I got a sinus infection. It took the entire course of antibiotics to knock it down. Within three months, I got a second, and it took two full courses of antibiotics to get rid of it. So I have to take extra medication (antihistamines and decongestants) daily to try to stay ahead of that. It works, but it's just more pills. I also take an extra 1000 mg of Vitamin C along with my daily multivitamin, also for the immune-boosting effects. I'm also on Vitamin D therapy because my vitamin D levels are chronically low, and I have to take 5000 IUs a day to keep them in check. I also take krill oil (instead of fish oil) to get my Omega-3's for the day.
