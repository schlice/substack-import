**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Yes. Active lawsuits against the HOA do not remove its authority or limit its ability to do its contractually mandated functions. As always, this can vary by state, and there could be some state-level limitation on the authority or function of an HOA during an active lawsuit, but I am unaware of such a limitation.
