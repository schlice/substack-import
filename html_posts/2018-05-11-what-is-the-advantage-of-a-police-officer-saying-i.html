Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} None. Because if this were the case, once you subpoena the dash cam footage from the officer\'s car and it shows no FST performed, the charge gets tossed. Therefore, I highly suspect that something which can be reasonably alleged to be a FST will actually appear on that recording. I\'d prepare for that, if I were you.
