**Content:** [Most police officers in the United States, over the course of their entire careers, will never discharge their firearms anywhere except for the range. It is a small percentage that are actually involved in \"officer-involved shootings.\"\ \ Drawing your weapon is a different story. Officers will draw their weapons many times, but actually discharging it is rare.]{.rendered_qtext}
