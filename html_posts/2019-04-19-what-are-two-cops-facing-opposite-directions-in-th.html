Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What are two cops facing opposite directions in the middle of the highway doing?** Talking. That's the only way you can get the two driver's side windows to come together, otherwise one of them would be shouting through his car to the other car. When they face opposite directions, the driver's side windows are together, and they can sit and talk as if they are sitting next to each other.
