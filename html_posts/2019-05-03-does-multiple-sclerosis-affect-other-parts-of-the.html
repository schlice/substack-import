**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Does multiple sclerosis affect other parts of the body besides the nerves?** Since you called out the nerves, I'll assume you meant that you want to know whether or not the MS disease process involves any other parts of the body besides the Central Nervous System. The answer is no, MS only affects the nerves of the CNS. The MS disease process does not impact any other physical part of the body. The ***result*** of that demyelination of nerves within the CNS can be seen throughout the body, but the actual demyelination process only affects the nerve cells of the CNS.
