**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} If the HOA has a start date or timeframe for decorations, they can enforce it. It seems that in this case, their covenants and rules have a take-down timeframe, but no setup timeframe. So in this particular case, the HOA cannot tell the homeowner to take down the decorations.
