**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You don't need the battery to power the phone...just plug it in. It should turn on while plugged in. If you have plugged it in and it still won't turn on, then perhaps you can get a replacement battery to simply power the unit on to restore your files. Although I'd be really surprised to find an Android phone which won't power on just simply being plugged in, dead battery or not. But you can try replacing the battery. If the data isn't backed up somewhere and you need access to it that badly, perhaps you could buy a replacement handset on eBay just for parts and swap parts in until it starts working. Alternatively, if the data is that valuable, you can take it to a local repair shop who should be able to get it running, and the cost would have to be less than the hassle to recreate the data.
