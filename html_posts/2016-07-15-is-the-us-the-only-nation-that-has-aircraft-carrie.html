--- title: "Is the U.S. the only nation that has aircraft carriers capable of launching and landing aircraft at the same time?" date: "2016-07-15" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Short answer: no. Detail: The following countries have CATOBAR (Catapult-Assisted Take Off But Arrested Recovery) aircraft carriers which have offset runways for both landing and launching simultaneously: **United States\ Brazil\ France** Three other countries run STOBAR (Short Take Off But Arrested Recovery)-style aircraft carriers, and while they cannot launch simultaneously as some of the flight path overlaps, they can alternate launch/recovery: **China\ India\ Russia**[\[1\]](#VxqRb){#cite-VxqRb .citation_link} Several other countries also have VTOL (Vertical Take Off and Landing) and STOVL (Short Take Off, Vertical Landing) carriers in service as well, but since the question asked about simultaneous launches and landings, I did not include them. Check the Wikipedia link for the full list of countries and their aircraft carrier types.
