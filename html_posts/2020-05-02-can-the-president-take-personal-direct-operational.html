**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can the president take personal, direct operational command of the military out in the field?** *Can* he? Yes, he can. He is the Commander-in-Chief. If he actually chose to do something like that, he could. *Would* he? No way in hell. Besides being advised by **every single aide and military representative, including every Chief of Staff and even his own SecDef**, to not do such a thing, it would be *political suicide* to do it, even if he was correct. The President knows his place. He gives orders, and his generals carry them out. He doesn't do the generals' jobs, just as he expects the generals to not do his job, and when they presume to do his job, he fires them.
