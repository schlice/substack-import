**Content:** [Nationalism is not something we experience much in the United States. I would say we experience *patriotism* far more than *nationalism*.\ \ Patriotism is love of country; it is the engine that drives national independence, and the motivation that keeps people defending it. Patriotism is the force which keeps a country like the United States a free country.\ \ Nationalism is patriotism to the extreme. It is the force that is initially patriotic, but develops ethnocentric overtones, believing that your country is *better* than other countries, and you should exert your influence over them.]{.rendered_qtext}
