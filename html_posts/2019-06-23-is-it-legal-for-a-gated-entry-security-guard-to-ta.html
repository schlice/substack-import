**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} If it is disclosed beforehand that a requirement for entry is a police review of driving record and a NCIC check for wants and warrants, then yes it is perfectly legal. I dispute whether or not it is actually done in practice. NCIC isn\'t a system which can be checked willy-nilly. Police are held to strict accountability for every records check they perform, and performing records checks for entry into a gated community, with no reasonable suspicion, let alone probable cause, sounds a bit improbable to me.
