--- title: '"Are military personnel stationed in Washington, Oregon and Colorado allowed to use marijuana off-duty?"' date: '2016-10-30' tags: - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} No. Military members are governed by the Uniform Code of Military Justice (UCMJ). In situations where federal or state law conflicts with the UCMJ, the UCMJ rules, and servicemembers are bound by that first. Marijuana usage is illegal under both federal law and the UCMJ.
