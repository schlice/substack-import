**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How do you remove a stuck battery from a Dell Latitude laptop? One of the sliding tabs is difficult to move.** Do not attempt to remove a stuck battery from a Latitude laptop. If you cannot remove the battery with your fingers only (don't try to use tools to pry the battery out), contact Dell Technical Support. Attempting to use tools to remove the battery could result in a pierced battery and a dangerous leak of battery acid. All Dell laptops come standard with one year of mail-in support. You can check your warranty status on [[our website](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=347c64&url=https://www.dell.com/support/article/SLN153567/en){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="dell.com"}]{.qlink_container}. If your warranty has expired, you can still contact Dell Technical Support who will diagnose your problem and provide you a quote to repair it. Once the quote is accepted, the technician will assist you with sending your laptop to Dell for repair.
