**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Will Michael Flynn lose his pension now that he has plead guilty to committing a felony while in Federal employ?** In general, no. Being convicted of a crime almost never causes a person to lose their military pension. The exception to this would be for crimes related to disloyalty to the United States: treason, espionage, etc. Flynn was convicted of lying to a Federal agent. Not enough to cause him to lose his pension.
