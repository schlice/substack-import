**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It is not necessary to turn off mobile data when your phone connects to Wi-Fi. Your device will automatically use Wi-Fi when it is on and connected. Even better, "Adaptive Wi-Fi" is included on most modern Samsung phones. Previously known as Smart Network switching, enabling the Adaptive Wi-Fi feature allows your network to automatically switch between Wi-Fi and mobile data automatically to maintain a stable network connection. - If your Wi-Fi signal becomes weak or unreliable, your phone will switch to mobile data automatically. - Wi-Fi will turn on automatically when you\'re within range of Wi-Fi networks you use frequently. This will not only keep you on the fastest connection available, it will also save battery by turning off Wi-Fi when you don't need it. \ Original question: When you join WiFi on your Samsung phone, do you have to turn mobile data off?
