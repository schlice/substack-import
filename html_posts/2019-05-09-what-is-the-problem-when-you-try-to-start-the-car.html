**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What is the problem when you try to start the car and you hear rapid fire clicking and the battery has already been charged?** The battery either can't hold a charge, or the battery hasn't been charged *enough*. Jump-start the car from another running car. Then drive down to your local auto parts store and have the battery tested.
