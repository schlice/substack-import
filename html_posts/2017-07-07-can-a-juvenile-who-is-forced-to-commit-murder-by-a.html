Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I dispute the fact that anyone can be "forced" to commit murder. Please explain how you can take a life with forethought and malice, against your will. I find it hard to believe that you can meet the requirements of the charge of murder when you are being compelled to act.
