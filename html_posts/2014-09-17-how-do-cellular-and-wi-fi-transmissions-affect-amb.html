Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [The amount of power needed to affect oxygen bonds (essentially splitting the O2 bond and creating free radicals) is far greater than the power output of even a picocell. This may be an issue surrounding an RF microwave tower, but not around a Wi-Fi base station, even a commercial access point doesn\'t provide the power needed to split oxygen bonds.]{.rendered_qtext}
