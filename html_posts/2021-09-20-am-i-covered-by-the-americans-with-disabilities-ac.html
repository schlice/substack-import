--- title: '"Am I covered by the Americans with Disabilities Act if I have cubital tunnel syndrome preventing use of a keyboard (and very minimally, writing) but not other manual tasks?"' date: '2021-09-20' tags: - veteran-benefits layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Cubital tunnel syndrome is usually not caused by a disability, and is normally a medical condition that can be corrected with surgery and/or physical therapy. If it cannot be corrected, work with your employer to try to get an accommodation. While it likely does not qualify as a "disability" under ADA, it should be something that your employer can accommodate.
