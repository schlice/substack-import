Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Well-regulated does not mean \"an abundance of good rules and regulations.\"\ \ Well-regulated means \"well-functioning.\" Think of a grandfather clock. Its \"regulation\" allows it to keep time accurately. A well-regulated grandfather clock will keep time accurately for years.\ \ Well-regulated, in terms of guns, means that their guns are serviceable, and able to be employed at a moment\'s notice.]{.rendered_qtext}
