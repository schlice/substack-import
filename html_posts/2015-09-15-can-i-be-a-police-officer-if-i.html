**Content:** [In most departments, this is allowed. However, if you are looking at joining a police department while taking antidepressants, make sure you are ***completely honest*** with the department about all medications you are taking. Make sure that when you do your psych eval, you talk to the doctor ***at length*** about why you\'re on antidepressants, what led you to using them, and if your condition is well-controlled on those medications.\ \ I can tell you from personal experience with this process that it is permitted as long as the condition is well managed by a doctor and the condition is well controlled while using the medication. The department will want proof of this, of course, but it is allowed.]{.rendered_qtext}
