Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} *He* doesn't. *You* do. And you shouldn't. You should learn to recognize His existence ***without referencing the Bible at all.*** It would make your argument for the existence of God much more compelling. To be more blunt: **you can prove the existence of a creator god** ***without ever referencing the Bible or any religion at all.*** That's a much more powerful tool than using the Bible as a reference for His existence.
