**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I'm not sure what you mean by "contradicts the CC&Rs" unless you're claiming that there is a line item which specifically forbids them from spending the money in the way that they voted to spend it. Otherwise, if in the name of maintaining the property for which they do have control, they vote to spend money to do that, it sounds like it could be considered a reasonable expenditure. And if the tree removal stops them from spending money in the future, isn't that a good thing? So unless there is a specific line item which says they cannot spend money in the way in which they are spending money, they can probably spend the money.
