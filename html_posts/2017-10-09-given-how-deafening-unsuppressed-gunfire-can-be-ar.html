--- title: "Given how deafening unsuppressed gunfire can be, are armed police officers issued any protection for their hearing?" date: "2017-10-09" tags: ["law-enforcement"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Hearing protection is only routinely worn at the range. Officers know that having to deploy their lethal weapon in the line of duty may be hazardous to their hearing. Thankfully, only very, very rarely does an officer have to do this. There's no realistic way to accomplish this unless an officer uses earplugs with electronic hearing protection and amplification built in.
