**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If convicted by investigations of the Southern district of New York is it still possible to get a presidential pardon?** No. The President can pardon ***Federal*** crimes. The President does not have pardon power over states. The ***governor*** is the only person who can pardon state crimes.
