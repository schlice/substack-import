Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Your other posters are spot-on. Generally, business regulations do not have the power to close a business because of violations of anti-discrimination laws. They can absolutely use violations of those laws as a basis for filing lawsuits, which will eventually become so costly that it will impact the business financially, rendering it unable to stay open.
