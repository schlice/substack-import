**Content:** [Drones are flying aircraft and are regulated by the FAA. Current FAA regulations prohibit armed civilian aircraft of any kind, which would by default include drones.\ \ If we want to allow autonomous personal flying self defense systems, we will have to process a modification to current FAA regulations.]{.rendered_qtext}
