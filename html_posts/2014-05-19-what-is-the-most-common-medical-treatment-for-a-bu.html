**Content:** [Burst eardrums usually heal on their own. While it is healing, keep it dry. Usually a swimmer\'s earplug or a cotton ball coated with Vaseline (on the outside) will help keep the eardrum protected. Your doctor can prescribe eardrops to prevent infection, but don\'t put any drops in your ear if they are not prescribed by your doctor.\ \ If there are problems healing, it can cause hearing loss as well as other issues. These should be treated by your doctor. As long as it is treated promptly and heals correctly, long-term effects should be minimal.]{.rendered_qtext} ***Disclaimer:*** ***This answer is not professional medical advice****, nor is it a substitute for professional medical advice.
