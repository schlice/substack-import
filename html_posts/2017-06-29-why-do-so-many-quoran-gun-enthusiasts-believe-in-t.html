Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Treating a gun as if it is loaded, and knowing that it is unloaded, are two different things. "I didn't know it was loaded" means someone was treating it as if it was unloaded when they didn't confirm for themselves. But there is a big difference in knowing for yourself that the gun is unloaded so that you can perform unloaded actions on it (such as disassembly for cleaning) and treating the gun as if it was unloaded without *knowing.*
