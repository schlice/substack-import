Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Whether or not you have MS doesn\'t affect whether or not you can open a business.\ \ Whether or not you are on disability doesn\'t affect whether or not you can open a business.\ \ Starting and maintaining your own business, however, is *stressful*. You will be inducing a lot of stress, and stress, as you know, can make your MS symptoms worse.\ \ Be cautious when starting your business. Have good understanding business partners who can run the business in your absence. Watch how hard you push yourself, as it can have an adverse impact on your health.]{.rendered_qtext}
