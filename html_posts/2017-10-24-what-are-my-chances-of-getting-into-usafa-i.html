**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Based on just what you've given us, your entrance package is pretty competitive already. Remember that the Academy is looking for that "well-rounded" person, who not only excels as a student-athlete, but is also involved in the community and who has shown leadership potential and the ability to make it at the Academy. Remember, sustained participation in select activities over the course of your high school career is preferred. Demonstrated leadership in athletic and non-athletic extracurricular activities will be crucial, but don't worry if you don't excel in all areas. Just remember you're trying to put the strongest package you can together for admission. And if you haven't taken [[Scott Parman](https://www.quora.com/profile/Scott-Parman)]{.qlink_container}'s advice yet, it's time to get on to the [[Academy Admissions](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=d1e87d&url=https://www.academyadmissions.com){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="academyadmissions.com"}]{.qlink_container} website and look it over. Also, f[[ind your Admissions Liaison Officer](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=3baf43&url=https://www.academyadmissions.com/admissions/outreach-programs/find-your-admissions-liaison-officer/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="academyadmissions.com"}]{.qlink_container} and start working directly with him/her. That person will assist you through every step of the admissions process. I will tell you though, based on what you have here, you are already a pretty strong candidate. You're on the right track! Work with your ALO to "fine-tune" your path, which will only make you an even stronger candidate. Thanks for asking, and good luck!
