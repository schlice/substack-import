--- title: '"Has the Bible given mankind everything that is going to happen in the future?"' date: '2024-06-05' tags: - theology layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} No, the Bible has not listed ***everything*** that is going to happen in the future. The Bible lists ***the most significant things to our eternity*** that will happen in the future. The book of Revelation is the last book in the Bible. It details a period of time where the apostle John was exiled to the island of Patmos, and while he was there, God showed him a vision of the future., the events which are to come, at the time when Christ returns. The book details what will happen in the lead-up to this event, the coming of the Antichrist, the "mark of the beast," and Jesus' return and destruction of Satan.
