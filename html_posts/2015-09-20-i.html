**Content:** [Thanks for the A2A, I\'ll limit my answer to the US Air Force.\ \ If you want to enlist in the Air Force, you have to be between ages 17 and 39, be a U.S. citizen or a permanent, legal resident, have a high school diploma, or have a GED with at least 15 college credits. You will have to take an aptitude test, and you will also have to take a physical test as well as undergo a full health exam. So if you want to join the US Air Force, this is where you start.\ \ If you can become a US citizen before age 34, you can take your degree and use it towards Officer Training School. OTS requires you be between the ages of 18 and 34 and already possess at least a Bachelor\'s Degree.\ \ [[United States Air Force](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=4ec95e&url=http://www.airforce.com){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="airforce.com"}]{.qlink_container}]{.rendered_qtext}
