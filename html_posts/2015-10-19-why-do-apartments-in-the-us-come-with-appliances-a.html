--- title: '"Why do apartments in the US come with appliances and fixtures built in?"' date: '2015-10-19' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Cost.\ \ It is much cheaper for an apartment complex to build out all of its units already appointed with appliances and lights, or in industry terms, \"move-in ready.\" The complex buys these items in bulk, which saves money. Installers\' time is not cheap either. Electricians would have to be hired every time someone moved in or out.\ \ Some apartments in the US can be rented fully-furnished (with furniture like couches and beds), but under most normal circumstances, apartments do not come with furniture.]{.rendered_qtext}
