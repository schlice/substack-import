**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Of course, if you take the power out of the hands of the parent, the child could be influenced by *anyone*: school officials, classmates, state health officials holding "vaccination rallies" in schools, etc. While I feel for people who believe that children are so at risk that they must be vaccinated (which, of course, flies in the face of the science), even against the will of the parent, I don't think the right answer is to remove the ability of a parent to make consequential medical decisions for their children.
