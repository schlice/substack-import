--- title: '"Can we travel between countries with MS (multiple scelorisis) diseases?"' date: '2016-12-01' tags: - health-autoimmune layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Unless your doctor has ordered you not to travel for some reason, simply having MS would not disqualify you from receiving a visa for travel to any country. MS is not contagious, so a person with MS poses no additional health risk to anyone.
