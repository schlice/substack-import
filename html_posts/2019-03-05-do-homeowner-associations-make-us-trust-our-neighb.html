**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Do homeowner associations make us trust our neighbors more or less these days?** I don't think they have much effect on whether or not we trust our neighbors. HOAs do acknowledge the fact that everyone has the right to do with their homes whatever they choose, but the choices that they make could have adverse effects on their neighbors (particularly, their home values), and their neighbors may not like that. HOAs give homeowners the comfort of knowing that if their neighbors do something stupid like paint their houses bright pink, they have some recourse to salvage their home values.
