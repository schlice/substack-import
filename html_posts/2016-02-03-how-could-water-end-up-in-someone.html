**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Pulmonary Aspiration.** Pulmonary aspiration is inhaling something into the windpipe that doesn\'t belong there. When related to eating, it\'s often called \"going down the wrong pipe.\" I suppose it could be possible that one could inhale enough mildly vaporized water to cause some form of appreciable water buildup in the lungs
