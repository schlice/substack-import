**Content:** [Law is a funny thing. It\'s really nothing more than words on a piece of paper. You can choose to follow it, or you can choose not to follow it. Humans are created with free will.\ \ In America, we choose to live together as members of a civil society. In order to do that, we have to cede some control to the law, so that all of us can live together in an ordered society. Without law, there would be anarchy. Rioting in the streets daily. Murder. Utter chaos.\ \ We choose not to live like that. In order to do so, we choose to follow the law.\ \ There will always be those who don\'t follow the law. For them, we have the police and the courts (the Executive and Judicial branches of government) who arrest the lawless and remove them from society, and then prosecute them under the law.\ \ The court system sometimes doesn\'t look \"fair.\" Sometimes, the court system looks downright cruel and unfair. But know that it is definitely fair, even though it may appear to be unfair. There are reasons for every decision.]{.rendered_qtext}
