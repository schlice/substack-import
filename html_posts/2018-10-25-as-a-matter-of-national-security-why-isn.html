**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **As a matter of national security, why isn\'t Donald Trump stopped from making calls on phones he knows aren\'t secure?** You only need to be concerned about the phone you're using when you're discussing things that are not unclassified. Just because you have the capability of discussing classified things doesn't mean you have to use secure phones 100% of the time. It comes down to policing yourself and knowing you're on an unsecured line and not discussing classified things on an unsecured line. You can't police everyone, least of all the President. All you can do is provide him the tools he needs to conduct classified discussions securely while giving him all the tools he wants to make any kind of call he needs. Additionally, don't forget that a secured phone call requires secure telephone devices ***on both ends*** or the call cannot be completed. That said, the President has all of the tools available to him at all times to make any classification of phone call he may need.
