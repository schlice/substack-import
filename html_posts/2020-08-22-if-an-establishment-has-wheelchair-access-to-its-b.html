**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If an establishment has wheelchair access to its building, but within that building the access significantly restricts wheelchair patrons, would it be a good idea to share those concerns with them, publicly on a web review, or both?** It is *absolutely* a good idea to share the concerns with them, both in person and in a public web review. But I would first share the concerns directly with the general manager / owner, and see if there is anything they can do. The quality of their response should weigh into the characterization of the establishment you choose to make in your web review.
