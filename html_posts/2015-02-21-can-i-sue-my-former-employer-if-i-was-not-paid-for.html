**Content:** [I would suggest you talk to a lawyer. If you had a job, you probably had an agreement of some kind that you signed when you hired on that specified when and how you were to be paid. If you did, and you did work in accordance with your work agreement, and you weren\'t paid for the work you did, you probably have a case.]{.rendered_qtext}
