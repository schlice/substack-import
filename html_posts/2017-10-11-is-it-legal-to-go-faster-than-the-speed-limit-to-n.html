--- title: '"Is it legal to go faster than the speed limit to not create a hazard for faster traffic?"' date: '2017-10-11' tags: - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Since you're asking a legal question, the answer is **no**, it is not legal to go faster than the speed limit for any reason. The correct response, if you are at the speed limit and must move out of the way of faster traffic, is to slow down until you can safely move over.
