There are a number of differences, however. \- The B-1 is used primarily as a strategic bomber where the Tu-160 is used primarily as a standoff missile platform.\ - Because the Tu-160 has a different mission than the B-1, it accordingly has a greater range and is slightly larger than the B-1. The B-1 has a greater combined payload based on its primary mission as a strategic bomber.\ - Neither of the aircraft is considered a stealth aircraft. The bottom of the B-1 is painted with RAM (radar-absorbent material) which gives it a black look in the sky, and is generally painted gray or brown on top. The Tu-160 is painted in anti-flash white (a paint which reflects some nuclear radiation, meant to protect the crew). The B-1 is nicknamed the \"Bone\" (B-One) by its pilots. The anti-flash white paint scheme of the Tu-160 is what gives it the moniker \"the White Swan\" by its pilots. Different planes, different roles.
