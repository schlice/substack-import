**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What is the policy regarding the safety pin of a firearm in the US Armed Forces?** Most firearms have a **safety** and a **firing pin**. Most firearms do not have a **safety pin**. There are no policies *across the US Armed Forces* that apply to any one of them.
