**Content:** [I can\'t answer for RCAF, but in the USAF, if you\'re a pilot, there\'s a virtual 100% chance that you\'re going to deploy at one time or another.\ \ The only difference may have to do with the airframe. Long-haul aircraft can do a three day mission overseas and return without requiring deployments. But even they will deploy when resources need to be staged closer to a particular theater. With global reach being the norm, shorter deployments are becoming more frequent.]{.rendered_qtext}
