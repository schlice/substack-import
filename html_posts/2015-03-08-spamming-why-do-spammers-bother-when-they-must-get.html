Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Spammers are all about volume. Clickthrough from their ad campaigns is very very low. Clickthrough is the term which describes when a user clicks on a link in a spam message.\ \ Since clickthrough is so low, they rely on volume. Even that one spam message that makes it on to social media might generate a clickthrough, even though they get kicked off of that site quickly. For that one spam account, they have 50 more doing the same thing, and not all of them are identified quite as quickly.]{.rendered_qtext}
