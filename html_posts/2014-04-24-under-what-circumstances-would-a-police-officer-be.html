**Content:** [There are certain conditions in which the municipality in question is trying to change its enforcement policy.\ \ As you know, a law is only as good as its enforcement. In my area, \"sliding\" is a traffic violation (i.e. making a left turn into the right lane). However, it has never been enforced other than to add it as an additional infraction during an accident investigation. A few years ago, the city started a public awareness campaign to improve compliance with this law. Our city and county officers and deputies were directed to make traffic stops for the infraction, but to only issue warnings for the behavior. This was an attempt to advise people that their driving behaviors were in fact illegal before actual enforcement began.]{.rendered_qtext}
