**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How hard is it to get the nomination required for the Naval Academy?** Nominations for any service academy are similar. You apply with the nominating official (usually your Congressman), complete an application, and be interviewed in front of a review board. It's not that the process is difficult, it's that you're usually up against quite a bit of competition. When I applied, there were roughly 100 applicants from my district. I was one of **two** selected by my Congressman, as he had two slots available at USAFA. So that year, I literally had a **2%** chance of getting a nomination, all else being equal. So it's not a difficult process, but it is a very narrow gate through which you must pass.
