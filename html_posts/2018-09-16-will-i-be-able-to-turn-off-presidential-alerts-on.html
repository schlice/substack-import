--- title: '"Will I be able to turn off Presidential Alerts on my phone, like I can turn off AMBER Alerts and Emergency Alerts?"' date: '2018-09-16' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} In Android, you cannot disable Presidential alerts. Start the Messages app and press the menu button (three dots) in the upper right corner, and select Settings \> Emergency alert settings \> Emergency alerts. There, you will see "Presidential alerts: Presidential alerts are mandatory and cannot be turned off." I imagine that if you root your phone, or use a third party SMS app, you may be able to disable Presidential alerts. I haven\'t looked it up yet. If I get a chance later, I\'ll see what I can find.
