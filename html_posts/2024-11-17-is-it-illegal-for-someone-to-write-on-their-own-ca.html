--- title: '"Is it illegal for someone to write on their own car if it is parked outside their house? Can the police charge them for this?"' date: '2024-11-17' tags: - law-enforcement - homeowners-assn layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} In general, no. There are specific things you write on the car which could be actionable, but the police probably won't be the one charging you. For example, if you wrote "My neighbor Greg is a child molester," you could be sued for libel and ordered to pay a big fine if you are wrong about that, but you don't get arrested or charged by the police. And you could run afoul of HOA regulations about cars with writing on them if your HOA has such a regulation, but that doesn't involve the police either.
