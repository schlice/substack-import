**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If I build a privacy fence on common property, does the Homeowner Association own it?** Yes they do. If you build a fence on someone else's property, that someone else (whether it be a private owner or an HOA) can tear down the fence and bill you for the cost of doing so. If you want a fence on that property, petition the Board for a fence. Tell the Board you'll be happy to pay the entire cost of the installation of the fence. Perhaps the Board will approve your request.
