--- title: '"Have you ever confronted a childhood bully, and if so, what was the result?"' date: '2016-08-21' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I beat him senseless. And it was the worst experience of my life. We were in Junior High school, so I was probably 13 or 14 at the time. I can remember exactly where I was in the school, during what you would call a "passing period" today. He had been bullying me (verbal harassment) non stop, daily, for months. I guess it was just bottled up for too long, and I lost it. He said something that I don\'t remember from right behind me (something about his supposed sexual exploits with my mother, I think), and I snapped. My vision clouded over. I threw my books down. I spun around, grabbed him around the neck with my arm, and punched him in the face. A lot. I also think I ran him into a locker. I don\'t remember much after that. He was on the ground, very bloody, but most of it was probably from his lips getting cut up on his braces. It\'s all pretty hazy after that, but the next thing I remember is being seated in the Dean\'s office. I looked down at my hands, and they were still balled into fists. I tried to open them, but I couldn\'t. I forced myself to open them, and found it very difficult to do so, and when they finally opened, I saw nail cuts on my palms from my own nails. I was traumatized and inconsolable for quite some time. It was an absolutely terrible experience.
