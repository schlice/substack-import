**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What gun laws would have prevented him from doing what he did?** Lanza's mother legally acquired her firearms, following all gun laws at the time. What exactly do you suggest we could do that would have prevented a madman from killing his mother and stealing her guns? What law would have prevented this? Let me answer for you: **there is no law that can stop someone from breaking the law.** If a law is going to be broken, you cannot stop the criminal by *passing a new law.*
