**Content:** [As most everyone else said, the local arrest records showed that Roof had a misdemeanor drug charge, not a felony. He was still legally allowed to purchase a firearm. He was arrested in a mall in South Carolina for being in possession of Suboxone, which is a drug for heroin addiction treatment. He was under indictment for that charge at the time. However, that drug charge was a misdemeanor, not a felony, and would not have prevented Roof from purchasing a gun under Federal law.]{.rendered_qtext}
