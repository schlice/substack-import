--- title: '"Will Hillary be able to effectively overturn the 2nd Amendment with her Supreme Court appointees?"' date: '2016-06-21' tags: - policy-analysis layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} A2A Short answer: if she alters the court, she can. Longer answer: While she may not be able to do things like outlawing ammunition (which has already been ruled as unconstitutional), she may be able to get the Heller decision overturned, having the court rule that the Second Amendment does not confer an individual right to gun ownership, but instead only provides a right to own a gun if the citizen is part of the militia, effectively making it illegal to own a gun if you are not a member of the militia.
