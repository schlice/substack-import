--- title: "How do I unblock a SIM that got blocked by putting it in another phone?" date: "2021-06-09" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} It's unlikely that your SIM was blocked by putting it into another phone. It's much more likely that the phone you put the SIM into does not accept the SIM from your carrier. If, however, your SIM was indeed blocked, you will need to contact the Customer Service department for your carrier to have it unblocked. There is no other way to unlock a SIM, as it is the carrier which controls access to its network through that SIM. If the phone you put the SIM into is locked to one type of carrier, you may need to unlock the phone to work on your carrier. When the phone hasn't been paid completely, carriers will lock the phone to their network. You can pay a fee to a third-party company to unlock your phone, or you can wait until the phone is completely paid off, and the carrier will unlock it for you. \ Original question: How do I unblock a SIM that got blocked by putting it in another phone?
