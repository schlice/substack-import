**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Will Rep. John Conyers and Sen. Al Franken voluntarily surrender to police to be booked and arraigned for felony sexual assault, or will they have to be arrested and handcuffed in their offices?** First of all, I don't think either of them are the subjects of criminal investigations. I don't see where any accusers have any legal standing to begin criminal investigations, as it appears that the allegations of misconduct have all happened many years ago, and the statutes of limitations have already expired. But even if there was a criminal charge and even if the District Attorney wanted to arrest them, they would work out a deal under which they would voluntarily surrender to police. They would not need to be handcuffed in their offices.
