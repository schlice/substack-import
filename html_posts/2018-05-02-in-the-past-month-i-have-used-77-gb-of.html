**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **In the past month, I have used 77 GB of "service". 74 GB of that was the hotspot on my iPhone. Is that bad?** The definition of "bad" is dependent on a lot of things. - It may be bad if you were billed for all 77 GB and you can't afford your bill. - It may be bad if you don't actually have any hotspot service on your mobile contract (i.e. you converted your phone to hotspot-capable without hotspot service on your contract) and they terminate your service for it. - It may be bad if you don't have hotspot service on your phone or your contract but you're being billed for it anyway.
