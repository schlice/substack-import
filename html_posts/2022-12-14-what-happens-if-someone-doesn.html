**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Not sure what you mean by "leaves the property." Are you saying that the person sells their house and leaves without paying their dues? Part of the disclosures during a home sale is confirmation of a clean account in the HOA. If the previous homeowner hasn't paid their dues, those back dues will have to be paid before the HOA will provide a certificate of account for the sale. In other words, if you're running a balance due with the HOA, you won't be able to sell your house.
