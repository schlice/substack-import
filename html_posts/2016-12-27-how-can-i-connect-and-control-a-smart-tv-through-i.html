Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} There is no standard behind the moniker "Smart TV." It's not like that implies some sort of standard feature set or standard build. It simply means the TV has an application framework built in, probably some default apps, and can connect to a wired or wireless network. That said, most Smart TVs have apps available which will use your network to connect to and control your Smart TV. Check the app store for your brand of TV, and you will likely find that the manufacturer has provided an app for your brand and model.
