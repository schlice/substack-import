Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The Eufy SoloCam S220 is unique because it requires no subscription fees to run. It is completely self-contained, with its own onboard 8GB storage. However, you can also connect it to an optional HomeBase 3 unit, which has expandable memory. Additionally, cloud storage is *available* for a fee, but it is not **required**. The HomeBase gives you some additional features, like AI face detection. It is battery powered, and has a built-in solar panel. Only two hours of direct sunlight a day is enough to keep the batteries essentially charged forever.
