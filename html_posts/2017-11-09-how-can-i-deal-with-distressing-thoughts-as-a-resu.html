Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Talk to a counselor.** There's a stigma in the United States around talking to counselors. Please do not fall victim to this stigma. Find a good counselor in your area and talk to them. Your doctor can help you find a good counselor if you don't know one. Please, please do this. It will help tremendously.
