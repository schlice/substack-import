**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What is the legal reasoning why it did not violate Federal law for the Steele dossier to be given the Clinton campaign? I am not looking for analysis of political or moral aspects here, just the legalities of the situation.** Because there is no Federal law which makes opposition research illegal. The only laws surrounding opposition research relate to campaign finance funding, where the money comes from, and on what it can be spent. Beyond that, researching your opponent is not illegal. Using outside agencies to research your opponent is also not illegal.
