**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Could Trump take big money donors on Air Force One for a couple of hours, if he covered the operating costs?** Not for a joy-ride, but if passengers are traveling on AF1, they must pay commercial-equivalent airfare for the trip. The RNC can certainly pick up that tab if they choose, but that fare will not be paid for by taxpayers. He can't just put the plane in the air, circle it around for a while and then land it. That would be a waste of money and wouldn't be tolerated by internal or external watchdog groups.
