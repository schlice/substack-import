**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If, as the White House says, Trump has no compromised relationship with Russia, why does he go out of his way to hide his interactions with Putin?** There could be several reasons for this. First, Trump is a businessman, not a politician. As a businessman and CEO, he routinely does things "on his own," without the assistance of aides and assistants. This may be one of his negotiating tactics which he uses to ensure that the person with whom he is negotiating feels open to interaction, because only Trump is the keeper of that information. Secondly, Trump's organization leaks like a sieve. Trump can never be sure that any of his aides or assistants can keep a secret. No amount of military-level classification of information will stop today's leakers from leaking. So he may be holding this information close to his vest specifically to keep it from leaking.
