**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Since we're in the MS Forum, I'll answer for MS. MS presents itself most generally between ages 20 and 50; however, it does present in some significantly younger and older people (Pediatric MS and Late Onset MS, or LOMS, respectively). Symptoms are the same as any other time.
