**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Why does president Donald Trump keep screaming "witch hunt" during Mueller's investigation when most of trumps campaign staff has already been indicted or charged?** There are several reasons. 1. Trump capitalizes on buzzwords. Crooked Hillary and Lyin' Ted were only a thing because Trump made them so. And you can't hear "Mueller probe" without thinking "witch hunt" somewhere in the recesses of your mind. It's very effective marketing. 2. Trump is trying to keep everyone focused on the central original goal of the Mueller probe: Trump colluding with Russia to steal the election from Hillary. To this day, no one who has been charged or indicted has been charged or indicted with any crime even tangentially related to Trump colluding with Russia to steal the election from Hillary. So he created the buzzword "witch hunt" to remind people that no one has yet been charged with anything related to Trump colluding with Russia to steal an election. Yes, it's true that those people committed crimes. They did fail to register as foreign agents, and they did lie to the FBI, and they did evade paying taxes. This is what prosecutors do. They throw the net wide, and they see who they catch. They then sit that person down and tell them that they're going to jail for a long time for their crime **unless** they agree to roll over on Trump. Whether or not anyone has done this is anyone's guess, but it is highly unlikely. Thus, Trump calls it a witch hunt.
