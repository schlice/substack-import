**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} My guess is you're looking at a Ford, a common law enforcement vehicle brand. If so, you're looking at the door lock keypad. :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-509d77bf5f7cf18680aad19a68d60d88){.landscape .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-509d77bf5f7cf18680aad19a68d60d88" master_w="670" master_h="503"} ::: :::: Ford is well known for having vehicles with a 5-button keypad on the driver's side B-pillar. Input a 5-digit code, and the driver's door opens. Then press the 3/4 button, and all doors open, or press the 5/6 button and the powered trunk/powered liftgate opens. If you press the 7/8 and 9/0 buttons together, all doors lock.
