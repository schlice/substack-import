**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Designing a downtown streetscape. Is the city responsible for building ADA accessible ramps into businesses if none currently exist and would technically be in the right-of-way, or is it the responsibility of the business owner?** The business is responsible for building ADA accessible ramps into businesses if they are required. The city is tasked with designing a streetscape which is capable of accepting ADA accessible ramps if they are needed. If the design can be accommodated, perhaps the design could obviate the need for a ramp at all (i.e. building the sidewalk at threshold level).
