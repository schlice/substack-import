**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **My Galaxy S8 keeps randomly crashing, even if I am not using it. Is that bad, and if so, can I stop it from getting worse or crashing?** 1. Start with a factory reset. This will wipe and re-install your phone, and your apps will download from Google again. Bear in mind that any data which is not stored in Google or Samsung Cloud will be lost; make sure you have good backups of any critical data. 2. If the issue still persists after a factory reset, contact your carrier's Technical Support. Make sure you tell them you just did a factory reset, because that will be their first suggestion.
