**Content:** [Do you remember the movie *Mask*? There is a scene where Rocky (who has Lionitis, or **craniodiaphyseal dysplasia**) teaches Diana (the pretty blind girl) what colors look like.\ \ He hands her a cotton ball and says, \"this is white.\" He hands her a hot rock and says, \"this is red.\"\ \ I have always imagined that this is how the blind \"see\" colors.\ \ If someone has been blind from birth, I do often wonder if they know what colors look like.]{.rendered_qtext}
