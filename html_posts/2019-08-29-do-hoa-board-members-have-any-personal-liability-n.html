Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Do HOA board members have any personal liability not covered by typical HOA insurance coverage?** Yes, and HOAs also carry personal indemnity insurance for Board members, in addition to typical HOA insurance coverage. It's called "D&O (Director and Officer) insurance." Board members typically have no additional exposure beyond what is covered by the liability insurance for Board members.
