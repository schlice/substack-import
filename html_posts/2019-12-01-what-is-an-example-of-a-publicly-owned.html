**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What is an example of a publicly owned "private space"?** The best example would be a private space within a publicly owned area. A restroom in the courthouse would be a good example. The restroom is publicly owned, as it's in a government building; but it is a private space, as it is one of the commonly accepted private spaces in any building. Public housing might be another example of a government building in which private spaces exist.
