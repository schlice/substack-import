Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} In general, yes. If the prohibition is listed somewhere in the Governing Documents (which includes any Rules and Regulations the HOA is authorized to create) then they can indeed prohibit commercial vehicles from parking in a resident's driveway. My last HOA did have this restriction, but my current HOA does not. But even in my last HOA, we made an exception for emergency vehicles (i.e. vehicles that you are required to have so you can respond from your home to emergencies).
