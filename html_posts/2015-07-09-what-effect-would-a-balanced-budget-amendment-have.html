Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [I agree with [[Robert J. Kolker](https://www.quora.com/profile/Robert-J-Kolker)]{.qlink_container} that social services would decline.\ \ But from a purely Libertarian perspective, they should. The government should not be redistributing people\'s wealth. Supporting the poor is something that individuals and charitable organizations do for each other. Government should get out of the welfare game; it was never designed to be a welfare supplier, and shouldn\'t be one now.\ \ The Declaration of Independence said that people have the right, and the government should protect, the \"pursuit\" of happiness. Government was never designed to \"supply\" happiness.]{.rendered_qtext}
