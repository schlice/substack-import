--- title: "Is it legal to shout/yell at people?" date: "2015-10-02" tags: ["law"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Depends on what you say. You can\'t disturb others with your shouting. You can\'t be slanderous with your speech. You can't threaten to hurt people. You can\'t incite a riot. But other than that, if you have the right to speak, you have the right to speak *loudly.*
