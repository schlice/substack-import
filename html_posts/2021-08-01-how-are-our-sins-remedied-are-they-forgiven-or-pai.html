Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Our sins have both been forgiven, and paid for. It is through the *grace of God* that our sins have been forgiven, and it is through the *blood of Christ* and his sacrifice on the cross that our sin debt has been paid. Christ stands there, in front of you, holding out a gift in his outstretched arms. It is the gift of eternal life. It was through the grace of God that Jesus was given the opportunity to pay for your sins, because God is love. And it was through Christ's sacrifice that the debt of your sin has been paid for. And this gift is freely available to you, through God's grace. And all you have to do is reach out and accept it. \ Original question: How are our sins remedied? Are they forgiven or paid for?
