--- title: "If a gun owner injures someone else ( a friend, child) accidentally with his gun and the victim is also uninsured should the victim be held liable for the medical expenses or the gun owner?" date: "2015-04-15" tags: ["policy-analysis"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Thanks for the A2A.\ \ If it was a true accident, then the \"victim\" is not a victim of anything but accidental circumstances.\ \ If there was *negligence* involved in the accident (i.e. someone left a fully loaded weapon with a safety off out on a table somewhere), you could make a claim that the gun owner was acting in a negligent manner and should shoulder some of the cost of the medical care, but a case of negligence would be decided by the court.]{.rendered_qtext}
