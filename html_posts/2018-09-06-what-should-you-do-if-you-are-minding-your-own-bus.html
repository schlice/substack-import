{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What should you do if you are minding your own business and an armed police officer approaches you, out of the view of others, and speaks to you in a threatening manner?** Answer his questions. Don't be a jerk; don't provoke him or snub your nose at him. If he arrests you, don't resist arrest. If you really felt that his behavior was unacceptable, feel free to report him. If you weren't arrested, you'll have to go down to the station to file an official report. Be prepared to be interviewed in an interview room. Be prepared to give your real name, address, and phone number. If you were arrested, inform your lawyer of the issue you have with the officer's conduct, and ensure that while your lawyer is addressing the charge at hand, he is also, separately, addressing the officer's conduct during the incident.
