Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Depends on the state. In some states, the Rules and Regulations documents do not need to be filed with the city or county, so it would be up to the HOA to get those to you. In others, the Rules and Regulations must be filed with the county or city, and you can get it from them. The quickest and easiest way would be to make a request to the HOA themselves (easiest way is to visit the HOA's website), or if you're having difficulty with that, ask your realtor for assistance. \ Original question: In buying a house, how do I get a copy of the HOA regulations before making an offer?
