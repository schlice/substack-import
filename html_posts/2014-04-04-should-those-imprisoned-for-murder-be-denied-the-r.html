**Content:** [I agree with [[Geoffrey](https://www.quora.com/profile/Geoffrey-Reemer)]{.qlink_container} on this, but let me add some legal background around why not.\ \ Even though they may be convicted felons, they are still citizens of the Realm and still retain the very large majority of rights simply by their status as a citizen. They already have certain rights curtailed while they are incarcerated. They are \"wards of the state\" while they are incarcerated, and it is the responsibility of the state to provide them a violence-free living facility. In this case, the state failed to keep him safe.]{.rendered_qtext}
