Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Zero.** No one who actually has MS has been cured of their MS. This shouldn't be a controversial position. We don't know what causes MS, so we don't have a cure for MS. Some people *claim* that they have a cure for MS. But to date, every cure someone believes they have has been shown to not work for everyone with MS. Therefore, *it's not a cure.* There isn't a diet that cures MS yet. There isn't a treatment that cures MS yet. There isn't a drug that cures MS yet. Said another way, if you "had MS," and you did something (a drug, a supplement, a treatment, a diet, etc) and it "cured your MS," you didn't have MS. Period, full stop.
