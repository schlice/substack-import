Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** [Your property is most definitely an area under the law. You have few rights related to the \"airspace\" over your property.\ \ When it comes to privacy, current law relates to a person on the ground. So if you have a 6 foot privacy fence around your yard, that would constitute a reasonable expectation of privacy for your property.\ \ As you can infer, privacy laws are sketchy on the air over your house; in most cases, anyone flying over your house can view your yard. This is changing; drones are driving that argument. So laws related to that are varied across the country.]{.rendered_qtext}
