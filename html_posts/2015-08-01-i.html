**Content:** [First of all, we cannot give specific legal advice on Quora. If you want specific advice, you will need to hire your own attorney.\ \ You may have signed an agreement when you entered practice with your clinic. That agreement may have contained a \"non-compete\" clause which would prevent you from taking patients with you for X amount of time after you leave the practice. If this is the case, it\'s generally legally binding and has been upheld more often than not in court.\ \ If there is no such \"non-compete\" clause, generally patients can see whatever provider they choose.\ \ I would hire an attorney and have him review your legal documents which you signed when you entered your clinic practice.]{.rendered_qtext}
