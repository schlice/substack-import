**Content:** [While I\'ve not committed a crime in my adult life, I did steal a few packs of trading cards from the local candy store when I was a boy. Initially, I thought it would be exhilarating, but it was the most exasperating experience I ever put myself through. For weeks, I had a knot in my stomach, constantly looking over my shoulder, afraid that someone saw me run out of the store and would see me again and remember what I did and tell my parents.\ \ I still feel that same twinge today thinking about that incident, which I think is a great lesson that I taught myself: if I feel this way after stealing a few packs of trading cards, how much worse would I feel if I stole something more substantial? Just based on what I know from stealing something very small, I know that there is no way in He11 that I would steal anything, ever again. I think it would kill me from the inside out.\ \ I absolutely regret what I did, and if that store was still in business today, I\'d go pay them back for the cards I took.
