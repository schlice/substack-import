**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Despite what all of my compatriots have suggested, there are good groups to join if you want to work on banning guns in the US. - [[Everytown for Gun Safety](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=b142f6&url=https://www.everytown.org/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="everytown.org"}]{.qlink_container} - [[Moms Demand Action](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=7a3faf&url=https://momsdemandaction.org/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="momsdemandaction.org"}]{.qlink_container} - [[Brady Campaign](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=0c17ad&url=https://www.bradyunited.org/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="bradyunited.org"}]{.qlink_container} If you believe strongly in this issue, by all means, join these groups and work hard to eliminate guns in the US. My colleagues here have done an admirable job explaining ***why these group's efforts will never achieve what you're looking for***, but hey, it's not up to me to fail to answer your question just because you're never going to achieve what you're trying to do. Still, if you want to join a group to work on banning guns in the US, these are the groups for you. Good luck!
