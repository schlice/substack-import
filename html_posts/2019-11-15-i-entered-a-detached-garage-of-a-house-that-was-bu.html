**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **I entered a detached garage of a house that was but is no longer under construction, without intent, but took something when I saw it. Is this burglary?** Technically, burglary is knowingly entering or unlawfully remaining on someone else\'s property with the intent to commit a crime (other than trespass). If you form the intent to commit a crime (in this case, theft) *after* you enter the property, it is not burglary. So the answer is this is not burglary. It is trespass and theft, but not burglary.
