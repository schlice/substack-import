**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Let me ask you this question: If you carry your baby to term and give it up for adoption, ***what difference does it make?*** If you have been mentally scarred by your experience, and you simply don't want to go through that again, and if it's truly "your body, your choice," then if you choose to carry the baby to term, what's the harm in that? In other words, if you feel it is going to be better for your mental health to carry the baby to term and then give it up for adoption, then why do you care what your mother thinks about abortion vs adoption? It doesn't matter whether or not abortion *actually* can cause mental damage; there will be plenty of opinions on either side of the aisle. None of them are relevant. What matters is what *you* think. I truly believe it is your body, and you do get to choose. I would *prefer* that you carry the baby to term and give it up for adoption rather than killing it, but that's my preference, it has no bearing on you or your life. **TL;DR: Don't worry about what your mom thinks.
