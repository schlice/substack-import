--- title: '"Can 2 different police districts charge you for the same offense?"' date: '2016-09-27' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} (US-specific answer) It's highly unlikely. Usually, a single offense takes place within a single jurisdiction. You don't normally have a single offense taking place in multiple jurisdictions. If, for some reason, you have a single offense which occurs in more than one jurisdictions, the two will collaborate on your prosecution. Ultimately, only one will actually move to indict.
