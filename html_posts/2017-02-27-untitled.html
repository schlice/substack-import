**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} *"guns have no place in an institute of learning."* Tell that to the school shooters, who don't generally believe that. In a college, students can choose to leave themselves defenseless*,* as they are legally adults. In the military, we call that a "soft target." Remember, shooters are not stupid. They may have warped values and morals, but they are most definitely not stupid. You don't see shooters shooting up a police station or a firing range, because those are "hard targets." :::: ui_qtext_image_outer ::: qtext_image_wrapper ![](images/qimg-1a5b15bbe8e0311fa97751230d9d62e0){.landscape .qtext_image .zoomable_in_feed master_src="images/qimg-1a5b15bbe8e0311fa97751230d9d62e0" master_w="462" master_h="350"} ::: :::: However, our primary schools are a different story. Our children do not have the option to leave themselves defenseless. It is our responsibility to ***keep*** them safe while they are in our charge at school.
