It\'s like making a new crime called killing when we already have crimes for murder and manslaughter that cover the instances when you unlawfully take the life of another.\ \ Hate crimes are generally modifiers to existing crimes. A hate crime is generally charged more severely than the actual crime itself. And the only thing we are determining is the thought, the attitude, behind the person committing the crime.
