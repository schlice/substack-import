**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} As with all legal questions, the answer is "it depends." The juveniles, if anything at all, will be handled by the juvenile justice system. This system does not involve criminal charges and does not involve lawsuits. It seeks to educate juveniles and parents so that juveniles can have positive transitions into adulthood. This may involve individual and group counseling. It could potentially involve some time in a juvenile detention facility, but not always. This, however, is all dependent on the state.
