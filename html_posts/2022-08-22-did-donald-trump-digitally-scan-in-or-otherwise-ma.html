**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} When we're talking about things like this, we should always remember the old adage: **Never attribute to malice what could easily be explained by stupidity.** People are attacking Trump over classified materials in his home. Yet people so quickly forget that Hillary (who was not the president, mind you) had an official government email server in her house, which was routinely handling tens of thousands of classified emails, and which was revealed by forensic analysis to have likely been compromised. The truth is that this document thing happens with presidents all the time when they leave office. It happened with Obama, and it happened with George W. Bush. Trump didn't personally order any of those documents removed from the White House. The GSA has contractors who packed up his documents and personal effects from the White House and moved them to Mar-a-Lago. There were many boxes of documents which were likely in storage, and he likely had no idea what was in there. And yet, Trump became the first former president to have a search warrant served at his private residence over documents. Sounds legit... And with the Secret Service security around that place, do you really think that those documents were wholly unprotected? All of the people who had access to that residence were thoroughly vetted and had clearance. Not necessarily clearance to see those documents, but they weren't "Joe off the street," and they definitely weren't "Russian spies." When the Trump Presidential Library is built (and I have mixed feelings while typing those words), the documents would have gone through a validation period and would have either been sent to the library, or back to DC. This validation is done because a third party does the move. **Never attribute to malice what could easily be explained by stupidity.** **And if you** ***are*** **starting with the opinion and the contention that it is malicious, you probably have an agenda.** \ Original question: Did Donald Trump digitally scan in or otherwise make copies of the Mar-a-Lago documents before the FBI got them?
