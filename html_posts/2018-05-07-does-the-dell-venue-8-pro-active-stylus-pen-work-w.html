{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Hello The Dell Active Stylus which works with the Venue 8 Pro does not work on capacitive touchscreens as a standard stylus. I tested one on my Galaxy Tab A and it did nothing. The Active Stylus is designed to pair and work with select Dell models: - Inspiron 7568 (FHD Only) (the wake up feature is not available) - Latitude 11 (5175) - Latitude 11 (5179) - Latitude 7275 - Venue 10 Pro (5056) - Venue 8 Pro (5855) - XPS 12 (9250) You can find more information and support information about the Active Stylus [[here](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=57b9e5&url=http://www.dell.com/support/article/us/en/04/sln303677/dell-active-pen-pn556w-pn557w-dell-laptop-and-tablet-compatibility-list?lang%3Den){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="dell.com"}]{.qlink_container}. \ *I work for Dell Technologies. #IWork4Dell*
