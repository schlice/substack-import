**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Are there laws against indecent skywriting?** While there isn't a specific law about skywriting, this would most likely be covered under any indecent display law. Additionally, since it's virtually certain that a child will see the skywriting, the offender can also be hit with a "contributing to the delinquency of a minor" charge as well.
