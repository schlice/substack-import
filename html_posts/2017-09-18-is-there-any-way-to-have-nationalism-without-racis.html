--- title: '"Is there any way to have nationalism without racism?"' date: '2017-09-18' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Yes, nationalism and racism are distinct and different. Nationalism is pride in your nation. Nationalism drives you to defend your own nation. Nationalism can appear to be racism if you have a homogeneous culture, but it's not about the race of the person, it's about the national origin. Extreme nationalism may be more closely linked to xenophobia than racism.
