**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} In almost every state I know, theft of property is classified based on the value of the property stolen and the circumstances of the crime. Since I have Colorado Revised Statutes readily available, here\'s our example: **Class 2 Misdemeanor Theft.** When the value of the property stolen is less than \$500, Colorado law classifies the theft as a class 2 misdemeanor (CRS §18-4-401(2)(b)), also known as \"petty theft.\" Punishment for a class 2 misdemeanor is a minimum sentence of three months and a \$250 fine, and a maximum sentence of 12 months and a \$1,000 fine. (§ 18-1.3-501(1)(a)). **Class 1 Misdemeanor Theft.** When the value of the property stolen is \$500 or more but less than \$1,000, the theft is a class 1 misdemeanor (§18-4-401(2)(b.5). Punishment is a minimum sentence of six months and a \$500 fine, up to a maximum sentence of 18 months and a \$5,000 fine (§18-1.3-501(1)(a)). **Class 5 Felony Theft.** If property is taken from the person of another, without force or intimidation (otherwise the offense could be bumped up to robbery \[theft through force\]) or extortion (theft through intimidation) (§18-4-401(5)), the theft constitutes a class 5 felony theft. It carries a sentence of one to three years imprisonment, including at least two years of parole (remember that in Colorado, parole is an additional punishment on top of jail time) (§18-1.3-401(V)(A)). **Class 4 Felony Theft.** If the value of the property stolen is \$1,000 or more but less than \$20,000, the theft is a class 4 felony (§18-4-401(2)(c)). The sentence is a minimum of two years with a maximum of six years imprisonment, with parole for a minimum of three years, plus a fine of \$2,000 to \$500,000 (§18-1.3-401(V)(A)). **Class 3 Felony Theft.** If the value of the property stolen is \$20,000 or more, it is a class 3 felony (§18-4-401(2)(d). A class 3 felony carries a sentence of a minimum of four years and a maximum of 12 years imprisonment, with at least five years of parole, plus a fine of \$3,000 to \$750,000. (§18-1.3-401(V)(A)).
