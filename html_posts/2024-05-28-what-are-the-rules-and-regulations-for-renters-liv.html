--- title: "What are the rules and regulations for renters living in homes governed by homeowner\" date: "2024-05-28" tags: ["homeowners-assn"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The specific rules for renters will vary by HOA, especially when it comes to things like usage of amenities. But in most cases, renters have the same rights as homeowners except that they can't vote in HOA votes. They must follow all the same rules as homeowners do. Check the CC&R for specific rules for renters. The landlord should have provided you with a copy of the rules.
