**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If I witness a murder am I obligated to call 911 to report it?** In the vast majority of states in the United States, no, failure to report is not a crime. A small group of states have written some failure to report laws for some crimes. For example, it is a Class A misdemeanor in Texas to fail to report any crime which results in bodily injury or death. Ohio has made it illegal to fail to report a felony. Mandatory reporting is usually only applicable for crimes against children, and usually only for people in professional fields related to or who work with children, such as police officers, doctors, nurses, school administrators, social workers, etc.
