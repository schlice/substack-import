--- title: "Is there a way to hide apps from the Apps list on a Samsung Galaxy Grand Prime?" date: "2016-02-04" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You can delete apps from the home screen, but they will always show up in the \"Apps Drawer\" or the \"Apps List,\" depending on what launcher you use. Additionally, they will always be visible in your \"Installed Applications\" list in your Settings. If you have a ***rooted*** phone, you can use an app called **AppHider** which is available in the Google Play store. Again, this app only works when you have a rooted phone. You dial a sequence into the Phone dialer and your apps become unhidden. AppHider can even be set to notify you to re-hide your apps once you\'re done using them. They will be gone from both the apps list and the \"Installed Applications\" list in your Settings.
