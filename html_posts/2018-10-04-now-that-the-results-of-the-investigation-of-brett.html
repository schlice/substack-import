**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Now that the results of the investigation of Brett Kavanaugh have shown his innocence, is the FBI or any other officer going to arrest Ford for falsely accusing someone for a crime and Diane Feinstein for withholding evidence of an alleged crime?** 1. Christine Blasey Ford may have made a reckless accusation without corroboration, *but she truly believes what she remembers*. **This does not qualify as a false accusation**. A false accusation is where you know the truth, but deliberately speak a falsehood in order to deceive. This is not what Dr. Ford did. 2. The Senate Judiciary Committee is absolutely going to investigate where the "leak" of Ford's letter came from. Whether or not they refer Sen. Feinstein to the Ethics Committee for failing to report information for investigation until the last minute remains to be seen. But there is no crime here, it's just an allegation of past bad behavior. There's no crime, so she didn't withhold evidence of a crime.
