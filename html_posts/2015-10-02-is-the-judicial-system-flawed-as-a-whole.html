**Content:** [A2A\ \ As with most systems created by people, the actual judicial system itself has few flaws in its construction. \"On paper,\" therefore, the system should work nearly flawlessly.\ \ The *execution* of the system is always flawed, because the system is made up of more than just laws that dictate its construction. Because there are *people* as part of the system, the system is, inherently, flawed.]{.rendered_qtext}
