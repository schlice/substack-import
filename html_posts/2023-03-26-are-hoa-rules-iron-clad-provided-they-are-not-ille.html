**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} If your HOA has rules which govern late assessments and other violations and your Board failed to follow those procedures, I strongly suggest you retain the services of a real estate attorney in your local area who specializes in HOA law and file a countersuit against the Board for failing to abide by the Governing Documents. The Board is required by law to follow the Governing Documents. Period.
