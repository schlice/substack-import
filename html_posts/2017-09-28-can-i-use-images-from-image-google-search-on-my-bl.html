--- title: "Can I use images from Image Google Search on my blog or website?" date: "2017-09-28" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The answer is a definite *maybe.* First, you have to determine what your blog post is about. Are you reporting on the picture? Are you providing facts about the picture? This may be considered *fair use* if you need the picture in order to comment on the picture. Is it just supposed to be a "stock photo" in your blog post? The picture may be protected by copyright, and using it without permission may get you a DMCA Takedown notice. Be sure you use Google's filter to show you only "Free to Use" photos when you search. In Google Images, click on Settings \> Advanced Search. Scroll down to the section titled, "Then narrow your search by..." and look at the "Usage Rights" section. Be sure to select a choice that includes "free to use or share."
