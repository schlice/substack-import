**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Is it legal to carry a sword or spear openly in Kentucky?** [[Kentucky Knife Laws](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=204877&url=https://knifeup.com/kentucky-knife-laws/){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="knifeup.com"}]{.qlink_container} According to this, it is legal to open carry any knife. It is legal to conceal a pocket knife or a hunting knife. Anything else is considered a "deadly weapon" and cannot be concealed. Whether or not you're going to be hassled about carrying a sword or a spear is up for debate, as neither are specifically listed in Kentucky law, as far as I can tell. You'll probably attract some unwanted attention carrying a spear or a sword.
