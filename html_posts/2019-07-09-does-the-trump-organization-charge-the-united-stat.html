**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Does the Trump Organization charge the Secret Service employees of the United States for rooms and service while they are protecting Donald Trump when he golfs at his properties?** No, Secret Service details are provided quarters at taxpayer expense when they are on duty. The same was true for any president regardless of where they were or what they were doing, including when they went on vacation with their families.
