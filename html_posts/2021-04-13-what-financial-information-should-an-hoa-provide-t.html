**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **What financial information should an HOA provide to owners?** An HOA must maintain and provide financial information exactly as it is stated in the laws of that state, or in the HOA's Governing Documents if state or local law does not specify. For example, here in Florida, state law lists the documents which are considered to be "official records" of the HOA. These records must be maintained by the HOA and must be produced to an owner for review upon demand. They can either be maintained in paper form within the community where an owner may view and photocopy or photograph them, or they may be supplied to parcel owners on a website where they can be viewed and downloaded.
