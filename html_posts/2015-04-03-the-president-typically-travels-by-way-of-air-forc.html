**Content:** [The Presidential Advance Team goes in and prepares a location for the President\'s arrival. The team includes Secret Service, White House staff, and military personnel (and volunteers!), and they pre-arrange and coordinate everything the President will be doing, as early as two weeks before the Presidnt is to arrive at a particular location. The vehicles get delivered by military transport aircraft, most notably the President\'s armored limo, affectionately referred to as \"the Beast,\" a day or two before the President arrives.\ \ [[A Look Behind the Scenes at Presidential Advance](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=4fbc7a&url=https://www.whitehouse.gov/blog/2010/01/21/a-look-behind-scenes-presidential-advance){.external_link rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank" onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);" qt-tooltip="whitehouse.gov"}]{.qlink_container}]{.rendered_qtext}
