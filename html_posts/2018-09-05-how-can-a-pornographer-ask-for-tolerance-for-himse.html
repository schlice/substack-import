**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **How can a pornographer ask for tolerance for himself when rape victims are not allowed to vote for a policy of gassing pornographers? Isn't it the same form of hatred, that leads to death?** Your question is flawed from the outset, because you are comparing two things which cannot be compared. In the United States, pornography (and production of pornography) is not a crime. Rape is a crime. Rape causes demonstrably more harm than pornography. A pornographer (someone who produces pornography) has no need to ask for tolerance because what he does violates no laws. Are you trying to say that *consumption of pornography leads to rape?* If that's what you're trying to say, then you are making an argument with no proof. Your justification is probably littered with logical fallacies and unproven accusations as well.
