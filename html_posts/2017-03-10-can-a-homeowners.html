**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The dock is part of the land, not part of the lake. If the HOA controls the land, it can regulate the docks. Once you are out on the water, you are no longer under the purview of the HOA. But assuming the HOA controls the land up to the water, they can control the dock attached to the land.
