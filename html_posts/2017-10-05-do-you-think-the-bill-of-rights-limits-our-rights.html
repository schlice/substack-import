--- title: '"Do you think the Bill of Rights limits our rights?"' date: '2017-10-05' tags: - uncategorized layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The Bill of Rights only limits the government. It does not limit the people. The Bill of Rights tells government what actions it is not able to take. It doesn't affect people's rights. It also doesn't grant (or limit) rights. It only indicates rights people already had which government cannot limit.
