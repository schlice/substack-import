--- title: "Can you be liable if someone does something illegal in your name or in the name of a group you\" date: "2017-06-25" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You are only liable if an *agent* of your organization does something in your name. So if the counter clerk at McDonald\'s orders 200,000 buns instead of 2,000, you are liable for that order, as the clerk is an agent of the company. If a *non-agent* does something on your behalf, you are not liable. So if a hacker hacks your site and pretends to be you, you are not responsible for what that hacker does.
