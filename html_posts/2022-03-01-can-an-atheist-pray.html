Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Sure, but it doesn't make sense. It makes as much sense for an atheist to pray as it does for me to go to the church of the Flying Spaghetti Monster. I can physically be in the room and do all the things they do, but if I don't believe in it, it doesn't make me a Pastafarian. Same holds true for the atheist. Atheists can pray, but if there is no belief behind what they say, they're just speaking words. It is the *faith* that gives the prayer its power, not the *words*. \ Original question: Can an atheist pray?
