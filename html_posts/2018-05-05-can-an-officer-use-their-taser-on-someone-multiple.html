Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can an officer use their taser on someone multiple times if they were not charged with a crime?** Yes. There is no requirement that a person must be charged with a crime before a Taser can be deployed. The Taser is a "pain compliance" device, so the only requirement is that there is a directive given by police with which the suspect is failing to comply. The Taser is used to "encourage" compliance with that directive.
