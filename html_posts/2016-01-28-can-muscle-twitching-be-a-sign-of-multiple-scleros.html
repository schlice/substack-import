**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} ***Can*** it be sign? Yes, it can be a sign ***Does it mean you have MS?*** By itself, it does not mean you have MS. There are hundreds of different explanations for muscle twitching. Some are common, some are not so common. Your doctor can help you sort through those. A diagnosis of MS is a diagnosis of *exclusion*, which means you have to exclude all other possibilities for the symptoms you see before you can diagnose someone with MS. Therefore, you need to start with your exclusions. Make an appointment with your doctor and talk to him/her about all of your symptoms. Go from there. Once they get to the point where they say, \"we have no other explanations,\" then you should see a neurologist to dig further.
