--- title: "What protection can a person with a pacemaker and/or ICD wear to protect against the malicious use of a stun gun?" date: "2021-08-19" tags: ["uncategorized"] layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Hello, thank you for the A2A. First of all, people with pacemakers are not at increased risk of their pacemaker malfunctioning after being hit with a Taser. Studies have been completed which proved that the Taser does not adversely affect pacemakers or ICDs.[\[1\]](#hWUTP){#cite-hWUTP .citation_link}[\[2\]](#PesrW){#cite-PesrW .citation_link} However, they probably have some underlying problem with their heart (which is why they have a pacemaker in the first place). And Tasers **can** affect your heart if you have an underlying heart condition. There really isn't any practical clothing which can stop the barbs from penetrating. They are very good at penetrating even thick layers of clothing. Unless it's the middle of winter and you're in heavy coats and many layers, the dart will probably penetrate. Your best possible defense could be chain mail, but it's really heavy and impractical to wear on a regular basis.
