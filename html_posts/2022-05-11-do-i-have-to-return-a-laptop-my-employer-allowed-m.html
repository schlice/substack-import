--- title: "Do I have to return a laptop? My employer allowed me to buy my work computer after I left my job. Later, they asked for it back saying they never had the right to sell it (they will refund me). Do I have to return it?" date: "2022-05-11" tags: ["law"] layout: post ---

**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I really like [[David Schwartz](https://www.quora.com/profile/David-Schwartz-9)]{.qlink_container}'s answer, billing them for your time in moving your data off of the machine and then returning it for refund plus expenses. The problem I have with this is that you now have to haggle with them over your pay rate, and if they reject it, you're stuck, and they still want your laptop back. I have a different opinion. **I think you have just as much right to keep the laptop.** You have a purchase receipt, and you purchased it from the entity which had "ownership" of the laptop at the time. Whether or not they had the right to sell it is not your problem, but they are trying to make it your problem. The "owner" made a sale decision, and as the "owner of record" at the time, they legally can bind their company to contracts, whether or not they had the authority to do so. Now, the leasing company is likely coming back to them and telling them that they actually want the hardware back, and they're trying to minimize what they have to pay out, along with not having to go back to the leasing company and admit that they actually sold the laptop when they were not authorized to do so. My answer: **tell your former employer to go kick rocks.** They legally sold it, I have a receipt, I paid fairly for it, it's mine. If they found out they didn't have permission to sell it, that's their problem, not mine. Looks like they need to pay the leasing company for their mistake. Don't put me in the middle of your contract issue with your leasing company. If they complain about this at all, you may need to retain the services of an attorney in your area to help you politely tell them to go kick rocks. \ Original question: Do I have to return a laptop?
