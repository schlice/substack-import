Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} I would suggest you first talk to eBay Customer Service. Purchases of business equipment like industrial printers are covered under a separate eBay insurance group (actually a third party). You should explain the situation to them; they may be able to mediate the dispute. If they cannot help you, you should consult with a property loss attorney in your local area.
