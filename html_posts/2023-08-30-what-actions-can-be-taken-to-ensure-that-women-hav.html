--- title: '"What actions can be taken to ensure that women have access to safe and legal abortions, regardless of their circumstances?"' date: '2023-08-30' tags: - policy-analysis - law layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} The *Dobbs* decision validated that abortion is not a Federal issue and it is not a Constitutional right. Therefore, rightly, abortion is a **state's rights** issue. You simply cannot **ensure** that all women have access to safe and legal abortions. Full stop. You can ensure that you live in a state which has added a right to abortion into its state constitution. According to a July 2022 report, 10 states have state constitutions that protect abortion rights and access. These states\' high courts have recognized that their state constitutions protect abortion rights. Beyond that, you can champion abortion rights in the other forty states which do not have abortion rights enshrined in their state constitutions. There are many groups which are actively working to modify laws in those remaining states. You can look to contribute to their efforts in some meaningful way. I would suggest you look at such groups as Planned Parenthood and NARAL.
