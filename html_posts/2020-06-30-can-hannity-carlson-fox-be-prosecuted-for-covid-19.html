**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **Can Hannity, Carlson, Fox be prosecuted for Covid-19 lies? If Someone can be prosecuted for yelling fire in a crowded theater, can Fox and Company be prosecuted for minimizing, trivializing, calling the coronavirus a hoax?** Shouting fire in a crowded theater actually creates a danger where no danger previously existed. This is illegal. Minimizing coronavirus is an opinion. So is trivializing and calling the virus a hoax (to the extent that this was actually done). These guys are opinion hosts. Stating your opinion is not illegal. There may be consequences, but none of them are legal consequences.
