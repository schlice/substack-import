Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} We\'ll call this a hypothetical question since you assume many things which are incorrect. - Dogs do not have rank. They are "assets," not \"personnel." Assets are unranked. They may receive honorary rank, but nothing official. - Officers are not dog handlers (sadly, because I would have loved to be a dog handler). - But even if they were, generals would not be in a job which required handling a dog. General officer level leadership positions drive the ship, they don\'t operate the throttle.
