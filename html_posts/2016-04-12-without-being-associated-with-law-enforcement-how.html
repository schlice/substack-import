**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} There are a few organizations that the manufacturer will sell to, but they want to make sure that the substances are actually used for acceptable purposes. You will have to be ready to present an official letter on company letterhead before they will sell to you. Typically, outside of law enforcement, orders for scent training kits are accepted from accredited dog trainers, training centers, and academic or research institutions.
