**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **The question is about the time between pulling my car over and before the police officer approaches me. What would be the appropriate/safest way to retrieve my registration and insurance info from the glove box while the officer approaches my car?** If you can get the paperwork before the officer approaches your car, get it and have it ready. If you can't get the paperwork before the officer approaches your car, *don't*. When the officer asks for it, tell him it's in the glove box and ask if you can get it. Then do what he tells you to do. Easy peesey.
