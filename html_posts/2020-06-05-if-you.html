**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **If you're going to defund law enforcement, who's going to confiscate all the guns when you vote to ban them?** It doesn't make logical sense to both defund the police and disarm the population. You have to pick one. You can't say, "You don't need guns, the police will protect you" and then say "The police are the most corrupt, institutionally racist organization in government and must be disbanded" and expect to be taken seriously. Either the police can protect me, or they can't. And if they can't, then it's up to me to protect myself. The truth is, it's *always* been up to me to protect myself. The fact that my taxpayer dollars fund a government organization which can take the first crack at defending me doesn't alleviate me of my responsibility to protect myself. You can't subrogate my self-protection responsibility to the police and then defund the police. Again, pick one.
