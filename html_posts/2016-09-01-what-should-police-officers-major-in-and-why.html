Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Since you're talking about college, I would look into a Bachelor's degree in Criminal Justice or something similar. Barring that (if your college doesn't offer), a Legal Studies or another Pre-law related Bachelor's degree would be useful. While a specific degree isn't required to become a police officer in any jurisdiction that I'm aware of, if you're in college getting a degree, focusing on understanding the foundation of the legal system in our country is a great way to focus your efforts towards any law-related field. If you have the ability to take a Criminal Justice and a Constitutional Law class, do it.
