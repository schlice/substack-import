**Content:** [The founding fathers saw much technological advance throughout their lifetimes. They watched warfare progress from the rock to the stick to the sharpened stick, the spear, the bow and arrow, then on to the sword and knife, and finally to a musket and rifle.\ \ The first \"Gatling\" gun was even being invented around this time.\ \ So to say that it is inconceivable that the founding fathers could not have seen technological progress occurring is far from correct.\ \ What they **did** do to overcome that issue was to say that the people have the right to keep and bear the same types of weapons that the military uses.\ \ Today, the military uses far more advanced weapons than civilians can use for legitimate purposes, because smart people saw that coming and addressed it.]{.rendered_qtext}
