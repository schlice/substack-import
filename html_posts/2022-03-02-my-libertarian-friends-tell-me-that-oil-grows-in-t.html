**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} No offense, but you need smarter friends. Oil doesn't grow in the ground. While oil is formed in the ground, it doesn't "grow" like a plant. And God may have created the world and set the conditions under which oil can develop, but he doesn't make the oil appear in the ground any more than he actually moves my muscles to make me walk. He built the world and created the natural rules under which the world operates, but he doesn't directly "cause the oil to appear with magic." > Petroleum, also called crude oil, is a fossil fuel.
