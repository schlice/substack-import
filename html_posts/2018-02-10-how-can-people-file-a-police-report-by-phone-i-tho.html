--- title: '"How can people file a police report by phone? I thought you had to sign and swear to it?"' date: '2018-02-10' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post ---

Answer

{.rendered_qtext}
**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} You can file a *report* by phone in most cases. Many departments these days even let you file non-emergency reports online. You have to give a **statement** or make a **complaint** in person and swear to it.
**Content language:** English
