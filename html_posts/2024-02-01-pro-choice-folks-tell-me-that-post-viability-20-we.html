First, less than 30% of babies born earlier than 22 weeks survive. So viability means "a minimum starting point before which survival outside of the mother's womb is, using current technology, virtually impossible." Second, as technology improves, so too will the date of viability. Abortion will become outdated and unnecessary, as pregnant women who no longer want to be pregnant will simply have their baby transferred to an artificial womb.
