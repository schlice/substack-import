**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} Sperm is a component of life, not a life in and of itself. Sperm cells are not, definitionally, "alive." While I shouldn't have to give you a biology lesson, sperm combines with egg to produce a zygote, the earliest form of human life. A unique life form is only formed at the time that a sperm fertilizes an egg and forms a zygote. This is a time period we call *conception*. It is at this time that a fully unique set of DNA is created, and thus, a wholly distinct human life. \ Original question: Is masturbation a form of abortion?
