**Content:** []{.rendered_qtext} **As an American man , how would you feel if you saw your wife having sex with any other man?** I also don't understand what the nationality has to do with the question. While the percentage is smaller, there are married men in America who participate in non-traditional marital arrangements. Some married couples practice what they call "open relationships" in which sex with multiple partners is permitted, sometimes even encouraged. Some married couples engage in group sex with multiple partners. In that case, those particular married men may be *excited* or *aroused* watching their spouse have sex with other men. But that's outside of my area of expertise. Perhaps [[Franklin Veaux](https://www.quora.com/profile/Franklin-Veaux)]{.qlink_container} can add some color there. For those who are in a monogamous marriage (one in which sex outside of the marriage is not permitted), there would be some very negative emotions which would follow seeing the events you described. Some of those emotions would be *rage, betrayal,* and *sadness*, and may even be described as a *broken heart.* For people who have monogamous relationships, one of the partners violating that trust leads to some very hurt feelings, and a difficult road to repair that relationship.
