--- title: '"Why do Americans make such a big fuss over free speech and the right to bear arms?"' date: '2014-12-17' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [Ultimately, it\'s not important what one person or another
believes is a really important freedom. What\'s most important is what
the founders thought were really important freedoms.\
\
Human dignity was something that the founders believed was so immutable,
so ingrained in the consciences of all men, that there was never any
thought that it would be violated. Even the King gave some modicum of
human dignity.\
\
The rights enshrined in the Bill of Rights are ones that the founders
thought needed to be protected in writing. Ones that the King routinely
violated. Ones that they believed would become the cornerstone for a new
type of free society.\
\
The longest-running democracy in the world can\'t be
wrong.]
