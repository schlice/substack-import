--- title: '"How do I legally start a business being disabled with Multiple sclerosis and about to be on disability?"' date: '2015-10-17' tags: - veteran-benefits - health-autoimmune layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [Whether or not you have MS doesn\'t affect whether or not
you can open a business.\
\
Whether or not you are on disability doesn\'t affect whether or not you
can open a business.\
\
Starting and maintaining your own business, however, is *stressful*. You
will be inducing a lot of stress, and stress, as you know, can make your
MS symptoms worse.\
\
Be cautious when starting your business. Have good understanding
business partners who can run the business in your absence. Watch how
hard you push yourself, as it can have an adverse impact on your
health.]
