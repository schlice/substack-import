--- title: '"How does gyroscopic stabilization work for tank guns?"' date: '2016-07-28' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

In a tank, the gyro feeds information to a computer which actively moves
the turret to keep it stable.

In smaller items like Steady-Cams, the gyroscope is large enough that
the inertia created by the gyro itself is enough to prevent the rig from
moving when the operator does.
