--- title: '"Can a plane fly with only one wing?"' date: '2015-07-08' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [An aircraft that is bilateral, with wings on both sides,
cannot fly with only one wing.\
\
Some aircraft are essentially \"flying wings\" and are, at their core,
one giant wing. Northrop\'s YB-45 and XB-39 are examples of flying
wings. The B-2 is just one wing.]
