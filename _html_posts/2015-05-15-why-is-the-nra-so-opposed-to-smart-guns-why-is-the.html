--- title: '"Why is the NRA so opposed to smart guns? Why is the NRA opposed to features that ensure that only the legitimate gun owner can use his or her gun?"' date: '2015-05-15' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [The gun is a very simple mechanical tool. It works in the
harshest of conditions. It works when it is needed. So far, the \"smart
guns\" out there are fragile, complex, can be bypassed, and don\'t
always work (i.e. sometimes they let other-than-registered shooters fire
the weapon, and sometimes they prevent the registered shooter from
firing the weapon).\
\
If a gun is a tool to be used in a life-and-death situation, mistakes
like these can cost you your life. Until the technology becomes as
reliable as the underlying gun itself, it\'s not a viable
technology.]
