If someone points a gun at me, I would be in fear for my life. In order
to use deadly force, someone must be engaging in a felony which places
me in imminent danger of death or great bodily harm.

I believe, therefore, that if someone is pointing a gun at me, I am
within my rights to use whatever force I can to stop that felony, up to
and including deadly force.
