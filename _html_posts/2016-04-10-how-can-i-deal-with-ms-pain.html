--- title: '"How can I deal with MS pain?"' date: '2016-04-10' tags: - health-autoimmune layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

[[Michael
Soso](https://www.quora.com/profile/Michael-Soso)]{.qlink_container} is
right on the money. Depending on the type of pain you are experiencing,
there are a variety of medications that are viable to manage
neurological pain.

[[This
page](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=122213&url=http://www.nationalmssociety.org/Symptoms-Diagnosis/MS-Symptoms/Pain){.external_link
rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank"
onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);"
qt-tooltip="nationalmssociety.org"}]{.qlink_container} over at the
National MS Society discusses the various types of pain that people with
MS may experience and the types of medications that have been found to
be useful treating that pain.

Ultimately, talking to your neurologist about the pain you are
experiencing will be the best way for you to find a good treatment that
works for you.
