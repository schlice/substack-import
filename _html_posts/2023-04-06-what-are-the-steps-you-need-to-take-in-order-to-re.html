--- title: '"What are the steps you need to take in order to rent out a condo in an HOA that does not allow it?"' date: '2023-04-06' tags: - homeowners-assn layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

You're going to have to try to get an exemption from the Condo Board.
You're going to have to make a good case that you should be the
exception to the rule (which was likely in place when you bought your
condo, so you should have known that you wouldn't be able to rent it
out).
