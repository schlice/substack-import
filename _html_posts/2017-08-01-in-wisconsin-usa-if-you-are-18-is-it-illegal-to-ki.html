--- title: '"In Wisconsin, USA, if you are 18, is it illegal to kiss someone who is 16?"' date: '2017-08-01' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Dating is not illegal.

Holding hands is not illegal.

Kissing is not illegal.

Sexual intercourse with someone under the age of 18 is
illegal in Wisconsin.

Also, Wisconsin does not have a "close in age" law, so if an 18 year old
has sex with a 16 year old, the 18 year old can be charged with
statutory rape.

Also in Wisconsin, if both participants are underage, they can both
be charged with statutory rape, although this would be the exception
rather than the rule.
