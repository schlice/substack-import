--- title: '"If a celebrity shines a laser into the lense of a pepperazzi and damages the camera, how much trouble will the offending celebrity be in?"' date: '2018-03-25' tags: - policy-analysis layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

The celebrity will be in exactly the same trouble as anyone else who
destroys the property of others. The celebrity will be sued for
destruction of private property, and will settle out of court by
replacing the device, or more specifically, paying to replace the
device.
