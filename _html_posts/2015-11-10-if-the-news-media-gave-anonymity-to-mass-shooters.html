--- title: '"If the news media gave anonymity to mass shooters, would that alone eradicate mass shootings (given how gun control is impossible at this point, and will maybe always be impossible)?"' date: '2015-11-10' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [As everyone else said, it won\'t completely eradicate mass
shootings.\
\
There is no silver bullet (no pun intended) that will eradicate mass
shootings.\
\
But it\'s a start.\
\
Many mass shooters have, in their manifestos or interrogations, praised
and idolized the mass shooters who went before them: Klebold and Harris
from Columbine, the Sandy Hook shooter, the numerous college campus
shooters. We do know that the publicity these shooters have garnered had
been a source of admiration (and sadly, emulation) for their progeny.\
\
If we send the message that you will get no fame or publicity, no one
will know you were the shooter, you will never be publicly vindicated,
and that \"anonymous shooter\" at X college campus is universally
vilified, this may remove some of the incentive some of these shooters
feel when performing their own copycat shooting
sprees.]
