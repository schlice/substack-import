Answer









Content: [You could, but it\'s unlikely. The problem here is that
you knew it was there, and it\'s your car. So you can face charges.
Depending on how much is there, you could face trafficking charges even
if it\'s not yours.\
\
However, if the passenger owns up to it and admits it\'s theirs, they
will be arrested and you will probably be sent on your
way.]
