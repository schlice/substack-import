--- title: '"How do I stop a Voodoo priest from harassing me and family? He has people networking and keeps us in a chaotic situation. What can I do to stop him?"' date: '2017-06-27' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

The same way you stop any other person from harassing you.

1.  Tell them to stop.
2.  If they don't stop, file for a restraining order, detailing the
    harassment.
3.  If they still don't stop, call the police and report his violation
    of the restraining order.

At some point, the harassment will stop.
