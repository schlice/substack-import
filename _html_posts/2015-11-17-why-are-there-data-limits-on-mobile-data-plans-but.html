--- title: '"Why are there data limits on mobile data plans but no data limits on at home internet services?"' date: '2015-11-17' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [As with anything, it\'s all about money.\
\
It\'s more expensive to transmit data to and from your handset than it
is to transmit data to and from your home internet gateway. Therefore,
in order for your handset data plan to be cost-effective, the carrier
must limit the amount of data you transmit to and from your handset.\
\
If not, people could effectively use their cellular handsets to replace
their home internet service. In a LTE (4G) area, service speeds are good
enough to support streaming services, so without limits on those plans,
the usage would skyrocket and overwhelm OTA network
providers.]
