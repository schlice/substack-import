--- title: '"Can home owner associations restrict their members from displaying Trump/Pence lawn signs on election day, even if other rules are suspended for that day?"' date: '2024-11-16' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

In general, no they cannot. They can restrict the display of *ALL*
signs, but they cannot restrict the display of specific signs which are
not generally considered to be offensive.

While some people take personal offense at a "Trump/Pence" sign, it is
not generally considered to be offensive.
