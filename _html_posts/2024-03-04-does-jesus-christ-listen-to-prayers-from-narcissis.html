Answer









Content: 

A doctor heals the *sick*, not the *well*.

> When the scribes of the Pharisees saw that He was eating with the
> sinners and tax collectors, they said to His disciples, "Why is He
> eating and drinking with tax collectors and sinners?" And hearing
> this*,* Jesus said to them, "It is not those who are healthy who need
> a physician, but those who are sick; I did not come to call the
> righteous, but sinners." --- Mark 2:16--17 ESV

That said, Jesus hears prayers from everyone. He seeks to help those who
pray for the wrong reasons.
