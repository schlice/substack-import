**Is arresting someone for not being vaccinated or having their kids
vaccinated, a violation of their constitutional right?**

No, but I'm pretty sure that there is no criminal intent in not
vaccinating a child, so I think it's unlikely that someone would be
arrested for not vaccinating a child.
