--- title: '"What is Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis?"' date: '2015-10-13' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [This is the scientific name for the disease otherwise
known as \"Lou Gehrig\'s Disease\" after the famous baseball player.
This is a degenerative brain disease that destroys neurons. ALS destroys
\"motor neurons\" which are the ones that control voluntary movement
within the body, eventually causing patients to lose the ability to
walk, eat, move, and breathe.\
