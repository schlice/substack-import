Answer









Content: [I think the implication of the question is that civilians
\"who live peacefully\" have no need for guns.\
\
Unfortunately in today\'s world, there are other people who are not at
all interested in living peacefully.\
\
These people are willing to use whatever force they choose to get what
they want.\
\
I don\'t need a semi-automatic gun because I live peacefully. I need a
semi-automatic gun because *others don\'t.*]
