--- title: '"Are HOA rules iron clad provided they are not illegal or are they just suggestions? I ask because my HOA has very specific language on enforcing late assessments and other violations. But the board skipped those procedures and served me with suit?"' date: '2023-03-26' tags: - law - homeowners-assn layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

If your HOA has rules which govern late assessments and other violations
and your Board failed to follow those procedures, I strongly suggest you
retain the services of a real estate attorney in your local area who
specializes in HOA law and file a countersuit against the Board for
failing to abide by the Governing Documents.

The Board is required by law to follow the Governing Documents. Period.
Any deviation from that must be for extraordinary extenuating
circumstances, and always in favor of the homeowner.
