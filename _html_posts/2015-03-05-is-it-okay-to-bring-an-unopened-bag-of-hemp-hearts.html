--- title: '"Is it okay to bring an unopened bag of hemp hearts (shelled hemp seeds) through customs from Canada to the U.S., by either air or car?"' date: '2015-03-05' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [No, unless the seeds have been sterilized. Unsterilized
hemp seeds are a Schedule 1 controlled substance and cannot enter the
U.S. without the appropriate DEA paperwork.\
\
For any other hemp products, if the hemp still contains THC and can
cause THC to enter the body, it cannot enter the U.S.]
