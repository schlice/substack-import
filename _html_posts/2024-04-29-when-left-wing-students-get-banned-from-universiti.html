--- title: '"When left-wing students get banned from universities for death threats to Jews, are their constitutional rights of free speech being violated?"' date: '2024-04-29' tags: - policy-analysis layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

If you believe that the right to free speech includes making threats to
people, you have much to learn about what activities are protected as
rights and where those limits are.

Verbal assaults are indeed illegal, and making death threats is indeed a
verbal assault. States have different names for these crimes, but they
all amount to roughly the same thing.
