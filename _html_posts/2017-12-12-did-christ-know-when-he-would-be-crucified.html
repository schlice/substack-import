--- title: '"Did Christ know when he would be crucified?"' date: '2017-12-12' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Not only did He know when He was going to be crucified, He knew exactly
how it was going to happen, and He knew that it *needed to* happen
in order to fulfill God's plan.

For you can't be resurrected until you die.
