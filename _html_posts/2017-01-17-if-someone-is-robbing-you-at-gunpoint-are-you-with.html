Content: 

In order to use lethal force against a person, you must be in fear for
your life.

I would speculate that someone who has a gun pointed at them probably
fears for his life at that very moment. This means that the use of
deadly force would be justified.

This is a Ruger LCP in a gun wallet. Inside your pocket, it "prints"
