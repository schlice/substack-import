Your logic reads:\
\

Guns require a person to pull the trigger.\
\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\
Therefore, Abortion is equal to guns.\

This is an example of fallacious logic.\
\
Additionally, the court has ruled that a fetus is not a person. Until

abortion. They\'re killing a collection of cells that vaguely looks like
a very tiny person, but really isn\'t.]
