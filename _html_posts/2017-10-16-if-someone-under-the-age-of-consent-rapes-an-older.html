--- title: '"If someone under the age of consent rapes an older person, could the older person be charged for statutory rape?"' date: '2017-10-16' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

It's unlikely, but it is possible. Statutory rape charges do not take
into account who was a "victim" and who was an "aggressor." They only
require sex with someone who cannot legally consent.

It's unlikely, however, that the state would bring charges against the
plaintiff, who is their client.
