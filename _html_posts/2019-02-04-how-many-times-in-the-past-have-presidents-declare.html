**How many times in the past have Presidents declared a national
emergency?**

More times than you think.

Presidents declaring national emergencies is not new, but you haven't
heard about it because the media hasn't told you. In case you were
unaware:

- Jimmy Carter declared two national emergencies, one of which is still
  active.
- Ronald Reagan declared six national emergencies, and none are still
  active.
- George H.W. Bush declared four national emergencies, and none are
  still active.
- Bill Clinton declared seventeen national emergencies, six of which are
  still active.
- George W. Bush declared twelve national emergencies, ten of which are
  still active.
- Barack Obama declared thirteen national emergencies, eleven of which
  are still active.
