**Can I sue someone who gave me a flu shot without me signing the
consent form? If so, what type of lawyer should I contact on this
matter?**

You can sue anyone for anything in the United States.

The question remains: will you prevail?

Historical precedent says you will not prevail.

> An adult\'s consent may be expressed in writing or declared verbally.
> Consent may also be implied by the actions of the adult in response to
> a request from the health professional. In *O\'Brien v Cunard SS Co*
> (1891) a woman failed in her claim for trespass against a doctor who
> injected her with smallpox vaccine on a ship bound for Boston. The
> court held that the woman had stood in line for the vaccination,
> offered her arm to the doctor and made no complaint when he injected
> her. Her behaviour indicated consent and the doctor was right to
> proceed.\[1\](#macSt){#cite-macSt .citation_link}

So, to that end:

- Did you present yourself to the doctor who gave you the shot?
- Did you know what you were being injected with? If not, what did you
  think it was?
- Did you complain at the time of injection?

Your behavior indicated consent, and the doctor was right to proceed.
