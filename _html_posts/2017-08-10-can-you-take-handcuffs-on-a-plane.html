--- title: '"Can you take handcuffs on a plane?"' date: '2017-08-10' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

According to the TSA website, yes. Handcuffs are allowed in both
checked and carry-on bags. But as with all things (according to the
website), "the final decision rests with the TSA officer on whether an
item is allowed through the checkpoint."
