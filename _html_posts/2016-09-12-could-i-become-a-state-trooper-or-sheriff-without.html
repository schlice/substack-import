--- title: '"Could I become a state trooper or sheriff without previous law enforcement experience?"' date: '2016-09-12' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

You can indeed! Generally, all you need is a high school diploma and a
clean driving record. Feel free to call or check out the website of the
agency you're interested in applying for. They usually have continuous
openings for troopers or deputies. Download and fill out an application.
