Content: 

Can God forgive Trump\'s adulteries even if Trump does not repent?

[[Toby
Dillon](https://www.quora.com/profile/Toby-Dillon)]{.qlink_container}
has the right idea; that's not the way forgiveness works.

> Ask and it will be given to you; seek and you will find; knock and the
> door will be opened to you. For everyone who asks receives; the one
> who seeks finds; and to the one who knocks, the door will be opened.
> --- Matthew 7:7--8

However, for any question ever which begins with the question "Can God,"
the answer is yes, God can.

I think the question you wanted to ask probably should have started with
Will God, and in this case, there is no Biblical evidence that God
forgives without some participation by the person.

In other words, if you're not actually seeking forgiveness, there's not
much chance that you'll find it.
