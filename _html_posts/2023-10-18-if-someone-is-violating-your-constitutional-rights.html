--- title: '"If someone is violating your constitutional rights, can you call the police?"' date: '2023-10-18' tags: - policy-analysis - law-enforcement - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

No. The police do not enforce the Constitution. The police enforce state
law.

If someone is violating the law, you can call the police. If someone is
violating your Constitutional rights, you call a lawyer.
