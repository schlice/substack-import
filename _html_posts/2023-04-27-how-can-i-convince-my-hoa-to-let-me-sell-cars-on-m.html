--- title: '"How can I convince my HOA to let me sell cars on my driveway?"' date: '2023-04-27' tags: - law - homeowners-assn layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

- Selling one car is a personal transaction.
- Continuously selling cars is running a business.

You can sell a car from your driveway. That's a private transaction,
and regardless, by the time anyone got wind of it, you'd have the car
sold (nothing to see here, move along.

Selling multiple cars, or receiving and reselling cars on an ongoing
basis, is a business and will rightly be regulated by the HOA and
your local zoning laws. The problems are numerous: increased traffic in
a residential area, danger to property, among others.

So it's not only your HOA you'd have to convince, but your local
government too. I wouldn't expect to get real far with that. They'll
tell you to open a storefront.
