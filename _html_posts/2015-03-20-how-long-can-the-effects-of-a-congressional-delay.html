Content: [The delay doesn\'t constitute a violation of due process

person can have his or her rights removed without due process of the
law. Puerto Rico becoming a state or not does not remove any rights that
citizens of the terrirtory already have.\
\
It seems that the US government has been more than willing to respond to
whatever Puerto Rico has wanted to do with their territory, since the
60s. And finally, in 2012, Puerto Rico has requested to become a state.\
\
The last time we have had a state join the union was in 1959 (Hawaii). I
don\'t suspect it will be a quick process, but I suspect it will
happen.]
