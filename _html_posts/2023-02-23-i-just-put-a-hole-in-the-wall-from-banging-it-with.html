--- title: '"I just put a hole in the wall from banging it with a hammer. Why is there a hole in the wall?"' date: '2023-02-23' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

A hole isn't something you can "make." There is no "substance" to a
hole. A hole is actually an *absence* of something.

For example, you can dig a hole in the ground, and unquestionably a hole
exists, but you didn't *make* the hole. You removed dirt. Therefore,
the hole is what we call an *absence* of something.

Similarly, when you hit the drywall with your hammer, you removed some
drywall. The condition of an absence of something can oftentimes be
referred to as a "hole." You didn't make the hole with ingredients.
You caused the hole to exist by removing something else.
