Answer









Content: 

Have you ever had any first aid training?

Yes, more than I can count. Both in the military and through the Red
Cross.

Do you keep a first aid kit in your home or car?

Yes, I often find myself running out there to get stuff for the house.

Have you used it?

Frequently. I have used it both on the road and at home.

**Would you help a stranger in need if he were injured and you were
there?**

If I was there and I had the capability to help, I absolutely would.
