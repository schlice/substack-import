Anyway, you can sue anyone for anything you choose. If you can find a
lawyer to represent you, I\'d say go for it.

However, in the case of a data breach where a large amount of people are
likely affected, it\'s probable that if there is cause, there is a
class-action lawsuit ongoing. I\'d definitely contact the lead attorneys
in the class-action suit and give them your information. I\'m certain
they\'ll want to talk to you.
