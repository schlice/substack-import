--- title: '"Is it true that pilots sleep during airline flights?"' date: '2016-05-19' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

International flights have two full cockpit crews. One crew will do the
takeoff and landing, the other crew will do the intermediate navigation.
On large aircraft, there is a crew rest room right off the cockpit where
the off-duty crew goes to sleep.

There are no domestic flights where crews will sleep.
