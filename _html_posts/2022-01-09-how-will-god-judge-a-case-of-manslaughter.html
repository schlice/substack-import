The same way he judges everything we do. He examines our heart, where
all sin actually takes place, not necessarily in our hands. Sin is a
condition of the heart, a conscious rejection of God's will in favor of
your own.

He is just and constant. He is unchanging. He knows the condition of
your heart, and that is how you will be judged. We will all be judged in
this way.
