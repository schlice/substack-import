--- title: '"How do cellular and Wi-Fi transmissions affect ambient oxygen?"' date: '2014-09-17' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [The amount of power needed to affect oxygen bonds
(essentially splitting the O2 bond and creating free radicals) is far
greater than the power output of even a picocell. This may be an issue
surrounding an RF microwave tower, but not around a Wi-Fi base station,
even a commercial access point doesn\'t provide the power needed to
split oxygen bonds.]
