Content: [First of all, for a claim of false advertising to have any
legs, you would have to find some advertising that says \"real wasabi\"
and then find out that they are using horseradish instead.\
\
Additionally, you have to show some harm for a false advertising claim.
Hurting your pride isn\'t really harm. Most people don\'t even know the
difference between real wasabi and artificial wasabi.\
\
In every Japanese restaurant in which I have eaten, I have simply asked
the chef for real wasabi and if he has it he will give it to
me.]
