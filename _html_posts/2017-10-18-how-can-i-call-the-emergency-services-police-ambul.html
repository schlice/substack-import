--- title: '"How can I call the emergency services (police, ambulance) of a European country when I am currently in the US? (If I hear my wife screaming at home while we are on the phone, how can I notify the police?)"' date: '2017-10-18' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

If you travel internationally, you should always record in your phone
the direct-dial number to your local emergency services back home. In
America, you actually reach the same 911 operator, but you use a
"non-emergency" line to do it. Being that you are actually out of the
country, I think that the operator would give you a pass for reporting
an emergency on the non-emergency line.
