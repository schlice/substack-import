Why do you go to jail if you help your wife to commit suicide?

Because in the United States, it is illegal to assist someone to commit
suicide.

In the states where physician assisted suicide is legal, there is a
specific process you have to go through to make it legal. It involves
multiple doctors, waiting periods, etc.
