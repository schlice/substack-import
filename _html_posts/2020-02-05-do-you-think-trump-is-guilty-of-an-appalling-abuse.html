Do you think Trump is guilty of an appalling abuse of public trust?

Maybe.

But even if that were true, this literally begs the next question:

*Is "abuse of the public trust" an impeachable offense?*

The answer to that question is definitely not. That's why we
have elections. Period. If you think the President is abusing the public
trust, don't re-elect him. Simple as that.
