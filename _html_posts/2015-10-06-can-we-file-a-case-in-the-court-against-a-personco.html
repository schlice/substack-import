--- title: '"Can we file a case in the court against a person/company who developed a very bad website UI and caused so much pain to the public?"' date: '2015-10-06' tags: - policy-analysis - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [You cannot sue a company because their UI is difficult to
use.\
\
First off, you voluntarily use their software. No one forces you to use
the software.\
\
Second, your discomfort is not an actionable offense, especially when
you affirmatively choose to actually use their software, and thus
consent to inflicting your discomfort upon yourself.\
\
Your legal remedy in such a case is to choose with your wallet. Don\'t
use their service. Use a competitor\'s service with a better UI. And if
a competitor\'s service with a better UI does not exist, I think you
just found yourself a business opportunity.]
