--- title: '"Why do traffic lights, and too many other applications, employ red and green when this is the most common form of colour blindness?"' date: '2017-10-25' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Traffic lights are also arranged in a specific pattern in the US. The
red light is on top, and the green light is on the bottom. This is why
color vision is not required to drive a car.
