Answer









Content: [I am a big fan of reducing the cost to government for any
service. In this case, however, providing a police force is one of the
actual jobs that government (in the US, under the Constitution) is
actually supposed to provide. So based on that alone, I don\'t think
governments ought to outsource police services.]
