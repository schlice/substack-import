the end of the Six Day War: *if you accept Israel\'s right to exist, if

escorted out of the county. Israel can no longer afford to allow them to
remain when all they desire to do is destroy Israel. They have no desire
for peace. They have no desire for compromise.

Just think about the chants that they have been shouting at the pro
Palestinian rallies. "From the river to the sea, Palestine shall be
free.\" Where\'s the river? It\'s the Jordan River, the eastern border
of Israel. Where is the sea? It\'s the Mediterranean Sea, the western
border of Israel. They want nothing more than the complete destruction
of Israel. Israelis can no longer afford such people on their shores.
