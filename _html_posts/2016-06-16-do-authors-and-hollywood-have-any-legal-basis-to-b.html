--- title: '"Do authors and Hollywood have any legal basis to bring legal action for other works using the same name as one of their own characters?"' date: '2016-06-16' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [You\'re talking about making a claim for copyright
infringement.\
\
In order to prevail, the portion filling the lawsuit would have to show
that the characters were similar enough that it could appear to the
average person that they might be the same character. Just having the
same name as another character would probably not meet that
standard.]
