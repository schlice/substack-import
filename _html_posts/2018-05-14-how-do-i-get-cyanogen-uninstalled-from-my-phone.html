How do I get Cyanogen uninstalled from my phone?

The same way you got Cyanogen on to your phone. Pick a new operating
system and install it onto your phone.

I assume that if you're installing Cyanogen in the first place that
you're comfortable with installing custom operating systems on your
phone (which is perfectly fine with me).

May I suggest
[LineageOS(https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=395593&url=https://lineageos.org/){.external_link
rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank"
onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);"
qt-tooltip="lineageos.org"}]{.qlink_container}. It is the follow-on to
the now-discontinued CyanogenMod project.
