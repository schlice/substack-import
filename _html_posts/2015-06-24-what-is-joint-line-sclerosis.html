--- title: '"What is joint line sclerosis?"' date: '2015-06-24' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [Joint-line sclerosis is actually called **subchondral
sclerosis**. It is a thickening, or an increas in bone density, in the
subchondral layer. It shows up as a thick white bone along the joint
line.\
\
Subchondral sclerosis often accompanies progressive
osteoarthritis.]
