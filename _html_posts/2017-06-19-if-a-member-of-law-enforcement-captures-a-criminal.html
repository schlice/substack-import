--- title: '"If a member of law enforcement captures a criminal that has a reward on his head can the officer/agent collect the bounty?"' date: '2017-06-19' tags: - law-enforcement - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

No, if a reward is offered, the reward is given to the department for
whom you were working at the time of the arrest.

I'm sure you'd get a nice commendation, but not the money.

Part of the reason for this is to discourage officers from intentionally
pursuing suspects *because* they have a bounty.
