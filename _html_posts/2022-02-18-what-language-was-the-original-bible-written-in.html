--- title: '"What language was the original bible written in?"' date: '2022-02-18' tags: - theology layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

The Bible is not one book, but is a collection of 66 different books
written over the course of 1500 years in three different languages:
Hebrew (the Old Testament), Greek (the New Testament), and Aramaic
(portions of the books of Daniel and Ezra, as well as portions of both
the Old and New Testaments).

\

Original question:

What language was the Christian Bible first written in?
