How do people hear about Christ?

Sometimes, someone hears directly from a person. Sometimes, they hear a
snippet of something and ask a trusted friend. Sometimes, they hear some
obscure thing about Christmas originally being about a person, and not
one who wears a big red suit covered in soot, and they ask about it.

But in all cases, a person truly "hears" about Christ *when they are
willing to listen.* When they ask someone about the life of Jesus and
why it was literally the most influential life which was ever lived, and
they are willing to listen to that account, then they will "hear."
