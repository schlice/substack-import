--- title: '"What are those protrusions on top of Air Force One?"' date: '2017-07-16' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Those are domes which house communications antennas, most likely
satellite communications arrays.

Many commercial aircraft have them today but only in aircraft with on
board Wi-Fi. And you usually only see one on a commercial aircraft, not
multiple.
