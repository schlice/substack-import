--- title: '"How much money do police agencies in the United States make when they book an individual in jail?"' date: '2017-05-14' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

None. Police do not get paid when they book someone in jail.

Police departments consume taxpayer dollars, which are budgeted by the
local government which runs that department. Effecting an arrest does
not change that number.
