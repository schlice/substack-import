--- title: '"Why does Solumedrol IV help Multiple Sclerosis flare?"' date: '2022-08-15' tags: - health-autoimmune layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Solumedrol is an anti-inflammatory glucocorticoid, which contains
methylprednisolone sodium succinate as the active ingredient.

In other words, it's an anti-inflammatory drug.

In a MS flare, inflammation in the nerves caused by the disease action
brings on the symptoms, and reducing systemic inflammation can help to
alleviate the symptoms faster.

Why does Solumedrol IV help Multiple Sclerosis flare?
