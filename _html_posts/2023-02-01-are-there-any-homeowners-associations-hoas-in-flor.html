--- title: '"Are there any homeowners associations (HOAs) in Florida or Texas? If not, why?"' date: '2023-02-01' tags: - homeowners-assn layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Florida has the highest concentration of community associations, with
25.1% of homeowners belonging to an HOA, and nearly 44% of all total
residents living in a community managed by an HOA.

Texas also has a very high concentration of HOAs, with 12.1% of all
homeowners, and 20.9% of all residents, living in or owning in a
community managed by an HOA.\[1\](#RFxNq){#cite-RFxNq .citation_link}
