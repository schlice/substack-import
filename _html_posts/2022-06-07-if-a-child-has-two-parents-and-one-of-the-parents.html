--- title: '"If a child has two parents and one of the parents file a restraining order on the other, can the child see both parents?"' date: '2022-06-07' tags: - policy-analysis layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Yes, but not at the same time.

Clearly, a restraining order will necessitate that the parents are
separated, if not already divorced. So custody will have to be arranged,
and visitation will also need to be arranged.

Generally, the assistance of the court will be required.
