--- title: '"Why did God take so long to send his messenger?"' date: '2022-11-29' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

God is timeless. For God, history is now. Now is now. The future is now.

"A long time" is something that only you can experience. For God, He's
playing the long game, but it's only long to you.
