--- title: '"Should a person be charged with murder if the victim is resuscitated?"' date: '2016-05-26' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

You cannot be charged with murder if the victim is alive. It's as simple
as that. Murder is the unlawful taking of a life, and depending on the
severity, with forethought and malice. If there is no taking of a life,
there is no murder.
