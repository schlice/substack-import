--- title: '"Can a gun go off by itself? In normal conditions, without being dropped, heated up, shaken, having design issues, or having its state altered in any other way, can it go off without anyone touching the trigger?"' date: '2016-08-06' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

When I was in the military, we learned to fire the M9, which was
essentially a Beretta 92F. That weapon has no fewer than one external
and three internal safeties which only moved when the trigger moved.
Although no one ever tried it, my instructor claimed that you could
hammer nails with a loaded M9, striking right on the hammer at the
rear of the firearm, and it would not discharge.

Assuming a normally functioning firearm, and under normal conditions, if
the trigger does not move, the gun does not fire. Period.
