--- title: '"What rights do criminal suspects have under the Fifth Amendment?"' date: '2022-11-06' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

They have the same rights as any other citizen of the United States:
**they do not have to be a witness against themselves, they have the
right to a grand jury, and they do not have to be subject to double
jeopardy.** In other words, the Fifth Amendment protects both the guilty
*and* the innocent.
