--- title: '"What are the chances that the NYPD will issue a carry permit to a lawyer who has been getting very severe death threats?"' date: '2017-03-19' tags: - law-enforcement - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

The chances are good. The chances improve depending on how many police
reports you have already filed regarding your death threats.

If you've not filed any reports regarding the threats, you may have a
more difficult time getting your permit approved, but if the threats are
credible, you have a much better chance of seeing your permit approved.
