--- title: '"Who has to present the minutes of the general body meeting in a residential society? Is it the outgoing secretary or the new, incoming secretary?"' date: '2017-08-16' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

While election of new officers takes place at the beginning of the
Annual Meeting, the new Board is not empaneled until the next regular
Board meeting.

Therefore, the currently empaneled Board fulfills their duties at the
Annual Meeting.
