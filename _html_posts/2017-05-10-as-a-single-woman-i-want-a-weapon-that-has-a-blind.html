I want to say I don't advocate carrying this device. I agree with the
other posters: get a gun. Learn how to use it. It will save your life.
You can always stick a tac light on the rail, or heck, carry a mini
light too.

But since you asked, this is a device which has a light, an alarm, and a
less-than-lethal weapon to stop an intruder.

The biggest problem that exists here is that in order to use this tool,
you have to be close enough to reach out and touch your assailant.
That's at least six feet too close for my taste. That's hand-to-hand
range, and I don't like putting you in that position. Therefore, I do
not recommend this device.

But as I said, you asked, so I delivered.
