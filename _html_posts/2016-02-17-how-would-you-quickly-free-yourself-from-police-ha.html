--- title: '"How would you quickly free yourself from police handcuffs?"' date: '2016-02-17' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Without the ability to dislocate any number of bones in your wrist and
hand, you can\'t easily or quickly free yourself from police handcuffs.

Your best bet: Avoid situations in which you would be subjected to
handcuffs in the first place.
