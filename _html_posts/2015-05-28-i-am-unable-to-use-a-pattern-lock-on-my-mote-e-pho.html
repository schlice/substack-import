Answer









Content: [Your phone has security policies in place based on
certificates you have on there. When you join certain company domains,
they can place restrictions on your phone, such as using a PIN lock
instead of a pattern lock.\
\
If you remove the certificates, it\'s likely you won\'t be able to
connect to your corporate servers anymore.]
