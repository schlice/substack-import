This is called a *faithless elector*. What happens to you if you are a
faithless elector is up to the state. If your state has faithless
elector laws, you would be subject to the penalties for violating those
laws. In 16

states, if you don't vote the way the state told you to vote, your vote
gets cancelled

and you get replaced. Five of those states impose a penalty on you for
doing so.
