No one can dodge a bullet. *Mythbusters* proved it. The bullet simply
moves too fast. If you move after the bullet is fired, you are hit.
Neither soldiers, law enforcement, nor martial artists can dodge a
bullet. It is physically impossible; you cannot move that quickly.
