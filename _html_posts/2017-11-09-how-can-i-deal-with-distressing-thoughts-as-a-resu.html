--- title: '"How can I deal with distressing thoughts as a result of being raped?"' date: '2017-11-09' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Talk to a counselor.

There's a stigma in the United States around talking to counselors.
Please do not fall victim to this stigma. Find a good counselor in your
area and talk to them. Your doctor can help you find a good counselor if
you don't know one.

Please, please do this. It will help tremendously.
