The logic begins and ends with the fact that Jesus is God.

Once you establish that Jesus is God, you can make the next logical
step, which is "Whatever Jesus (who is God) says is true."

Then, you can make the *next* logical step, which is to note that Jesus
said that He is the only way to God (or more specifically, eternal life
with God in heaven).

Therefore, Christians think only Jesus can lead to God because Jesus
(who is God) said that He is the only way.

> *"Let not your hearts be troubled. Believe in God; believe also in me.
> In my Father\'s house are many rooms. If it were not so, would I have
> told you that I go to prepare a place for you?*  *And if I go and
> prepare a place for you, I will come again and will take you to
> myself, that where I am you may be also. And you know the way to where
> I am going." Thomas said to him, "Lord, we do not know where you are
> going. How can we know the way?" Jesus said to him, "I am the way, and
> the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father except through
> me." --- John 14:1--6 ESV*
