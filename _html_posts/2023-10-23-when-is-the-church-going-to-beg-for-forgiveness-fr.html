--- title: '"When is the church going to beg for forgiveness from common people for killing Giordano Bruno?"' date: '2023-10-23' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

They already did, since Bruno was executed during the Inquisition.

> In 2000, Pope John Paul II began a new a new era in the church's
> relationship to its history when he donned mourning garments to
> apologize for millennia of grievous violence and persecution --- from
> the Inquisition to a wide range of sins against Jews, nonbelievers,
> and the indigenous people of colonized lands --- and sought pardon
> "for the divisions among Christians, for the use of violence that some
> have committed in the service of truth, and for attitudes of mistrust
> and hostility assumed towards followers of other
> religions."\[1\](#dsOSA){#cite-dsOSA .citation_link}
