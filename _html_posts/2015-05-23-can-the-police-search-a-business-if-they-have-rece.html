--- title: '"Can the police search a business if they have received a phone call indicating that the business carries stolen goods?"' date: '2015-05-23' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [They can use the call (known as a \"tip\") to open an
investigation. If the investigation reveals enough information to get a
warrant, a judge will issue a warrant allowing the police to search for
something specific. The police can then execute that warrant, looking
for the items detailed in the warrant.]
