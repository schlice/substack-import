**If we can convince enough young people to not join the military, could
we starve them of enough recruits that they would have to abandon their
overseas colonial ambitions?**

You'd have to assume a lot in order to make this hypothetical scenario
work. For the sake of this hypothetical, let's assume you're talking
about the United States.

First, you 'd have to assume that it is the military leadership which
drives any perceived "overseas colonial ambitions." In fact, the
military gets its strategic marching orders from the President in
concert with the Joint Chiefs of Staff, who implement the President's
military policy directives. So if there are any "overseas colonial
ambitions" to be had, they would belong to the President.

Second, you'd have to assume that the United States has "overseas
colonial ambitions," which we currently do not. In fact, we're not
interested in annexing any territories or countries anywhere in the
world. We have plenty of other options: for starters, we can annex
Puerto Rico, or any one of the [[13 unincorporated US
territories](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=eb82d3&url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unincorporated_territories_of_the_United_States){.external_link
rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank"
onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);"
qt-tooltip="wikipedia.org"}]{.qlink_container} we currently possess, and
we don't even need to engage the military to do it. We can just make
them new states, with nothing more than a supermajority of Congress.

Finally, you'd have to assume that a resource shortage of new recruits
to active duty would somehow curtail the President's plans for anything.
Don't forget that we have a Reserve force which is double the size of
our Active Duty force which can be recalled to Active Duty very quickly.
If we have a personnel shortfall and we have operational needs, the
Reserves will be recalled to Active Duty to fill in the gaps.

Therefore, for these reasons, it's extremely unlikely that your scenario
is even feasible, or even if it was feasible, that it would achieve its
aims.
