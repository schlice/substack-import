--- title: '"How do police officers feel about the laws they might have broken in the past before becoming an officer?"' date: '2015-06-11' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [Doing bad things as a kid means you\'re human. Doing bad
things and then *stopping* doing bad things means you\'re normal.\
\
Believe it or not, police departments don\'t look negatively at this.
They would look negatively if you tried to *cover up* your past bad
actions: lying is a deal-breaker when trying to become a cop. As long as
you are open and honest about your past, the police will understand
this.\
\
Everyone makes mistakes as a youth. Growing up and growing out of that
phase is also mandatory if you want to become a cop.]
