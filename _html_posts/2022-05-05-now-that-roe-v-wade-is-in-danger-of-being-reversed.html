--- title: '"Now that Roe v. Wade is in danger of being reversed, what about Loving v. Virginia (interracial marriage), and Obergefell v. Hodges (gay marriage)?"' date: '2022-05-05' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

*Loving* and *Obergefell* were both legally sound decisions.

*Roe* was built on shaky ground from the outset. It was based on the
legally-tenuous *Griswold v. Connecticut* decision, and it was just a
bad decision all around.

*Loving* and *Obergefell* are not in danger of being overturned just
because *Roe* is.
