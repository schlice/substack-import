**How can US citizens prevent illegal contraband from being delivered by
drones to their neighborhoods when UAVs are protected by federal laws?**

Pay your taxes.

Your taxes fund law enforcement. They need that money to investigate
these things.

Your federal taxes fund the DEA. They are the agency tasked with
enforcing drug laws in overfly areas.
