--- title: '"Is it legal for police officers to steal (loan) vehicles during emergencies?"' date: '2018-06-22' tags: - law-enforcement - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

It is legal, and it is called "commandeering" a vehicle.

My dad did it once. He actually stopped a driver on the street and asked
him to drive him a few blocks up so they could catch a criminal who was
on foot.

The driver took him a few blocks up and dropped him off. Easy peesey.
