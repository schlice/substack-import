--- title: '"What is a better school for undergraduate computer engineering, the Air Force Academy or Stanford?"' date: '2017-09-25' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Stanford traditionally ranks higher than USAFA as an engineering school.

But if you're comparing the two schools and trying to figure out which
would be a better choice *if you want to be a computer engineer,*
then the correct answer is not a military academy.

*You go to a military academy to become a military officer.* You are
an officer first, and a career second.

That doesn't mean that USAFA is a bad engineering school. Far from it.

> The Air Force Academy ranks high among the nation\'s top universities
> in engineering and liberal arts courses, according to the 2015 Best
> Colleges Ranking report released Tuesday by U.S. News & World Report.
>
> Specifically, the Academy has the nation\'s fifth-best undergraduate
> engineering program, and its second-best aerospace, aeronautical and
> astronautical program, a position it maintains for the 14th
> consecutive year. The Academy has the nation\'s second-best computer
> engineering program, its third-best electrical engineering program and
> its sixth-best in mechanical engineering
> program.\[1\](#sABqx){#cite-sABqx .citation_link}

But I return to my original point. If you want to be a computer
engineer, go to any college except for a military academy. If you want
to be a military officer, go to a military academy.
