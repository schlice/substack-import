You'll have to read your Association's Governing Documents to find out.
It's different for every Association, and different in every state.

Also note that beyond your HOA or Condo Board, you may have state
requirements for installing security cameras (such as signage indicating
that security cameras are in use). So be sure to check your state laws
as well as your HOA/Condo Board Governing Documents.
