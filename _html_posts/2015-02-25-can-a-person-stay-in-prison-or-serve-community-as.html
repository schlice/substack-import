--- title: '"Can a person stay in prison or serve community as an earning to pay off before you commit a crime?"' date: '2015-02-25' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [You can\'t be sentenced until you\'ve been tried. You
can\'t be tried until you\'ve been arrested. You can\'t be arrested
until you make overt actions towards committing a crime.\
\
So no, you can\'t pre-pay for your crime under the US justice system.
Your time in jail is for a specific reason, not as a hobby.\
\
Jails are too overcrowded to accommodate hobbyists.]
