Here's the problem.

The basic definition of rape is sexual intercourse without consent.
Consent is a conscious decision to approve of an activity, without
impaired judgement. If you are drunk, your judgement is impaired, so you
cannot give legal consent to sex.

The law says that if you know a person is drunk, and you press forward
with sex, it's considered rape. But it's decidedly less clear-cut if you
are *both* drunk. The details in any given situation vary, so it's
difficult to determine if anyone has consented.
