Answer









Content: 

The twelve tribes of Israel are descendants of Jacob, son of Isaac, and
grandson of Abraham (who was renamed Israel, who the 12 tribes are named
after). The patriarchs of the 12 tribes of Israel are the children of
Jacob.

But there are a lot of other descendants than just the direct lineage
between Noah and Jacob and the 12 Tribes.
