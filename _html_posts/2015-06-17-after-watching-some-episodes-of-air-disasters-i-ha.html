--- title: '"After watching some episodes of air disasters, I have a question in terms of punishment towards some ground crews (mechanicals) which in some cases it is clearly their fault and they caused the plane to crash. Do mechanics get punished and if so, how bad it will be?"' date: '2015-06-17' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [In addition to the other answers is the theory of
*corporate responsibility.* A mechanic acts as an agent of the
corporation, so the ultimate liability is with the corporation.\
\
The mechanic who does shoddy work will be terminated with prejudice,
which means it\'s a bad conduct termination and unless he lies about his
past, he\'ll never get another job as an aircraft mechanic.\
\
The mechanic who *intentionally* causes a part to fail will be held
individually criminally responsible. The mechanic who is not criminally
negligent but just bad at his job will be fired, and the company will be
held financially responsible for the accident.]
