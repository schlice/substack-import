--- title: '"How do I identify a cop? Can I ask for identification?"' date: '2015-04-03' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [It\'s unlikely that an ID card gets faked, but it\'s far
more likely that a badge or shield gets faked.\
\
You can always ask for ID, even of officers in uniform. I have never
heard of an agency that does not require their officers to carry their
ID cards along with their badges. It\'s part of an officer\'s
\"credentials,\" or \"creds\" for short.]
