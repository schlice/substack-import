--- title: '"How is the USAF able to track the hundreds of planes in the US?"' date: '2018-08-19' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

The Air Force does not track the thousands of aircraft aloft over
the United States at any one time. Air Traffic Control, which is part of
the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) tracks them.
