--- title: '"How common is it for a law enforcement officer to retire (after a few decades of service) having never fired their gun at a suspect?"' date: '2016-05-26' tags: - retirement - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

While every cop trains to use his firearm, and they put thousands of
rounds downrange at the firing range, very few will ever fire their
weapons outside of a firing range. It looks as if current numbers are
anywhere between 95 and 99% of officers will never fire their guns
outside of the range.
