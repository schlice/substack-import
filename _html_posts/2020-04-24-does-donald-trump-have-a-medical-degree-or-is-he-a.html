**Does Donald Trump have a medical degree or is he a quack doctor
presenting serious health risks to many Americans?**

Donald Trump does exactly what I do here on Quora. He provides GENERAL
MEDICAL INFORMATION. The information is timely, and needs to be widely
distributed so doctors can interpret it for their patients.

He does not provide medical advice, which requires a
doctor-patient relationship. He cannot prescribe medications, so unless
people are finding illegal ways to obtain medicine solely based on
general medical information, he's not introducing any serious risks to
Americans.
