By not doing it.

Time for Constitution 101.

Lesson 1: The Constitution does not grant rights.

Our founding documents are very clear on this. They say that we have
been endowed *by our Creator* with certain inalienable rights.

Our Creator granted us rights.

So why are our rights listed in the Constitution? The whole point of the
Constitution is to *constrain government*. So our rights are listed
there so as to instruct government of the rights which they may not
violate.
