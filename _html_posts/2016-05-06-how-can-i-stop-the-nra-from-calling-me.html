Answer









Content: 

When they call you, use these words:

\"Please remove my number from your calling list. Thank you.\"

Pretty simple. If they continue to call, please report the call to your
state Attorney General\'s office, the agency which handles Do Not Call
violations.

Make sure you add your number to the government\'s Do Not Call list at
[www.DoNotCall.gov(https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=aef6a8&url=http://www.DoNotCall.gov){.external_link
