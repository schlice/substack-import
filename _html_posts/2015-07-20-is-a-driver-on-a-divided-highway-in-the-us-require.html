--- title: '"Is a driver on a divided highway in the US required to pull over for an emergency vehicle (police, fire, ambulance) that is going the opposite direction?"' date: '2015-07-20' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [In Colorado, you are only required to move over if the
emergency vehicle is on your side of the road on a divided highway.\
\
On a non-divided highway, all vehicles on both sides of the road must
move.]
