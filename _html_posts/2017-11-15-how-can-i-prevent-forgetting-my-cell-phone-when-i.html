Content: 

In addition to the wireless leash that [[Konstantinos
Konstantinides](https://www.quora.com/profile/Konstantinos-Konstantinides)]{.qlink_container}
wrote about, most smartwatches can also notify you that you have moved
away from your phone. Additionally, my car links to my phone when I get
in. When it doesn't, I start checking pockets and usually find that my
phone is still inside.
