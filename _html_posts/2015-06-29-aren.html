worst, being ready in case you may need it, then you may run into

in the first place. You will be investigated each and every time you
brandish and/or use your weapon, even if you were completely legal in
your action. Even if you potentially stopped a mass shooting with your
weapon, you would still face weapons charges for unlawfully concealing a
weapon. The only way you *might* avoid prosecution is if public opinion
was so far in your favor that the DA declined to press charges. Other
than that, if you ever hope to actually *use* your weapon for more
than just a lump in your waistband, in doing so, you subject yourself to
prosecution.\
\
It\'s a hollow victory when you save your life or the life of another,
only to be prosecuted on weapons charges.]
