**Did Trump accomplish anything by stalling the signing of the stimulus
bill? Was it all just high drama, or are there consequences?**

Actually, he pressured Congress to move forward with, and vote, Monday
on an add-on bill which will increase relief payments from \$600 to
\$2000, will begin Congressional investigations into election fraud, and
will repeal Section 230 protections for big tech.
