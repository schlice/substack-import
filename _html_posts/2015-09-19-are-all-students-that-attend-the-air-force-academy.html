--- title: '"Are all students that attend the Air Force Academy in the Air Force?"' date: '2015-09-19' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [There are a number of exchange students who attend the
Academy from foreign military schools. They will go home and become
members of their own air forces.\
\
The American students who attend the Air Force Academy will graduate and
be commissioned into the Active Air Force as Second
Lieutenants.]
