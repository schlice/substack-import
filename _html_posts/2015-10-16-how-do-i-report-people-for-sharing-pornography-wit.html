> *Question details:*
>
> *I was in a group chat on KIK, and one of the users on the chat sent
> dick pics, followed by another guy who sent pictures of boobs and
> butts. I warned them that I would report them, and they have since
> removed me and banned me from the group. The group has users from age
> 15-20(That\'s only the few in the group who\'ve told me. There are
> probably younger). How do I go about reporting these sickening
> people?I live in Louisiana USA. They\'re probably scattered around the
> map.*

You have to provide the usernames of these individuals to Kik Support.

To send a report about someone in Kik, you can do it from within the
chat itself:

- Open the chat with the user you want to report
- Tap their profile picture
- Tap your Menu button (usually three vertical dots)
- Tap Report.

If you don\'t have a chat with a person you wish to report, use this
form to [[submit a request for
assistance.](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=018ba2&url=https://kikinteractive.zendesk.com/anonymous_requests/new){.external_link
