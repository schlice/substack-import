**Why can\'t the Chief Justice act as a real judge in the impeachment
trial?**

Because it's not a real trial.

It's a political process, not a legal trial. The Senators are not
impartial jurors. They are supposed to act in a "fair" and "impartial"
manner, but this is by far not a trial.
