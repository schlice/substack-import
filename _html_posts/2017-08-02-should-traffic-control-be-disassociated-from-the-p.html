--- title: '"Should traffic control be disassociated from the police due to a lack of fixed traffic personnel (frequent transfers to other functions)?"' date: '2017-08-02' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Traffic control should only be disassociated from the police if the
function is not germane to their mission.

If the problem is a lack of fixed traffic personnel, the police should
work to remedy that specific issue.
