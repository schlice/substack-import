What warms your heart about your community?

For all of its faults (and there are many), *my community* *loves its
public servants.* Our hearts collectively break when any officer is shot
or killed, but it hurts that much more when it's one of our local
officers or deputies.

Here's the forinstance I'll give you: in early August, Officer Cem Duzel
of the Colorado Springs Police Department [[was shot in the
head](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=dfb9bd&url=https://gazette.com/news/colorado-springs-police-officer-wounded-in-shootout-is-stable-but/article_4b627cb0-981b-11e8-9486-e38a04dfbe55.html){.external_link
