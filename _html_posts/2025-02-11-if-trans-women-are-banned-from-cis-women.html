It's not complex at all. I don't understand why we make it so complex.

If you want to participate in gender-separated sports, you participate
as the gender recorded on your birth certificate at birth. If a birth
certificate doesn't exist (or there is question as to whether the gender
was correctly recorded at birth), then biology determines your
gender for the purposes of participation in sports, not personal
feelings.

That said, the problem of intersex people is a much harder problem to
tackle, *but a much less pressing issue for protecting the integrity of
women's sports.*
