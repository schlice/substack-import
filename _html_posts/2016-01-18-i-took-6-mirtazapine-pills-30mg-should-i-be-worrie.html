As for mirtazapine (Remeron), it is a drug that needs to build up in
your body in order to be effective. So long-term, you\'ll probably be
fine from an overdose of mirtazapine. I would probably go to the ER if I
were you. If you do go to the ER, please tell them why you took so much
mirtazapine. They will help you from there.

But even if you don\'t go to the ER, please please please please call
the appropriate number up at the top of this page. Those folks are
miracle workers and they really do want to listen to you. Please call
them now.
