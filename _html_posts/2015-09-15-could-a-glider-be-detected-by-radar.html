--- title: '"Could a glider be detected by radar?"' date: '2015-09-15' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [Depends on the type of glider. Aluminum skinned gliders
which are towed or launched reflect enough radar signals to be seen on
radar. Lighter gliders like hanggliders do not have enough flat metallic
surfaces to reflect radar, and so will likely not be
seen.]
