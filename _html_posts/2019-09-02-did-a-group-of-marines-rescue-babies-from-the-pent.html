Did a group of marines rescue babies from Pentogon on 9/11?

I dunno. I wasn't there. I was at home on 9/11, watching aircraft hit
the towers on live TV.

But I do know how to use Google. And as it turns out, Snopes (the always
reliable, always fair, never politically leaning fact-checkers that they
are) actually had an accurate accounting of what might have been
misinterpreted as "marines rescuing babies:"

> The tale about 40 Marines charging to the rescue (which began its
> online life in September 2008), possibly resulted from a mishearing,
> misunderstanding, or just plain exaggeration of the actions taken that
> day by Army colonel [[Dave

> protective perimeter around the children and blocked one lane of
> westbound traffic on the George Washington Memorial Parkway to
