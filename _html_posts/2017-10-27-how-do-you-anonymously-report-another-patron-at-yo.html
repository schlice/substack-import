--- title: '"How do you anonymously report another patron at your local library who is doing something illegal?"' date: '2017-10-27' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Reporting someone is not difficult --- just call 911. The problem is
staying anonymous while you do it. Here are a few ways:

- Find a pay phone and make a phone call.
- See if there is a public use phone somewhere in the library. Make sure
  you keep yourself covered --- hats and glasses are useful.
- [[Use a disposable phone
  number](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=15b282&url=http://blog.coverme.ws/en/201604-4-easy-ways-to-get-a-disposable-phone-number/){.external_link
  rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank"
  onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);"
  qt-tooltip="coverme.ws"}]{.qlink_container} on your mobile phone.
