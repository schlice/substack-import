--- title: '"How does the Supreme Court decision to block nationwide vaccine mandate for Corporations with approximately 85 million workers, but allow healthcare worker vaccine mandate to take effect, will influence the US economy?"' date: '2022-01-14' tags: - policy-analysis - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

It will ensure that workers will not be forced to quit or be fired for
expressing their *perfectly legal* individual desire to not be
vaccinated. This will ensure that we will not be committing what we call
in baseball an "unforced error." That error would have been by *making
the labor shortage worse,* and thus ensuring that this decision didn't
incidentally hurt the US economy.
