Basically, tranqulizers don\'t work on people quickly or equally. Use
too much and you kill them. Use too little, and they just get a little
groggy.\
\
Plus, tranquilizers don\'t work the same way you see in the movies. If
you don\'t hit a vein, it could take up to 30 minutes before the
sedative starts to take effect, which makes the dart effectively useless
as a self-defense weapon.]
