Answer









Content: 

It seems to be.

One of its best features: the batteries are rechargeable. Additionally,
the Pebblebee Clip (the one that mimics the small puck-sized AirTag) has
its own built-in carry loop, unlike Apple's version which requires a
third party accessory.

Prices are on par with AirTags, and they work seamlessly with Apple's
Find My network, so they're a solid competitor to AirTags.
