--- title: '"Can people truly change if they apologize and admit their wrongs but do not take action to correct their faults?"' date: '2025-04-15' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

If you're walking in one direction, can you truly go the other direction
if you simply apologize and admit that you're going in the wrong
direction but never turn around?

If you don't actually *implement* any changes, you'll never change.

Will Rogers said, "When you find yourself in a hole, stop digging."
You'll never fix anything until you stop doing the thing that caused you
to break in the first place.
