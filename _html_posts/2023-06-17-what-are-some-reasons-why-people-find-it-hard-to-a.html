--- title: '"What are some reasons why people find it hard to accept that the Bible contains contradictions, errors, and historical inaccuracies?"' date: '2023-06-17' tags: - theology layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Because to date, every "contradiction, error, and historical inaccuracy"
which has ever been "discovered" in the Bible is none of those.

Usually, they are simply misunderstandings of the Bible, the context of
the writing, or the understanding that the people hearing the Bible at
that time would take from the writing.
