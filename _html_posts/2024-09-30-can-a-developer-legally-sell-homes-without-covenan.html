--- title: '"Can a developer legally sell homes without covenants or deed restrictions to prevent future homeowners associations (HOAs) from being created?"' date: '2024-09-30' tags: - homeowners-assn layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

No.

Once the developer is no longer developing, the homeowners can do as
they like.

Even if there are deed restrictions which prevent the formation of an
HOA, if 100% of the homeowners in a given area agree, they can petition
a judge for a Restriction Release.
