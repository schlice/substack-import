**As a libertarian, do you support laws banning non-medical circumcision
of male babies?**

Libertarians believe that parents have the right to raise their children
in the way they see fit, "according to their own standards and beliefs,
provided that the rights of children to be free from abuse and neglect
are also protected."\[1\](#dxFbh){#cite-dxFbh .citation_link}

Therefore, in order to support such a law, you have to make the case
that circumcision is abuse or neglect.

While it may be unnecessary cosmetic surgery, it is not harmful and
causes no long term damage. No bodily functions are harmed by the
surgery. Therefore, it cannot be classified as "abuse" or "neglect."

Since we have determined that circumcision is cosmetic surgery, in order
to support such a law, you have to make the case that parents do not
have the right to consent to cosmetic surgery on behalf of the children
for whom they are responsible.

A Libertarian would not make that case.

Therefore, a Libertarian would not support a law banning non-medical
circumcision of male babies.
