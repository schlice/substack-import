Is a president allowed to curse while in the White House?

Of course he is. The 1st Amendment says (emphasis mine):

> Congress shall make no law respecting an establishment of
> religion, or prohibiting the free exercise thereof; or **abridging the
> freedom of speech**, or of the press; or the right of the people
> peaceably to assemble, and to petition the Government for a redress of
> grievances.

It probably doesn't comport with the decorum due to a place like the
White House, but it's certainly not illegal.
