--- title: '"How do you know if someone is a police informant?"' date: '2018-02-09' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Ideally, the only way you find out is when you see them on the witness
stand at trial and you're like, "Hey, why is Bob on the stand?" And then
he starts to speak and you're like, "Wow, he was a police informant! I
had no idea!"

Otherwise, you probably wouldn't know.
