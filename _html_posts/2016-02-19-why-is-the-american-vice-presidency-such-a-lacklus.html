--- title: '"Why is the American vice presidency such a lackluster position with so few visible responsibilities?"' date: '2016-02-19' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [Because the Constitution.\
\
Constitutionally, the Vice President has two jobs: the president of the
Senate, and sitting around waiting for the President to die.\
\
Presidents, therefore, give the VP lots of jobs to do. They delegate
responsibilities to the VP to ease the burden of the Presidency.\
\
He may be the second string QB, but he still has to be a qualified
QB.]
