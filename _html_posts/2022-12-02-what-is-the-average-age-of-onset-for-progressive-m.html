--- title: '"What is the average age of onset for progressive multiple sclerosis (MS)?"' date: '2022-12-02' tags: - health-autoimmune layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

The only known "average age" is the amount of time between being
diagnosed with Relapsing Remitting MS (RRMS) and moving to Secondary
Progressive MS (MS) when you are untreated.

That time frame is approximately 10 years.
