--- title: '"How do surface-to-air anti-aircraft missiles work?"' date: '2014-07-17' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- 




Answer








Content: [The missiles have active guidance systems on board that
guide the missile to the target. While there are several different kinds
of anti-aircraft missiles, the two most common are radar-guided and
heat-seeking missiles. The one assumed to have shot down the
Malaysian Airlines aircraft would presumably have been a radar-guided
missile.]
