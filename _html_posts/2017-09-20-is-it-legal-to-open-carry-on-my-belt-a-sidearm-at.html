--- title: '"Is it legal to open carry (on my belt) a sidearm at 18 years old on a motorcycle for my protection in Tennessee?"' date: '2017-09-20' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

A license is required to carry a loaded firearm in Tennessee, whether
open or concealed.

Licenses are issued to people 21 years old or older, or 18 years old if
the applicant is active duty, reservist, guardsman, or honorably
discharged from their branch of service. Their DD-214 must indicate
pistol qualification in order to bypass the mandatory safety course.
Tennessee also recognizes any valid out-of-state carry permit, so long
as the person is not a resident of Tennessee.

So the short answer is not without a license.
