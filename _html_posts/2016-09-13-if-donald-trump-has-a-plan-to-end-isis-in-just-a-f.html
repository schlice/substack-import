So, being an uneducated deplorable, this sounds to me as if he will
actually get a plan in January, 2017. That doesn't mean he will share it
with all of us, all the time. Because "we" are not going to do any

they don't have any ideas on how they will take on ISIS, right at this
very moment. It means that whatever plans they have are still
classified. Classified things are released on a "need to know" basis
(unless you're Hillary, then you give the terrorists access to
classified email whenever they feel like hacking your personal server).
Since you don't "need to know," you don't know.

Kinda makes sense, doesn't it? Your football team doesn't usually
publish its playbook on the Internet for everyone to read. We tend to
keep that sort of information "close to the vest" so that we can
(somewhat) retain the element of surprise. Maintaining "operational
security" (OPSEC) is an important tenet of warfighting.
