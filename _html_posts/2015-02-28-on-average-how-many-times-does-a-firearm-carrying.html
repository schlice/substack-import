--- title: '"On average, how many times does a firearm-carrying officer fire their weapon during active duty in the course of their officer career?"' date: '2015-02-28' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [Most police officers in the United States, over the course
of their entire careers, will never discharge their firearms anywhere
except for the range. It is a small percentage that are actually
involved in \"officer-involved shootings.\"\
\
Drawing your weapon is a different story. Officers will draw their
weapons many times, but actually discharging it is
rare.]
