--- title: '"Can you enlist as an officer in the Air Force if you have 2 years of JROTC under your belt along with 2 years of trade school experience?"' date: '2019-02-11' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

You cannot "enlist" as an officer.

There are four paths to becoming an officer in the United States Air
Force:

- Graduate from the US Air Force Academy (USAFA)
- Attend Officer Training School (OTS) after getting your Bachelor's
  Degree
- Attend AFROTC in college
- Enlist as an Airman and work towards becoming an officer

As you can see, you need at least a Bachelor's Degree to become an
officer. With the exception of OTS, you can get your Bachelor's Degree
while you train to be a military officer. If you go the OTS path, it's
because you already have your Bachelor's Degree.
