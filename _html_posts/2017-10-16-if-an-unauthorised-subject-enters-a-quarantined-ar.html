--- title: '"If an unauthorised subject enters a quarantined area does the military have the right to open fire?"' date: '2017-10-16' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

If the military is protecting that area and it is posted as "Use of
Deadly Force Authorized," and the subject fails to follow directives,
the military can shoot to protect the facility.
