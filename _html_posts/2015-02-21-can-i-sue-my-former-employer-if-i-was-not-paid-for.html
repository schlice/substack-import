--- title: '"Can I sue my former employer if I was not paid for a month?"' date: '2015-02-21' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [I would suggest you talk to a lawyer. If you had a job,
you probably had an agreement of some kind that you signed when you
hired on that specified when and how you were to be paid. If you did,
and you did work in accordance with your work agreement, and you
weren\'t paid for the work you did, you probably have a
case.]
