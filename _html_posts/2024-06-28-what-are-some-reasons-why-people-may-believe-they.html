There is only one reason to believe that you're not going to hell: **you
have been saved.**

Repentance and belief in Christ is what saves you. It's really that
simple.

It's not about being *exempt* from God's wrath. It's that God visits His
wrath on everyone. We're all sinners, and we must all pay for our
sin. That's not wrath, that's justice.

Except in our case, God made a way for us to have our sin paid for.
That's mercy.

In our case, Jesus paid the penalty for our sins. That's grace.

Why do we know we are not going to Hell? Jesus promised us that
repentance and faith pay the penalty for our sins which we deserve.
