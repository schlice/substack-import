--- title: '"How can I log into my old Facebook but have no access to the old phone number?"' date: '2017-09-13' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

When the authentication question comes up, if you don't have access to
the phone registered on the account any more, click the "Need another
way to authenticate?" button down at the bottom.

It will present you with a variety of options. Some options include
authenticating from a device on which you are already logged into
Facebook (if you have one).

If none of the options listed will work, click the "Other options" link
at the bottom. You will be asked to either provide a government-issued
ID, or take a picture of yourself holding a sign with a hand-written
code on it. Once you provide one of these, Facebook will review your
submission and you will be allowed access to your account again.

Be sure to update your phone number so that you can receive login codes
again.
