Do Samsung Galaxy S8 cases fit in a Samsung Galaxy S7?

No. The dimensions of both phones are different, so the cases are not
interchangeable. You will have to purchase a case specifically designed
to fit that specific model (S7 or S8).

(Source: I have both in my family)
