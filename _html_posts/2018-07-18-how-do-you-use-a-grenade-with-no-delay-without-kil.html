--- title: '"How do you use a grenade with no delay, without killing yourself?"' date: '2018-07-18' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Tie a string to the pin. The string should be the same length as the
blast radius of the grenade. Tie the other end of the string to your
finger.

Throw the grenade. When it reaches the end of the string, the pin will
be yanked out and the grenade will immediately explode.
