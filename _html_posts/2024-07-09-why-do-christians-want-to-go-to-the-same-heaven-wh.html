--- title: '"Why do Christians want to go to the same heaven where the devil was created and frequently visited?"' date: '2024-07-09' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Because the fact that notorious creatures *came from* there doesn't
indict the place. It indicts the creature.

Heaven is still the ultimate destination for us. I'm looking forward to
it.

Besides, Satan holds no power over us. We are adopted into God's family.
We are children of God. As such, we have full authority over Satan, and
can command him to flee from us. So he's just an annoying little gnat to
me.
