Does iPhone x have sim cards?

Yes, for the iPhones designed for GSM carriers, the iPhone X supports
SIM cards. There is a standard SIM tray which accepts a nano SIM card.

The iPhone XS has *dual-SIM* capability: a standard nano SIM tray, and
an *eSIM* internal, non-removable SIM card.
