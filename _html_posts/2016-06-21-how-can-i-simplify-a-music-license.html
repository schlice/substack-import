--- title: '"How can I simplify a music license?"' date: '2016-06-21' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

You can publish a "human-readable" license as long as your artists agree
to the wording of the actual license. Your human-readable version will
have to start with something like "This is a condensed version of the
license agreement. By agreeing to these terms, you are agreeing to the
terms of the actual license which you can read \."

The problem is the interpretation between the actual license text and
the "human-readable" version.

If you produce a human-readable version of your license, your attorney
will have to approve it to ensure that it captures the major facets of
the actual agreement.
