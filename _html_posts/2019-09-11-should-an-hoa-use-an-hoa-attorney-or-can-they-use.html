Content: 

**Should an HOA use an HOA attorney or can they use a Real estate
attorney to make changes to covenants and bylaws?**

We tend to use Real Estate attorneys *who specialize in HOA law,* when
we need the assistance of an attorney.

So the answer to the question is yes.
