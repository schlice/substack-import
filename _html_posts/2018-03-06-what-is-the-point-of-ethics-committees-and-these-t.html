and they advised her about the relevant law (the Hatch act). From the
article you quoted:

> \T\he White House Office of Legal Counsel reported that lawyers had

> Stefan Passantino wrote that after speaking to Conway, \"We concluded
> that Ms. Conway acted inadvertently and is highly unlikely to do so
