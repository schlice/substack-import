--- title: '"How can I find out if someone has truly filed for a divorce?"' date: '2017-04-29' tags: - policy-analysis layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

"Filing" for divorce involves filing in court.

If you are a party to the divorce, you can contact the clerk of the
court in the jurisdiction in which the divorce would be filed in order
to determine if such a filing was made.
