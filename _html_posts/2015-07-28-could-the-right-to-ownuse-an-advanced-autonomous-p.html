--- title: '"Could the right to own/use an advanced autonomous personal weapon system (like a hovering, armed drone, for instance) be protected by the second amendment?"' date: '2015-07-28' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [Drones are flying aircraft and are regulated by the FAA.
Current FAA regulations prohibit armed civilian aircraft of any kind,
which would by default include drones.\
\
If we want to allow autonomous personal flying self defense systems, we
will have to process a modification to current FAA
regulations.]
