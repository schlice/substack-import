--- title: '"Is there a legal case in support of SexBnB, an online marketplace to buy and sell sex?"' date: '2015-08-15' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [The website operator will likely have to claim immunity
under Section 230 of the Communications Decency Act.\
\
The website is responsible for the content it authors. But under Section
230, website operators have \"safe harbor\" protections for third-party
activity in most cases. Unless the website is creating or \"developing\"
the illegal transaction, it\'s likely that it will be
protected.]
