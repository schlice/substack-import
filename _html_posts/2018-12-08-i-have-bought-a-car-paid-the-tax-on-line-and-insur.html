**I have bought a car, paid the tax on line and insured it, but it is
not registered in my name, can I still drive it?**

(US answer)

Yes, you can. You are not required to be the registered owner of the car
in order to drive the car. So long as you have the permission of the
registered owner, you can drive the car.
