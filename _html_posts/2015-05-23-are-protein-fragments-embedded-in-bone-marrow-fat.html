Content: [While there are many possible theories about what causes

\
Protein fragments in bone marrow fat is not one I have heard of being
investigated yet. We know that there is a genetic predisposition to
getting it, along with a lack of Vitamin D and an infectious trigger
that sets the whole thing in motion. We still don\'t know what the
infectious trigger is. Epstein-Barr is one possibility. There are many
that are being investigated.]
