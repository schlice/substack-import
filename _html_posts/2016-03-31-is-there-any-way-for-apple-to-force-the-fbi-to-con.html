--- title: '"Is there any way for Apple to force the FBI to continue the case to get a precedent?"' date: '2016-03-31' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Short answer: no.

Longer answer: A respondent cannot force a plaintiff to continue to
press charges. If a plaintiff chooses to drop the charges, the judge
dismisses the case. The respondent does not have the option of saying
\"No, I don\'t want the case against me dismissed.\"
