--- title: '"How are Schwann cells affected in multiple sclerosis patients? How is this condition treated?"' date: '2016-06-02' tags: - health-autoimmune layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Schwann cells produce myelin in the Peripheral Nervous System (PNS).
Oligodendrocytes produce myelin in the Central Nervous System
(CNS).

MS affects the CNS, not the PNS. MS affects myelin and oligodendrocytes,
not Schwann cells.
