--- title: '"Does owning a gun increase aggression or is it the personality type and anger of a gun owner that would draw them to use violence?"' date: '2015-10-02' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [Owning a gun does not increase one\'s aggression any more
than owning a fork increases one\'s hunger or owning a pencil increases
one\'s proclivity to write a letter.\
\
The person uses the tool to commit an act. The tool does
not force the person to commit an act. Nor does the tool
make one more or less likely to commit an act.]
