--- title: '"Assault & Battery: What can be done in order to see the criminal held accountable?"' date: '2015-09-30' tags: - policy-analysis layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [It depends on your definition of \"held accountable.\"\
\
If \"held accountable\" means \"retribution,\" you may not be satisfied.
The court may not rule against the criminal, in which case you won\'t
get your \"pound of flesh.\"\
\
If \"held accountable\" means \"judged fairly by the court,\" then the
adjudication the criminal receives should be enough to see that the
person is \"held accountable.\"]
