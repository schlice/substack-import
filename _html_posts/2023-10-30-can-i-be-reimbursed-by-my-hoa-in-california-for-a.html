--- title: '"Can I be reimbursed by my HOA in California for a Violation disclosure for a patio cover that was removed according to their instruction prior to closing escrow only to find out later that it was approved by the HOA less than 6 months earlier?"' date: '2023-10-30' tags: - law - homeowners-assn layout: post --- Quora Answer

Content: 

Certainly! But I think you may be unclear on the method by which you
obtain your "reimbursement."

Yeah, that's right, lawsuit. Retain an attorney and "request" that they
refund the money you sunk into removing the cover that they approved,
and when they don't pay (they won't), file a lawsuit.

In other words, there's no statutory way to be "reimbursed." If you were
monetarily damaged by another's incompetence, the remedy is a lawsuit.

Good luck.
