--- title: '"How does my community get rid of our second HOA, in Florida? The first HOA is for the common areas, the separate second HOA controls what people can do with their house."' date: '2025-03-17' tags: - law - homeowners-assn layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Sounds like a pretty unique situation, even for Florida.

You will likely need to consult with a real estate attorney who
specializes in HOA law. Dissolving any HOA is a complex, and not free
thing to do. It also involves assent by a supermajority of the
homeowners who are affected.
