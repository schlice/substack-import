--- title: '"Is it actually illegal to collect rainwater in Colorado?"' date: '2015-08-15' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [It is.\
\
Unless you have a well.\
\
In that case, the law says that you can collect your own rainwater,
because you were going to pump it from the ground anyway.\
\
If you run off of a city water system, it is technically illegal to
collect rainwater yourself. I don\'t suspect someone is going to come
searching your backyard for water tanks, though.]
