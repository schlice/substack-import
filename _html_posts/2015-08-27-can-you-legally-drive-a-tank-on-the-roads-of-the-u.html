--- title: '"Can you legally drive a tank on the roads of the US?"' date: '2015-08-27' tags: - environment layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [If you can get license plates for it in your state, you
can legally drive it on the roads.\
\
Check with your state to see what restrictions on licensing are placed
on vehicles. Usually the gross vehicle weight is the limiting factor,
but there are others, such as width, treads, height, emissions,
etc.]
