So why are we discussing this in a question about hacking apps?

The apps themselves are not illegal. Creating an app which can hack
other things is an exercise in programming, nothing more.

*Using that app could be illegal*.

It could be legal if you use it against your own systems.

It could be legal if you use it against a system you were asked to
analyze for security holes.

But it would probably be illegal if you use it to try to access a site
that you did not have authorization to access.

It's not about the *tool*. It's about *how you use the tool.*
