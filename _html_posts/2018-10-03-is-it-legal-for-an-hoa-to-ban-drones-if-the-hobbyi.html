**Is it legal for an HOA to ban drones if the hobbyist is complying with
federal laws?**

Yes, it is legal for an HOA to ban drones.

HOAs have broad authority over the areas which they control. They can
absolutely pass a Rule change which bans the use of drones **from within
the neighborhood. This means that you can't take off or land in the
neighborhood.**

Note the bolded statement above. If you are outside the neighborhood and
you *overfly* the neighborhood, this is legal, so long as you comply
with all other local regulations (which usually follow FAA regulations)
for drone flight. The FAA controls airspace above your house, not the
HOA.
