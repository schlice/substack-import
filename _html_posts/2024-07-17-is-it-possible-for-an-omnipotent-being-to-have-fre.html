--- title: '"Is it possible for an omnipotent being to have free will and choose not to be omnipotent?"' date: '2024-07-17' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Yes, an omnipotent being has free will. No, this being cannot choose to
not be omnipotent.

This would require said omnipotent being to become something that it is
not, something that violates its very nature, which it cannot do.
