Content: 

There are several formats you can use. The key, though, is having an
application that can parse the image and get information from it, in
your case, a URL.

[[This
website](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=d33a00&url=http://www.scandit.com/types-barcodes-choosing-right-barcode/){.external_link
rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank"
onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);"
qt-tooltip="scandit.com"}]{.qlink_container} has a great tutorial of the
different types of barcodes available and what they hold. Don't forget
that you need to have a compatible app to read whatever barcode you want
to produce.
