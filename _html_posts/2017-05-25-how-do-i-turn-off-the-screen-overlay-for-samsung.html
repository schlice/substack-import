--- title: '"How do I turn off the screen overlay for Samsung?"' date: '2017-05-25' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

- Swipe down from the top of the screen and select the gear icon, this
  will take you into Settings.
- Select Applications
- Select Application Manager
- Click MORE in the upper-right hand portion of the screen.
- Select "Apps that can appear on top"
- De-select any apps that you no longer wish to allow permission to draw
  over other apps.
