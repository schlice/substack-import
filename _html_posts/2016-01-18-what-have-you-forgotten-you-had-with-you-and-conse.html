finds it.

By the way, these wallets are great if you, like me, sit on your leather
wallet and often break credit cards because the wallet bends.

Long story short, I ping going through the magnetometer. I recognize my
wallet is still on me, so thinking my wallet pinged the machine, I send
it through the X-ray, at which time the metal blade of the knife becomes
fully visible on the screen. It was the knife blade that pinged the
magnetometer, not the aluminum wallet.

Anyway, I told them to throw it away, because it would have cost me more
to send it home than I paid for it (which was nothing).
