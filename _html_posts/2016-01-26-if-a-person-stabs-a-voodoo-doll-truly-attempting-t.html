--- title: '"If a person stabs a voodoo doll truly attempting to kill someone, should that person be charged with attempted murder?"' date: '2016-01-26' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [To be charged with an attempted crime, a suspect must take
a *substantial step* towards the commission of that crime. So unless
it\'s illegal to kill a doll, using a voodoo doll does not amount to
attempted murder against a person.]
