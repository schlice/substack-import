Answer









Content: [Understand that not all disabilities are visible.\
\
Also understand that the law doesn\'t require that a service dog wear a
vest, or that a person with a service animal have a visible disability.\
\
Business owners are allowed to ask only two questions of someone with an
animal in a business:\
\
]

- Is that a service animal specially trained to treat a recognized
  disability under the Americans with Disabilities Act?
- What is this animal trained to do?

\
That\'s it. Beyond that, if someone has a service animal with them, they
cannot be restricted from any public area in which people are allowed to
be.
