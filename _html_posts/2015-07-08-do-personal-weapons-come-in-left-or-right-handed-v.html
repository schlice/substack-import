--- title: '"Do personal weapons come in left or right handed variations?"' date: '2015-07-08' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [Most semi-auto pistols have controls that, if they are
one-sided, can be reversed for left-handed shooters. Most of their
safeties go clear through the pistol and can be operated by either
hand.\
\
And as has already been noted, rifles have adjustments for left-handed
shooters.]
