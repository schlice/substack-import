--- title: '"What is unacceptable about police shooting on an armed criminal of any race?"' date: '2015-08-20' tags: - law-enforcement - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [It is only illegal for a police officer to shoot someone
(anyone, armed or unarmed) if the officer does not have legal cause to
do so.\
\
Legal cause would generally involve fear for his life or physical
safety, or fear for the safety of the public. These are all variable
based on the facts of the situation at hand.\
\
Military police can shoot people for more reasons, like fleeing with
dangerous or classified materials.]
