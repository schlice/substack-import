--- title: '"Can you be punished for insulting somebody else?"' date: '2015-10-02' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [If your speech incites a riot, you can be charged with
that offense. If you use slanderous speech which causes harm to another
person, you can be charged with that offense.\
\
But if you simply insult someone (\"Hey, you\'re ugly\"), that\'s not
against the law in the United States, as it is protected by the First
Amendment.]
