--- title: '"Is there a way to contact the police through the internet in an emergency?"' date: '2015-09-12' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [It\'s up to the individual department.\
\
The vast majority of police departments only maintain public affairs
presences online. Some now allow you to report very basic crimes online
(i.e. vandalism), but for the most part, as is true in most industries
these days, you will get your fastest and best response over the
phone.]
