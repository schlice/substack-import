You're a married Christian. The end.

If you are a *true* Christian, you already know that marriage is
forever.

> So they are no longer two but one flesh. What therefore God has joined
> together, let not man separate. --- Matthew 19:6 ESV

You may *want* to marry a Hindu, but you are married already, and that's
forever.

So I can help you. Recommit yourself to your current spouse. Do a
marriage retreat. Take a vacation with just your spouse. Reconnect with
that person.

And finally, remember that you made a vow. How well people should
trust you is rightly dependent on how well you keep your promises. Be
the kind of person who people would want to trust.
