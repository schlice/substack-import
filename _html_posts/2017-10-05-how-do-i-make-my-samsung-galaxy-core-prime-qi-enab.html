--- title: '"How do I make my Samsung Galaxy Core Prime QI enabled?"' date: '2017-10-05' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

In order to Qi-enable a device which is not natively Qi-enabled, you
must add an external Qi adapter, usually part of a phone case. The case
and the Qi adapter will plug in to your USB port on the bottom of the
phone. You can then set your phone onto a Qi charging pad and it will
work.
