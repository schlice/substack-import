--- title: '"In what percentage of US states is it legal to use tasers for personal protection?"' date: '2016-05-19' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

The answer is 90%, as 45 out of 50 states allow people to own TASER
devices. Not all states allow people to carry them, and some cities have
tighter restrictions than the state as a whole, so always check your
local laws before you carry.
