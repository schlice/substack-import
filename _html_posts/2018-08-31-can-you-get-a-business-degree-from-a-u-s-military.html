**Can you get a business degree from a U. S. Military Academy? If so,
why do they offer it?**

They do not offer business degrees from USAFA, but they do offer
management degrees.

Remember that all of the degree programs are designed to meet the needs
of Air Force officers, so Management is more appropriate than Business.
