--- title: '"What is the living philosophy of your life?"' date: '2015-07-08' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [\"In three words, I can sum up everything I\'ve learned
about life \-- it goes on.\" \-- Robert Frost\
\
Life goes on. There will be ups and downs, good times and bad times. You
have to count on them all happening, because they will.\
\
But one thing is for certain through all of the ups and downs: life goes
on. So live your life. Don\'t dwell on the downs or the ups. Just
live.]
