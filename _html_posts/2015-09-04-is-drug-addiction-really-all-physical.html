The physical addiction symptoms are actually resident in the brain. The
symptoms manifest in the body, but ultimately the problem is one of the
brain, not of the body.\
\
Yes, addiction is a physical disease. \"Disease\" means an improper
functioning of a body system, and addiction is an improper functioning
of the pleasure/response feedback loop system in the brain.\
\
The improper functioning of this system feeds the physical cravings.\
