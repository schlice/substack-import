--- title: '"Who had sex for god to be created, and who had sex to create the people who had sex for god to be created, and so on?"' date: '2023-03-06' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

God was not created. God is *eternal*, which means He has no
beginning, and no end. He just *is*. He is the eternal First Cause.

It is necessary that He be eternal, because if He is not (i.e. He was
created), then your question would be logical, as something or someone
would have needed to create Him.
