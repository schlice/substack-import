--- title: '"A stranger pulled a knife on me and my son over a misunderstanding. Can I get him arrested?"' date: '2017-10-13' tags: - law-enforcement - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

The charge in most jurisdictions is "brandishing a weapon," but if you
didn't call the police right away, I doubt they will charge the
individual now. You can always feel free to call the police department
(non-emergency number, please) and ask to speak to an officer about the
situation.

You can also sue for assault, as brandishing a weapon is also an assault
in tort. Check with a lawyer on this one.
