--- title: '"Can a juvenile who is forced to commit murder by an adult be prosecuted?"' date: '2017-07-07' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

I dispute the fact that anyone can be "forced" to commit murder.

Please explain how you can take a life with forethought and malice,
against your will. I find it hard to believe that you can meet the
requirements of the charge of murder when you are being compelled to
act.
