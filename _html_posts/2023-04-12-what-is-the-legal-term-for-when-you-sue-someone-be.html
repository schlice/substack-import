--- title: '"What is the legal term for when you sue someone because their product was defective and caused an accident/injury to yourself or others?"' date: '2023-04-12' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

The legal term is a product liability claim.

A consumer good that is deemed defective gives rise to a claim for
damages when it results in harm or illness. A victim of a product defect
has the right to file a lawsuit to receive compensation for their
medical expenses, lost wages, and other damages.
