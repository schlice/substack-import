**How can my employer force the vaccine on me or fire me? I like my job
and don't have any skills or a degree. I live in Texas and I have worked
there for five years at a senior retirement home.**

Texas is a "right to work" state, and their retaliation laws are weak.
They have anti-retaliation laws for discrimination and for very specific
federal law violations, but they do not have anti-retaliation law
regarding failing to participate in a violation of federal law. While
forcing someone to get a vaccine or face termination would be grounds
for a wrongful termination lawsuit, retaliation for failing to
participate in a violation of federal law is not protected under Texas'
anti-retaliation law.
