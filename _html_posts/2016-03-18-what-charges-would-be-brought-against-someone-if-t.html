--- title: '"What charges would be brought against someone if they burned down an entire library?"' date: '2016-03-18' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Some of the charges which could be brought:

- Arson
- Destruction of government property (since many libraries are owned by
  local government, but not all, and many libraries contain repositories
  of government documentation)
