--- title: '"Can the Federal government protect law enforcement officers at all levels from persecution by their superiors for enforcing the Federal immigration law?"' date: '2017-11-08' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Short answer, no.

Local law enforcement cannot enforce Federal law, just as Federal law
enforcement cannot enforce state law.

It is not local law enforcement's responsibility to enforce Federal
immigration law.
