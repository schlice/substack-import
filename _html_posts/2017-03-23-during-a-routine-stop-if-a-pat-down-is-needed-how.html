--- title: '"During a routine stop, if a pat down is needed, how does law enforcement check the bra of a female? Is this done at the jail, or are they trained in a way that would not be invasive?"' date: '2017-03-23' tags: - law-enforcement - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

If a same-sex officer cannot be brought to the scene within a reasonable
timeframe, we can use the back of the hand to search any sensitive
areas.

The back of the hand is still sensitive enough to feel if anything
besides body is inside the undergarments, and it's exceedingly difficult
to be accused of groping someone with the back of your hand.
