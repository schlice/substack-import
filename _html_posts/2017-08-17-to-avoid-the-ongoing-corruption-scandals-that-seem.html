--- title: '"To avoid the ongoing corruption scandals that seem to dog police forces everywhere, would it be wise for police forces to evaluate candidates not only on physique and intellect, but also their moral character, through some situational testing?"' date: '2017-08-17' tags: - veteran-benefits - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Psychological testing already takes ethical decision-making into
account. Additional testing would have to show that there is marginal
benefit for the cost, and to date, additional testing has not been shown
to provide a significant enough marginal benefit for the amount of time
and cost it would add to the pre-employment screening process.

Basically, the testing they have is sufficient to screen candidates to
the level they deem is appropriate.
