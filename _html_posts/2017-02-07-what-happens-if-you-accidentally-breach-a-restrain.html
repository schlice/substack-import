--- title: '"What happens if you accidentally breach a restraining order?"' date: '2017-02-07' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Restraining orders bar you from knowingly coming within a certain
distance (when a distance is specified) to a protected person.

Encountering a protected person accidentally is only a problem if the
restrained person does not immediately conclude his or her business and
move the appropriate distance away.
