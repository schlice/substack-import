--- title: '"Do I have to clean up my act before God can forgive me?"' date: '2022-08-09' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

No.

God will accept you right now, as you are. Broken, battered, and at
the end of our rope, not perfect and shiny.

> But God demonstrates his own love for us in this: While we were still
> sinners, Christ died for us.\
> --- Romans 5:8 NIV

Jesus didn't come to heal the *well*, he came to heal the *sick*. And
all you have to do is repent and believe.

It's as simple as ABC:

- Admit to God (and to yourself) that you are a sinner and without
  him you are lost.
- Believe that Christ died as a penal substitute for your sins
- Confess that Jesus Christ is your Lord and savior, and Commit
  to turn away from your old life of sin.

AFTER you are saved, God will begin the long work of helping you
clean up your act. It's a lifelong process called *sanctification*. With
each passing day, the old ways will fade away, and you'll learn a new
way to live. It takes effort, but keep it up, because God will never,
ever give up on you.
