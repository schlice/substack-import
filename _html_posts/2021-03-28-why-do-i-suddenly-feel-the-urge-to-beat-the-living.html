**Why do I suddenly feel the urge to beat the living crap out of
someone?**

Because you're a human being. Feeling emotions is normal. Feeling anger
is normal. Wanting to take out your aggression on someone is also
normal.

What *isn't* normal is having this feeling *all of the time* or
*most of the time*.

If you feel this occasionally, and you're able to control yourself,
congratulations, you're a human. If you feel this all the time, seek
help.
