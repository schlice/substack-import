For the uninitiated, what we're actually discussing here is a
three-minute video called *Meet Baby Olivia.* It is an *animated* video
depicting the fertilization of an egg and the development of a baby.

The video is not *offensive*, it is controversial.

First, it is controversial because it was produced by *Live Action*, the
company founded by Lila Rose. Rose became famous when she went
undercover to Planned Parenthood facilities exposing some activity which
they claim they did not participate in. She and her organization have
become very polarizing since then.

Secondly, some of the things the video talks about are disputed. For
example, the video references fetal "heartbeat" and this is disputed. At
six weeks, the embryo isn't yet a fetus and doesn't have a heart, so
some embryologists object to the term "fetal heartbeat."

The video also describes the animated figure\'s motion and actions with
words like "playing," "exploring,\" "sighing" and making "speaking
movements." Some doctors say that those words assign human traits and
properties to a fetus that are more sophisticated than medicine can
prove. There are signals that the brain is developing and functioning,
but the opposing doctors claim that the video\'s language implies a
"level of intention" in the fetus' actions that cannot currently be
proven.
