Answer









Content: [Recipes can\'t be patented, trademarked, or copyrighted.
So if you happen to have the ingredient list and the recipe, you can use
it to make your own product even if a competitor uses 100% of the same
products.\
\
Recipes can be considered a \"trade secret\" if it is protected as
such.]
