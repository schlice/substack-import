My suggestion: don't work so hard to get a "smart TV." Get a great TV
with a great display and plug smart things into it.

Plug in a game console, or plug in a Chromecast, or a Kindle Fire TV
Stick, or an Apple TV, or a Roku, or any number of similar smart
devices. You will save more in the long run, and you will have better
support for apps on your device than you will on a "smart TV."
