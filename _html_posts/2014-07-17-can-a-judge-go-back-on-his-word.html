Answer








Content: [Anyone can go back on their word. A judge\'s \"word\" is
as good as anyone else\'s. But if your judge\'s \"word\" wasn\'t in
writing somewhere, as in a ruling dropping your charges contingent on a
clean record for the next 60 days, then you\'re SOL.]
