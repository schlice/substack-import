--- title: '"How do people believe in God without evidence? Are there any other instances where lack of evidence has led to belief?"' date: '2024-02-26' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Some people may believe without evidence, but most people believe in God
with very good evidence. It's just not the evidence that you're
thinking. It's not science, because you can't use science tools to find
an immaterial God. We have to use other tools to provide the evidence of
God's existence.
