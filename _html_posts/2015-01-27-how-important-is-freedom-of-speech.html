--- title: '"How important is freedom of speech?"' date: '2015-01-27' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [A2A, and this specifically addresses the United States\
\
When the colonists were settling in the Americas, they were not allowed
to speak out against the king or to question his policies.\
\
As the new government was being formed, the people wanted to ensure that
they retained the right to always voice their opinions, especially about
the government. They wanted to ensure that the government could not tie
their hands like the king did.]
