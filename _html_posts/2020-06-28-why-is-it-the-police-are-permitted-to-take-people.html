**Why is it the police are permitted to take people\'s lives? It is not
the job of the police to inflict punishment on people.**

*(US answer only)*

Because in the United States, police officers have the same right to
self-defense as any citizen does.

Despite what you see in the media, police in general are not just out
there indiscriminately killing people. Police, on average, kill about
1000 people per year, the vast majority of those people are armed, and
nearly all of them pose a threat to the officer of death or grave bodily
harm.

Remember, just because a person is not armed with a weapon does not mean
that they do not pose a risk of death or grave bodily harm to the
officer.
