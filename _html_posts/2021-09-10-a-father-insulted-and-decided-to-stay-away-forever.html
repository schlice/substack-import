He has *a right* to not talk to his daughter again.

*But he bases his decision on fear-mongering rather than science, and
that is incredibly foolish.* He ignores her individuality and her right
to choose for her own body what is best, instead imposing his own
morality over hers. This is incredibly insensitive and foolish. In other
words, the father is a complete moron.

The CDC doesn't help if you ask them, because they use the 18--29 age
group range as the "reference range." This is what happens when your
"scientific study" doesn't have a "control group" to study against.

This isn't "science." It's "educated guesswork." But you can at least
see that the risk scales upward the older you get.

In short, it is *exceedingly unlikely* that a normal, healthy 20-year
old would even be hospitalized, much less die, from COVID infection
(barring any comorbidities, of course). And knowing that there are risks
for 20-year olds taking the vaccine that older adults do not face also
should rightly weigh on the mind of the 20-year old and should
influence their decision about whether or not to vaccinate.

**TL;DR: He can do whatever he wants with his daughter, but he is
terribly foolish to do so. The science does not agree with his
decision.**
