--- title: '"Why do Americans ride their car or bike slowly?"' date: '2016-07-07' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

In the United States, we have very few places which allow speeds over
120 kph (roughly 75 mph). Most places don't allow speeds that fast.

Watch any episodes of *Top Gear* where the presenters shot clips in the
USA, and you'll see them lamenting about how they can't go over 55 mph
on some roads (roughly 88 kph).
