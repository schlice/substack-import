--- title: '"Can I have LASIK surgery on my eyes if I have multiple sclerosis?"' date: '2017-01-25' tags: - health-autoimmune layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

LASIK surgery does not impact any area of the eye affected by MS, so you
should be fine. As with any medical procedure, you should always check
with your neurologist first.
