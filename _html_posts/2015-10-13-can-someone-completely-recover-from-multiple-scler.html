--- title: '"Can someone completely recover from multiple sclerosis ever?"' date: '2015-10-13' tags: - health-autoimmune layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [There is no cure for multiple sclerosis. So the short
answer is no, you cannot recover from multiple sclerosis.\
\
Some people have achieved remission to the point where it\'s nearly
impossible to tell that they have MS in the first place. Some people
have a form of MS called \"benign MS\" which exhibits one symptom and
then goes into remission, for as many as 10 years or more.\
\
However, no one has ever \"completely recovered\" from
MS.]
