DNA is a message. It's what we call *specified complexity.* DNA contains
the equivalent of 1,000 encyclopedias of specific information, **in the
smallest single-celled organism.**

Science has shown us that messages are only created by intelligence.
Messages are not created by random chemical interactions. Messages only
come from mind.

We know that human beings were intelligently designed.

We know that the universe was not the intelligent designer, because the
universe is not eternal. The universe had a beginning. Therefore, the
universe must have had a *beginner*, or someone to create it. Either the
universe is eternal and uncaused, or the universe had a beginning and a
cause. Can't be both. It's either/or.

Therefore, God must exist.
