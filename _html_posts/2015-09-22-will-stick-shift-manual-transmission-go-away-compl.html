Content: [As long as gas-powered engines remain, manual

a car just isn\'t a car if it doesn\'t have a \"proper gearbox.\" Even
some flappy-paddle manuals are more electronic then electromechanical.
Supercars with flappy-paddle shifters are electromechanical. Mid-level
sedans with \"auto-manual\" transmissions are just electronic; not a
proper gearbox, so to speak.]
