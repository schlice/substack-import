We don\'t have retributive justice in America. That\'s why Justice wears
a blindfold. She only weighs the facts, nothing more. Your desire for
retribution plays no part in her decision.

People would like a retributive justice system here. These people are
the same ones who say \"justice was not served\" when a court finds
someone not guilty, when \"clearly, everyone I know says he was totally
guilty\" and we have to have \"an eye for an eye\" for what he did.

That\'s not justice. That\'s vengeance. Do you want justice, or do
you want revenge? Do you want to take from them what they took from you,
or do you want the *right* person to be convicted?

If you\'re only looking for revenge, it wouldn\'t make much difference
to you if the person might not be guilty. If *you* think he is guilty,
he must be guilty, and the court was obviously biased, or the whole
system is rigged, or something.
