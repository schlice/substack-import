--- title: '"How can you seek justice if you have no rights?"' date: '2017-11-17' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

If you have no rights, then you do not have the right to justice. You
cannot seek justice if you have no rights.

You can only seek whatever passes as personal vengeance to attempt to
"balance the scales" in your own personal system of morality.
