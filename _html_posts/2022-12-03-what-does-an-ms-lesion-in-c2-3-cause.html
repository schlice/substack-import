In general, lesions on your spine cause similar symptoms as lesions in
other areas of your brain and spine: numbness, tingling, weakness, and
balance issues.

In addition, cervical spine lesions can cause loss of sensation in the
shoulders and arms.

Don't forget though, symptoms are unique to each person; not everyone
experiences MS in the same way. Even if you and someone else have
exactly the same lesions, you may still experience different symptoms.
