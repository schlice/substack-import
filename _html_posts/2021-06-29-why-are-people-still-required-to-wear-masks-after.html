You should never be *required* to wear a mask. Masking should be your
*choice*. Period. Whatever your reasons, whether you want to protect
yourself or others, you should be able to choose whether or not you wear
a mask.

The government should never require you to wear a mask.

That said, can a *business* still force customers to wear masks? Yes, of
course they can. Businesses can set whatever requirements they like for
their businesses. If you can't wear a mask because of a disability, they
must provide some form of accommodation, like contactless ordering or
pickup, or they could be in violation of the ADA. But they can still
force you to wear a mask in their establishment.
