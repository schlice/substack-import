The telegraph, replaced by the telephone, now replaced by the cellular
phone.

The candle, replaced by the incandescent light bulb, now replaced by the
LED light bulb.

The abacus, replaced by the calculator, replaced by the computer.

There are a million examples of the advancement of technology rendering
old technology obsolete.
