Is it illegal to sell nudes?

The answer to legal questions is, as always, "it depends."

While it is not illegal to sell pictures of your body, even your naked
body, care must be taken that you do not expose children to those
photos. Therefore, the *way* in which you sell those pictures is the
question, not that you actually do it.

Other things you'll need to consider are your state and local business
licenses (depending on where you live) which require you to register
your business and pay self-employment taxes.

Using a *service* to assist you with those sales is likely the safest
way to accomplish what you want: getting money for your photos without
running afoul of the many laws regarding sales of pornographic
materials.
