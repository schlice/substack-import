--- title: '"Does a posthumous pardon have any real legal effect?"' date: '2022-01-07' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Not really. Most of the benefits of a pardon are conferred to someone
who is alive to have their rights restored.

It's possible that some rights of the next of kin may be restored with a
pardon, but it's unlikely.

Posthumous pardons are mostly symbolic gestures.
