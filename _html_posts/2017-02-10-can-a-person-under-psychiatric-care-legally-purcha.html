--- title: '"Can a person under psychiatric care legally purchase a weapon?"' date: '2017-02-10' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Under current US law, you must be adjudicated by a judge as being
mentally incompetent or having a mental defect or deficiency before your
mental state will bar you from purchasing a weapon.

Just seeing a psychiatrist is not enough to flag you in NICS as a person
who is prevented from purchasing a weapon.

That bar was set high following deinstitutionalization.
