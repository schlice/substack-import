**Can you bring drinks on a plane if you drink it right in front of the
TSA agent\'s face to prove that it\'s a drink?**

First of all, the problem isn't bringing drinks on a plane. The problem
is bringing drinks through the security checkpoint. You can bring drinks
on a plane; you just have to buy them inside the sterile area.

Wanna hear the cool logic about why not?

If I'm a suicide bomber, and I want to use a liquid bomb, I'll be happy
to drink some of the liquid in front of you.

Because I'm going to die in a horrible mid-air explosion anyway. So
what's a little bit of poison before I die?

If I'm truly committed to the cause, I'd be *happy* to drink poison in
front of you.

So that's the logic.

But here's the good news: you can bring *empty bottles* through the
security checkpoint and fill them once you get inside. Many large
airports have "bottle stations" which deliver filtered water straight
into your bottle.
