--- title: '"Can state flags fly to the same height to the flag of United States?"' date: '2015-09-14' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [It depends on the configuration of flags. According to US
Flag Code (which is part of Federal law), in a group of flagpoles, the
US flag has to be farthest right (from the perspective of the building
that they adorn, but they can all be the same height).\
\
]

If you have a group of flagpoles and the center pole is higher than the
others, the US flag must fly on the highest pole, and the other flags
can be distributed around it.\
