Content: [In short, nothing.\
\
Specifically, the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) says that
disabled persons are not required to provide proof of disability in
order to receive accommodation.\
\
In order to answer your question more thoroughly, I have to know a
little more about specifically what you think you need to prove, and to
