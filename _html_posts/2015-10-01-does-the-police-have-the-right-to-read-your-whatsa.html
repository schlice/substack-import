--- title: '"Does the police have the right to read your whatsapp messages from the whatsappserver itself, in case of suspicion?"' date: '2015-10-01' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [The police can subpoena whatever data they reasonably
presume contains evidence required in an investigation. They need a
*warrant* to do this, and the warrant is delivered to the company which
then must turn over whatever it has.\
\
If it\'s like WhatsApp, and they don\'t retain your messages, there\'s
nothing to turn over.]
