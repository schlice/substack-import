--- title: '"Is there any chance that Trump will be able to get the 14th Amendment changed?"' date: '2017-02-21' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Trump can *lobby* to change the 14th amendment, but he cannot do it
himself. He still needs Congress and the states to change an amendment,
just like the constitution demands. If enough people believe as he does,
the amendment will be changed by passing a new amendment.
