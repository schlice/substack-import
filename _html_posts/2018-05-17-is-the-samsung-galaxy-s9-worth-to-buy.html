enamored with its camera. In fact, he has convinced several people I
know to get the Pixel 2 because of that outstanding camera. But as a
test, he started carrying and using the S9 (actually, the S9+, but the
comparison is similar). He then did a video comparing the S9+ to the
Pixel 2 XL. He went into the video without a preconception about which
device he was going to choose.
