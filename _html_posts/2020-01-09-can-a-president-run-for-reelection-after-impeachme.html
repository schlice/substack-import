Can an unresolved impeached US president run for reelection?

The only time a President cannot run for re-election is after the Senate
votes to remove the President from office by a supermajority of Senators
voting, AND THEN votes to not allow the impeached and removed
President to hold Federal office again (by simple majority).

Until that happens, the President can run for re-election and can be
re-elected.
