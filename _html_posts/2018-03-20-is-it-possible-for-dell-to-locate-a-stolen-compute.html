**Is it possible for Dell to locate a stolen computer if you have the
serial number/ service tag number by pinging it on the internet
somehow?**

Sorry to hear about the laptop, but the short answer is no.

Unless you installed a specific anti-theft application (like [[LoJack
for
Laptops](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=40097c&url=https://www.dell.com/content/topics/segtopic.aspx/lojack?c%3Dus%26cs%3D19%26l%3Den%26s%3Ddhs){.external_link
rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank"
onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);"
qt-tooltip="dell.com"}]{.qlink_container}) when you bought your laptop,
Dell cannot directly trace your laptop through the Service Tag or Serial
Number.

You should first notify the police that your laptop was stolen, then use
[[this
link](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=264aaf&url=http://www.dell.com/support/incidents-online/us/en/4/email/stolen){.external_link
rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank"
onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);"
qt-tooltip="dell.com"}]{.qlink_container} to notify Dell that your
laptop was stolen. This way, Dell can work with law enforcement when
they contact Dell for information regarding your laptop.
