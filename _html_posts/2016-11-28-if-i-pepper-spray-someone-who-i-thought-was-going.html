--- title: '"If I pepper spray someone who I thought was going to attack me, will I get arrested?"' date: '2016-11-28' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Short answer: yes.

Longer answer: You will most likely be attested. You may get convicted.
Preemptive defense is not normally permitted (it\'s an assault),
unless you can clearly articulate that you were in fear for your
safety. It will be up to a jury to decide if your defensive action was
reasonable, given that you were not actually attacked.
