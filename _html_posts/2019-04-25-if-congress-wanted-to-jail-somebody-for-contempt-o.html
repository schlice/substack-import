--- title: '"If Congress wanted to jail somebody for contempt of congress, which police force could they use to arrest him?"' date: '2019-04-25' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Yes, Congress can request that persons be incarcerated, as the other
answers have stated. However, this authority does have limits. Congress
must demonstrate that the testimony of the person in question will aid
in its ability to create laws. If not, the original contempt charge
would be invalid.
