--- title: '"If I took a flight to Florida from Illinois, and turned 18 while I was there, would my parental figure still be able to make me come back to Illinois?"' date: '2021-06-21' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Once you are 18, you are legally an adult. If you're already out of the
state, no one can make you go back.

\

Original question:

If I took a flight to Florida from Illinois, and turned 18 while I was
there, would my parental figure still be able to make me come back to
Illinois?
