--- title: '"Should I call the police to report again the man who threatened to stab me?"' date: '2015-08-09' tags: - policy-analysis - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [If you feel you are in danger, or you fear for your life,
you should call 911. You will have to make an official statement and you
may have to testify in court, but you are within your rights to call the
police if you fear for your safety.]
