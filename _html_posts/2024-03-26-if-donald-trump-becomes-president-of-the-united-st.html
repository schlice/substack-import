interview](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=1ff710&url=https://www.israelhayom.com/2024/03/25/trump-to-israel-hayom-only-a-fool-would-have-not-acted-like-israel-on-oct-7/){.external_link

conflict:

> He \Biden\ can\'t talk. He\'s a very dumb person. He\'s a dumb

> you look at people that were in other administrations with him, they

Trump then noted that the disinformation campaign being waged by Hamas
is actually hurting Israel's reputation, and he recommends that they
conclude the conflict:

> You have to finish up your war. To finish it up. You gotta get it
> done. And, I am sure you will do that. And we gotta get to peace, we
> can\'t have this going on. And I will say, Israel has to be very
> careful, because you\'re losing a lot of the world, you\'re losing a
> lot of support, you have to finish up, you have to get the job done.
> And you have to get on to peace, to get on to a normal life for
