And as I mentioned earlier, I\'m not referring to photos taken in an
ostensibly public place but where people have a reasonable expectation
of privacy. I gave the example of the public bathroom. The room is open
to the public, so it is a public space. But there is a reasonable
expectation of privacy in a bathroom, so you can\'t take pictures inside
a bathroom and publish them.\
\
Similarly, there is a reasonable expectation of privacy inside a public
AA meeting, and so you can\'t take pictures there
either.]
