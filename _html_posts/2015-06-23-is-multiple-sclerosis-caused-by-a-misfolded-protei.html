--- title: '"Is multiple sclerosis caused by a misfolded protein in the CD5 cell surface receptor which prevents apoptosis of virally damaged T and B cells?"' date: '2015-06-23' tags: - health-autoimmune layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [We don\'t know what causes Multiple Sclerosis, so saying
that misfolded proteins is the cause of MS is incorrect.\
\
The answer is \"it could be.\"\
\
We know there is a genetic component, we know there is a hormonal
component, we know that there is an infectious trigger that starts the
disease process. We know that vitamin D has a prophylactic effect.\
\
But we do not know the cause.]
