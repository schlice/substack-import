Answer









Content: [Until you can make an ARM processor that processes as fast
as an Intel or an AMD processor, and use the same amount of battery,
no.\
\
The major difference between phones/tablets and laptops is the
processor. Most phones/tablets run some form of ARM processor, while
laptops usually run some form of Intel or AMD processor. The difference
in computing power is currently pretty large.]
