--- title: '"Are psychologists and other mental health professionals required or allowed to keep dangerous information from authority under client privileges?"' date: '2015-06-13' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [Yes, communications between all healthcare professionals
and their patients are covered by \"doctor-patient privilege.\"\
\
The point at which that privilege no longer applies is when the
professional learns that the patient may be a danger to himself or to
others. At this point, the exact opposite becomes the case: the
professional now has a *professional obligation* to contact law
enforcement and/or ensure the patient is delivered safely to a hospital
inpatient setting for emergency mental health
intervention.]
