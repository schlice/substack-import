Answer









Content: [It\'s not against the law to pass an unconstitutional law.
That\'s why the Supreme Court exists: to make sure laws are indeed
constitutional.\
\
Plus, there is a lot of gray area around Constitutionality of a law.\
\
Finally, there are actually consequences to passing unconstitutional
laws. If voters actually cared about that stuff, their legislators
wouldn\'t be re-elected.]
