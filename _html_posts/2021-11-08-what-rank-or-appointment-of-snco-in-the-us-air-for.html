Technical Sergeant (TSgt) - E-6

It's actually not five chevrons. It's three *stripes* and two *rockers*,
for a total of 5 lines. The rank insignia looks like this:

This position is a senior Non-Commissioned Officer (NCO). He or she is
technically proficient, and has been working at his job for several
years. The average time in service before a Staff Sergeant is promoted
to Technical Sergeant is 12 years. The TSgt is usually a shift lead or a
shift supervisor, with several lower-grade Airmen and NCOs who work for
him.

The next rank above this is Master Sergeant (MSgt), and the rank
insignia would add a single *chevron* over the top:
