--- title: '"If a bike rack is going to block my license plate, is a photo copy of the plate on the back window a viable alternative that is acceptable to police and highway patrol?"' date: '2017-08-12' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Of course, every jurisdiction is different, but in general, photocopies
of the license plate are not valid. You can either find a rack that has
a spot for a license plate, or find a bracket that attaches your license
plate to your bike rack. You must relocate the plate so that it is
visible.
