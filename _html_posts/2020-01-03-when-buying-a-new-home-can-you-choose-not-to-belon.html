When buying a new home, can you choose not to belong to the HOA?

If the HOA exists when you buy your home, it is mandatory and cannot be
"opted-out." It's built in to the neighborhood.

The only thing you can do to avoid being part of an HOA is to buy a
house that is not part of an HOA.
