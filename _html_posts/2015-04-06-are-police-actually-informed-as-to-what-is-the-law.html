--- title: '"Are police actually informed as to what is the law?"' date: '2015-04-06' tags: - law-enforcement - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [When police go through their respective police academies,
they learn the law, or more specifically, the section of the law dealing
with criminal justice, which is a smaller subset of state law.\
\
Police also receive monthly \"in-service training\" which allows them to
get refreshers on laws as they change.]
