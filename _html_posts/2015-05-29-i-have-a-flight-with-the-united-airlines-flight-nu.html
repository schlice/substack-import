--- title: '"I have a flight with the United Airlines flight number but the flight itself is on another airline. Where do I check in, United or Air Canada?"' date: '2015-05-29' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [Always check in with the carrier who issued the ticket. If
the ticket says \"United Airlines\" on it, that\'s where you check in.
If it says \"Air Canada,\" that\'s where you check in.\
\
Some co-branded flights are indeed operated. But you always check in
with the airline that issued the ticket, regardless of the
co-branding.]
