Content: 

Suicides are never included in *accidentai gun death statistics*
unless someone is intentionally lying to you.

Suicides are included in *All Gun Death* statistics, even though the
gun wound was self-inflicted, when the *intent* of the statistic is to
show incidents in which one person shoots another one.

Suicides are added because 33,000 deaths a year looks like a much bigger
number than 11,000 deaths a year (when you exclude suicide).
