--- title: '"If the US passed gun control laws confiscating 150 million guns, how would these guns be found and removed, and what compensation would be offered to owners for the loss of what had once been legally-owned property?"' date: '2017-07-04' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

You can't just *pass a law* and make guns illegal. Any such law would be
unconstitutional.

You have to start by repealing most of the Bill of Rights first,
primarily the Second, Fourth, and Fifth Amendments.

*Then* you could pass a law.

But *then* you'd have to find someone crazy or stupid enough to try to
enforce that law. Good luck there.

This would clearly be the start of the next Revolution.
