**What is the Democratic vision for America's future? Is it modifying or
overturning the 1st Amendment, the 2nd amendment, obliterating women's
rights, MLK's vision of the color-blind character & rewarding for risk?
I'm obviously missing the end game.**

mouth.

of America is, simply look at the party platform.
