Content: [Sure. Law enforcement does this by increasing the amount

\

arrest \"unnecessary?\" And how exactly can you arrest someone if they
don\'t break the law? I suppose the officer could fabricate a charge,
but you can\'t really prevent that, just like you can\'t prevent
citizens from breaking the law. You can only bring them to justice once
they do.]
