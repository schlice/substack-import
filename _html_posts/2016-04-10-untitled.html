The intent of the phrase was not to prevent *any* law that has anything
to do with religion, or a strict separation between church and state
would not have been something needed to be defined later on, it would
have been inbuilt. The intent was to prevent the establishment of a
state religion, in relation to the way the Church of England was
established as a state religion, and other churches went underground.
