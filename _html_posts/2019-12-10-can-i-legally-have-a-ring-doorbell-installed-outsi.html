**Can I legally have a ring doorbell installed outside of my apartment
door if I have other neighbors?**

Yes you can! If you can look out of your peephole and see your
neighbors, you can install a Ring cam and see them, too.

But I wouldn't install a standard Ring cam, because it risks damaging
the door or siding. You'd have to get permission for that, and there's
no guarantee they'd let you.

IMPORTANT NOTE: As always, check your lease agreement to see what is
allowed and what is not. Most lease agreements allow you to make
modifications as long as you can revert the changes when you leave, and
the modification does not damage the facility or you will be liable for
the repair cost. In my last complex, we could change out our deadbolt
and replace it with a smart deadbolt if we chose, but we'd have to put
it back when we were done, and the landlord would still need a key. We
could also change out our peephole if we wanted to, with the same
caveats. Our maintenance guys were cool too; when I changed out my
peephole, the door paint was off underneath. They gave me a small
container (less than a pint) of door-colored paint to touch up the spots
around the original door knocker/peephole that I removed. No damage,
