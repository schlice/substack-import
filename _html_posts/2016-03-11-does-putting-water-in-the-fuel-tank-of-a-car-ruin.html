--- title: '"Does putting water in the fuel tank of a car ruin the car?"' date: '2016-03-11' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

The car probably won\'t run as well.

Seriously, water in the engine generally means expensive engine repairs.
There is a water filter on the gas line, but it filters water out of
gas. It doesn\'t filter water out of water.
