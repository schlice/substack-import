The question contains fundamental flaws in logic.

The logical flaw is what is called an "argument from omniscience." You
claimed that the U.S. thinks gun ownership is an inalienable \sic\
right given that **literally all of the developed world doesn't feel the
same way**. You would literally have to know what "all of the developed
world" knows, and you cannot know that.

Your argument is simply illogical, which makes it wrong.
