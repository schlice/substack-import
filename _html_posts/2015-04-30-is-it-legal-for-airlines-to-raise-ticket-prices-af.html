Content: [Why is changing your prices illegal?\
\
The whole concept of a free-market system is that prices are not fixed,
and you as a business can charge whatever you choose for a product or
service.\
\
Similarly, your competitors can charge whatever they choose for the same
product or service.\
\
The one with the best price wins.\
\
How would such a scheme as you have suggested serve the business? How
would it earn the company more money, unless you are talking about
hidden fees?\
\
And isn\'t what you\'re taking about called supply and demand? If
demand increases, all else being equal, price goes up. If supply
increases, all else being equal, price goes down. If demand on a
particular service increases, all else being equal, price goes up for
that service.\
\
How the airline determines when to raise the price for a ticket is an
unexplained sniglet of the universe that I will never
understand.]
