--- title: '"What items should an infant first-aid kit contain?"' date: '2018-10-19' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

The only items I can think of off the top of my head that would be
different than any other first aid kit would be an infant CPR mask and
maybe a bulb syringe. Other than that, dressings and bandages, while
smaller, should be the same as a full size kit.
