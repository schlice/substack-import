Answer









Content: 

The original iPhone had model number A1203. This was the 1st Generation
iPhone. The original. The OG.

The next iPhone to be released was the iPhone 3G, and was identified by
model number A1324 or A1241. This was the second generation iPhone.

The next iPhone was the iPhone 3GS and sported model numbers A1325 and
A1303. This was the third generation iPhone.

Starting with the iPhone 4, the generation was in the name (mostly).
