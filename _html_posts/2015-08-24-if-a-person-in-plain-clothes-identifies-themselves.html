--- title: '"If a person in plain clothes identifies themselves as a police officer and then goes on, by request or otherwise, to display what are supposedly his/her credentials, what are the chances they are fake and that you, a civilian could spot that?"' date: '2015-08-24' tags: - policy-analysis - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [The chances are not very good.\
\
You can buy badges from any number of places. ID cards, however, are
much harder to fake. Unless you\'ve seen the agency\'s ID cards before,
you\'re not likely to be able to tell a fake from a real ID card.\
\
You can always call 911 and ask the dispatcher to assist you with
identifying the officer. It should only take a few minutes, and these
days a plainclothes officer or detective should allow you that luxury.
There is no set policy, usually, around doing this, but I would expect a
plainclothes officer would understand if someone is
wary.]
