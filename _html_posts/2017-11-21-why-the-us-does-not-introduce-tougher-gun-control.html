--- title: '"Why the US does not introduce tougher gun control laws?"' date: '2017-11-21' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

If you're asking whether or not the US government will ever change gun
laws in such a way that guns will effectively be unavailable to the
public, the answer is no. The reason is because *they can't*.

The Second Amendment prevents them from banning guns. In order to ban
guns, they must first repeal the Second Amendment.
