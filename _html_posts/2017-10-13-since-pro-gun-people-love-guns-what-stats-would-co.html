--- title: '"Since pro-gun people love guns what stats would convince them less access to guns and certain guns being banned would result in a safer America?"' date: '2017-10-13' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Really, the only statistics that would make a difference are ones that
show how restricting access to guns to law-abiding citizens makes
non-law-abiding citizens less able to do their jobs, and thus makes
everyone safer.

Since these statistics will never be honestly produced, the effective
answer is that there are no statistics that will ever change the minds
of the pro gun camp.
