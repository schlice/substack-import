--- title: '"Can suddenly having the inability to complete a sneeze most of the time be a rare symptom of MS?"' date: '2021-05-21' tags: - health-autoimmune layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Most likely not, but the actual answer is none of us can say for sure.

The neurological components which are involved in the inability to
sneeze are most generally not affected by MS, but your results may vary.

> Literature suggests that the most common cause of unexplained
> inability to sneeze is psychiatric. \[1\](#AyJFp){#cite-AyJFp
> .citation_link}

You can only be actually diagnosed by a neurologist.

\

Original question:

**Can suddenly having the inability to complete a sneeze most of the
time be a rare symptom of MS?**
