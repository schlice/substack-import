--- title: '"What types of happiness can money surely buy?"' date: '2022-01-27' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Money can buy happiness, but only up to a certain point.

Numerous studies have found that there is a dollar-for-dollar increase
in happiness (or reduction in negative emotions) up until about
\$70,000. Then, as dollars increase, the reduction in negative emotions
decreases, up to a zero reduction at around \$200,000.
