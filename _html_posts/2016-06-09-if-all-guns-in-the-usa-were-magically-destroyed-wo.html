--- title: '"If all guns in the USA were magically destroyed, would you vote for the same gun rules in place today?"' date: '2016-06-09' tags: - law layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

Unless you can simultaneously destroy all guns from existence along with
the knowledge of how to build one, destroying all guns in one location
will only create a new market for illegal guns.

If you outlaw guns, you'll create a tremendous black market for guns,
and only the people willing to violate the law will have the guns which
they will smuggle into the country illegally or manufacture illegally or
3-D print illegally.
