**You are driving down a narrow road and are passing between parked cars
on both sides when you meet an oncoming driver who forces his way
through. What do you do?**

Well, it seems you're at an impasse now, aren't you.

One of you is going to have to move. If it were me, I'd back up until I
could move over. I'm just not in that big of a hurry that I have to
force my way through.
