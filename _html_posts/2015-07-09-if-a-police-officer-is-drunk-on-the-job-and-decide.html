--- title: '"If a police officer is drunk on the job and decides to arrest someone, is the arrest valid?"' date: '2015-07-09' tags: - law-enforcement layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [Yes, the arrest is valid, assuming that the grounds on
which the suspect was arrested are valid.\
\
If the officer was actually drunk on the job, he should have immediately
been reported.]
