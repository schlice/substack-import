They are still humans. Plus, they are still Americans and are protected
by the 8th Amendment, which prevents the government from inflicting
"cruel and unusual punishment."

If lethal injection is being challenged as cruel punishment, there\'s no
way that using criminals as lab rats will pass muster either.
