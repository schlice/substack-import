NIV, and the 1995 version of the NASB.

And my favorite app to do that is
[YouVersion(https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=6ecbdf&url=https://app.bible.com/app-android){.external_link
rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank"
onclick="return Q.openUrl(this);"
qt-tooltip="bible.com"}]{.qlink_container}. Some of the things I love
about it:

- Read yourself, or listen to an audio version of any chapter or verse
  you happen to be reading.
- Easily switch between multiple Bible versions while reading
- Highlight important verses, and save your highlights across YouVersion
  apps
- Add Bookmarks, Highlights, and public or private Notes to any verse in
  the Bible
- Share Bible verses on social media
- Create Bible Images, which are shareable Bible art using stock photos
  or your own pictures
- Built-in community Friends features help you experience the Bible with
  others
- Offline versions allow you to download many of the Bible versions
  directly to your device.
