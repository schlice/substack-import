Content: 

Hello

The Dell Active Stylus which works with the Venue 8 Pro does not work on
capacitive touchscreens as a standard stylus. I tested one on my Galaxy
Tab A and it did nothing. The Active Stylus is designed to pair and work
with select Dell models:

- Inspiron 7568 (FHD Only) (the wake up feature is not available)
- Latitude 11 (5175)
- Latitude 11 (5179)
- Latitude 7275
- Venue 10 Pro (5056)
- Venue 8 Pro (5855)
- XPS 12 (9250)

You can find more information and support information about the Active
Stylus
