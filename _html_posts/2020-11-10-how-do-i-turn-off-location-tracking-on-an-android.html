How do I turn off location tracking on an Android & iPhone?

On Android (current versions), go to Settings and select
Biometrics and security. Scroll down to Location and turn it
off.

If you want to make changes to individual apps instead, go to
Settings, Apps, scroll to the app in question and click it, and
scroll down to App Settings. Click Permissions and you can
individually enable or disable each of the permissions for that app.

On iOS, go into the Settings app and click Privacy. Then click
Location Services and turn it off.

If you want to make changes to individual apps instead, leave Location
Settings on and scroll down to the individual app. Select it and change
Location access to *Always, While Using the App, Ask Next Time,* or
*Never.*
