--- title: '"Is it possible to be an atheist if one is unsure about the existence of God but still believes that God does not exist? If so, how can this be explained?"' date: '2024-08-05' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

An *atheist* says, "I know there is no god." An *agnostic* says,
"I don't know whether or not there is a god."

Agnostics come in two flavors: *ordinary* agnostics say, "We don't know
whether or not God exists." *Ornery* agnostics say, "We *can't* know
whether or not God exists.

So it sounds like this person really isn't an atheist, because an
atheist says that they know that there is no God. It is only the
agnostic who says that they don't know whether or not there is a God.
