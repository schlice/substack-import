This is a great time to introduce the basics of gun safety to your
child.

You really shouldn't need to go into the "sometimes killing is
acceptable" schtick because it's a bit beyond the 7 year old mind. For
now, you can tell your 7 year old that guns are tools, but they can be
very dangerous if they are not used correctly. If you ever see one:
stop, don't touch, leave the area, and tell an adult.

By the way, I shamelessly stole that from the NRA's Gun Safety Program,
with Eddie Eagle. This would be a really really good time to [[introduce
your child to Eddie
Eagle](https://www.quora.com/_/redirect?sig=714943&url=https://eddieeagle.nra.org/){.external_link
