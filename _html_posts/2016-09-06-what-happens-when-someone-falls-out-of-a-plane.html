--- title: '"What happens when someone falls out of a plane?"' date: '2016-09-06' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

It's not the fall that kills you. It's the rapid deceleration at the
conclusion of the fall that tends to be problematic.

It's a fairly safe bet that you're going to die. Although it is possible
to fall from that height and live, it's highly unlikely.
