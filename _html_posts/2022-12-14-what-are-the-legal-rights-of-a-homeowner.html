Answer









Content: 

The homeowner doesn't pay dues after foreclosure, because the homeowner
no longer owns the home.

Once the foreclosure completes, the home is sold, and the proceeds from
the sale are used to pay the back dues, late fees, and legal fees owed
to the HOA.
