In order for anyone (regardless of political party) to care, Mueller's
investigation needs to find conclusive evidence that *candidate*
Trump worked directly, or through his surrogates, with Russia in an
attempt to influence the outcome of the election.

To this point, no such evidence exists. And until such evidence
exists, this is a non-starter. This whole thing is a giant waste of
time, money, and attention.
