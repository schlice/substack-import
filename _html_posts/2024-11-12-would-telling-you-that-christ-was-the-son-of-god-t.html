Content: 

Sure it is! Of course, it's only more plausible if you're trying to find
a *natural* explanation for a *supernatural* event.

Nevertheless, the truth is that Jesus was born of a virgin through a
*supernatural* event. "Supernatural" literally means "beyond the
natural." That means that it is something which *cannot* be
explained using natural means.

So if you're trying to explain a *supernatural* event using
*natural* means, you might end up missing the significance of what
happened.

So why is being born of a virgin important?

Because sin is passed down from generation to generation through the
*male.*

> Therefore, just as sin came into the world through one man, and death
> through sin, and so death spread to all men because all sinned ---
> Romans 5:12 ESV

But when Jesus was conceived, it was without a man. Therefore, Jesus
was born without original sin. He lived His entire life without sin.
Thus, he was able to take on the sin of the world and redeem humanity.
He was able to do this *because* He was sinless. He is the only person
who could do it.

Thus, Jesus *not* being conceived by a man was the linchpin in His
ability to take on the sins of the world.
