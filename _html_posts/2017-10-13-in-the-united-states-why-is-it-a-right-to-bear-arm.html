--- title: '"In the United States, why is it a right to bear arms when it is only a privilege to drive a motor vehicle?"' date: '2017-10-13' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

In the United States, our rights are defined in the Constitution. The
right to bear arms is listed in the Constitution. The right to drive a
car is not listed.
