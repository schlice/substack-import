--- title: '"What would happen if a president refused to take their oath of office? Would there be no president until they decided to take it?"' date: '2025-01-22' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: 

If the President-elect fails to take the oath of office, then he does
not become President. Section 3 of the 20th Amendment says that the Vice
President, who took the oath just before the President-elect, would
serve as the President, and he would appoint a Vice President.

In theory, the President-elect should be able to later take the oath
and assume the office of the President to which he was elected, but this
is untested territory.
