--- title: '"Why do we put more regulations on green built homes than on handguns?"' date: '2015-05-15' tags: - uncategorized layout: post --- Quora Answer 





Answer









Content: [Because there is no right in the Constitution to own a
green-built home. We can put whatever restrictions we choose on buying
green-built homes.\
\
We can make it exceedingly difficult to buy a green-built home because
you have no right to own one. We can\'t make it exceedingly difficult to
own a gun because you have a right to own one.]
