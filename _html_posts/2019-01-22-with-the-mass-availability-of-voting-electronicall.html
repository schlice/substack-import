**With the mass availability of voting electronically, will the US ever
have national ballot measures like states do? (like a vote on the border
wall) Is that currently even possible constitutionally?**

No. We do not have a system in the United States for a "national
referendum."

As we are a representative republic, our representatives handle voting
on our behalf. So a vote on a national ballot measure would be something
that Congress votes for.
